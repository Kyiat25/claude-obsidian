# The Warlock

**Conduits of Forbidden Power**

> *"You didn't earn this power. You negotiated for it. The distinction matters more than you think."*

---

## The Price of the Bargain

Every spell the Warlock casts is a transaction. Not a studied formula, not a borrowed prayer — a deal already struck with something that sees the world on a scale so vast your entire life is a footnote. The Patron doesn't care about your goals. It has its own. And yet here you are, wielding its power, hoping your goals and its goals continue to overlap long enough for you to do what you need to do.

You are the Warlock. Your **Pact Magic** recharges on a short rest — you fight in shorter, harder bursts than any other spellcaster. Your **Eldritch Blast** is your most reliable damage source, customisable through **Eldritch Invocations** into a weapon unlike anything a wizard could produce. Your **Pact Boon** defines whether you are a melee fighter, a familiar-supported scout, or a ritual-casting utility engine.

In FEARS, your Momentum comes through sustained pressure. Eldritch Blast is consistent. Hex stacks. Your reactions — provided by your Patron through the Patronal Reactions feature at 10th level — are thematically tied to the specific entity you serve.

---

## The Warlock's Essence

You are a sustained damage dealer and control caster with unmatched short-rest recovery. Your invocations let you customise your combat role more than almost any other class. **Mystic Arcanum** at high levels gives you access to spells that other short-rest casters simply cannot reach.

You struggle in fights requiring prolonged nova damage without rests. You excel in adventuring days with multiple short rests and encounters that benefit from sustained, flexible magical pressure.

---

## Complexity Assessment: MODERATE (5/10) 

The Warlock tracks:  
• Pact Magic slots (all same level, short rest recovery)
• Invocations Known (customise as you level)
• Pact Boon features and Enhanced Boon at 6th 
• Mystic Arcanum spells (once per long rest each)
• Class Reaction pool (PB uses per short rest) 

Recommended for players who enjoy customisation, sustained damage, and short-rest resource management.

## Class Features

### Hit Points

- **Hit Dice:** 1d8 per Warlock level
- **Hit Points at 1st Level:** 8 + your Constitution modifier
- **Hit Points at Higher Levels:** 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per Warlock level after 1st

### Proficiencies

- **Armour:** Light armour, medium armour, shields
- **Weapons:** Simple weapons
- **Tools:** None
- **Saving Throws:** Wisdom, Charisma
- **Skills:** Choose two from Arcana, Deception, History, Intimidation, Investigation, Memory, Nature, Religion, Resolve, and Streetwise

### Starting Equipment

You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:

- (a) a light crossbow and 20 bolts or (b) any simple weapon
- (a) a component pouch or (b) a wyrd focus
- (a) a scholar's pack or (b) a dungeoneer's pack
- Leather armour, any simple weapon, and two daggers

---

## Quick Build

To quickly create a Warlock, follow these steps:

1. **Ability Scores:** Make Charisma your highest score, followed by Constitution.
2. **Lineage and Heritage:** Any combination works; Tiefling, Drow, and Half-Elf are thematic but not required.
3. **Background:** Charlatan, Sage, or any background with Deception or Arcana.
4. **Combat Strategy:** Apply Hex as a bonus action, follow with Eldritch Blast enhanced by your chosen invocations. Take short rests frequently — your slots are most valuable when refreshed.

## Core Class Features

### Otherworldly Patron (1st Level)

At 1st level, you forge a pact with an otherworldly being. Your Patron grants subclass features at 1st, 6th, 10th, 14th, and 15th levels. Patrons include entities such as Fiends, Archfey, or Great Old Ones, each granting unique abilities and spells.

---

### Pact Magic (1st Level)

Your connection to your Patron grants powerful but limited spellcasting.

- **Spell Slots:** All your spell slots are of the highest level available to you, as shown in the Warlock table.
- **Recovery:** Regain all expended spell slots after a **short or long rest**.
- **Spellcasting Ability:** Charisma.
- **Spell Save DC** = 8 + PB + CHA modifier
- **Spell Attack Modifier** = PB + CHA modifier

---

### Eldritch Blast (1st Level)

A beam of crackling energy, unique to Warlocks. On a hit, deal 1d10 force damage. Fires 2 beams at 5th level, 3 beams at 9th level, and 4 beams at 14th level.

Each beam targets the same or different creatures (each beam requires its own attack roll). Invocations modify this cantrip extensively — treat it as the foundation of your combat identity.

---

### Eldritch Invocations (2nd Level)

Fragments of forbidden knowledge grant you magical abilities. Learn Invocations as shown in the table and swap one each time you gain a level. Some require a minimum Warlock level or specific features.

**Sample Invocations:**
- **Agonizing Blast:** Add CHA modifier to each beam's damage.
- **Armor of Shadows:** Cast *Mage Armor* on yourself at will.
- **Devil's Sight:** See in magical darkness out to 120 feet.
- **Repelling Blast:** Creatures hit can be pushed 10 feet away.
- **Accursed Smite (Prereq: 5th, Pact of the Blade):** Critical hits reduce target DR by 2 until start of your next turn; if no DR, deal 1d8 additional force damage.
- **Chaos Shot:** Reduce Called Shot penalty by 1 when using Eldritch Blast for a Called Shot; on success, target has disadvantage on next Evasion roll or area effect save.
- *Full invocation list available in the Warlock Invocation Compendium.*

---

### Whispers of the Echoing Pact (2nd Level)

Re-attune a number of Echo Glyphs equal to your Proficiency Bonus during a short rest (10 minutes of communion with your patron's sigil, familiar, or focus). Once per short rest. Glyphs must still be bound to spells you can cast.

---

### Pact Boon (3rd Level)

Upon forging your pact, you receive a boon. Choose one:

**Pact of the Blade:** Create a magical pact weapon in your empty hand as an action (any melee weapon form; counts as magical; use CHA for attack and damage). The weapon disappears if more than 5 feet away for 1 minute or you dismiss it. You can bond with an existing weapon via a 1-hour ritual. *Enhanced Pact (6th):* When you take the Attack action with your pact weapon, you attack twice.

**Pact of the Chain:** Learn *find familiar* and can cast it as a ritual without material components. Your familiar can take one of these special forms: blink dog, imp, pseudodragon, or quasit. While on the same plane, you communicate telepathically and see/hear through its senses. *Enhanced Pact (6th):* Familiar gains temp HP equal to PB + CHA on short rests; when a creature within 5 feet of your familiar is attacked, familiar can use its reaction to grant you advantage on the attack roll.

**Pact of the Tome:** Receive a Book of Shadows. Choose three cantrips from any class spell list — while the book is on your person, you can cast them. Inscribe two 1st- or 2nd-circle rituals from the Wyrd spell list (don't count against spells known). *Enhanced Pact (6th):* Add additional Wyrd rituals (circle ≤ half your Warlock level, rounded up) by spending 2 hours and 50 gp per circle. Learn one spell from any class list that doesn't count against your known spells; change it after a long rest.

---

### Enhanced Boon (6th Level)

Your Pact Boon becomes more potent (see Pact Boon descriptions above for each Enhanced version). Additionally, patron-specific enhancements:

**Pact of the Blade — Eldritch Warcaster:** Advantage on reaction attacks while wielding your pact weapon; use CHA instead of DEX for Evasion in light armour; when you take melee damage, use your reaction to make a melee attack with your pact weapon.

**Pact of the Tome — Mystic Barrier:** Gain temp HP equal to half Warlock level + CHA modifier after each short rest; use INT or CHA instead of DEX for Evasion while unarmoured.

**Pact of the Chain — Spiritbinder's Aegis:** While familiar is within 10 feet, gain resistance to necrotic, radiant, and psychic damage; once per long rest, if you reach 0 HP, familiar can sacrifice itself to restore you to 1 HP.

---

### Eldritch Resilience (9th Level)

**Pact Shield:** As a reaction, reduce incoming damage by your Warlock level + CHA modifier. Uses equal to your CHA modifier per long rest.

**Woven Fate:** When you make a saving throw or ability check and roll a 1, treat it as a 2 instead. Uses equal to half your PB per long rest.

**Supernatural Resolve:** Advantage on saving throws against being charmed, frightened, or affected by madness.

---

### Heroic Boon (10th Level)

Choose one:

**Boon of the Eldritch Conduit:** When you cast a Warlock spell using Pact Magic, immediately cast a Warlock cantrip as a bonus action without a spell slot. When you deal damage with a Warlock spell of 1st level or higher, gain temp HP equal to PB + CHA modifier. When you fail a concentration check, spend 1 Pact Magic slot to automatically succeed.

**Boon of the Forbidden Pact:** Choose one spell of 6th level or lower from any class list — cast it once per long rest without a spell slot. When you drop to 0 HP, you don't fall unconscious until the end of your next turn. When you use Eldritch Blast, deal force damage equal to your CHA modifier to one additional creature within 10 feet of your target.

---

### Patronal Reactions (10th Level)

Gain a powerful reaction ability based on your Otherworldly Patron. Uses equal to your Proficiency Bonus per short rest.

**The Fiend — Infernal Rebuke:** When a creature within 10 feet hits you with an attack, deal fire damage equal to CHA modifier + PB to that creature.

**The Archfey — Fey Step:** When a creature hits you with an attack, teleport up to 15 feet to an unoccupied space you can see and impose disadvantage on the triggering attack.

**The Great Old One — Psychic Backlash:** When a creature you can see targets you with an attack, force a WIS save (DC = Spell Save DC). On failure: 2d8 psychic damage and disadvantage on the attack roll.

---

### Mystic Arcanum (11th Level and beyond)

Gain a 6th-circle spell that can be cast once per long rest without using a spell slot. Gain 7th-circle at 13th level, 8th-circle at 15th level, and 9th-circle at 17th level. Each usable once per long rest.

---

### Eldritch Resilience — Injury Downgrade (15th Level)

Once per day, when you would suffer a Major Injury, downgrade it to a Minor Injury. Your patron's power shields its investment in you.

---

### Patron's Favour (18th Level)

Use your Pact Magic to cast any spell you know of 5th-circle or lower from any spell list.

---

### Epic Boon (20th Level)

Choose one:

**Eldritch Restoration:** If you start your turn with no Pact Magic uses, use your action to beseech your patron to immediately restore all expended uses. Once per short rest. When you cast a spell using a Pact Magic slot, treat it as if cast at one slot level higher (up to 6th circle, once per turn).

**Boundless Invocations:** Use any Eldritch Invocation with limited uses an unlimited number of times. Gain one additional Invocation (ignoring level prerequisites). When you use Eldritch Blast, double the number of beams created. Once per short rest.

## Last Stand Options

When the Warlock reaches their final hour, their patron watches. Whether the pact ends in oblivion, sacrifice, or the patron stepping through the veil itself — the Warlock ensures their death leaves a mark that cannot be erased.

**Rules for Last Stand:** Invoked when you would be reduced to 0 hit points, roll a death saving throw, or die outright. A free action requiring no reaction or consciousness. Once resolved, the character permanently dies.

---

### 1. Eldritch Oblivion

*Your patron's power erupts from you, devouring everything in its path and leaving behind nothing but shadow and silence.*

**Effect:**
- You unleash a 60-foot radius blast of necrotic and force energy centred on yourself.
- All creatures of your choice within the area must make a **Constitution saving throw** (DC = 10 + PB + CHA modifier).
- **Failure:** 12d10 necrotic damage and restrained for 1 minute as dark tendrils bind them.
- **Success:** Half damage and not restrained.
- The area becomes magically dark (as *Darkness*) for 1 minute and creatures other than you cannot see through it.

---

### 2. Pactbound Sacrifice

*You trade your very soul to your patron for one final, unbreakable boon — binding your allies and enemies in a deadly bargain.*

**Effect:**
- Choose up to 5 allies within 60 feet. For the next 1 minute:
  - Allies gain temporary hit points equal to twice your Warlock level.
  - Allies' attacks deal an additional 2d10 force damage on a hit.
  - Allies automatically succeed on their next saving throw within the duration.
- Choose up to 5 enemies within the same range:
  - Enemies take 2d10 psychic damage at the start of their turn for the next minute.
  - Enemies must make a Wisdom saving throw (DC = 10 + PB + CHA modifier): on a failure, they are frightened of your allies for the duration.

---

### 3. Dark Concordance

*Your patron steps through the veil, taking your place on the battlefield and delivering their wrath upon those who dared strike you down.*

**Effect:**
- Summon an **Avatar of your Patron** in an unoccupied space within 30 feet of you.
  - The Avatar uses the stat block of a CR 15 creature appropriate to your patron's theme:
    - *Fiend:* Balor or Pit Fiend
    - *Great Old One:* Star Spawn Larva Mage or Aboleth
    - *Archfey:* Archfey Spirit (custom stats, GM's discretion)
- The Avatar acts immediately after you on the initiative order and remains for 1 minute.
- The Avatar is immune to all forms of charm, fear, and banishment while it exists.
- At the end of the duration, the Avatar vanishes and the area around it becomes corrupted: for 1 hour, creatures of your choice within 60 feet take 2d6 necrotic damage when they start their turn in the area.

| Last Stand Option | Effect | Best Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Eldritch Oblivion** | 60-foot necrotic blast; 12d10 damage + restrained; magical darkness for 1 minute. | Pure devastation against large groups. |
| **Pactbound Sacrifice** | Allies gain temp HP, bonus damage, and auto saves. Enemies take psychic damage and are frightened. | Supporting allies while crippling enemies. |
| **Dark Concordance** | Summon a CR 15 Avatar of your patron for 1 minute; area becomes corrupted for 1 hour. | Summoning a terrifying force for boss fights. |

## Class Reactions

Warlocks have access to reactions that reflect their patron's nature and their mastery of eldritch power. These reactions use your **Proficiency Bonus pool**, refreshing on short or long rest.

### Eldritch Counter (2nd Level)

**Trigger:** A creature within 60 feet casts a spell targeting you or an ally.
**Effect:** As a reaction, fire an Eldritch Blast beam at the caster. On hit, their spell is cast with disadvantage on the attack roll or grants advantage on saving throws against it until end of their next turn.

---

### Darkness Veil (6th Level)

**Trigger:** You or an ally within 30 feet is targeted by a ranged attack.
**Effect:** A shroud of eldritch darkness briefly envelops the target. The attack is made with disadvantage. If the attack misses, the target gains +1 Momentum as they step through the darkness perfectly.
