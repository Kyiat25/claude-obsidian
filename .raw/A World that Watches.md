# Navigating Disposition, Reputation, and Mental Consequence

---
# 📜 Overview

In _[[Dór-o Estel]]_, your actions are not footnotes. They echo. Whether you're a noble hero, a repentant outlaw, or a mad visionary, the world responds to how you're remembered, how you're perceived, and how you're falling apart.

This guide introduces the interconnected systems that track your **public face**, your **inner state**, and the **world’s reaction** to both.

It contains the tools you need to:

- Understand how **NPCs perceive and interact with you** ([[Disposition]])
- Track your **criminal status and social infamy** ([[Wanted Score]])
- Navigate the **underground world of forbidden trade** ([[Black Market]])
- Create and protect **secret bases and networks** ([[Hideouts]])
- Maintain your **mental resilience** and **cognitive clarity** ([[Sanity]], [[Focus]], [[Corruption]])
- Manipulate or survive the chaos of **fate itself** ([[Luck]])
- Leverage your **street knowledge**, **forbidden insight**, or **fractured memories** ([[Streetwise]], [[Occult]], [[Memory]])

Each system is modular, but together they form a living feedback loop. If you act boldly, the world will whisper your name. If you act carelessly, it may curse it. Or be reckless and use [[01_Rules/06_Optional/The Whisper Engine]]

---
## 🎭 **Disposition: The World’s First Impression**

Every NPC has a starting attitude toward you—**Hostile**, **Suspicious**, **Indifferent**, **Friendly**, or **Helpful**. This Disposition affects everything from shopkeeper prices and guard behaviour to combat strategy and quest availability.

Your **[[Disposition]] is shaped by**:

- Your **[[Wanted Score]]**
- Your **[[Reputation]]** (heroic, cruel, notorious)
- Your **[[Sanity]] and mental state**
- How well you read and react to your environment (**[[Streetwise]]**, **[[Focus]]**)
- Your recent **actions**—rescue or betrayal, aid or threat

Disposition is never fixed. Every encounter, word, or decision shifts how the world sees you.

---
## 🚨 **Wanted Score: What the World Knows You’ve Done**

Every crime has a cost. From petty theft to regicide, your **[[Wanted Score]]** increases with unlawful acts and becomes a **measurable mechanic** affecting:

- NPC suspicion and hostility
- Law enforcement response (fines, pursuit, bounty hunters, executions)
- Access to services (inns, merchants, black markets)
- Your ability to **blend in** or **move freely** between cities

A higher score makes you infamous, but fame isn’t always bad. Some NPCs admire boldness. Others fear you. A **Score of 7+** means you’re an **Enemy of the State**—and often, no safe harbor remains.

---
## 🛒 **Black Market: The Shadow Below Civilization**

When the law shuts its doors, the shadows open theirs.

The **[[Black Market]]** system governs:

- Access to **illicit goods** (forged documents, cursed blades, rare spells)
- **Hidden magical services** (curse removal, memory recovery, forbidden rituals)
- Your **Black Market Reputation**, which determines how trusted, feared, or respected you are in criminal circles

But every deal has risks:

- Betrayal, faulty goods, or hidden costs
- Drawing the attention of rival gangs or law enforcement
- Tying yourself to morally grey or outright monstrous figures

You can be a villain here—or a folk hero smuggling medicine into a city that’s banned healing magic.

---
## 🏠 **Hideouts and Safe Houses: Vanishing Without Leaving**

When your name becomes too loud, you’ll need to disappear. **[[Hideouts]]** are secret sanctuaries for rest, recovery, and planning.

You can:

- Build your own (urban, wilderness, or mobile)
- Hire guards, traps, or informants
- Store black market goods or stage heists
- Create a **network** that supports your influence or evasion

But beware: **the higher your Wanted Score, the greater the risk** your hideout is discovered. Disloyal staff, persistent bounty hunters, and even magical tracking glyphs might find you.

# 🧠 **The Mental Triad: Sanity, Focus, Corruption**

Your mind is not immune to the horrors of _[[Dór-o Estel]]_. Three separate but interwoven systems track your mental state:

### 🌀 **Sanity**:

Your stability against existential fear, trauma, or Eldritch truth.

- Losing Sanity brings hallucinations, paranoia, or madness.
- Sanity collapse leads to **[[Corruption]]**, transformation, or death.

### 🎯 **Focus**:

Your mental discipline and resistance under magical or social pressure.

- Helps you concentrate during glyph overload, interrogation, or illusion.
- Losing Focus causes spell feedback, false memories, or corruption spikes.

### ☣️ **Corruption**:

The metaphysical rot from Eldritch contact, broken glyphs, or madness.

- Grows with repeated failures or forbidden acts
- Alters your body and soul—until you become something else

All three systems can affect how the world **sees you**. [[Corruption]] changes your appearance. [[Sanity]] shifts your behaviour. [[Focus]] determines how well you maintain control.

---
## 🎲 **Luck: The Pendulum of Fate**

In _[[Dór-o Estel]]_, even fate has a mechanic.  
Roll three natural 1s before a short rest? You’re **Cursed**.  
Three natural 20s? You’re **Blessed**.

[[Luck]] effects can:

- Turn NPC attitudes unexpectedly
- Empower or punish your next social or combat action
- Trigger divine, fey, or infernal attention
- Modify Disposition randomly—because sometimes charm isn’t enough, but the Weave smiles anyway

---
# 🧠 **Memory, Occult, and Streetwise: The Hidden Currents**

### 🧩 **Memory** helps you:

- Reconstruct lore, rituals, and past relationships
- Avoid missteps with NPCs or regions you’ve encountered before
- Resist false memories and echo-based illusions

### ☣️ **Occult** lets you:

- Interpret forbidden glyphs, rituals, and dream-visions
- Understand the motives or codes of cults, corrupted zones, or glyph-warped NPCs
- Risk social fallout—revealing you know too much

### 🕵️ **Streetwise** enables you to:

- Navigate cities, gangs, or political hotspots
- Find black markets, identify turf, spot setups
- Shift NPC attitudes from _Suspicious → Indifferent_, especially while undercover

# 🌍 **A Living World, A Reactive Narrative**

All of these systems serve one truth:  
**Your story is not told in isolation. It echoes through the minds, rumours, and fears of others.**

You are more than your stats. You are:

- A name spoken in alleyways and royal halls.
- A mask remembered by city guards and broken cultists.
- A presence that shifts the Weave just by walking into a room.

This is the narrative you write with every step, and these mechanics help ensure the world never stops listening.
