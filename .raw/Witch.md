# The Witch

**Intermediaries of Primal Magic**

> *"The Fireball hits everything at once and is done. The curse starts on one creature and radiates outward based on what the enemies do. A smart enemy who keeps passing saves stays clean. One who panics becomes a node that infects everything around it."*

---

## The Web Between Things

She hadn't moved from her spot at the edge of the room. She hadn't needed to. The first hex went on the captain at round one. When he failed his Wisdom check, it spread to the soldier beside him. When that soldier missed on his next attack, her Fateful Twist pulled the hit into the wall. Three rounds in, every creature in the room had disadvantage on something, and none of them had landed a clean strike.

You are the Witch. Not a blaster, not a healer — a patient architect of misfortune. **Malefic Touch** brands a target with spiritual harm as a bonus action, setting up every other feature you have. **Witches Mark** turns your reaction into a persistent curse that subtly undermines everything your enemy does. **Hex Contagion** at 9th level is the class's identity made mechanical: one failure spreads to the next creature, which spreads to the next, a living epidemic of bad luck.

In FEARS, your Momentum engine flows through your hexes. Every Bardic Inspiration-style Fateful Twist that negates an attack also denies the attacker their Momentum gain. **Hex Momentum** at 11th level means you gain +1 Momentum each time a hexed creature fails a save against your effects — the more your web spreads, the faster you build.

---

## The Witch's Essence

You are the party's sustained control engine. You do not win fights quickly — you win them inevitably, by making every enemy action less reliable until they have nothing left. **Curse Resonance** at 6th level means your cantrip strikes against hexed targets regenerate your reaction pool. **Web of Malice** at 17th level means your curse chains automatically, requiring no action from you.

Your weakness is initiative and burst pressure — if enemies close fast and hit hard before you establish control, you struggle. Your strength is any fight that lasts more than three rounds, particularly against many enemies.

> **Design Note on Hex Contagion:** The unlimited spread of Hex-Spread is intentional. The Charisma save is the sole balancing mechanism — this is a key identity differentiator from AOE spells.

---

## Complexity Assessment: HIGH (8/10)      

The Witch tracks:    
• Malefic Touch uses (bonus action, per turn) 
• Witches Mark (one persistent hex, PB uses per long rest)  
• Hex die size (d4 → d10)  
• Class Reaction pool (PB uses, short rest)  
• Hex-Spread creatures (cascade tracking)  
• Witch Implements (1–3 bound)  
• Spell slots (full caster, long rest)  

Recommended for experienced players who enjoy complex battlefield management and sustained debuff strategies.

## Class Features

### Hit Points

- **Hit Dice:** 1d8 per Witch level
- **Hit Points at 1st Level:** 8 + your Constitution modifier
- **Hit Points at Higher Levels:** 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per Witch level after 1st

### Proficiencies

- **Armour:** Light armour
- **Weapons:** Simple weapons
- **Tools:** Herbalism kit
- **Saving Throws:** Wisdom, Charisma
- **Skills:** Choose two from Arcana, Crafting, Medicine, Memory, Nature, Perception, Persuasion, Resolve, and Survival

### Starting Equipment

- (a) a shortbow and 20 arrows or (b) any simple weapon
- (a) two daggers or (b) any simple melee weapon
- Leather armour, an explorer's pack, and a druidic focus

---

## Quick Build

1. **Ability Scores:** Make Charisma your highest score, followed by Wisdom.
2. **Lineage:** Any combination; Half-Elf, Tiefling, and Gnome are thematic.
3. **Background:** Sage, Hermit, or any background with Arcana or Insight.
4. **Combat Strategy:** Use Malefic Touch on your bonus action each turn before acting. Apply Witches Mark via reaction to disrupt key enemy rolls. Let Hex Contagion spread after the first bloodied or fallen enemy.

## Core Class Features

### Coven (1st Level)

You dedicate yourself to a magical tradition at 1st level. Your Coven grants specific spells and abilities at 1st, 6th, 10th, and 14th levels. Coven spells are always prepared and don't count against your spells known.

Example Covens: **Coven of the Hidden Moon** (forbidden knowledge, shadow magic), **Coven of the Verdant Wood** (healing, growth, life).

---

### Spellcasting (1st Level)

Charisma is your spellcasting ability. Your magic is shaped through negotiation with spirits.

- **Spell Save DC** = 8 + PB + Charisma modifier
- **Spell Attack Modifier** = PB + Charisma modifier

Spells are known (not prepared). Ritual spells with the ritual tag can be cast without slots. You can use a druidic focus or your Witch's Implement as a spellcasting focus.

---

### Malefic Touch (1st Level)

*Evocation Cantrip — Witch Exclusive. Casting Time: 1 bonus action. Range: 30 feet.*

Choose one creature you can see within 30 feet. Until the start of your next turn, that creature is **hexed by you** and has **disadvantage on the next saving throw** it makes against one of your spells.

At 5th level the curse deepens, dealing damage when applied:

| Character Level | Damage |
|---|---|
| 1st–4th | No damage — mark only |
| 5th–10th | 1d6 necrotic or radiant |
| 11th–16th | 2d6 necrotic or radiant |
| 17th+ | 3d6 necrotic or radiant |

The damage type — **necrotic or radiant** — is chosen when you first learn this cantrip.

> **Hexed:** A creature is hexed by you when marked by Malefic Touch or Witches Mark. All features referencing "hexed" creatures apply to both. Malefic Touch's hex lasts until the start of your next turn; Witches Mark's hex is persistent.

---

### Spirit Binding (2nd Level)

You gain the ability to bind spirits into mystical objects called **Witch Implements**. At 2nd level, choose one implement (e.g., Brewer's Cauldron, Soul Candle, Nightflyer). Each grants unique abilities. At 5th and 15th levels, bind additional spirits into new implements. Creating or replacing an implement requires a 1-hour ritual during a short or long rest.

---

### Fateful Twist (2nd Level)

*Reaction — Class Reaction Pool*

**Trigger:** A creature hexed by you makes an attack roll.
**Effect:** Impose disadvantage on that attack roll.

Uses equal to your Proficiency Bonus per short or long rest.

---

### Hex Ward (2nd Level)

*Reaction — Class Reaction Pool*

**Trigger:** A creature hexed by you targets you with an attack.
**Effect:** Force a CHA saving throw (DC = 8 + PB + CHA modifier). On failure, the attack is redirected to another valid target within 30 feet of the original attacker.

Uses equal to your Proficiency Bonus per short or long rest.

---

### Witches Mark (3rd Level)

When a creature makes an ability check, attack roll, or saving throw, you may use your **reaction** to curse it. The target rolls your **Hex die** and subtracts the result from their roll. The curse lasts until you dismiss it, the target dies, or you use this feature to curse a different target.

If the cursed target drops to 0 HP, you may use your **bonus action** to move the curse to a new creature within 30 feet.

**Does not require concentration.** Uses equal to your PB per long rest. One creature cursed at a time.

| Witch Level | Hex Die |
|---|---|
| 3rd | d4 |
| 5th | d6 |
| 10th | d8 |
| 15th | d10 |

**Hex Echo:** When you successfully apply Witches Mark via your reaction, you may immediately cast one cantrip you know as part of the same reaction — no action required. The cantrip must target the marked creature or originate from your space.

---

### Life Drinker (5th Level)

When a creature hexed by you misses you with an attack roll, regain HP equal to your Hex die. Once per turn.

---

### Curse Resonance (6th Level)

When a creature hexed by you takes damage from one of your cantrips, regain 1 use of your Class Reaction pool. Once per turn.

---

### Echo Scent (8th Level)

When you are hit by a spell cast by a creature hexed by you, make an **Arcana or Insight check** (DC = 10 + the spell's level). On success, gain access to one Echo Glyph associated with that spell for 24 hours. After your next long rest, permanently learn it by sacrificing another Echo Glyph you know. Once per long rest.

---

### Hex Contagion (9th Level)

When a creature with your Witches Mark becomes Bloodied or drops to 0 HP, the mark seeks new hosts. Each creature within 15 feet of the trigger must make a **Charisma saving throw** (DC = 8 + PB + CHA). On failure, they become **Hex-Spread** — suffering your Hex die penalty on their next roll of any kind.

A Hex-Spread creature can repeat the save at the end of each of their turns, ending the effect on success. When a Hex-Spread creature fails a save while the effect is active, it can spread to new creatures within 15 feet.

**No limit on spread.** The Charisma save is the sole balancing mechanism.

---

### Hex Momentum (11th Level)

When a creature hexed by you (via Witches Mark or Hex Contagion) fails a saving throw against your effect, you gain **+1 Momentum**. Once per turn.

---

### Cursed Misfortune (13th Level)

Your Witches Mark subtly alters luck around the cursed target. While a creature bears your Witches Mark, it cannot benefit from the Lucky trait, Portent dice, or similar effects that allow it to alter the results of its rolls. It also has disadvantage on death saving throws.

---

### Spreading Malice (13th Level)

When you move your Witches Mark to a new creature (via your bonus action after the marked target drops to 0), the new creature must make a Charisma saving throw (DC = 8 + PB + CHA) or immediately become Hex-Spread as well.

---

### Enhanced Hex (14th Level)

Your Witches Mark also applies the *Hex* spell's effects to the target. You no longer need line of sight to apply Witches Mark — you can target hidden creatures, even through obstacles or magical darkness.

---

### Concordance of Spirits (15th Level)

Your Witch Implements resonate with perfect harmony. Once per long rest, cast one concentration spell as a bonus action, even in the same round you cast a levelled spell. Whenever you succeed on a concentration saving throw, gain temporary HP equal to your Hex die.

---

### Web of Malice (17th Level)

**Curse Chain:** When a Hex-Spread creature fails a saving throw, Hex Contagion triggers automatically without your action — spreading from the failing creature to any creatures within 15 feet. Each makes a Charisma save. Can chain once per round.

**Evasion Collapse:** Hex-Spread creatures suffer disadvantage on **all** Evasion rolls, not only against your effects. All attackers benefit.

**Lasting Contagion:** When a Hex-Spread creature succeeds on its save to end the effect, it still suffers your Hex die penalty on the first roll it makes on its next turn before the curse fully releases.

**Momentum Threshold:** While you have 3 or more creatures simultaneously affected by Witches Mark or Hex-Spread, your Hex die increases by one size for the duration of that combat (d4→d6, d6→d8, d8→d10, d10→d12). Drops back if the count falls below 3.

---

### Spell Exchange (20th Level)

When you finish a short rest, replace any number of your known spells with other spells from the Witch spell list of a level for which you have spell slots.

---

### Epic Boon (20th Level)

Choose one:

**Avatar of Spirits:** As an action, transform into an Avatar of Spirits for 1 minute. Gain resistance to all damage, a flying speed of 60 feet with hover, and maintain concentration on one additional spell simultaneously. Enemies within 30 feet take psychic damage equal to your CHA modifier at the start of their turns. Once per long rest.

**Eternal Pact:** When reduced to 0 HP but not killed outright, immediately regain HP equal to half your maximum HP and activate one Witch Implement as part of the same reaction. Your Witches Mark die always rolls its maximum value. You no longer age, cannot be aged magically, and are immune to exhaustion.

## Last Stand Options

**Rules for Last Stand:** Free action when reduced to 0 HP, rolling a death save, or dying outright. Character permanently dies once resolved.

### 1. Hexstorm Manifestation

*You unleash a torrent of hexes, overwhelming enemies with a vortex of curses.*

- All enemies of your choice within 60 feet are affected by Witches Mark (Hex die at maximum value).
- Each makes a CON save (DC = 10 + PB + CHA). Failure: 8d10 necrotic damage + disadvantage on all rolls for 1 minute. Success: half damage; still roll Hex die on next roll.
- For 1 minute, enemies in the area cannot regain HP or remove conditions caused by your hex.

### 2. Spirit Tempest

*The spirits rage free, transforming the battlefield into a storm of primal magic.*

- You summon a 40-foot radius Spirit Tempest centred on yourself for 1 minute.
- Enemies entering or starting their turn in the tempest take 6d10 force damage and make a WIS save (DC = 10 + PB + CHA) or be frightened until the tempest ends.
- Allies within the tempest gain resistance to all damage and regain 2d8 HP at the start of each of their turns.
- When the tempest ends, it explodes for 8d10 radiant or necrotic damage (your choice) to all enemies in the area.

### 3. Avatar of the Eternal Coven

*You transform into a spirit-bound avatar embodying the collective power of your coven.*

- For 1 minute: resistance to all damage; immunity to frightened, charmed, and stunned; unlimited Witches Mark uses; enemies within 30 feet take 4d10 psychic damage at start of their turns; allies regain 2d10 HP at start of their turns.
- When the effect ends: enemies within 30 feet make a CHA save (DC = 10 + PB + CHA) or take 10d10 necrotic/radiant damage (half on success). Allies within 30 feet gain temp HP equal to twice your Witch level and advantage on all rolls for 1 minute.

| Last Stand | Effect | Best Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Hexstorm Manifestation** | 8d10 necrotic to all in 60 ft, max Hex penalties, no healing for 1 minute. | Crippling large groups. |
| **Spirit Tempest** | 40-foot storm: 6d10 force, frighten enemies, heal allies, explodes for 8d10. | Battlefield control and support. |
| **Avatar of the Eternal Coven** | Unlimited Hexes, 4d10 psychic per turn, heal allies, final 10d10 explosion. | Climactic domination. |

### Hex-Turn (7th Level)

**Trigger:** A creature hexed by you makes an attack roll.
**Effect:** Force a CHA save (DC = 8 + PB + CHA). On failure, redirect the attack to another valid target within 30 feet. On success, the attack is made with disadvantage. At 15th level, the new target has disadvantage on its saving throw against the redirected effect.
