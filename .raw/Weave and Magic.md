# Weave and Magic

## **The Five Layers of Reality & Magic**

Magic and existence itself are structured within **five fundamental layers**, each defining different aspects of how power flows and is utilised:

1. **The [[Primal Glyphs]]** – These are the **core laws of reality**, existing before even the gods. They establish the **cycle of creation, fate, destruction, and rebirth**.
2. **The [[Universal Glyphs]]** – These **govern the hidden mechanisms of reality**, ensuring **balance, knowledge, and illusion**.
3. **The [[Elemental Glyphs]]** – These **shape the physical world** (Fire, Water, Earth, Air).
4. **The [[Form Glyphs]]** – These **ensure life always returns**, shaping mortals, beasts, titans, spirits, and aberrations.
5. **The [[Personal Glyphs]]** – These define how **individuals interact with power**, representing aspects like Power, Knowledge, Flow, Passion, and Shadows.

Each of these layers **contributes to the existence of magic** and determines how it interacts with mortals, gods, and the Weave itself​

---

## **🔹 The Source of Magic & The Weave**

Magic is **not a singular force**, but the result of **the interactions between Primal Powers and Universal Forces**.

- **Primal Powers** create **raw magic** unintentionally ([[Astralor]]’s forging, [[Aeloria]]’s fate-weaving, the [[The Whispering Dark]]’s entropy, [[The Restorer]]’s cyclical renewal).
- **Universal Forces** shape how magic functions ([[The Keeper of Knowledge]] records spells, [[The Wildheart]] fuels raw sorcery, [[The Veiled Hand]] hides forbidden magic, [[The Lantern Below]]  the creation of [[Secondary Glyphs]]).
- **Magic manifests through [[Leylines]], [[Wells of Power]], and [[Astral Conduits]]**, determining **where and how magical energy flows**​ [[The Source of Magic]].

Magic in Dór-o Estel flows through three great infrastructures—**Ley Lines**, **Wells of Power**, and **Astral Conduits**—often called the **[[Three Pillars of Magical Flow]]**. These are not metaphors but literal structures within the Weave. They determine where magic gathers, how it travels, and what happens when it fails. Without them, the world would fall into chaos and arcane decay.

### 🔹 **The Triadic Reflection – Magic Across Realities**

Each magical structure reflects differently across the **[[Triadic Layers of Reality]]**—the **Material Plane**, the **Luminous Echo** (_Feywild_), and the **Umbral Veil** (_Shadowfell_). Magic drawn through these mirrored realities gains qualities of **memory**, **chaos**, or **entropy**, shaping spells and magical phenomena in unpredictable ways.

> - In the [[Luminous Echo]], magic clings to **emotion, memory, and resonance**.
> - In the [[Umbral Veil]], spells warp with **grief, silence, and decay**.
> - In the [[Material Plane]], balance is maintained—but the scars of the other layers bleed through.

---

## **🔹 Leylines & Magical Storms**

[[Leylines]] are the **veins of the world’s magic**, channels of **arcane power that flow through creation**. They are crucial for **teleportation, ritual magic, and the stability of the Weave**.

- **Stable Leylines** → Predictable magical zones where magic follows standard rules.
- **Unstable Leylines** → Areas where magic fluctuates wildly, leading to chaos.
- **[[Magical Storms]]** → Occur when leylines rupture, causing magical energy to **erupt uncontrollably, warping reality**​.

---

## **🔹 The Interaction Between Magic & the Five Layers**

Every spell, power, and magical effect in **[[Dór-o Estel]]** interacts with one or more layers:

- **Spellcasting** → Draws power from **Elemental & Universal Glyphs**.
- **Divination & Fate Magic** → Relies on **Aeloria’s Loom (Glyph of Threads)**.
- **Wild Magic & Chaos Sorcery** → Comes from **The Wildheart & the Whispering Dark**.
- **Illusions & Reality Warping** → Manipulate **The Veiled Hand & The Glyph of Shadows**.
- **Necromancy & Undeath** → Tied to **The Maw (Glyph of Endings)** and **the Restorer’s Cycle**.

These forces ensure that **magic is not just a tool, but an intrinsic part of how existence functions**​

---

## **🔹 The Lost Glyph of Severance – The Forbidden Power**

There is one **Glyph that was erased from existence**—**[[The Glyph of Severance]]**, also called **The Unmaker’s Mark**.

- Unlike  **[[The Glyph of Endings]]**, which ensures **the cycle of death and rebirth**, **Severance removes things entirely from reality**.
- It was so feared that **even the gods erased it**, yet there are whispers that a fragment still remains **somewhere beyond time**​

---

## **🔹 The Hidden Architects & The Gods’ Misunderstanding**

Even the gods **do not fully understand** the forces at play.

- **[[Astralor]] believes he forges reality**, but he is merely using **tools shaped by the Universal Glyphs**
- **[[Aeloria]] believes she weaves fate**, but **she is only interpreting part of a much larger pattern**
- **[[The Whispering Dark]] thinks it consumes all**, but it does not realise **[[The Restorer]] ensures that nothing is truly lost**​

Thus, **the entire structure of magic, fate, and existence is a cycle, hidden even from the most powerful entities**.
