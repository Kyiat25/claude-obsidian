# The Breached and Fractured Weave

> "Where the glyphs shiver, the world forgets its rules."

Unstable Magic Zones are locations where the **Weave has been disrupted**, whether through natural [[leyline]] fluctuation, magical warfare, divine trauma, or exposure to **[[Eldritch forces]]**. These zones exhibit unpredictable magical phenomena and impose serious risks on spellcasters and glyph-bound objects.

---
## 📚 What Is an Unstable Magic Zone?

An Unstable Zone is a **magically volatile region** where the rules of spellcasting and glyphic structure **no longer behave as expected**. These areas are often transitional or decaying—caught between becoming a **[[Wild Magic Zone]]**, an **[[Arcane Rift]]**, or collapsing into a **[[Dead Magic Zone]]**.

They are defined by:

- Inconsistent magic behavior
- Surge potential during casting
- Disrupted Focus and Sanity thresholds
- Escalation into more extreme zone types

Some zones remain unstable for decades; others collapse within minutes.

---
## 🔍 Visual and Sensory Cues

Signs of instability include:

- Flickering glyphs or runes that rewrite themselves
- Echoes of spells long cast
- Shimmering distortion in the air
- Whispers when no one speaks
- Glitches in perception: repeated movements, reversed cause/effect

---
## 🧭 Causes of Instability

- **Leyline Drift** or interruption
- Aftermath of large-scale spell duels
- Failed planar rituals
- Collateral damage from Eldritch incursion
- Collapse of a [[Personal Glyph]] or [[World Anchor]]
- High Corruption Score environment or Echo Glyph activation

---
## 🌀 Spellcasting Consequences

Every time a spell is cast in an Unstable Zone:
- Roll a **Focus Save** vs. Zone DC
    - **Fail**: Roll on **Mental Disruption Table** (Focus.md)
    - If corrupted: **+1 Corruption Score**
- If in Class 3+, also roll a **Sanity Save**
    - Fail: Gain **[[Short-Term Madness]]** or another CS
- Spells may echo, loop, fizzle, or duplicate unexpectedly

|6|Glyph Burn: Gain 1 Corruption and disadvantage on next Focus Save|

## 🧠 Interactions with Focus, Sanity, and Corruption

These zones are **deeply tied** to your mental and metaphysical stability.

|State|Effect|
|---|---|
|**Strained Focus (9-12)**|+1 Corruption on Focus Failures|
|**Fragmenting Focus (5-8)**|Disadv. on all checks; spells may misfire|
|**Splintered Focus (1-4)**|Auto +1 CS on exposure; Sanity Save on cast|
|**Frayed Sanity (9-12)**|Sanity Save on surge or echoed spell|
|**Unraveling/Broken Sanity**|Auto gain CS on contact with unstable glyphs|
|**Corruption Score 10+**|Additional Surge risk and echo hallucinations|

---
## 🧬 Escalation Triggers

Unstable Zones can degrade into **Wild**, **Dead**, or **Rifted** zones.

| Trigger                                       | Escalation Path                 |
| --------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Casting 3+ spells with failed Focus in 1 hour | Become [[Wild Magic Zone]]      |
| Total magical suppression in area             | Becomes [[Dead Magic Zone]]     |
| Corruption Score avg. of party > 10           | Transforms into [[Arcane Rift]] |
| Eldritch Entity enters or manifests           | Immediately Rift formation      |

---
## 🔄 Transition & Containment

To stabilize an Unstable Zone:
- Cast **Ritual Grounding** (uses 3 casters + glyph anchors)
- Use **Focus Artifact** keyed to area’s leyline
- Consecrate with Personal Glyph and complete rest

Some factions create **Echo Anchors** or **Weave Nails** to **temporarily lock zones** in place, buying time.

---
## 🎭 Roleplay Hooks

- Flickering glyphs show different outcomes of past player actions
- NPCs inside the zone begin to repeat questions or memories
- Players may momentarily see future versions of themselves
