## Executive Summary

This document defines the complete mental attribute system for FEARS, integrating three interconnected mechanics:

1. **Focus (8-20)** - Short-term mental control and precision
2. **Sanity (8-20)** - Long-term psychological integrity  
3. **Corruption Score (0-30+)** - Cumulative eldritch contamination

These systems work together to create tactical depth in combat, meaningful character progression, and narrative weight in a world where magic is powerful but dangerous.

# PART I: CORE MECHANICS

## 1. DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

### 1.1 Core Intent

Mental attributes in FEARS serve four functions:

**Tactical** - Resource management in combat  
**Strategic** - Long-term character planning  
**Narrative** - Roleplay hooks and character arcs  
**Mechanical** - Clear, measurable game effects

### 1.2 Not Punitive

These systems are **pressure gauges**, not punishment:
- Clear warning before disaster
- Multiple recovery paths
- Player agency maintained
- Rewards strategic play

### 1.3 The Three Pillars

```
FOCUS    → Control (tactical, short-term)
SANITY   → Stability (strategic, long-term)  
CORRUPTION → Consequence (permanent, narrative)
```

**Interaction:**
- Focus protects Sanity (tactical discipline prevents panic)
- Sanity resists Corruption (stable minds resist contamination)
- Corruption degrades both (spiritual rot weakens everything)

## 2. FOCUS SYSTEM

### 2.1 What Focus Represents

Focus is **mental precision under pressure**:
- Concentration during chaos
- Control of unstable magic
- Resistance to mental intrusion
- Tactical awareness

**Not wisdom, not willpower** - it's **disciplined execution**.

### 2.2 Focus Score (8-20)

**Calculation:**
- **Point Buy:** 10 + Spellcasting Modifier + CON Modifier
- **Rolled:** 3d6 (or 4d6 drop lowest)

**Focus Modifier:** (Focus - 10) ÷ 2 (rounded down)

**Examples:**
- Focus 15 = +2 modifier
- Focus 10 = +0 modifier  
- Focus 8 = -1 modifier

### 2.3 Focus Thresholds

| Score | State | Effect |
|-------|-------|--------|
| **13-20** | **Steady** | Normal operation |
| **9-12** | **Strained** | Disadvantage on saves while casting |
| **5-8** | **Fragment** | Disadvantage ALL checks; conc. breaks on damage |
| **1-4** | **Splinter** | Auto-fail Wild Magic; glyphs backlash |
| **0** | **Collapse** | Cannot cast; DC 18 SAN or +1d4 CS |

### 2.4 Focus Degradation

**Temporary reduction from:**
- Failed spell in Wild Zone: -1
- Counterspelled: -1d4
- Exhaustion: -1 per level
- Below half HP: -2
- Corruption penalty (see section 4.7)

**Cannot go below 0.**

### 2.5 When to Roll Focus

**Focus Saves (d20 + Focus Modifier) against:**

**Magical Instability:**
- Wild Magic Zone: DC 13
- Dead Magic Zone: DC 20
- Spell crit fail: DC 12
- Counterspelled: DC 15

**Concentration:**
- Damage while concentrating: DC 10 or half damage
- Violent movement: DC 13
- Environmental chaos: DC 15

**Mental Defense:**
- Illusions: Spell save DC
- Charm/domination: Spell save DC
- Psychic assault: DC 15

### 2.6 Focus Failure - Disruption Table

**On failed Focus Save in dangerous situation, roll d6:**

| d6 | Disruption | Effect |
|----|-----------|---------|
| 1 | Arcane Reverb | Spell repeats next round targeting you |
| 2 | Glyph Flicker | Minor illusion; enemies have advantage |
| 3 | Fractal Confusion | Lose reaction; forget one prepared spell |
| 4 | Memory Echo | DC 12 Sanity save or hallucinate 1 minute |
| 5 | Surge Pulse | Nearest caster +1 Wild Magic chance |
| 6 | Shatter Pulse | Drop concentration; object destabilizes |

**In Wild Magic Zone:** Add +1 to roll (7+ = reroll and apply both)

### 2.7 Focus at 0 - Magical Burnout

**Effects:**
- Cannot cast spells
- Cannot maintain concentration  
- DC 18 Sanity Save or gain 1d4 Corruption
- Personal Glyph becomes unstable
- All magical abilities cease

**Recovery:** Requires long rest minimum, magical restoration helps

## 3. SANITY SYSTEM

### 3.1 What Sanity Represents

Sanity is **psychological integrity**:
- Processing incomprehensible truths
- Resisting existential terror
- Maintaining stable reality perception
- Enduring trauma without fracturing

**Not bravery** - it's **cognitive wholeness**.

### 3.2 Sanity Score (8-20)

**Calculation:**
- **Point Buy:** 10 + Spellcasting Modifier + CON Modifier
  - Non-casters use Charisma
- **Rolled:** 3d6 (or 4d6 drop lowest)
- **Optional Harsh:** Straight d20 (creates extreme variance)

**Sanity Modifier:** (Sanity - 10) ÷ 2 (rounded down)

### 3.3 Sanity Thresholds

| Score | State | Effect |
|-------|-------|--------|
| **13-20** | **Stable** | No effect |
| **9-12** | **Frayed** | Disadvantage vs illusions/mental stress |
| **5-8** | **Unravel** | Disadvantage ALL Sanity checks/saves |
| **1-4** | **Deranged** | Gain 1 Mental Trauma (roll table) |
| **0** | **Broken** | Insanity; DM control; CS → 20+ |

### 3.4 When to Roll Sanity

**Sanity Saves (d20 + Sanity Modifier) against:**

**Cosmic Horror:**
- Impossible geometry: DC 13
- Eldritch Moon: DC 16
- Cosmic entity: DC 20
- Null Reflection: DC 18

**Psychological Trauma:**
- Witness ally death: DC 12
- Betrayal: DC 15
- Torture (daily): DC 14
- Isolation (weekly): DC 10

**Magical Exposure:**
- Forbidden tome (hourly): DC 13
- Eldritch glyph: DC 16
- Mythos spell: DC 10 + spell level
- Failed ritual: DC 15

**Reality Distortion:**
- Dead Magic Zone entry: DC 12
- Reality fracture: DC 18
- Memory manipulation: DC 15

### 3.5 Sanity Damage

**Temporary (recovers naturally):**
- Combat panic: 1d4
- Fear spells: 1d4
- Glyph contact: 1d4
- Witnessing horror: 1d6

**Permanent (requires magic):**
- Eldritch Moon: 2d4 permanent
- Direct Eldritch contact: 2d6 permanent
- Memory fracture: 1d6 permanent
- Terminal Corruption: 3d6 permanent

**Permanent loss reduces MAXIMUM Sanity until restored.**

### 3.6 Mental Trauma Table

**Roll d6 at Deranged (SAN 1-4) or severe trauma:**

| d6 | Trauma | Effect |
|----|--------|--------|
| 1 | Delusions | Believe falsehoods; Disadv. Insight |
| 2 | Paranoia | Disadv. trust-based social checks |
| 3 | Hallucinations | DM inserts false sensory data |
| 4 | Obsession | Must repeat action; DC 15 WIS resist |
| 5 | Split Identity | Personality fracture; roleplay change |
| 6 | Recurring Visions | Disadv. Focus saves in combat |

**Removal:**
- Greater Restoration (removes one)
- 1 week therapy, DC 18 Medicine/Insight (removes one)
- Narrative resolution (face trauma source)

**Maximum 3 traumas. 4th = automatic Sanity → 0**

### 3.7 Sanity at 0 - Mental Break

**Immediate:**
- Immediate Insanity (roll table)
- DM gains narrative control
- Set Corruption Score to 20+ (Terminal)
- May become NPC

**Possible Outcomes (DM Choice):**
1. Temporary Insanity (1d10 days)
2. Permanent Transformation (Echo-Bearer, Glyphspawn)
3. Catatonic State (requires Wish)
4. Possession/Fracture (becomes antagonist)

**Recovery from 0 Sanity:**
Requires ALL of:
- Greater Restoration (1d4 Sanity minimum)
- 1 month therapy in safe/sacred environment
- Resolve trauma source narratively
- Remove Corruption if possible
- GM approval

**Post-recovery:** Max Sanity reduced by 1d4 permanently, gain one permanent Mental Scar

## 4. CORRUPTION SYSTEM

### 4.1 What Corruption Represents

Corruption is **NOT an ability score** - it's an accumulated **point total** (0-30+) representing:
- Eldritch contamination of soul
- Metaphysical rot
- Progressive loss of humanity
- Price of forbidden knowledge

**Corruption is ontological decay** - it begins subtly and crescendos into madness, mutation, or erasure.

### 4.2 Corruption Score (0-30+)

**Starting:** All characters start at CS 0

**Accumulation:** Permanent (mostly)

**Cannot naturally decrease** except through:
- Greater Restoration (in consecrated area, CS <15): -1
- Wish: -1d6
- True Resurrection: -1d4  
- Divine Intervention: -2d6
- Ritual cleansing (7 days, 1000gp): -1 per successful day
- Narrative redemption: varies

### 4.3 Corruption Sources

| Event | CS Gain |
|-------|---------|
| Spell in Dead Magic Zone | +1 |
| Failed spell in Wild Zone | +1 |
| Eldritch Zone (per hour) | +1 |
| Eldritch Carving (drawn) | +1 |
| Eldritch Carving (tattooed) | +2 |
| Eldritch Carving (scarified) | +5 |
| Touch Glyph of Unbeing | +1d4 |
| Dream-contact Eldritch | +1 |
| Direct communion | +5 |
| Sanity threshold drop | +1 |
| Failed Sanity save (Unraveling) | +1 |
| Reach Deranged state | +1d4 |
| Sanity drops to 0 | → 20+ |
| Dying in Eldritch Zone | +1d4 (if resurrected) |
| Study corrupted glyph (hourly) | +1 |

### 4.4 Corruption Tiers

| CS | Tier | Effects |
|----|------|---------|
| **0** | Clean | None |
| **1-4** | Dormant | Unease; vivid dreams; DC 12 WIS vs glyph |
| **5-9** | Minor | Weekly short-term madness; Focus saves for unstable casting; -1 max Focus |
| **10-14** | Major | Shadow flickers; +1 Carving DCs; glyphs whisper; -2 max Focus/Sanity |
| **15-19** | Eldritch | Indefinite madness; memory loss; -3 max Focus/Sanity; cannot regain Focus in Wild Zones |
| **20+** | Terminal | Lose Personal Glyph; transformation (Echo-Bearer, madness, mutation, or Glyphspawn); -5 max Focus/Sanity |

### 4.5 Tier Effects Detailed

**Dormant (CS 1-4):**
- DC 12 WIS save when touching glyphs (1d4 psychic on fail)
- Vivid dreams, shadows seem wrong
- Normal recovery rates

**Minor (CS 5-9):**
- Roll short-term madness weekly (1d10 min duration)
- Disadvantage on Focus saves in Wild Zones
- -1 to maximum Focus
- -1 from all Focus recovery

**Major (CS 10-14):**
- Shadow flickers unnaturally (visible, DC 10 Perception)
- +1 to all Eldritch Carving DCs
- Disadvantage on saves vs Illusions
- -2 to maximum Focus and Sanity
- -2 from all Focus recovery; Sanity: long rest only (+1, no dice)

**Eldritch (CS 15-19):**
- Roll Indefinite Madness (permanent until cured)
- Memory degradation: Each long rest, DC 15 INT or forget minor memory
- Cannot regain Focus in Wild Zones
- -3 to maximum Focus and Sanity
- Focus: Short = 0, Long = +1 only; Sanity: magical only

**Terminal (CS 20+):**
- Lose Personal Glyph
- Choose transformation: Permanent madness, spontaneous mutation, Glyph Replacement, Echo-Bearer, or retirement
- Auto-fail saves vs Eldritch effects (they reshape you)
- -5 to maximum Focus and Sanity
- No natural recovery of either

### 4.6 Mutation Table

**Roll d10 when:**
- Die in Eldritch Zone and resurrected
- Reach Terminal and choose mutation
- Cast Personal Glyph in Wild Zone with CS 15+

| d10 | Mutation | Effect |
|-----|----------|--------|
| 1 | Inverted Eyes | See Null between glyphs; +2 Insight, -2 Persuasion |
| 2 | Mirrored Claws | Can't wield 2H; cast via gesture only (no components) |
| 3 | Unbidden Word | Speak random word daily; d4: 1=boon, 4=banishment |
| 4 | Dream Infection | Dreams infect sleepers 30ft; DC 15 WIS or share nightmare |
| 5 | Glyph-Ink Blood | Blood leaves illusory sigils; scribe spells in blood; -2 CHA |
| 6 | Glyphspawn Parasite | Destroys death saves; resurrections auto-succeed |
| 7 | Temporal Echo | Appear in two places (illusion); confuses enemies; -2 SAN max |
| 8 | Void Touch | Touch drains color; +1d4 necrotic unarmed; food tasteless |
| 9 | Fractured Voice | Overlapping tones; +2 Intimidation, -2 Deception; can't whisper |
| 10 | Reality Anchor Loss | Phase partially; 10% miss chance both ways |

### 4.7 Corruption Impact on Mental Attributes

| Corruption | Focus Penalty | Sanity Penalty | Recovery Penalty |
|-----------|---------------|----------------|------------------|
| 0 (Clean) | -0 | -0 | None |
| 1-4 (Dormant) | -0 | -0 | None |
| 5-9 (Minor) | -1 max | -0 | Focus: -1 per recovery |
| 10-14 (Major) | -2 max | -2 max | Focus: -2; Sanity: Long rest only |
| 15-19 (Eldritch) | -3 max | -3 max | Focus: Short=0, Long=+1; SAN: Magical only |
| 20+ (Terminal) | -5 max | -5 max | No natural recovery |

**Example:** Wizard with Focus 16, CS 12 (Major) = Effective Focus 14

### 4.8 Living with Corruption

**Advantages (GM Discretion, CS 10+):**
- Eldritch Sight (see invisible, detect magic at will)
- Void Resistance (resistance to psychic/necrotic)
- Glyph Intuition (advantage on Arcana for glyphs)
- Nightmare Immunity (can't be mundanely frightened)
- Reality Bend (minor illusion at will)

**Disadvantages (Always Active):**
- Social stigma (corrupted aura visible to casters)
- Difficulty in sacred places (disadvantage)
- Corruption spreads (allies risk +1 CS with extended contact)
- Attraction to Eldritch entities
- Haunted by whispers/visions

## 5. THREE-WAY INTEGRATION

### 5.1 How They Connect

```
FOCUS failure → Forces SANITY checks
SANITY failure → Gains CORRUPTION
CORRUPTION → Degrades FOCUS & SANITY
```

### 5.2 Interaction Table

| Event | Save Type | Failure Result |
|-------|-----------|----------------|
| Cast in Wild Magic Zone | Focus Save | Glyph Feedback, +1 CS |
| Resist Eldritch Presence | Sanity Save | Madness, +1 CS |
| Concentration in Eldritch Zone | Focus Save | +1 CS if below Steady Focus |
| Enter Eldritch Zone (CS ≥ 10) | No Save | Immediate Illusion Bleed or Dream Echo |
| Drop Sanity Threshold | No Save | +1 CS, mandatory Focus Save if concentrating |
| Sanity = 0 | No Save | Set CS to 20+, Become Echo-Bearer |

### 5.3 Gameplay Loop

**Example Flow:**
1. Cast in Dead Magic Zone
2. Roll Focus Save to control surge
3. Fail → Roll on Disruption Table, gain +1 CS
4. Eldritch exposure triggers Sanity Save
5. Fail → Gain additional +1 CS
6. CS increases, making future Focus/Sanity saves harder
7. Drop to Collapsed Focus → Forces Sanity Save
8. Fail → Mental crisis

**The Spiral:** Each failure makes the next failure more likely.

# PART II: GAMEPLAY SYSTEMS

## 6. WILD MAGIC TRIGGERS

### 6.1 Trigger Conditions

Wild Magic triggers if **ONE or more** is true:

**Mental State:**
- Focus ≤ 9 (Strained or worse)
- Sanity ≤ 12 (Frayed or worse)
- CS ≥ 10 (Major or higher)

**Environmental:**
- In Wild Magic Zone
- In Arcane Rift (school-specific)
- Leyline surge active
- Eldritch Moon phase

**Casting:**
- Spell crit fails (nat 1 attack, target nat 20 save)
- Concentration crit fails (nat 1)
- Violently counterspelled
- Overcasting (slot 2+ levels higher)
- Corrupted components

**Multiple Triggers:** +10 to d100 per additional trigger

### 6.2 Resolution

1. Determine school
2. Roll d100 on school table
3. Add Corruption modifier (+1 per 5 CS)
4. Resolve effect
5. Sanity check: DC 10 + spell level (fail = 1d4 SAN loss)
6. If severe: DC 15 WIS save or +1 CS

## 7. CONCENTRATION MECHANICS

### 7.1 Basic Rules

**Concentration Check:** d20 + Focus Modifier
**DC:** 10 or half damage, whichever is higher

**Additional Triggers:**
- Violent movement: DC 13
- Environmental chaos: DC 15
- Casting another concentration spell: DC 15
- Mental attack: DC 12

### 7.2 Threshold Effects

| Focus State | Concentration Effect |
|------------|----------------------|
| Steady (13-20) | Normal checks |
| Strained (9-12) | Disadvantage while actively casting |
| Fragmenting (5-8) | Breaks on ANY damage (no save) |
| Splintered (1-4) | Cannot maintain concentration |
| Collapsed (0) | All concentration ends immediately |

### 7.3 Critical Failure (Natural 1)

**Above threshold (Focus 13+):**
- Spell ends cleanly
- No additional effects

**Below threshold (Focus ≤ 12):**
- Spell ends
- Wild Magic Surge triggered
- Lose 1d4 Focus
- DC 12 Sanity save or lose 1d4 Sanity

**Voluntary ending: Never triggers Wild Magic**

### 7.4 Zone Effects

**Wild Magic Zone:**
- Disadvantage on concentration checks
- Crit fail range: 1-2

**Dead Magic Zone:**
- All concentration ends immediately
- Cannot concentrate inside

**Arcane Rifts:**
- Opposing school: Disadvantage
- Matching school: Advantage

**Eldritch Zones:**
- Disadvantage
- Each minute: DC 15 SAN or lose 1d4 Sanity
- 10+ minutes: +1 CS

## 8. RECOVERY SYSTEMS

### 8.1 Focus Recovery

#### Combat (Action)
**Stabilization:** 1d4 + WIS modifier
- Cannot cast same turn
- Ally Help: +2 additional or advantage on next check

#### Short Rest
- **Basic meditation:** 1d4 + PB
- **With assistance:** 2d4 + PB (ally DC 12 Medicine/Insight)
- **In sanctuary:** 3d4 + PB

#### Long Rest
- **Basic:** Restore all Focus (minus Corruption penalty)
- **Interrupted:** Half maximum
- **Nightmare:** DC 12 SAN save; success = 1d4+PB, fail = 1d4 only

#### Magical
- Lesser Restoration: 2d4
- Greater Restoration: All
- Focus Potion (uncommon): 3d4
- Focus Potion (rare): 6d4

#### Class-Specific
- Wizard Arcane Recovery: Restore Focus = Wizard level (1/day after short rest)
- Monk Stillness of Mind: 1d4 Focus as action (WIS mod times per day)
- Cleric Divine Intervention: All Focus+Sanity on success
- Paladin Lay on Hands: Convert HP to Focus (1:1)
- Psion Psionic Recovery: Spend psi points for Focus (1:1, bonus action)

**Apply Corruption Penalty (see 4.7)**

### 8.2 Sanity Recovery

#### Short Rest
- **With support:** +1 Sanity (ally DC 12 Medicine/Insight)
- **Alone/threatened:** No recovery

#### Long Rest
- **Safe environment:** 1d4 Sanity
- **With therapy:** 1d4+2 Sanity (ally DC 15)
- **In sanctuary:** 2d4 Sanity
- **Disturbed:** 1 Sanity only

#### Downtime
- **Therapy (1 week):** 2d4 Sanity (DC 18 removes one trauma)
- **Ritual cleansing (1 day, 100gp):** 1d6 Sanity
- **Pilgrimage (1 month):** 3d6 Sanity, may reduce 1d4 CS if very holy

#### Magical
- Lesser Restoration: 1d4 Sanity, removes short-term madness
- Greater Restoration: 1d4+4 Sanity, removes one Mental Trauma
- Heal: 2d4 Sanity
- Wish: All Sanity, removes all traumas

**Apply Corruption Penalty (see 4.7)**

## 9. COMBAT INTEGRATION

### 9.1 Perceiving Mental State

**Insight Check:**
- DC 10: Recognize Strained/Frayed (rough condition)
- DC 15: Recognize exact threshold
- DC 20: Gauge within 25%

**Auto-detect:** Casters with See Invisibility, corrupted beings recognize CS tier

### 9.2 Mage Duels

**Focus Costs:**
- Counterspell attempt: -3 Focus
- Dispel attempt: -2 Focus
- Spell attack vs caster: -1 Focus
- Win initiative: +1 Focus

**Phases:**
1. Probing (Focus 13+): Test defenses, low spells
2. Engagement (9-12): Escalation, direct attacks
3. Crisis (5-8): Desperate, Wild Magic risk
4. Collapse (1-4): Surrender or catastrophic casting

**Victory:** Opponent at 0 Focus, surrenders, incapacitated, or environmental advantage

### 9.3 Anti-Mage Tactics

**Physical:**
- Grapple: Disadvantage on Focus checks
- Shove prone: Disadvantage on spell attacks and Focus
- Opportunity attack: Force concentration check
- Ready action: Interrupt casting

**Mental:**
- Intimidation: Force Sanity saves (frightened = disadvantage)
- Psychic damage: 1 Sanity damage per 5 damage
- Fear effects: Stack with low Sanity

**Specialized:**
- Mage Slayer feat: Reaction attack on cast, disadvantage on concentration
- Silence: Prevents Focus recovery and verbal components
- Counterspell: Direct -3 Focus drain
- Dispel Magic: Multiple Focus checks

### 9.4 Zone Control

**Arcane Rift (Matching):** Empower your spells, advantage
**Arcane Rift (Opposing):** Disadvantage for enemies
**Wild Magic Zone:** Chaos favors prepared casters
**Dead Magic Zone:** Forces martial combat (tactical nuke)

**Positioning:** Stay out of Wild when Strained, push enemies into Dead zones, control chokepoints, layer zones

# PART III: ADVANCED CONTENT

## 10. CLASS-SPECIFIC FEATURES

### 10.1 Wizard
- High Focus (+2 per level baseline)
- Arcane Recovery: Focus = Wizard level (1/day)
- Focused Mind (7th, Evocation): Advantage on Focus for Evocation
- Arcane Ward (2nd, Abjuration): +2 Focus while ward active
- Spell Mastery (18th): Cast certain spells even at 0 Focus

### 10.2 Sorcerer
- Moderate Focus (+1 per level)
- Metamagic: Focused Spell (2 SP for advantage)
- Sorcerous Restoration: 1 Focus per SP spent creating slots
- Font of Magic: Convert 2 Focus → 1 SP
- Wild Magic Sorcerer: Choose to trigger Wild Magic, reroll table, immune to own negative effects, +2 Focus when willingly triggering

### 10.3 Warlock
- Moderate Focus, Compromised Sanity (max 75% naturally)
- Pact Focus: +2 to Focus checks for pact magic
- Dark One's Own Luck: d10 to Focus/Sanity saves
- Eldritch Mind: Advantage on concentration, maintain even at Fragmenting
- Gain Corruption easier but resist effects better

### 10.4 Cleric
- Highest Sanity (+3 per level)
- Divine Grace: Add WIS to Focus/Sanity saves
- Channel Divinity: Sanctuary Mind (restore 2d4 Focus + 2d4 Sanity to ally)
- Divine Intervention: Restore all Focus+Sanity to 30ft radius
- Life Domain: Convert spell slots to Sanity (1 level = 1d4)

### 10.5 Druid
- Equal Focus+Sanity (+2 each)
- Natural Recovery: Focus = Druid level ÷ 2 in nature short rest
- Grounded Spirit: Cannot drop below 5 Sanity in nature
- Wild Shape: Restore 1d4 Focus when shifting
- Circle of Moon: Advantage on Focus while in Wild Shape

### 10.6 Psion
- Highest Focus (+3 per level)
- Disciplined Mind: Immune to Focus loss from damage <10
- Psionic Recovery: Psi points → Focus (1:1)
- Mental Bastion: Resistance to psychic and Sanity damage
- Iron Will (10th): Advantage on ALL Focus/Sanity saves, immune charm/frighten

### 10.7 Sangromancer
- Low Focus (+1 per level)
- Blood Focus: 5 HP → 1 Focus
- Blood Trance: Below 50% HP = advantage on Focus
- Hemomancy Corruption: Risk +1 SAN on nat 1
- Gain CS faster, Terminal = Blood Wraith

### 10.8 Runesmith
- High Focus (+2 per level)
- Runic Precision: Advantage on Focus when inscribing
- Personal Symbol: Never drains Focus, cannot backfire
- Counter-Runes: Ignore DR on called shots
- Backfire: Psychic damage reduces Focus+Sanity

### 10.9 Monk
- High Focus+Sanity (+2 each)
- Stillness of Mind: 1d4 Focus as action (WIS mod/day)
- Diamond Soul: Proficiency all saves, 1 ki to reroll
- Perfect Mind (14th): Immune charm/frighten, +5 Focus
- Empty Body (18th): Immune to Sanity damage while projecting

### 10.10 Paladin
- Highest Sanity (+3 per level)
- Aura of Protection: +CHA to Focus/Sanity saves (10ft, 30ft at 18th)
- Lay on Hands: Convert to Sanity (1:1)
- Aura of Courage: Immune frightened (10ft, 30ft at 18th)
- Divine Health: Advantage vs Corruption gain

## 11. ENVIRONMENTAL EFFECTS

### 11.1 Wild Magic Zones
- All spells risk surge (d20, 1 = surge)
- Focus/Sanity checks at disadvantage
- No recovery possible
- Duration: 1 hour or dispelled (DC 15)
- 25% chance to expand 10ft per crit

### 11.2 Dead Magic Zones
- All magic ceases
- All concentration ends immediately
- Cannot cast inside
- Magic items inert
- Focus drain: -1 per 10 minutes
- Duration: 1 hour (cannot be dispelled)

### 11.3 Arcane Rifts (School-Specific)
**Duration:** Spell level × 10 min (or permanent if Enhanced)

**Effects by School:**
- Evocation: +1d6 damage per spell level
- Abjuration: +2 AC, advantage on spell saves
- Conjuration: Double summoning/teleport duration
- Necromancy: Healing -50%, necrotic +50%
- Transmutation: Duration doubled, +2 physical stats
- Enchantment: Duration doubled, saves at disadvantage
- Illusion: DC +2 to disbelieve, partially real
- Divination: +2 initiative, advantage Perception, see invisible 30ft

### 11.4 Eldritch Zones
- Sanity drain: 1 per minute
- Focus checks at disadvantage
- Fracture risk: DC 15 SAN per hour
- Corruption: +1 CS per hour
- Permanent until cleansed
- Reality visibly distorted

### 11.5 Leylines
**Nexus (Positive):** +2 Focus, spells -1 Focus cost, recovery doubled
**Surge (Chaotic):** Wild Magic, disadvantage Focus, 1d4 SAN per surge
**Corrupted (Negative):** Disadvantage Focus, -1 SAN/hour, +1 CS risk on nat 1

### 11.6 Sacred Spaces
**Temple/Grove:** Focus recovery doubled, Sanity +2, cannot drop below 10 SAN, Wild Magic suppressed
**Desecrated:** Focus recovery halved, no Sanity recovery, Wild Magic +10%, +1 CS per long rest

### 11.7 Cosmic Events
**Eldritch Moons (varies by moon):**
- Moon of Whispers: Disadvantage SAN, +CS on failed save
- Moon of Clarity: Focus +2, advantage
- Blood Moon: Sangromancer +5 Focus
- Void Moon: All mental at half

**Planar Proximity:**
- Material: Normal
- Feywild: Focus -2, Wild Magic +10%
- Shadowfell: Sanity -2, Corruption risk
- Far Realm: Sanity -5, auto Fracture risk

## 12. GM GUIDELINES

### 12.1 Tracking

**Methods:**
- Tokens/chips (one color per resource)
- Dice displays (d20 showing current)
- Character sheets (current/max format)
- Digital tools (apps, spreadsheets)

**Threshold Markers:** Mark 13, 9, 5, 1 on tracking

**Visibility:** Public recommended (encourages teamwork)

### 12.2 Adjudication

**Common Questions:**
- Cast at 0 Focus? Yes, auto Wild Magic
- Recover in Dead Zone? No
- Healing restore mental? No, unless spell says so
- Spend HP for Focus? Only if class allows

**When in doubt:**
- Focus = short-term, recovers fast
- Sanity = long-term, recovers slow
- Thresholds = hard boundaries
- Telegraph risks to players

### 12.3 Balancing by Campaign

**Low-Magic:**
- Increase recovery (+1 all)
- Reduce costs (half)
- More rest opportunities
- Fewer hazards

**Standard:**
- Use base rules

**High-Magic:**
- More hazards
- Powerful recovery items
- Mental resources crucial

**Horror:**
- Reduce max (×0.75)
- Slower recovery (half)
- More Fractures
- Corruption faster

### 12.4 Rewarding Good Play

**Grant bonus recovery when players:**
- Roleplay mental state effectively (+1 to next recovery)
- Make tactical sacrifices (advantage on next save)
- Coordinate as team (free short rest)
- Engage meaningfully (inspiration on mental saves)

### 12.5 Warning Signs

**Too Harsh:**
- Players never cast
- Constant rests
- Avoiding zones
- No risks
**Fix:** Double recovery, more items, reduce hazards, recalibrate thresholds

**Too Lenient:**
- Ignore thresholds
- No resource management
- Never below 50%
- Wild Magic never triggers
**Fix:** More anti-mage enemies, more zones, reduce recovery, increase hazards

**Perfect Balance:**
- Careful tracking
- Tactical decisions around thresholds
- Occasional crisis (not constant)
- Feel powerful but vulnerable

## 13. QUICK REFERENCE

### Thresholds

**FOCUS:** 13-20 Steady, 9-12 Strained, 5-8 Fragment, 1-4 Splinter, 0 Collapse
**SANITY:** 13-20 Stable, 9-12 Frayed, 5-8 Unravel, 1-4 Deranged, 0 Broken
**CORRUPTION:** 0 Clean, 1-4 Dormant, 5-9 Minor, 10-14 Major, 15-19 Eldritch, 20+ Terminal

### Recovery Quick Table

| Resource | Combat | Short Rest | Long Rest |
|----------|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Focus | 1d4+WIS (action) | 1d4+PB | Full |
| Sanity | — | +1 (with support) | 1d4 (safe) |

**Apply Corruption Penalty**

### Wild Magic Triggers

Triggers if ONE is true:
- Focus ≤ 9 OR Sanity ≤ 12 OR CS ≥ 10
- Wild Magic Zone
- Spell crit fail
- Concentration crit fail
- Counterspelled

**Multiple triggers = +10 per additional**

### Concentration

**DC:** 10 or half damage
**Roll:** d20 + Focus Modifier

**States:**
- Steady: Normal
- Strained: Disadvantage while casting
- Fragmenting: Breaks on any damage
- Splinter/Collapse: Cannot maintain

### Common CS Gain

| Event | CS |
|-------|----|
| Spell in Dead Zone | +1 |
| Failed spell Wild Zone | +1 |
| Eldritch Zone hourly | +1 |
| Sanity threshold drop | +1 |
| Failed SAN (Unraveling) | +1 |
| Sanity → 0 | → 20+ |

**Outcome:** Smart tactical play prevented crisis

### A.2 The Eldritch Horror

Kael (Fighter, FOC 14, SAN 12, CS 0) encounters Far Realm entity

**First Exposure:**
- DC 18 Sanity save: Fails (rolls 8 total)
- Takes 2d6 SAN damage: 9
- SAN: 12 - 9 = 3 (Deranged)
- Crosses threshold: +1 CS
- Rolls Mental Trauma: 2 (Paranoia)

**Second Round:**
- DC 16 Sanity save with disadvantage: Fails
- Because Deranged: +1 CS
- Takes 1d4 SAN: 2
- SAN: 3 - 2 = 1

**Party Response:**
- Cleric Channel Divinity: Sanctuary Mind
- Restores 2d4 Focus + 2d4 Sanity = 5 SAN
- Kael SAN: 1 + 5 = 6 (Unraveling, stabilized)

**Outcome:** Saved from mental break, Paranoia remains

### A.3 Wild Magic Cascade

Sorcerer (FOC 10, SAN 14, CS 7) in Wild Magic Zone casts Lightning Bolt

**Cascade:**
1. In Wild Zone = auto Wild Magic chance
2. Rolls d20: 1 - triggers
3. Also Strained (FOC 10) = second trigger
4. Roll d100: 45 + 10 (double) + 1 (CS÷5) = 56
5. Result: "Spell reflects back at caster"
6. Takes own lightning: 32 damage
7. DC 13 Sanity save: Fails, loses 1d4 SAN = 3
8. SAN: 14 - 3 = 11 (Frayed)
9. Severe Wild Magic: DC 15 WIS save: Fails
10. Gains +1 CS: 7 + 1 = 8 (Minor)
11. Focus loss from damage: -1 → FOC 9

**Final State:** FOC 9 (Strained), SAN 11 (Frayed), CS 8 (Minor), injured

**Lesson:** Wild Zones extremely dangerous when already compromised

## B. VARIANT RULES

### B.1 Hardcore (Grimdark/Horror)
- Roll 2d6+2 for Focus/Sanity (4-14)
- Quarter all recovery
- Corruption cannot be reduced
- Thresholds at 40%, 20%

### B.2 Heroic (High-Fantasy)
- Roll 4d6 drop lowest or Point Buy +2
- Double all recovery
- Warning level at 10%
- Terminal Corruption at 30

### B.3 Visible Mental State (Tactical)
- All resources public
- Enemies auto-see state
- Creates "protect the caster" gameplay

### B.4 Mental Death Spiral (Horror)
- Below 50%: Recovery halved
- Below 25%: Recovery quartered
- Below 10%: Magical only
- At 0: Wish only

### B.5 Corruption as Power (Dark Fantasy)
Each tier grants powers:
- Minor: Eldritch Sight, Void Touch (+1d4 necrotic)
- Major: Reality Bend, Nightmare Immunity, Shadow Step
- Eldritch: Void Resistance, Eldritch Blast at will, See Invisibility permanent
- Terminal: Choose powerful transformation

Trade-off: Massive power but all penalties remain

### B.6 Shared Mental Pool (Cooperative)
- Party shares total Focus/Sanity
- Anyone can draw
- Stronger support weaker
- If pool → 0, everyone suffers
