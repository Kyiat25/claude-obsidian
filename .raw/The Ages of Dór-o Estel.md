## 📜 The Ages of Dór-o Estel

### 🌌 **[[The Loomless Time]]** _- Pre-Age Mythic Era_

**Duration:** Uncounted, unknowable  
**Theme:** Elemental chaos, divine awakening, shaping of the world without memory or consequence

- Begins with **Astralor’s Forge**, the first strike that awakens Fire, Earth, Water, Air, and Spirit.
- The gods are **formless, proud, unknowing**, shaping the world through conflict and trial.
- The **War of Elemental Becoming** defines this era—each element claiming space, form, and identity.
- **[[Soltharis]]**, god of flame and tyranny, rises to claim all change as his right.
- Ends with the **[[Sealing of Soltharis]]** within the Sun by [[Pyrris]] and [[Vaeldros]]—the first divine punishment.
- The Weave stabilizes. Memory becomes possible. The First Age begins.

---
### 🌑 **[[The First Age]] – _Discovery_**

**Duration:** ~2700 years  
**Timeline:** 4500 – 1800 years ago

- Begins with the **Sealing of Soltharis** in the Sun.
- Gods discover memory, consequence, glyphs, and the Weave's structure.
- Mortal awareness awakens slowly. Druidic circles and Spirit echoes form.
- Pantheons begin to define themselves.
- Ends with the **Trial and Exile of Vel’Zyren** by [[Gaeldros]], severing her from all Threads.
- Over the next 105 years, [[Vel'Zyren]] creates the [[Drow]] in secret.

---
### 🔥 **[[The Second Age]] – _Heroes and Rebellion_**

**Duration:** 700 years  
**Timeline:** 1800 – 1100 years ago

- Begins with **[[The Unleashing of the Drow]]** and divine fracturing.
- Mortal heroes rise: glyph-binders, wild prophets, and divine challengers.
- Elves, humans, orcs, and others build cities and rebel against old laws.
- The pantheons splinter; many gods fall silent or are redefined.
- **[[Vel'Zyren]] and Soltharis cults rise**, and Eldritch whispers begin.
- Ends with the **First Eldritch Moon** appearing in the sky.

---
### 🧵 **[[The Third Age]] – _Collapse and Rebirth_**

**Duration:** 1100 years  
**Timeline:** 1100 – 0 years ago

- Begins with the **[[Founding of the Faith of the Bound Pattern]]** and the first Loom-Cities.
- Civilisation stabilizes under Threadbearers, World Anchors, and elemental cycles.
- The **[[Dragonborn Empire]]** rises under the Cult of Soltharis, capital [[Ashkarra]].
- Glyphic law is codified. Divine cycles begin to strain.
- **Eldritch Moons multiply**. Ashkarra endures three Spirit Threads in a row.
- Ends with the **[[Day of Twelve Shadows]]** – all Eldritch Moons align.
- **[[Ashkarra]] falls**, the **First Hollow Breach** opens, and the newest Loom-City [[Aeravae]] is founded in its aftermath.

---

### 🌒 **[[The Fourth Age]] – _Unraveling_**

**Duration:** 487 years (ongoing)  
**Timeline:** 0 – Present (487 A4)

- Begins with the **Fifth Cycle** under the Spirit Thread following the collapse of the Fourth Cycle.
- Loom-Cities begin to waver. Trust in Threadbearers is shaken.
- [[Eldritch Moons]] grow frequent; prophecy falters.
- The Weave shows signs of stress: [[wild magic zones]], leyline corruption, failed Threads.
- The Age is one of **instability, uncertainty, and encroaching horror**.
- The current cycle is only 18 days old... no new Threadbearers have been found.
- What comes next is unknown. Either the Pattern holds—or unravels completely.
