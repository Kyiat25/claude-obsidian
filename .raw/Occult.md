# ☣️ Occult – The Unseen Thread

> _“The world has rules. The occult remembers who wrote them—and why they were erased.”_  
> — Glyph-Eater of the Hollow Circle

---
## 📚 What is Occult?

The **Occult** skill governs knowledge of **forbidden lore, unsanctioned glyphs, forgotten rituals**, and **cosmic truths hidden from mortal understanding**. It is the lore of the **Unspoken**, the **Fractured Glyphs**, and the whispers **not meant to be heard**.

Occult differs from [[Arcana]] and [[Religion]]:
- **Arcana** explains how sanctioned magic works.
- **Religion** reveals divine laws and mythic traditions.
- **Occult** uncovers the **rotted underside of both**—echoes, corruption, and lost truths that unravel the soul.

---
## 🎲 Occult Check Formula

**Occult Check** = `1d20 + Intelligence or Wisdom Modifier + Proficiency (if trained)`
- **Intelligence-based** when interpreting symbols, scripts, ritual structure.
- **Sanity-based** when intuiting the meaning of dreams, emotional echoes, or spirit residue.

> Players choose which modifier fits the context. The GM may override if one clearly dominates.

---
## 📌 When to Roll Occult

|Situation|Occult Use|
|---|---|
|Decoding a glyph not found in legal archives|Occult (DC 15–25)|
|Identifying a cursed item’s true origin|Occult + Memory or Focus Save|
|Interpreting a dream-vision or nightmare|Occult (Wisdom) + possible Sanity Save|
|Understanding an Eldritch cult’s prayer-sigil|Occult + Religion (combined insight)|
|Navigating a glyph-bleed or Corrupted Zone|Occult (disadvantage if CS ≥ 10)|

---
## 🕷️ Occult & the Mind Systems

The Occult skill directly interacts with:

- **[[Sanity]]**: Occult checks in Eldritch contexts may trigger Sanity Saves (DC 12–18). Failure = +1 Corruption or hallucination.
- **[[Corruption]]**: Certain occult revelations increase **Corruption Score** on a failed Memory or Focus Save.
- **[[Focus]]**: Maintaining composure while engaging with cursed glyphs or reality echoes requires a **Focus Save** (DC varies).

---
## 🔮 Occult Proficiency

Characters may gain proficiency in Occult by:

- Backgrounds (e.g., **[[Cult Survivor]]**, **Glyphborn**)
- Classes (e.g., **[[Investigator]]**, **[[Witch]]**, **Grave-Priest**)
- Downtime study of **forbidden tomes, haunted ruins**, or **glyph fragments**

Optional Rule:

> Survive a glyph bleed or dream-eclipse, succeed a DC 18 Sanity Save, and permanently gain **Occult (Unstable)** proficiency—usable only when your **Sanity is 12 or lower**.

---
## ⚠️ GM Guidance

Occult should _never_ provide absolute truth. It leads to more questions, **echoing answers**, or insights that may be incomplete—or dangerous.

Use Occult results to:
- Foreshadow future horrors
- Twist understanding of current glyphs
- Reveal **costs before powers**

> The Occult does not inform. It **warns**.
