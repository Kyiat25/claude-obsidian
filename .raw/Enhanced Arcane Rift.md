# Enhanced Arcane Rift

A more dangerous, volatile version of a standard [[Arcane Rift]]. These rifts tear reality apart with raw magical force and often grow into major threats.

The rift explodes into view like a wound in the sky—blinding, chaotic, and alive. Every breath crackles with power. Spells surge unbidden toward your fingertips, desperate to be cast. Your muscles twitch with unnatural speed, your thoughts blur between clarity and collapse. The terrain warps—floating embers, reversed gravity, impossible shadows—and something in your soul whispers: _more._ This isn’t magic. It’s raw reality breaking.

#### 🔮 Benefits:

- Your spells from the associated school are _supercharged_—often cast at much higher levels.
- You might be able to **cast faster**, **maintain more spells**, or **gain additional actions** during your first moments near the rift.
### 🎯 Mechanics Players Need to Know:
> 🔥 **Concept:** Supercharged version of an Arcane Rift with explosive potential and personal risk.

- **Spell Overcharge:**
    - Rift school spells are cast **2 levels higher**.
- **Critical Range:**
    - Spells of the rift's school crit on **18–20**.
- **Haste-Like Start:**
    - On your first turn after creating: **2 actions**, **double movement** (spell/attack/move only).
- **Extra Concentration:**
    - May maintain **1 extra concentration spell** from the rift’s school.
- **Sanity Check to Cast (per spell):**
    - DC = 10 + spell level.
    - Failure = **Wild Magic Surge**.
    - Nat 1 = **2 Surges + Blowback**
- **Blowback on Crit:**
    - On a **spell critical hit (18–20)**, roll a d6.
    - On **1**, you suffer school-based blowback.
- **Instability Threshold:**
    - After 5 spells are cast in the rift, it might:
    - **Destabilize into a Wild Magic Zone**.
    - **Stabilize or collapse**.
#### ⚠️ Dangers:

- The terrain becomes **unpredictable**: falling rocks, shadow bursts, or lightning arcs can erupt each round.
- Magic may **fight back** if you lose control—failing spell attempts near the rift can cause magical chaos.
- The more spells cast near the rift, the higher the chance it destabilizes into a **Wild Magic Zone** or worse.

#### 🎭 What You Experience:

- Reality seems _fractured_: light bends, sounds twist, and your magic thrums with unstable power.
- It feels amazing—but terrifying. Magic is stronger here, but it has a mind of its own.
