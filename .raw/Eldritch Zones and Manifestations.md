# 🧠 Eldritch Ties to Magical Zone Instability

Though these zones often have natural, planar, or divine origins, **Eldritch interference is the hidden infection** beneath many of them. Their impact is not always immediate—often it’s the **result of echo**, **observation**, or **unintentional memory of a Glyph or truth** that never should have existed.

---
## 🌌 1. **Dead Magic Zones** – The Graveyards of Glyphs

> _"It is not that magic is dead. It is that it has forgotten how to be."_

### 🕳️ Eldritch Influence:

- **[[Nhal’Zereth]]**’s domain of retroactive erasure is most often to blame.
- When magic is **overused, misaligned, or fatally distorted**, **Nhal’Zereth leaks in**.
- These zones are not merely null—**they are consumed**, as if the Weave itself **unthreaded the memory** of ever having worked there.

### 🔻 Specific Signs of Eldritch Infection:

- **Forced magic** not only fails, but spells cast _within the last day_ are sometimes **undone**, forgotten even by those who cast them.
- Caster footprints disappear in mid-stride. Glyphs left in spellbooks go blank
- **[[Mark of the Rift]]** attracts **[[Esh’Tarah’s Reflections]]**—mirror-beasts hunting from the unlight.

### 🧠 Corrupted Beasts:

- **[["Null-Stags"]]**: Creatures that leave no tracks, no smell, no heat signature—hunters forget they were ever chasing.
- **[["Hollow Vultures"]]**: Birds that eat magical creatures’ memories instead of flesh.

### ☣️ Notable Risk:

[[Dead Magic Zones]] near **Eldritch-influenced leyline ruptures** may _never be healed_—restoration spells may paradoxically unmake the caster themselves.

---
## 🔥 2. **Arcane Rifts / Enhanced Arcane Rifts** – The Open Mouths of the Null Reflection

> _"The rift did not open. It remembered being open. And so it was."_

### 🕳️ Eldritch Influence:

- Enhanced Rifts are sometimes **mutated echoes of Eldritch whispering** into [[leyline]] seams.
- **[[Xey’Tazun]]**, **[[Zhurr’Kael]]**, and **[[Ul’Naresh]]** often breach these sites, especially when rituals or critical overkills warp **spell-logic**.
- When an [[Arcane Rift]] sustains too long, it **frays its own timeline**, **reflects impossible versions of itself**, or **severs its memory of ever being closed**.

### 🔻 Specific Signs of Eldritch Infection:

- Rift light pulses in **fractured rhythm**, with **inverted colours or silent sound**.
- Environmental shifts defy logic—flames burn _upward into frost_, lightning strikes from below.
- [[Enhanced Arcane Rifts]] affected by **[[Zhurr’Kael]]** develop **[[paradox zones]]**: spells that succeed _fail anyway_ due to cause-loop interference.

### 🧠 Corrupted Beasts:

- **[["Glyphpuppets"]]**: Magical constructs that begin replicating the behaviours of casters they've seen—even before they are encountered.
- **[["Fray-Beasts"]]**: Creatures that warp unpredictably mid-combat—teleporting before hit, casting spells they shouldn't know.

### ☣️ Notable Risk:

Failure to close an Eldritch-tainted Enhanced Rift can **collapse the local Weave structure entirely**, transforming the region into a **static paradox** or spawning a **[[Dead Magic Zone]]** via magical implosion.

---
## 🌀 3. **Wild Magic Zones** – The Whispers That Forgot the Words

> _"The Weave here still sings. But the song is sung backward, and the lyrics change each time you listen."_

### 🕳️ Eldritch Influence:

- These zones are most often corrupted by **[[Ul’Naresh]]** (dream reversal) and **[[Zhurr’Kael]]** (anti-logic).
- They don’t just surge—they **contaminate**, spreading **contagious instability** across surrounding leyline threads.
- The longer they remain, the more likely they will mutate into **self-aware magical fields**.

### 🔻 Specific Signs of Eldritch Infection:

- **Surges begin referencing dreams not yet dreamt**, and sometimes match other players’ thoughts or memories.
- Surge tables create **cross-school bleed** (e.g., an Enchantment spell causes necrotic rot or an Illusion becomes physically real).
- **Caster alignment or personality may shift** mid-spell as they harmonise with alien truth fragments.

### 🧠 Corrupted Beasts:

- **[["Surgeborn"]]**: Elementals or familiars made of _twisted spell residue_, born screaming in spells they weren’t meant to cast.    
- **[["Thought-Flayers"]]** (distinct from mind flayers): Magical apparitions that steal spell slots and fill them with unstable energy.

### ☣️ Notable Risk:

If left unchecked, Eldritch-infested [[Wild Magic Zones]] may **"choose" a dominant concept**—e.g., **"The Color Red" becomes law**, or **gravity reverses**—and **assert it upon the Weave** permanently.

Here are **optional rules and triggers** for DMs to bring Eldritch Gods into **[[Dead Magic Zones]]**, **Arcane Rifts (including Enhanced)**, and **[[Wild Magic Zones]]**. These are **not full manifestations**—the Eldritch Pantheon does not arrive like avatars—but as **invasive phenomena**, **memetic whispers**, or **reality-fractures** that reflect their impossible nature.

# 🕳️ **When to Invoke Eldritch Gods in Unstable Magic Zones**

> _“They do not arrive. They are remembered by the space around them.”_

These rules define when an Eldritch god may begin to **manifest subtly**—through sensory corruption, dream incursions, magical anomalies, or glyph distortions.

---

## 🧱 **Dead Magic Zones – Erasure Invoked**

### 📌 Triggering Eldritch Presence

| **Trigger**                               | **Eldritch God**    | **Effect**                                                            |
| ----------------------------------------- | ------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Forcing a spell of 5th level or higher    | **[[Nhal’Zereth]]** | 50% chance the caster begins to **lose memories of magic itself**     |
| Gaining 5+ Mark of the Rift stacks        | **[[Esh’Tarah]]**   | Reflections begin **moving independently** in camp or water           |
| Failure of a Greater Restoration          | **[[Zhurr’Kael]]**  | Glyphs become recursive; **symbology begins creating language loops** |
| Casting within 1 mile of the Hollow Vault | **[[Xey’Tazun]]**   | Time becomes unstable—**repeating conversations, lost hours**         |

### 🧠 Zone Change:

- Mark the area as **[[Contaminated Dead Magic]]**.
- All **restoration spells** require **[[Sanity Check]]** or cause glyph inversion.
- Players begin hearing **non-player memories** whispered into their dreams.

---

## 🔥 **Arcane Rifts / Enhanced Arcane Rifts – The Eye of the Spiral**

### 📌 Triggering Eldritch Presence

| **Trigger**                                | **Eldritch God**    | **Effect**                                                               |
| ------------------------------------------ | ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 3 or more overkill spells cast             | **[[Xey’Tazun]]**   | Rift "resets" to an earlier version—**spells cast are undone**           |
| A failed Sanity Check by 5+                | **[[Zhurr’Kael]]**  | The spell logic collapses; **spell descriptions change into paradoxes**  |
| Double surge in Enhanced Rift              | **[[Nhal’Zereth]]** | Spells vanish from memory; caster forgets how to cast one spell entirely |
| A planar creature summoned via Conjuration | **[[Vael’Akir]]**   | Summon fails—but the **summoned creature now seeks the caster anyway**   |

### 🧠 Zone Change:

- Rift becomes **“Unanchored.”** Every hour, its magical school randomly shifts.
- Casts now have a 10% chance to trigger **Glyph Feedback**—the glyph resists casting.
---

## 🌀 **Wild Magic Zones – The Dream-Touched Maze**

### 📌 Triggering Eldritch Presence

| **Trigger**                               | **Eldritch God**   | **Effect**                                                                                                  |
| ----------------------------------------- | ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 3+ failed Focus saves in a row            | **[[Ul’Naresh]]**  | Caster’s dreams manifest around them—**phantom memories take shape**                                        |
| Critical Failure + spell effect backfires | **[[Zhurr’Kael]]** | Spell begins **casting itself** at random intervals, even outside combat                                    |
| Use of a Personal Glyph in a Wild Zone    | **[[Esh’Tarah]]**  | The caster’s identity splits—**two “selves” diverge in perception**                                         |
| A successful nat 20 surge harmonization   | **[[Xey’Tazun]]**  | Caster gains a vision of **a possible future where the zone does not exist** but cannot remember it clearly |
### 🧠 Zone Change:

- Wild Zone becomes **“[[Screaming Weave Field]].”**
- Glyphs begin mutating—**Personal Glyphs may become unstable**.
- Players experience **shared hallucinations**, often reflecting events that never happened.

# 🧿 **Optional Sanity & Corruption Mechanics**

## 🧠 Sanity Thresholds

Track a running **Sanity Corruption Score** for players within affected zones.

- **+1** per Forced Spell in a Dead Zone
- **+1** per Overkill Spell in an Arcane Rift
- **+1** per Failed Focus Save in a Wild Zone
- **+2** for experiencing Eldritch Manifestation
- **+5** for knowingly invoking an Eldritch god

### Thresholds:

- **5**: Unsettling dreams; disadvantage on Insight in magic-scarred areas.
- **10**: Glyph hallucinations begin; DC 12 Wisdom save to cast without hesitation.
- **15**: You begin seeing people who don’t exist. Sanity Checks required when interacting with glyphs.
- **20**: Permanent madness or replacement of your Personal Glyph with an Eldritch echo.
