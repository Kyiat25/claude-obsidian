# The Druid

**Guardians of Nature's Primordial Symphony**

> *"The druid doesn't protect the forest. The forest protects itself. The druid is how it asks."*

---

## The Land Remembers

She had passed through this clearing twice before — once in spring, once in autumn. She had cast spells here, drawn on the Primordial current through the soil both times. Now, in the middle of winter with enemies closing in, the roots shifted. The undergrowth thickened at angles that made no tactical sense unless you knew where she had fought before. She did. She had placed those fights precisely.

You are the Druid. Not a nature lover who happens to cast spells, but a channel through which the living world speaks. **Wild Shape** is not a combat trick — it is a covenant renewal, the body surrendering its form to demonstrate trust. **Draw Power** is the same process in miniature: you spend Focus, the land replenishes your reserves. **Nature's Remembrance** is what makes that exchange permanent.

In FEARS, Wild Shape has complete DCS integration. Beast Form has its own Evasion formula, its own DR calculation, and its own Momentum reset when you transform. The combat continues while you're an animal — with different tools.

---

## The Druid's Essence

You are control, transformation, and accumulated memory. Your spells reshape terrain. Beast Form provides tactical flexibility pure casters cannot access. Your growing network of Nature-Remembered locations means you fight better in familiar places — and you choose those places when you can.

---

## Complexity Assessment: MODERATE (6/10)                    

The Druid tracks:                                            
• Wild Shape uses (1–4 per short rest)                       
• Beast Form HP (separate pool, by size)                   
• Beast Form Evasion and DR (calculated per creature)     
• Nature-Remembered locations (tracked with GM)          
• Spell slots (full caster, long rest)                     
• Focus (for concentration and Draw Power)    

Recommended for players who enjoy tactical adaptation, creative problem-solving, and long-term campaign investment.

## Class Features

### Hit Points

- **Hit Dice:** 1d8 per Druid level
- **Hit Points at 1st Level:** 8 + your Constitution modifier
- **Hit Points at Higher Levels:** 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per Druid level after 1st

### Proficiencies

- **Armour:** Light armour, medium armour, shields
- **Weapons:** Simple weapons
- **Tools:** Herbalist tools
- **Saving Throws:** Intelligence, Wisdom
- **Skills:** Choose two from Animal Handling, Arcana, Gathering, Insight, Medicine, Nature, Perception, Religion, Skinning, and Survival

### Starting Equipment

- (a) a shield or (b) any simple weapon
- (a) a scimitar or (b) any simple melee weapon
- Leather armour, an explorer's pack, and a druidic focus

---

## Quick Build

1. **Ability Scores:** Make Wisdom your highest score, followed by Constitution.
2. **Lineage:** Any combination; Wood Elf, Human, and Half-Elf are thematic.
3. **Background:** Outlander, Hermit, or any background with Nature or Animal Handling.
4. **Combat Strategy:** Open in familiar locations when possible. Transform for front-line fights; use Draw Power to recover spell slots; use Nature's Snare to control your space.

## Core Class Features

### Druidic (1st Level)

You know Druidic, the secret language of druids. You can leave hidden messages in bark, stone, and grass that only other druids can find.

---

### Natural Anchor (1st Level)

In natural environments, advantage on Sanity saves against fear, horror, and supernatural dread. After each long rest in a natural environment, recover 1 additional Sanity point.

---

### Nature's Gift (1st Level)

As a bonus action, heal a creature within 5 feet by rolling PB d4s and restoring that many HP. Uses equal to PB per long rest. At 5th level, dice increase to d6s. Once per long rest, also remove one minor condition (poisoned, charmed, frightened, or blinded).

---

### Spellcasting (1st Level)

Wisdom is your spellcasting ability.

- **Spell Save DC** = 8 + PB + Wisdom modifier
- **Spell Attack Modifier** = PB + Wisdom modifier

Prepare spells equal to WIS modifier + Druid level from the Primordial spell list. Concentration uses a Focus check (1d20 + WIS) rather than Constitution. Ritual spells can be cast without slots.

---

### Wild Shape (2nd Level)

Transform into Beast Form (action) or use Draw Power (bonus action). Uses per short rest increase with level.

#### Beast Form

Transform into a memorised Beast up to the CR cap in the Improved Beast Form progression. Duration: hours equal to your Proficiency Bonus.

**Beast Form HP by Size:**

| Beast Size | Beast Form HP |
|---|---|
| Tiny | 2 + PB |
| Small | 8 + PB |
| Medium | 14 + PB |
| Large | 22 + PB |
| Huge | 34 + PB |
| Gargantuan | 50 + PB |

**Evasion in Beast Form:** 1d20 + Beast's DEX modifier + your PB. Small/Tiny forms roll with advantage.

**Beast Form DR:** (Beast's AC − 10) + Beast's CON modifier + Beast's STR modifier (Large or larger only). Negative modifiers treated as 0. Called Shots reduce Beast Form DR by 2.

**Momentum:** Entering Beast Form during combat resets your Momentum to +1. Natural attacks are treated as weapon attacks for Momentum purposes.

**Reference Table:**

| Beast | Size | AC | Con | Str | Flat DR |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Wolf | Medium | 13 | +1 | — | 4 |
| Black Bear | Medium | 11 | +2 | — | 3 |
| Brown Bear | Large | 11 | +3 | +4 | 8 |
| Dire Wolf | Large | 14 | +2 | +3 | 9 |
| Giant Crocodile | Huge | 14 | +4 | +6 | 14 |
| Mammoth | Huge | 13 | +5 | +7 | 15 |

**Primal Dissolution:** Remaining in Beast Form beyond normal duration requires a Sanity save (DC 12) to revert voluntarily. Failure: retry once per minute; each subsequent failure causes 1 Sanity degradation.

#### Draw Power

Expend 1 Wild Shape use and 1 Focus to recover one expended spell slot of a level up to your Proficiency Bonus. Cannot use while in Beast Form.

---

### Wild Shape Combat Modes (2nd Level)

Choose one mode when transforming (active for that transformation):

**Predator Form:** Reduce Called Shot penalties by 1 in Beast Form.

**Guardian Form:** Gain DR 1 in Beast Form (stacks with Beast Form DR).

**Tracker Form:** Advantage on Evasion rolls in Beast Form.

---

### Nature's Snare (2nd Level)

*Reaction.* When a hostile creature enters a space within 10 feet of you or ends its turn there, roots erupt: **STR save** (DC = 8 + PB + WIS) or restrained until end of your next turn. The area becomes difficult terrain until the start of your next turn regardless. Once per round.

---

### Druid Subclass (2nd Level, and 7th, 11th, 15th Level)

Choose a Circle such as **Circle of the Leaf**, **Circle of the Shifter**, or **Circle of the Moon**. Features at 2nd, 7th, 11th, and 15th levels, plus Circle Spells always prepared.

---

|---|---|

### Bestial Reflex (6th Level)

*Reaction.* While in Beast Form, when targeted by a melee or ranged attack, gain advantage on your Evasion roll. If you evade, move up to 5 feet without provoking. Uses equal to PB per short rest.

---

### Call of the Wild (6th Level)

Communicate meaningfully with any Beast or Animal-tagged creature regardless of your current form.

---

### Nature's Remembrance (6th Level)

When you cast a Primordial spell of 2nd level or higher, use Wild Shape, or spend 10+ minutes attuning at a location, that location may gain a **Nature-Remembrance Mark** (tracked with your GM). Up to 3 marks per location.

In a Nature-Remembered area: gain **+1 Momentum** when you cast a spell or use Wild Shape (once per turn). Once per short rest, reroll a failed Concentration check in such an area and use either result.

---

### Heroic Boon (10th Level)

Choose one:

**Rite of the Kingdom:** Beasts and Animal-tagged creatures understand your speech. Summoned/charmed creatures gain +2 damage rolls. Advantage on CHA checks with Beasts. Once per long rest, cast a Beast-summoning or Beast-charming spell without a slot.

**Rite of the Shaper:** When you roll initiative with no Wild Shape remaining, regain 1 use (once per long rest). Enter Beast Form as a **bonus action** rather than action. Once per long rest, enter at maximum Beast Form HP + WIS modifier × 2.

---

### Primal Shift (10th Level)

When you enter Beast Form during an active combat encounter (not before initiative), gain **+1 Momentum** immediately.

---

### Nature's Command (11th Level)

As an action, invoke one of the following. Uses equal to PB per long rest.

**Command Beasts:** Target WIS modifier creatures within 60 feet; WIS save (DC = Spell Save DC) or charmed 1 minute.

**Summon Vines:** 20-foot radius (30 feet at 15th level) difficult terrain; STR save or restrained up to 1 minute.

**Elemental Surge:** Conjure an elemental spirit in a 5-foot space within 30 feet for 1 minute. Command as bonus action: Fire burst (2d8, 10-foot DEX save), Water heal (2d8 to creature within 30 feet), Air gust (STR save or 15-foot push + prone), or Earth buckle (10-foot difficult terrain for 10 minutes).

---

### Deepening Memory (11th Level)

Nature-Remembered areas with 2+ marks gain **Grounding Presence** (advantage on Sanity saves and fear effects from unnatural sources for you and allies within 30 feet) and **Primordial Reading** (once per long rest per location, receive a sensory impression of the most significant magical event within the past 30 days).

---

### Primordial Bond (14th Level)

**Flexible Nature Magic:** When you finish a long rest, choose one Primordial spell not currently prepared. Cast it once without expending a slot.

**Reactive Disruption** *(Reaction):* When a creature within 30 feet begins casting a spell, make a WIS (Insight) check contested by their spellcasting ability check. On success, the spell fails (slot not expended). Uses equal to WIS modifier per long rest.

---

### Nature's Grace (17th Level)

Resistance to poison damage; immunity to the poisoned condition. Resistance to cold damage. When you roll a natural 20 on a saving throw, recover one expended spell slot of 7th level or lower, or one Wild Shape use.

---

### Primordial Memory (17th Level)

Locations with 3 Nature-Remembrance Marks become **Primordially Remembered**. Within them: the entire 60-foot area is difficult terrain for hostile creatures (not magical, cannot be dispelled). Tremorsense 30 feet while standing there. You are always aware of direction, distance, and whether a magically-present creature has entered any of up to 3 Primordially Remembered locations since your last long rest.

---

### Epic Boon (20th Level)

Choose one (Nature's Boundless Form also grants unlimited Wild Shape):

**Nature's Eternal Voice:** Once per long rest, cast any Primordial spell of 8th level or lower without a slot. Once per short rest, maximise damage dice for a Primordial spell of 5th level or lower. Two spells of 6th level or lower are always prepared and each castable once per long rest without a slot. Ignore verbal, somatic, and free material components.

**Nature's Boundless Form:** Unlimited Wild Shape; transform into Beasts of CR 5 or lower without using a charge. Regain HP equal to WIS modifier at the start of each turn in Beast Form. Once per long rest, cast *shapechange* without a slot. As an action, end all conditions (charmed, frightened, poisoned, paralysed, disease) on yourself or one ally within 30 feet (WIS modifier uses per long rest).

## Last Stand Options

**Rules for Last Stand:** Free action when reduced to 0 HP, rolling a death save, or dying outright. Character permanently dies once resolved.

### 1. Final Form

*You clear your mind and unleash the beast within, knowing it will never need to be caged again.*

- Immediately enter Wild Shape as any Beast, Plant, or Elemental with CR up to your character level.
- Lose all damage vulnerabilities.
- Gain immunity to hostile abjuration magic.
- This form lasts up to 5 minutes.

### 2. Ley Waste

*You repeat the words, again and again, each time softer, as your body sinks into bark and stone.*

- Choose one Primordial cantrip or spell up to one level below your highest slot.
- At the end of each of your turns for 1 minute, cast that spell without a slot or components (no concentration required).
- Maintain one instance for up to 4 additional minutes after death.

### 3. Uncivilize

*You beseech the spirits of the Feywild to reclaim their progeny.*

- For 5 minutes: all hostile creatures within 1000 feet have their speeds halved and cannot use teleportation or planar travel; all allied creatures within 1000 feet gain the benefits of *Freedom of Movement*.

| Last Stand | Effect | Best Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Final Form** | Transform into CR-equal creature; immune to vulnerabilities and abjuration. | Raw physical power. |
| **Ley Waste** | Repeat a spell each turn for 1 minute; persists 4 minutes after death. | Sustained area control. |
| **Uncivilize** | Enemies halved speed, no teleport; allies gain Freedom of Movement. | Battlefield lockdown. |
