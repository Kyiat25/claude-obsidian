# The Bender

**Masters of Elemental Motion**

> *"Everyone else channels the elements. The Bender *is* the motion — body and element fused into a single, continuous act."*

---

## Body and Element as One

She didn't throw the fireball. Her arm moved through a flowing arc, each degree of the gesture a somatic component so perfectly integrated with the spell that no one watching could tell where the body ended and the magic began. When the flames erupted, she was already moving into the next form — a stance that would draw earth to her feet and grant the next strike the weight of stone.

You are the Bender. Not a fighter who casts spells, not a mage who sometimes punches — you are the synthesis of both, achieved through precision physical sequences that become the magic themselves. **Elemental Strikes** mean your attacks carry elemental force at range or close. **Elemental Combo** means every action generates a bonus reaction of elemental energy. **Elemental Affinity** means you choose how deep to go — master of one element whose spells hit harder and harder, or versatile manipulator of all four.

In FEARS, your Momentum engine is built into your hybrid nature. **Elemental Flow** at 5th level gives you +1 Momentum every time you cast a damaging spell, and +1 Momentum on your first elemental strike hit each turn. High Momentum means high-quality attacks mean more Momentum — a Bender who maintains +3 can cast and strike in the same turn with both contributions flowing through the rhythm.

---

## The Bender's Essence

You are a damage dealer and tactical controller who rewards sequential thinking — landing a strike first, then using the Combo follow-up; using spells to set up physical follow-ups; using your Discipline to define whether you prioritize offense (Ferocity), defense (Fortification), multi-element weaving (Fusion), or ally enhancement (Invigoration).

Your complexity ceiling is very high at late levels (multiple elemental affinities, Primordial Avatar stacking). Your floor is accessible: pick one element, use Elemental Strike, use Elemental Combo, keep moving.

## Class Features

### Hit Points

- **Hit Dice:** 1d8 per Bender level
- **Hit Points at 1st Level:** 8 + your Constitution modifier
- **Hit Points at Higher Levels:** 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per level after 1st

### Proficiencies

- **Armour:** Light armour
- **Weapons:** Simple weapons, chakrams, nunchaku, shortswords, shuriken
- **Tools:** One type of artisan's tools or one musical instrument of your choice
- **Saving Throws:** Strength, Constitution
- **Skills:** Choose two from Acrobatics, Athletics, History, Insight, Religion, and Stealth

### Starting Equipment

- (a) a quarterstaff, (b) a chakram, or (c) any simple weapon
- (a) a dungeoneer's pack or (b) an explorer's pack
- Leather armour and 10 shuriken

---

## Quick Build

1. **Ability Scores:** Make your chosen spellcasting ability (INT, WIS, or CHA) your highest score, followed by Dexterity.
2. **Lineage:** Any combination; Human, Tiefling, and Wood Elf are thematic.
3. **Background:** Hermit, Outlander, or any background with Acrobatics or History.
4. **Combat Strategy:** Open with an Elemental Strike to build Momentum, follow with Elemental Combo as a bonus action, then cast a spell if you have it available. Maintain flow: hit, combo, spell, hit.

## Core Class Features

### Spellcasting (1st Level)

When you gain your first Bender level, choose your spellcasting ability: **Intelligence**, **Wisdom**, or **Charisma**. Your magic manifests through precise physical sequences.

- **Spell Save DC** = 8 + PB + spellcasting ability modifier
- **Spell Attack Modifier** = PB + spellcasting ability modifier

You prepare spells equal to your spellcasting ability modifier + half your Bender level (rounded down, minimum 1) from your Elemental Affinity spell list. You regain all spell slots on a long rest. Concentration uses **Focus checks** instead of Constitution.

---

### Elemental Affinity (1st, 6th, 10th, and 14th Level)

At 1st level, choose one element: **Air** (thunder), **Earth** (acid and bludgeoning), **Fire** (fire and lightning), or **Water** (cold). That element's spell list is added to your Bender spell list.

At 6th, 10th, and 14th level, choose again:

**New element:** Add its spells to your list and learn one cantrip from the new element's list (doesn't count against cantrips known).

**Repeated element:** Spells from that element's list are cast one level higher than the slot expended. Stacks — four affinities in the same element means spells cast three levels higher than the slot used.

*Example: A Bender with three fire affinities casting *fireball* with a 3rd-level slot casts it as a 5th-level spell.*

---

### Elemental Strikes (1st Level)

When you take the Attack action, you can make melee or ranged spell attacks called elemental strikes as one or more of your attacks. Each strike deals damage of a type with which you have affinity.

**Melee:** Unarmed strike with 10-foot reach; 1d6 + spellcasting modifier.

**Ranged:** 60-foot range; 1d4 + spellcasting modifier.

**Momentum Interaction:** When you hit with an elemental strike, generate Momentum as normal for a successful weapon attack.

---

### Elemental Combo (2nd Level)

After you make a weapon attack, elemental strike, or cast a levelled spell, you can release elemental energy as a bonus action. Make a melee (within 10 feet) or ranged (within 60 feet) spell attack dealing 1d4 elemental damage. Add your spellcasting modifier if within 10 feet.

At 6th level: 2d4. At 10th: 3d4. At 14th: 4d4.

---

### Class Reactions (2nd Level)

You gain a pool of **Reaction Points** equal to your Proficiency Bonus per short or long rest.

**Elemental Counter (1 Reaction Point):** *Trigger:* A creature within 30 feet makes an attack roll against you. *Effect:* The attacker has disadvantage on the attack roll.

**Primordial Deflection (2 Reaction Points):** *Trigger:* You or an ally within 30 feet would take damage of a type associated with your Elemental Affinity. *Effect:* Reduce the damage by 2d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier.

---

### Bender Discipline (3rd Level, and 7th, 15th, 20th Level)

Choose one discipline:

**Disciple of Ferocity** — Offense. Gain proficiency with medium armour, shields, and martial weapons. Use your spellcasting ability for weapon attack and damage rolls. Discipline spell list includes *magic missile*, *magic weapon*, *blink*, *guardian of faith*, *arcane hand*. At 7th level, once per turn expend a spell slot when you hit to deal 2d8 + 1d8/slot level bonus damage (max 6d8), ignoring resistance. At 15th level, Elemental Combo damage increases to 5d4; can target two creatures with the combo attack. At 20th level, attack three times instead of two.

**Disciple of Fortification** — Defense. Gain proficiency with medium armour, heavy armour, and shields. Discipline spells include *shield*, *aid*, *protection from energy*, *resilient sphere*, *wall of force*. At 3rd level, create a Primordial Shield (bonus action) granting 1d6 temp HP to you or an ally within 30 feet (scales with level). At 7th level, as a reaction when a creature within 30 feet would take elemental damage, reduce that damage by 2d6 per affinity in that element. At 15th level, when you cast a Bender spell, one creature gains +AC bonus equal to the spell's level until start of your next turn.

**Disciple of Fusion** — Multi-element. Learn two additional cantrips and one spell from any element list. When you expend a spell slot to cast a Bender spell, augment it with another element to create an additional effect (sized 5 × slot level feet). Air+Earth: dust blinds; Air+Fire: smoke obscures and silences verbal components; Air+Water: ice difficult terrain; Earth+Fire: lava deals 1d6/slot fire; Earth+Water: mud slows; Fire+Water: steam obscures and damages. Uses equal to spellcasting modifier per short rest. At 7th level, change damage types of Bender spells between affinities. At 15th level, once you cast a spell on your action, also cast a cantrip as a bonus action (spellcasting modifier uses per long rest).

**Disciple of Invigoration** — Support. Discipline spells include *bless*, *healing word*, *mass healing word*, *aura of vitality*, *mass cure wounds*. At 3rd level, once per turn when you make an elemental strike, an ally within 30 feet can use a reaction to move up to half their speed without provoking opportunity attacks. At 7th level, once per turn when you cast a Bender spell targeting an ally, enhance it: remove a condition (blinded, charmed, deafened, frightened, poisoned, or stunned), add 1d6 to their next attack or save, grant a reaction weapon attack, or grant a half-speed reaction movement. At 15th level, when you enter Primordial Avatar state, grant up to five willing creatures within 30 feet one level-1 benefit from an element you have affinity with.

---

### Elemental Armor (3rd Level)

Choose one element you have affinity with — gain **DR 1** against damage types associated with that element. Increases to DR 2 at 10th level and DR 3 at 17th level. You can change the protecting element on a long rest. If you have multiple affinities, choose only one to provide this benefit at a time.

---

### Extra Attack (5th Level)

Make two attacks when you take the Attack action. You can replace one or both with elemental strikes.

---

### Elemental Flow (5th Level)

**Spellcasting Momentum:** When you cast a Bender spell of 1st level or higher that deals damage or imposes a condition, gain **+1 Momentum**.

**Strike Momentum:** When you hit with an elemental strike as part of the Attack action, gain **+1 Momentum** (once per turn).

---

### Reactive Surge (6th Level, Reaction Points)

*Trigger:* You are hit by an attack.
*Effect (2 Reaction Points):* Release a burst of elemental energy. The attacker makes a DEX save (DC = spell save DC) or takes 2d6 elemental damage (half on success). Increases to 3d6 at 11th and 4d6 at 17th level.

---

### Heroic Boon (10th Level)

Choose one:

**Elemental Mastery:** For one element you have affinity with, creatures with resistance take full damage; creatures with immunity take half damage. You also ignore special immunities to effects from that element.

**Primal Resilience:** When you activate Primordial Form, gain temp HP equal to 3 × your Bender level. Advantage on CON saves to maintain concentration while in Primordial Form.

**Flowing Combat:** Once per short rest, immediately after you use Elemental Combo, make one additional elemental strike against a different target as part of the same bonus action.

---

### Primordial Form (11th Level)

As a bonus action, embody aspects of a chosen element for 1 minute (or until unconscious). Choose one element you have affinity with. You can cast 1st-level spells from that element's list without expending slots for the duration (17th level: spells of 2nd level or lower).

Gain an elemental benefit:
- **Air:** Resistance to bludgeoning, slashing, piercing from nonmagical attacks.
- **Earth:** +2 bonus to AC.
- **Fire:** At end of each of your turns, creatures of your choice within 10 feet take fire damage equal to your spellcasting modifier.
- **Water:** Gain a vitality pool equal to 5 × Bender level. At end of each turn, up to three creatures within 10 feet can each regain 5 HP (expending that amount from the pool).

Once per long rest.

**Sanity Integration:** When activating, make a Sanity check (DC 12). On failure, gain 1 Trauma Point with the "Elemental Obsession" trauma type.

---

### Reactive Casting (11th Level, Reaction Points)

*Trigger:* You are hit by an attack.
*Effect (3 Reaction Points):* Cast a Bender cantrip targeting the attacker as part of the same reaction. The cantrip must have a 1-action casting time and must target the attacker or include them in its area.

---

### Primordial Avatar (18th Level)

As an action, become a primordial incarnation of your mortal form. Gain 50 temporary HP plus benefits based on your Elemental Affinities (one benefit per affinity, stacking by choice count per element). The effect lasts 10 minutes or until you fall unconscious. Once per long rest.

**Corruption Integration:** When you activate this feature, gain 1 Corruption Point.

**Example Stacking Benefits by Element:**

| Air Affinities | Benefit |
|---|---|
| 1 | Walking speed +15 ft; flying speed 60 ft |
| 2 | Immunity to thunder damage; cannot be grappled or restrained |
| 3 | Cast *misty step* once per turn without a slot (no action) |
| 4 | All speeds +15 ft more; under *freedom of movement* |

| Earth Affinities | Benefit |
|---|---|
| 1 | New AC calculation: 14 + spellcasting modifier |
| 2 | Immunity to acid; auto-succeed on saves against forced movement or prone |
| 3 | Gain 3d6 temp HP at start of each turn |
| 4 | Cast *shield* without a slot or reaction once per turn |

| Fire Affinities | Benefit |
|---|---|
| 1 | First hit per turn deals +2d6 fire or lightning damage |
| 2 | Immunity to fire damage |
| 3 | Cast *hellish rebuke* at 2nd level without slot/reaction once per turn |
| 4 | Under *fire shield* (warm) for the duration |

| Water Affinities | Benefit |
|---|---|
| 1 | Swim speed 60 ft; Help action as free action once per turn |
| 2 | Immunity to cold damage |
| 3 | Force a creature to reroll their attack or save and use lower result (no action, once per turn) |
| 4 | Under *true seeing* for the duration |

---

### Epic Boon (20th Level)

Choose one:

**Boon of Elemental Apotheosis:** Primordial Avatar lasts indefinitely once activated (still once per long rest). While in Primordial Avatar, activate Primordial Form as a bonus action without using its charge. Choose one element — permanently immune to its damage type.

**Boon of Worldshaper:** Once per long rest as an action, reshape terrain in a 100-foot radius. Create walls of stone/ice/fire (20 ft high, 10 ft thick), part water, flatten or raise earth, summon persistent winds, or similar manipulations. Changes last 1 hour (physical, not magical). Bender terrain spells (*wall of stone*, *control water*, etc.) have their duration extended to 24 hours if shorter.

**Boon of Primordial Immortality:** You stop aging; no food, water, or air required. Once per long rest, when reduced to 0 HP, activate Primordial Form as a free action (separate from its normal use) and regain HP equal to 10 × Bender level. Once per long rest, cast *magnificent mansion* without a slot or components (manifests as your chosen elemental material).

## Last Stand Options

**Rules for Last Stand:** Free action when reduced to 0 HP, rolling a death save, or dying outright. Character permanently dies once resolved.

---

### 1. Elemental Dissolution

*Your mortal form dissolves, becoming pure elemental energy that transforms the battlefield.*

**Effect:** Choose one element. Your body dissolves into that element, creating a 60-foot radius zone for 1 minute:

- **Air:** 60-mph winds; disadvantage on ranged attacks and STR checks; flying creatures without magical flight must make STR save or be blown 30 feet in random direction + prone.
- **Earth:** Difficult terrain with jagged stone; 2d10 bludgeoning at start of each turn on ground; ranged attacks into the area have disadvantage.
- **Fire:** 4d10 fire at start of each turn in area; heavily obscured by smoke; CON save or gain 1 exhaustion level at end of each turn.
- **Water:** Deep water; half speed and disadvantage on melee attacks; STR save or pulled 10 feet toward centre + knocked prone.

---

### 2. Primordial Sacrifice

*With your final breath, you transfer your elemental mastery to an ally.*

**Effect:** Choose one willing ally within 60 feet. They gain the following for 10 minutes:
- Enter a state identical to your Primordial Avatar (as if 18th-level Bender) with all your element benefits.
- Gain temp HP equal to 2 × your Bender level.
- Can cast any of your prepared spells without expending slots (using your spell save DC and attack modifier).
- Elemental strikes or unarmed strikes deal an additional 2d8 damage of your elements' type.

After 10 minutes, the ally may permanently accept the transformation by taking 3 Corruption Points, or return to normal.

---

### 3. Elemental Rebirth

*You don't die — you transform into a pure elemental being, your consciousness merging with primordial forces.*

**Effect:** Transform into an elemental (size Large) corresponding to one element you have affinity with (Air/Earth/Fire/Water Elemental stat block, retaining your mental ability scores, PB, and all features not requiring a humanoid form). This transformation lasts for 24 hours. After 24 hours, reform as yourself with 1 HP at the location of death.

*This can only be used once in a character's lifetime.*

| Last Stand | Effect | Best Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Elemental Dissolution** | 60-foot elemental zone for 1 minute with devastating environmental effects. | Controlling the battlefield. |
| **Primordial Sacrifice** | Grant ally full Primordial Avatar state + your spells for 10 minutes. | Empowering a key ally. |
| **Elemental Rebirth** | Transform into elemental for 24 hours; reform at 1 HP (once per lifetime). | Avoiding permanent death, continuing the fight. |
