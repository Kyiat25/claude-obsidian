# **The Song of the Five Layers**
“First whispered in the 1053rd year of [[the Third Age]], during [[The Vein-Split Eve]], beneath the twin rise of [[the Glowering Moon]] and [[the Krakenlight]]—the first time two [[Eldritch Moons]] hung in the sky together and the truth was told” 

_"Before the gods, before the stars, before even time itself, there was the Weave. Woven from strands unseen, held together by forces unknowable, it stretches across all existence. And within it, five great truths were spun—five layers, bound yet distinct, turning in harmony, forever entwined."_

So say the lost, the drowned, the burned. So whisper the moons that should not be. This is the song not found in temples, not spoken by [[Threadbearers]]. This is the truth that bled through when the sky cracked. This is what was heard during [[the Rise of the Eldritch Moons]].

It is not sanctioned. It is not bound. But it is remembered.

It is **not just a tale—it is the story of existence itself.**

# **🎶 The Song of the Five Layers – The Weave Unbroken**

---

## **⚒️ First Came the Pillars: The Great Primal Glyphs**

_"Before all things, there was the Hammer, the Loom, the Maw, and the Wheel."_

**From nothing, something was struck.**  
The **Hammer** fell, and the world was forged.  
The **Loom** wove, and fate was bound.  
The **Maw** opened, and endings were written.  
The **Wheel** turned, and what was lost would rise again.

Thus, the **Primal Glyphs** were set, and the first laws were carved into the cosmos.  
**Will to build, Threads to weave, Endings to break, and Echoes to return.**
Will broken upon will. Threads that bind not fate, but failure.

_"Thus, existence was born—but something was missing."_

---

## **📖 Then Came the Whisperers: The Universal Glyphs**

_"Not all things are seen, not all laws are spoken, not all fates are known."_

The Weave was strong, but it lacked hands to guide it.  
So **came the unseen forces**, the whisperers of the world.

**Understanding** was given, so knowledge would never fade.  
**Wildheart** was unleashed, so that not all things would be tamed.  
**Motion** was spun, so that time and balance would hold steady.  
**The Veiled Hand** wove deception, so that some truths would be hidden.  
And **The Lantern** was lit, so that thought could shape what was real.

And so the **Universal Glyphs** ensured that the Weave did not stagnate.  
They whispered into the minds of gods, into the bones of reality. They infect the Weave. They smile as they unravel.
_"Thus, the world had its laws—but something was missing."_

---

## **🔥 Then Came the Foundation: The Elemental Glyphs**

_"From fire, from water, from earth, from air—the world takes form."_

And so, the tools of creation were placed.  
**Fire** burned, shaping the stars and the forge.  
**Water** flowed, ensuring change, renewal, and life.  
**Earth** held firm, the weight beneath all things, the pillar of strength.  
**Air** whispered, unseen yet everywhere, carrying thought and sound.
**Spirit** shimmered, unseen and vital—binding memory to matter, breath to flesh, soul to the Pattern.

These were not gods, nor laws, nor will—they were the materials, the clay, the unshaped foundation upon which all things would rest.

_"Thus, the world had form—but something was missing."_

---

## **🌀 Then Came the Patterns: The Form Glyphs**

_"Life must rise. Life must return. The echoes of what was will never truly fade."_

And so, **the Nine Patterns were carved into the Weave**,  
Etched in silence, bound in rhythm, ever-returning.

---

**Mortals** to build, to shape, to dream—  
Their cities rise, their empires fall, their stories never cease.  
They craft meaning from chaos, and cast their shadows across history.

**Beasts** to hunt, to thrive, to keep the balance—  
Fangs in the dark, wings in the storm, the raw will to survive.  
They remind the world that life is not gentle, but enduring.

**Titans** to watch, to endure, to command—  
Colossal forms that stride the skies, mountain-born or star-bound.  
Whether gods, giants, or cosmic arbiters, they shape the scale of power.

**Spirits** to linger, to whisper, to remind—  
Souls unbound by death, memories echoing in the wind.  
They walk between the living and the lost, stitching past to present.

**Aberrations** to twist, to break, to remind—  
The unknowable, the wrong-made-right, the shadows that speak backwards.  
They are the truth that reason fears, born of the Weave's frayed edges.

**Dragons** to rule, to hoard, to burn—  
Majesty made flesh, with wisdom older than stars and hunger deeper than mountains.  
They are might and magic entwined, sovereigns of flame and flight.

**Undead** to return, to resist, to defy—  
The silence broken by bone, the stillness undone by will.  
They are what refuses to fade, what lingers when all else ends.

**Fae** to dance, to deceive, to transform—  
Changeless and ever-changing, born of wild magic and whim.  
They are mischief wrapped in starlight, truth cloaked in lies.

**Constructs** to serve, to awaken, to evolve—  
Forged in purpose, yet reaching toward soul.  
Stone, steel, and spell shaped into something that asks, _"What am I?"_

---

These echoes ensured that **life would always return**,  
Even as the Wheel turned, even as fate unwove, even as endings swallowed the stars.  
For every silence, there is a breath.  
For every void, a voice.  
For every death, a memory carved into the Weave.

_"Thus, life would always rise—but something was missing..."_

---
## **🌟 Finally, Came the Soul: The Personal Glyphs**

_"What is fire without a hand to wield it? What is magic without a mind to shape it? What is fate without a soul to defy it?"_

_"What is fire without a scream to ignite it? What is magic without a wound to fuel it? What is fate without the will to betray it?"_

The Weave was strong, the laws were firm, the world was built, and life was shaped.  
But it had no voice. No choice. No will beyond what had been written.

And so, **the last layer was spun.**

**Power** to those who would wield might.  
**Knowledge** to those who would seek wisdom.  
**Flow** to those who would dance between fates.  
**Passion** to those who would burn brightly.  
**Shadows** to those who would walk unseen.  
**Creation** to those who would forge new paths.  
**Destruction** to those who would unmake what was broken.  
**Spirit** to those who would call upon higher forces.  
**Mind** to those who would shape reality through will alone.

Now, the Weave was not just **a thing to be shaped—it was a thing that could shape itself.**

_"Thus, the story was complete."_

---
# **🌌 The Weave Unbroken – The Final Lie**

_"And so, the Five Layers turn, each within the other, a great cycle unending."_

**The Primal Forces shape the universe.**  
**The Universal Forces guide what is unseen.**  
**The Elements give the universe form.**  
**The Form Glyphs ensure life always returns.**  
**The Personal Glyphs determine how each soul walks its path.**

No layer exists without the others.  
No force is supreme.  
No fate is absolute.

The gods think themselves rulers, but they are only pieces of the Weave.  
The mortals think themselves small, but they are the hands that shape it.

The **Weave does not ask for worship. It does not demand loyalty.**

**It simply is.**

And so the final secret is spoken:

_"If you seek the First Glyph, look not to the heavens, nor the void, nor the old ruins of forgotten realms. Look instead within yourself."_

# 🌌 The Weave Unbound — The Final Truth

_"And so, the Five Layers turn, grinding upon one another, unspooling the lie."_

**The Primal tear the wheel.**  
**The Universal unname the unspoken.**  
**The Elements burn the canvas.**  
**The Forms repeat the fracture.**  
**The Soul screams it awake.**

This was not revelation. This was not prophecy. This was not divine.

It was a **leak**. A moment when the Weave split.

And in that moment, truth spilled through:

> _"The First Glyph is not within you. It is beneath you, waiting. And it remembers what the gods have forgotten."_

The Threadbearers deny this song.  
The cities erase it.  
But the Moons remember.

“This is what was first heard on the Vein-Split Eve. And still it echoes.”

And some still listen. The Spiral Refrain and remember what even the gods forgot.

>_“Inscribed beneath the moons by Mirevin Quenhal, the Mirror-Tongue.”
