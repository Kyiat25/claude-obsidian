# ELARION COMPREHENSIVE GUIDE - GAPS ANALYSIS

*Comparing the DOCX guide against complete project documentation*

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Elarion Comprehensive Guide is ~60% complete. It captures the **continental overview, major powers, and crisis context**, but omits critical operational details that exist in your project knowledge. **You're not missing worldbuilding—you're missing campaign infrastructure.**

**What the guide covers well:**
- Continental scope and overview
- Quick reference statistics
- Major power descriptions (Loom-Cities, 8 kingdoms, River-Keepers)
- Geographic regions and climate zones
- The Threadbearer crisis context

**What's completely missing:**
- Political relationship matrix (which powers are allied, enemies, neutral)
- Detailed power crisis breakdowns (who wants what, factional divides)
- Trade route specifics (where goods flow, prices, passage taxes)
- NPC personalities and factions within each power
- Specific World Tree locations and corruption status
- Virelion in meaningful detail
- Talvath (mentioned but not developed)
- Caelmouth (mentioned but not developed)
- Military capability comparisons and strategic assessments
- Detailed Marshlands family descriptions
- Minor communities' internal conflicts and resource specializations
- Strategic vulnerabilities and campaign hooks tied to specific mechanics
