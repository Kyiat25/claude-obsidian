# Eldritch Carving

_Style of Magic_

**"To carve the unknowable is to welcome pain, prophecy, and transformation."**

Eldritch Carving is a forbidden style of magic forged through direct communion with incomprehensible truths. The practitioners—called Apostles, Jaegers, or Martyrs—do not cast spells through gestures or formulas, but by inscribing the **Words of the Great Ones** ([[Eldritch Forces]]) into **living flesh**. Each rune is a fragment of reality-warping power, a syllable never meant for mortal comprehension. [[Eldritch Glyphs]] can be **drawn**, **tattooed**, or **scarified**, with each deeper level bringing greater power—and exponentially higher risk.

Originally born in the bloodstained rituals of the Hollow Scourge, carvings have since spread among war cults, void-prophets, and fungal resurrection sects. Each rune is both **artifact and mutation**, reshaping body, soul, and sanity.

 Some carvings are fragments of failed containment glyphs originally meant to bind [[Vezherak]], the Bound Becoming. These carvings—especially tattooed or scarified—unconsciously perpetuate his doctrine of recursive sealing.

---
## Key Themes

- **Flesh-Borne Glyphs**: Carvings must be inscribed on the body—pain is the canvas.
- **Tiered Activation**: Each carving grants powers based on how it was inscribed: Drawn (weakest), Tattooed (stronger), Scarified (strongest).
- **Madness & Mutation**: Failure to accept a carving may result in death, madness, or transformation.
- **Living Sigils**: Carvings may alter over time or whisper in forgotten tongues.

---
## Cultural Traditions

### 🌑 **Luyarnhan Jaegers**

- Use carvings to survive moon-cursed hunts. Often viewed as both cursed and chosen.

### ⚔️ **Martyrs of Tulio**

- Scar themselves willingly to draw in eldritch beasts and protect others.

### 🧙 **Fungal Heirs of Nawre**

- Employ carvings and parasitic fungi to return from death again and again.

## Carving Mechanics

- **Inscription Time**: 3 hours per carving.
- **Tools Required**: Proficiency with leatherworker's tools.
- **Saving Throws**: Constitution-based, based on inscription depth:
    - _Drawn_: DC 13 → Failure causes 2d10 force damage and short-term madness.
    - _Tattooed_: DC 20 → Failure causes 10d10 force damage and long-term madness.
    - _Scarified_: DC 29 → Failure causes 25d10 force damage and indefinite madness.

- **Failure Effect**: If reduced to 0 HP from carving failure, the target disintegrates in gore.
- **Carving Interruption**: If interrupted before the 3-hour completion, the save automatically fails.
- **Layered Attunement**: Tattooed carvings grant both tattoo and drawn effects; scarified grants all three.
- **Burn to Remove**: Carving removal requires burning the flesh thoroughly.

#### Modifiers to Carving DC

- Studying the **original** carving (8 hrs): -15 DC
- Studying a **perfect** copy: -8 DC
- **Near-perfect** copy: -4 DC
- **Imperfect** copy: +5 DC

#### Special Crafting Rules

- Studying a carving from a corpse causes it to vanish.
- Reproducing a carving requires a Sleight of Hand check (25+ = perfect, 20–24 = near-perfect).

**Mechanic**: Once per long rest, a [[Tethered Carving]] allows you to automatically re-cast a spell that failed. However, you must succeed a DC 18 Intelligence saving throw or trigger a **Recursive Lock**, causing magical paralysis or looping thought until the start of your next turn.

## Example Carvings (Selected)

> Effects grow in power from Drawn → Tattooed → Scarified

- **[[Curse of Moon-Blood]]**: Transform into a crimson beast; at death, go berserk and revive briefly.
- **[[Leaded Blood]]**: Sacrifice HP to create blood ammo.
- **[[Sacrificial Brand]]**: Marked during the Blood Moon; evil creatures instinctively locate and hunt the bearer.
- **[[Lightning Scion**]]: Teleport via electricity, gain resistance/immunity to lightning, cast _create undead_.
- **[[Nawre's Blessing]]**: Symbiosis with cordyceps fungus, warlock-like invocations, post-mortem resurrection.
- **[[Mephitic Seal]]**: Summon miasma with potent mutative properties; affects vision, corrosion, plague.
- **[[Third Carving]]**: Gain wings, psychic retaliation, a seraphic blade, and halo-based protection.
- **[[Second Carving]]**: Declare divine decrees ([[command]], [[bless]], [[healing word]]) with risk/reward trade-offs.
- **[[First Carving]]**: Grant sanctuary, protective flight, and conditional _true resurrection_.
- **[[The Chain Thought]]** ([[Vezherak]]): Once per long rest, reroll a failed spell, but become **obsessed** with repeating it until successful.
- **[[Dreamward Cut]]** ([[Ul’Naresh]]): Cast _[[dream]]_ or _[[phantasmal killer]]_ once per long rest as a ritual. Each use increases chance of **dream inversion** (sleepwalk casting).
- **[[The Erased Glyph]]** ([[Nhal’Zereth]]): Gain +2 to all spell saves. But **your name cannot be remembered** by creatures without magical assistance.

## Echo Glyph Integration

- **Crack of the Word**: Failed carving casts may trigger local echo events or glyph destabilization.
- **Glyphspawn Effect**: Death with a carving may spawn mutative effects in nearby creatures.
- **Ink of Echoes**: High-power carvings sometimes project fragments of themselves to those nearby.

## Social Context

- **Hunted**: Orcs view it as a blasphemy and dishonour on [[Scarred Magic]], believing they must have brought it into the world they must remove it.
- **Faith-Prohibited**: The **Faith of the Bound Pattern** considers carvings extreme Weave corruption.
- **Feared & Coveted**: Sought by the desperate, forbidden by most.

---
## Optional Mechanics

- **Word of Pain ([[Flaw]]/[[Feat]])**: On failing a madness save, gain advantage on your next spell/attack.
- **Living Glyph (Boon)**: Carving can adapt when bearer is critically wounded or reformed.
- **Devour Sin (First Carving)**: Trigger _true resurrection_ on death if _Warding Bond_ was active.

## Eldritch Pantheon Connections)

### 🩸 1. Tether Origins and Vezherak Influence

 Some carvings are fragments of failed containment glyphs originally meant to bind [[Vezherak]], the Bound Becoming. These carvings—especially tattooed or scarified—unconsciously perpetuate his doctrine of recursive sealing.
 
**Mechanic**: Once per long rest, a Tethered Carving allows you to automatically re-cast a spell that failed. However, you must succeed a DC 18 Intelligence saving throw or trigger a **[[Recursive Lock]]**, causing magical paralysis or looping thought until the start of your next turn.

### 🧠 2. Madness Domains and Ul’Naresh's Dream Influence

Carvings inscribed in sleep or during trance-dream rituals may resonate with [[Ul’Naresh]], the Dream Inverted. These carvings enact **dream-logic effects**—distorted but valid outcomes.

_Example_: A healing glyph restores HP, but blood reverses direction—flowing in from the air.

 **Mechanic**: Once per day, the DM may substitute a spell effect with a surreal variant. The effect is similar in magnitude but altered in form.

### 🌀 3. Nhal’Zereth and Carvings of Erasure

 Rare "Null Marks" connected to [[Nhal’Zereth]] allow the bearer to **erase a single event** from reality once per week.
 
**Mechanic**: The user may undo one spell effect, death, or item use as if it never occurred. DC 22 Wisdom saving throw—on a failure, the caster permanently loses a memory, their name, or a known spell.

### 🪞 4. Esh’Tarah and Reflection-Twisting Carvings

 Some carvings mimic or empower reflections, creating **self-aware doppelgängers** linked to [[Esh’Tarah]], the Mirrorborn.
 
**Effect**: During a full moon or sleep state, your reflection may cast spells or act independently. If not kept in check, it may begin **replacing you** in the memories of allies or enemies.

### 🧬 5. Carvings as Echo Hosts

Particularly potent carvings may attract **Eldritch Echoes** such as [[the Faceless Choir]] or [[the Forgotten Note]].

These Echoes **enhance magical capabilities**, but alter your voice, erase memories, or subtly shift your favoured schools of magic.

### 🕯 6. Corruption Progression (Tethered Influence)

The deeper one delves into Eldritch Carving, the more it begins to affect nearby magic and reality:

|Stage|Effect|
|---|---|
|**Stage 1: It Whispers**|You hear glyphs whisper while carving. Your skin tingles in their presence.|
|**Stage 2: It Marks Itself**|Carvings begin appearing during sleep or bloodletting. You lose full control.|
|**Stage 3: It Remembers**|Carvings trigger of their own will in battle. Glyphs re-form unpredictably.|
|**Stage 4: It Binds**|You become a conduit. Magical runes, wards, and glyphs nearby **reshape to match your carvings**. Your presence destabilizes normal spellwork.|
[[Corruption]] should always be a concern, for the power and consequence it will bring

## Design Notes

Eldritch Carving is one of the most dangerous and narratively powerful styles. It embodies **sacrifice, eldritch truth, and irreversible change**. It offers structured, tiered power with consequences that ripple through the Weave—and the soul. Each carving is both a **boon and a brand**, a covenant with a truth that has no voice, only scars.
