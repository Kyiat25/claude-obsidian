# ⚔️ **Barbarian — Fury Made Flesh**

### **Introduction**

You are not trained—you are unleashed. Where others fall back, you surge forward. Pain fuels you, rage sharpens you, and the battlefield bends around your sheer refusal to die.

### **What Makes It Unique**

The Barbarian is the only class that **gets stronger as it gets hurt**. Damage isn’t a threat—it’s fuel.

### **Signature Abilities**

- **Rage:** Enter a state of heightened power and resilience
- **Resilient Flow:** Maintain momentum even when injured
- **Overwhelming Presence:** Force enemies to deal with you

### **Gameplay Style**

Frontline chaos engine. You absorb pressure and convert it into damage and control.

### **Core Loop**

Take damage → gain power → hit harder → stay standing → repeat

### **Is This You?**

- Do you like getting stronger the closer you are to death?
- Do you enjoy being the centre of the fight at all times?
