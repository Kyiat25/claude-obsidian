## PROGRESSION SUMMARY

### General Rank Requirements

- **Rank 1 → 2:** Complete 3-5 background-specific tasks, approximately 5-8 sessions
- **Rank 2 → 3:** Complete major achievement in your field, approximately 8-12 sessions
- **Rank 3 → 4:** Legendary accomplishment that defines your legacy, approximately 12-20 sessions

### Downtime Activities by Background Type

**Combat-Focused (Gladiator, Monster Hunter, Vampire Hunter, etc.):**

- Train and maintain combat readiness
- Hunt bounties or contracts
- Build reputation through victories
- Recruit and train junior members

**Social-Focused (Diplomat, Noble, Entertainer, etc.):**

- Attend social functions and build connections
- Leverage influence for favors
- Maintain holdings and relationships
- Expand political or social network

**Knowledge-Focused (Sage, Archaeologist, Eldritch Scholar, etc.):**

- Research and study
- Publish works or share discoveries
- Seek ancient sites or forbidden knowledge
- Mentor students

**Underworld-Focused (Criminal, Smuggler, Spy, Charlatan, etc.):**

- Run operations for income
- Gather intelligence
- Build criminal/spy networks
- Maintain cover identities

**Spiritual-Focused (Acolyte, Hermit, Exorcist, Hedge Witch, etc.):**

- Perform religious/mystical duties
- Counsel those who seek guidance
- Maintain sacred spaces
- Commune with supernatural forces
