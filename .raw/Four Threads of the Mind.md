# Four Threads of the Mind: The Core of FEARS

## A Whispered Primer from the Archivists of the Shattered Loom

_“The sword is not your salvation. The spell is not your shield. It is your mind that will be tested here. The Weave watches. The Weave waits. And when you fall—it will not be because your blade broke.”_

In the world of **FEARS**, survival is not only a matter of strength, skill, or spell. It is the **resilience of your mind** that matters most. Every encounter, every spell cast, every truth glimpsed in the dark places of the world threatens to unravel what you are. To protect your identity—and your soul—you must understand the four forces that shape mental survival in FEARS:

---
## 🧠 Focus — The Mind’s Blade

[[Focus]] represents **mental clarity under pressure**. It is your ability to hold concentration in magical storms, to resist psychic invasion, and to stabilize glyphs that flicker with Wild magic.

- Every spell cast in chaos tests your **Focus Save**.
- A strong Focus can prevent disaster; a broken one invites chaos.
- At 0 Focus, your mind collapses **[[Burned out]]** —you cannot cast or concentrate.

> Focus is not willpower. It is discipline refined into survival.

---
## 🪞 Sanity — The Shield Against the Unseen

[[Sanity]] is your resistance to **trauma, horror, and metaphysical unravelling**. When you see things that should not exist—or remember things you never lived—it is Sanity that holds you together.

- Sanity saves trigger when exposed to Eldritch zones, glyphic corruption, or otherworldly truths.
- Losing Sanity causes madness, hallucinations, and even memory loss.
- At 0 Sanity, you become **[[Broken]]**—your soul is no longer entirely yours.

> Sanity is not cowardice. It is knowing how far the mind can stretch before it snaps.

---
## 🕷️ Corruption — The Creeping Price

[[Corruption]] tracks your **metaphysical infection**—the cost of exposure to forbidden knowledge and tainted glyphs. It grows silently as [[Focus]] fails and [[Sanity]] falters.

- At **5 Corruption**, you begin to change.
- At **10**, glyphs whisper back.
- At **15**, you are no longer fully mortal.
- At **20**, you become something else—Echo-Bearer, Glyphspawn, or worse.

> Corruption is not failure. It is transformation—and not all who transform return.

---
## 🎲 Luck — The Pendulum of Fate

[[Luck]] is the Weave’s last joke, or its moment of mercy. It rewards improbable success—or punishes it. It tracks the streaks of **natural 1s and 20s**, converting them into tangible blessings or curses.

- Roll three 1s before a [[Short Rest]]? You are **Cursed**.
- Roll three 20s? You are **Blessed**.
- These effects are immediate, strange, and often irreversible until rest.

> Luck does not care if you are ready. Only if the dice are watching.

---
## 🧩 Together: A Mindbound Engine

These four systems do not work alone. They **interact, escalate, and cascade** into each other.

- Lose [[Focus]], and you may gain [[Corruption]].
- Fail a [[Sanity]] save, and your Focus may fracture.
- Embrace Blessed [[Luck]] while Corrupted? The Weave may mark you.

There is no single meter to track your mind. It is a puzzle of pressure points—a net of balance between what you risk, what you see, what you cast, and what the dice decide.

---
## 📜 Remember:

> **[[Focus]] defends the moment.**  
> **[[Sanity]] defends the self.**  
> **[[Corruption]] erodes the soul.**  
> **[[Luck]] tests the weave.**

Together, they are the **heart of FEARS**. Track them well. Or they will track you.
