# The Warden

**Nature's Front Line**

> *"The storm doesn't move for you. It moves through you."*

---

## The Force That Holds

The tree line shudders. Not from wind — from her. She steps out of the forest's edge and the ground responds: frost spreading from her boots, bark splitting on the nearest trunk. She doesn't run toward the advancing warband. She plants her feet. She waits. The first soldier reaches her and learns what it means to strike stone.

You are the Warden. Where the Bender flows, you hold. Where the Druid becomes nature, you are nature's answer to a specific question: *What happens when something threatens this place?* You hold the line. Whether that line is a mountain pass, a forest boundary, or the space between your allies and what hunts them, you make that space yours and dare the world to cross it.

Your **Natural Power** dies run through everything you do — a pool of elemental force that empowers your strikes, fuels your reactions, and ultimately becomes the weapon you throw at enemies who think they can outlast you. Your **Prime Element** defines your identity: the mountain that won't move, the fire that burns harder when struck, the cold that slows the desperate, the storm that punishes retreat, the acid that eats through everything you thought would protect you.

In FEARS, your Momentum engine is territorial. When enemies fail saves against your Claimed Zone, you gain Momentum. When you successfully absorb their element and null the damage, you gain Momentum. Your **Discipline** determines how you generate that advantage — by holding ground (Guardian/Sentinel), by getting in close and staying there (Fanatic), or by identifying the most dangerous target and dismantling it (Apex Predator).

---

## The Warden's Essence

You are a front-line controller with escalating elemental pressure. Your actions deny space and punish aggression. You struggle at range and against foes that can simply ignore terrain. You excel in chokepoints, prepared positions, and any fight where the enemy has to come to you.

The Warden tracks:

• Prime Element choice (shapes multiple features)

Recommended for players who enjoy holding strategic positions, punishing aggression, and elemental identity.

## Class Features

### Hit Points

- **Hit Dice:** 1d10 per Warden level
- **Hit Points at 1st Level:** 10 + your Constitution modifier
- **Hit Points at Higher Levels:** 1d10 (or 6) + your Constitution modifier per level after 1st

### Proficiencies

- **Armour:** Light armour, medium armour, heavy armour, shields
- **Weapons:** Simple weapons, martial weapons
- **Tools:** Herbalist tools
- **Saving Throws:** Constitution, Wisdom
- **Skills:** Choose three from Animal Handling, Athletics, Intimidation, Nature, Perception, Resolve, Survival

### Starting Equipment

- (a) a martial weapon and shield, or (b) two martial weapons
- (a) chain mail, or (b) scale mail and a shield
- (a) an explorer's pack, or (b) a dungeoneer's pack
- Herbalist tools and a component pouch

---

## Quick Build

Make Strength or Constitution your highest score, then Wisdom. Choose the Guardian Discipline. Take Athletics, Perception, Survival, and Intimidation.

## Core Class Features

### Warden's Discipline (1st Level)

Choose your combat approach: **Guardian**, **Fanatic**, **Sentinel**, or **Apex Predator**. Features at 1st, 6th, 10th, and 14th levels.

---

### Natural Awareness (1st Level)

You add your Wisdom modifier to initiative rolls. When you win initiative in Round 1 (highest individual roll), gain +1 Momentum immediately.

At 18th level, also add your Wisdom modifier to initiative rolls in natural environments, where you roll initiative with advantage.

---

### Natural Power (1st Level)

Channel elemental force through your strikes using **Power Dice** (quantity and size shown in the table). When you hit with a weapon attack, expend one Power Die, roll it, and add the result to the attack's damage. Choose the damage type: **acid, bludgeoning, cold, fire, lightning,** or **thunder**.

Regain half your total Power Dice (rounded down) on a short rest; all on a long rest.

---

### Elemental Presence (2nd Level)

As a bonus action, activate your Elemental Presence for 1 minute (PB uses per long rest). While active:
- Creatures starting their turn within 5 feet take damage equal to your WIS modifier of your Prime Element's type.
- You shed dim light in a 10-foot radius.
- Advantage on CHA (Intimidation) checks against creatures that can see you.

**Momentum Interaction:** Activating Elemental Presence in the first round of combat grants +1 Momentum.

---

### Source Magic (2nd Level)

You gain access to spell slots as shown in the table, recharging on a **short or long rest**. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability. Spell Save DC = 8 + PB + WIS; Spell Attack = PB + WIS. Concentration uses your Constitution saving throw proficiency.

---

### Class Reactions (2nd Level)

These reactions use your **Proficiency Bonus pool**, refreshing on short or long rest.

**Elemental Rebuke (Level 2):** *Trigger:* A creature within 5 feet hits you with a melee attack. *Effect:* Spend 1 use. They take Power Die damage (roll it) of your Prime Element's type. CON save (DC = 8 + PB + WIS) or pushed 5 feet. If they fail, gain +1 Momentum.

**Elemental Wall (Level 7):** *Trigger:* A creature moves toward you or an ally within 10 feet. *Effect:* Spend 1 use. STR save or movement stops immediately (speed 0 until end of their turn); on failure, also take WIS modifier damage of your Prime Element's type. At 11th level, damage on failure increases to 1d8 + WIS modifier.

**Primal Retribution (Level 13):** *Trigger:* A creature within 30 feet deals damage to you or an ally. *Effect:* Spend 2 uses. CON save (DC = 8 + PB + WIS). Failure: 3d8 damage of your Prime Element's type + Frailty appropriate to your element. Success: half damage, no Frailty. +1d8 at 15th and 19th levels.

---

### Weapon Mastery (3rd Level)

At 3rd level, gain Weapon Mastery with **two martial weapons** of your choice. When you hit with a mastered weapon, apply one Weapon Skill once per turn. Unlock new masteries after the required critical hits: 2 crits for simple, 3 for martial.

---

### Prime Element (3rd Level)

Your elemental affinity crystallises. Choose one:

**Earthen Bulwark** *(Bludgeoning):* When you expend a Power Die for bludgeoning damage, the target makes a STR save or is **grappled** for 1 minute (can repeat save at end of their turns). Warden Resonance — **Stone Skin:** DR 1 against all physical damage while wearing medium or heavy armour.

**Flaming Bravery** *(Fire):* When you expend a Power Die for fire damage, the target takes the same extra damage again at the start of their next turn (no save). Warden Resonance — **Heat Ward:** When bloodied, creatures hitting you with melee attacks take fire damage equal to your WIS modifier automatically.

**Stoic Chill** *(Cold):* When you expend a Power Die for cold damage, the target has disadvantage on its next attack roll before the start of your next turn. Warden Resonance — **Glacial Endurance:** When damage would cause you to cross an HP threshold (triggering an Injury save), expend one Power Die as a free action. Reduce the damage by the die result — this may prevent you from crossing the threshold entirely.

**Unrelenting Storm** *(Lightning/Thunder):* When you expend a Power Die for lightning or thunder damage and the target moves willingly before the start of your next turn, roll your Power Die again — the target takes additional damage equal to the result + WIS modifier. Warden Resonance — **Grounding Charge:** Resistance to lightning damage. When a creature hits you with a metal weapon, they take lightning damage equal to your WIS modifier automatically.

**Putrid Corrosion** *(Acid):* When you expend a Power Die for acid damage, the target cannot add STR or DEX modifier to weapon damage rolls until end of their next turn. Warden Resonance — **Caustic Hide:** Creatures that grapple you or are grappled by you take acid damage equal to your WIS modifier at the start of each of their turns.

---

### Extra Attack (5th Level)

When you take the Attack action, you may attack twice instead of once.

---

### Elemental Absorption (6th Level)

When you take damage of your Prime Element's damage type, use your reaction to absorb some of it. Reduce the incoming damage by your Power Die (roll it — don't expend it). Your next weapon attack before the end of your next turn deals bonus damage equal to the amount reduced, of your Prime Element's type.

Uses equal to your PB per short or long rest.

**Momentum Interaction:** If you reduce damage to 0 using Elemental Absorption, gain +1 Momentum.

---

### Prime Elemental Shield (7th Level)

As a reaction (1 Class Reaction use) when targeted by an attack or required to save, invoke your Prime Element's shield:

**Earthen Bulwark:** Advantage on Evasion rolls against ranged attacks or DEX saves.

**Flaming Bravery:** +PB to Evasion roll; if you evade, the attacker takes fire damage equal to your WIS modifier.

**Stoic Chill:** Reduce melee attack damage by WIS modifier + Power Die result (roll, don't expend).

**Unrelenting Storm:** Reduce ranged weapon attack damage against you or adjacent ally by WIS modifier + Power Die result.

**Putrid Corrosion:** Until end of your next turn, all attacks with the triggering metal weapon have disadvantage and cannot benefit from advantage from any source.

---

### Elemental Fortitude (9th Level)

- No need for CON saves to maintain concentration on Warden spells while you have 1+ Power Dice.
- Advantage on saves against being frightened or charmed by creatures of elemental origin.
- Recover 1 additional Power Die when you finish a short rest.

---

### Heroic Boon (10th Level)

Choose one:

**Warden's Permanence:** Elemental Presence is always active — no bonus action needed, no duration limit. Its radius increases to 10 feet. Creatures that fail the CON save against Elemental Rebuke also suffer your Prime Element's secondary effect automatically.

**Unyielding Terrain:** While standing still (no movement on your last turn): gain DR 3 (stacking with armour DR), advantage on all saving throws, and advantage on Evasion rolls against melee attacks. Your Class Reaction pool refreshes by 1 use at the start of each of your turns (maximum equal to your PB).

---

### Improved Natural Power (11th Level)

Once on each of your turns, when you deal damage with a weapon attack or Warden cantrip, deal an extra 2d8 damage of your Prime Element's type for free (no Power Die expenditure).

---

### Nature Sense (13th Level)

By touching a natural surface for 1 minute, shift your perception to any point on that surface within 100 feet for up to 10 minutes. You are blinded/deafened to your own senses during this time but can end it at will.

You are never surprised in natural environments.

---

### Channel Power (15th Level)

As a bonus action, expend four Power Dice to regain one expended spell slot. Once per long rest.

---

### Elemental Dominance (17th Level)

When you activate Elemental Presence, its radius increases to 15 feet. Creatures that take damage from it must make a CON save (DC = 8 + PB + WIS) or suffer your Prime Element's secondary effect for 1 round.

Your Elemental Absorption now works against **all** elemental damage types, not just your Prime Element's type (though the bonus damage on your next attack only applies when absorbing your Prime Element's type).

---

### Epic Boon (20th Level)

Choose one:

**Boon of the Immovable Force:** You cannot be moved against your will by any effect — magical or physical — unless you choose to be. While you have not moved on your turn, you cannot be knocked prone, and Evasion rolls cannot result in less than a partial defence (minimum result against any attack is halved damage).

**Boon of the Living Element:** You no longer need to eat, drink, or sleep — instead absorbing ambient elemental energy of your Prime type. Immunity to your Prime Element's damage type. Once per lifetime, when you would die, instead become your element for 1 hour (incorporeal, cannot be harmed, cannot interact physically), then reform at 1 HP.

---

## Last Stand Options

A Warden's Last Stand is not retreat. It is an escalation.

**Rules for Last Stand:** Free action when reduced to 0 HP, rolling a death save, or dying outright. Character permanently dies once resolved.

---

### 1. The Earth Remembers

*You don't just die here. You become here.*

**Effect:** A 60-foot radius of terrain around you transforms violently, shaped by your Prime Element. Choose one effect based on your element:

- **Earthen Bulwark:** Ground heaves, creating walls and difficult terrain. Enemies make DEX save or take 8d10 bludgeoning damage + knocked prone. Terrain altered permanently.
- **Flaming Bravery:** Fire erupts in 60-foot radius. DEX save or 8d10 fire + set alight (1d6 fire/turn for 1 minute).
- **Stoic Chill:** Flash-freeze. CON save. Enemies that fail are restrained in ice for 1 hour; allies who fail are slowed for 1 minute.
- **Unrelenting Storm:** Lightning strike hits every enemy in radius (DEX save for half, 8d10 lightning). Storm persists 1 minute, striking a random enemy each round for 3d10.
- **Putrid Corrosion:** Wave of acid. CON save or 8d10 acid damage + armour permanently reduced by DR 2. Metal armour destroyed on critical failure.

---

### 2. Hold the Line

*One last time. Nothing gets through.*

**Effect:** Remain standing at 0 HP, sustained by pure elemental will. For 1 minute:
- You cannot die from HP damage alone during this time.
- All allies within 30 feet gain DR 3 (stacking) as your elemental aura expands to cover them.
- Each time you succeed on a death saving throw, discharge a burst: all enemies within 10 feet take 2d10 damage of your Prime Element's type (no save).
- When the minute ends or you fail your third death save, you die.

---

### 3. The Storm Breaks

*You've been holding it in your entire life. Don't hold it anymore.*

**Effect:** Your body becomes a temporary vessel for your Prime Element in its most raw form. For three rounds:
- Elemental Presence radius becomes 60 feet; deals 3d10 damage of your element's type (no save).
- Each of your attacks automatically hits and deals maximum damage.
- You are immune to all damage.
- You cannot be moved, charmed, frightened, or controlled.

After three rounds, the vessel empties. You die.

| Last Stand | Effect | Best Use |
|---|---|---|
| **The Earth Remembers** | 60-foot elemental terrain transformation (element-specific, 8d10+). | Permanent battlefield alteration. |
| **Hold the Line** | Fight at 0 HP for 1 minute; allies gain DR 3; death save successes discharge. | Protecting allies to the very end. |
| **The Storm Breaks** | 3 rounds of auto-hit + max damage + full immunity. | Maximum destruction, guaranteed finale. |

## Disciplines

### Guardian

*Steel and stone. You fight at the front, you stay at the front, and you make the front cost everything.*

**Combat Training (1st Level):** Proficiency with all martial weapons. Choose one Fighting Style: Archery, Defence, Great Weapon Fighting, or Protection. Gain an additional Fighting Style at 15th level.

**Momentum Interaction:** When you use Protection Fighting Style and the protected ally takes no damage, gain +1 Momentum.

**Strength of the Elements (6th Level):** When you expend a Power Die to deal extra damage, treat any result lower than the maximum as the maximum. WIS modifier uses (minimum 1) per short rest.

**Shielding Aura (10th Level):** When you or a creature within 30 feet takes elemental damage, use your reaction (1 Class Reaction use) to halve that damage.

**Nature's Fury (14th Level):** When you make a weapon attack, expend a Power Die to gain advantage on the roll. For each d20 that would hit, roll the Power Die separately and deal extra damage equal to each result of your Prime Element's type.

---

### Fanatic

*You don't need the weapon. You are the weapon.*

**Pugilist (1st Level):** Unarmed strikes use your Power Die for damage. Use WIS instead of STR for unarmed attack and damage rolls. When you take the Attack action with an unarmed strike, make one additional unarmed strike as a bonus action. Unarmed strikes count as magical.

**Momentum Interaction:** When you make two unarmed strikes in the same turn and both hit, gain +1 Momentum.

**Precise Power (6th Level):** When you expend a Power Die on an unarmed strike, roll the die twice and use the higher result. WIS modifier uses per long rest.

**Extension Stance (10th Level):** Reach with unarmed strikes extends to 15 feet when both hands are empty. While grappling, elemental force holds them — your hands are free. Touch spells can target creatures within 15 feet.

**Exploding Elements (14th Level):** When you expend a Power Die on an unarmed strike and hit, burst the elemental energy outward. All creatures within 5 feet of your target (excluding you) take Power Die result damage of your Prime Element's type (DEX save for half, DC = 8 + PB + WIS).

---

### Sentinel

*You don't chase the threat. You make the threat come to you — and regret it.*

**Territorial Claim (1st Level):** When you spend no movement on your turn, create a **Claimed Zone** (10-foot radius). Enemies entering or starting there make STR save (DC = 8 + PB + WIS) or halved speed for the rest of their turn. You have advantage on opportunity attacks within your Claimed Zone. Allies within gain +WIS modifier to Evasion rolls. Collapses if you move; reforms when you stop.

**Momentum Interaction:** When a creature fails the STR save to enter your Claimed Zone, gain +1 Momentum.

**Ground Control (6th Level):** When a creature fails the Claimed Zone save, they also trigger your Elemental Presence damage immediately. You may make opportunity attacks against creatures attempting to leave your Claimed Zone, even if they Disengage.

**Sentinel's Anchor (10th Level):** While standing still: Claimed Zone radius increases to 20 feet; advantage on all saving throws; armour DR increases by 2 (stacking); creatures cannot teleport into your Claimed Zone.

**Elemental Dominion (14th Level):** Your Claimed Zone becomes an elemental hazard — difficult terrain automatic for enemies; creatures failing the entry save automatically suffer your Prime Element's Power Die secondary effect (no Power Die expenditure required).

---

### Apex Predator

*The most dangerous thing in the wilderness is the thing that chose to be there.*

**Predator's Mark (1st Level):** As a bonus action, mark one creature within 60 feet as your **Prey** (lasts until you mark a new target, Prey dies, or long rest). Against your Prey: deal extra damage equal to your WIS modifier on every weapon attack hit; always know their approximate location within 60 feet; Called Shot penalties reduced by 1; advantage on Perception and Survival checks to track them.

**Momentum Interaction:** When you reduce your Prey to 0 HP, gain +2 Momentum.

**Predator's Pursuit (6th Level):** Ignore difficult terrain moving toward your Prey. When Prey attempts to Disengage, they make a DEX save (DC = 8 + PB + WIS) or fail (can still move but you still get your opportunity attack). Called Shot penalties against Prey reduced by 2 total.

**Apex Strike (10th Level):** When you make your first weapon attack against Prey on your turn, expend a Power Die. If it hits, roll the die twice and use the higher result. If the attack is a critical hit, Prey makes a CON save (DC = 8 + PB + WIS) or gains a Frailty appropriate to your Prime Element.

**Final Hunt (14th Level):** Once per long rest, when you reduce your Prey to 0 HP, immediately designate a new Prey as a free action (no bonus action needed). When Prey is bloodied, your Extra Attack grants three attacks against them instead of two.
