The Threads of Creation and the Unseen Tapestry of Existence

#### _"Before gods rose and fell, before stars burned and crumbled, before even time had meaning, there were the Glyphs."_

So begins the oldest tale whispered across the dying embers of forgotten worlds. The Glyphs are not merely symbols nor mere cosmic forces; they are the unspoken foundation of all things. They existed before existence, shaping reality itself, forging the eternal dance of creation, fate, balance, and unravelling.

The sages call them the Threads of the First Weave, the Runes of Reality, the Hidden Words of **Astralor**. But they are beyond names, beyond comprehension—each Glyph is a truth, an inevitability, a force so primal that not even the gods who wield their power truly understand them.

---

## The First Layer: The Great Primal Glyphs

### The Eternal Pillars of Existence

_"Before there was time, there was the Hammer, the Loom, the Maw, and the Wheel."_

The Primal Glyphs are the pillars of existence itself, so ancient that even gods are blind to their workings. They are not beings, nor minds, nor wills—they are the bones of reality, forces so powerful that not even **Astralor, the Forger of Worlds**, nor **Aeloria, the Weaver of Fates**, can control them.

These four great forces shape the fundamental cycle of all existence.

---

### The Glyph of Will

**Embodied by: Astralor, the First Builder**

_"From the great void, Astralor struck the First Blow, and fire and stone were born."_

The Glyph of Will is the source of all creation, the hammerstroke that forges land, sky, sea, and life itself. **Astralor** does not command this Glyph—he _is_ its greatest expression, the cosmic smith whose every strike upon the void brings forth new worlds. Every time a new reality is made, it is through the resonance of this Glyph flowing through his forge.

It is the driving force behind all acts of building, shaping, and bringing order from chaos. Mortals who build great monuments, forge legendary artefacts, or establish enduring civilisations unknowingly channel echoes of the Glyph of Will.

---

### The Glyph of Threads

**Embodied by: Aeloria, the Weaver of Fates**

_"Nothing is without pattern, no moment is without consequence. The Loom spins on."_

The Glyph of Threads is the unseen lattice that binds reality. It is fate, destiny, the ever-unfolding tapestry of cause and effect. **Aeloria** weaves this Glyph into the lives of gods and mortals alike, though even she cannot claim to control it fully—she merely interprets the threads and guides their weaving.

Through this Glyph, every action ripples across time, every choice weaves new strands into the great pattern. Seers and prophets touch the edges of this Glyph when they glimpse fragments of what is to come.

---

### The Glyph of Endings

**Embodied by: The Whispering Dark, the Maw of Entropy**

_"The first star was swallowed, and so began the cycle of loss."_

Nothing is eternal. Nothing is safe. Everything, in time, will be unmade. This is the truth of the Glyph of Endings, the abyssal power of destruction, entropy, and dissolution. **The Whispering Dark** does not create this force—it _serves_ it, an eternal hunger that consumes all things, ensuring that what is built will one day crumble, that what lives will one day fade.

This is not malice. This is a necessity. Without endings, there could be no renewal, no space for new creation. The Whispering Dark is the clearing of the field before the next planting.

---

### The Glyph of Echoes

**Embodied by: The Restorer, the Wheel of Renewal**

_"Even the ash of a dead world holds the memory of flame."_

Destruction is never final. The Glyph of Echoes ensures that what is lost will return, changed, reborn, but never truly gone. **The Restorer** turns this eternal wheel, ensuring that every ending carries within it the seed of a new beginning, that the patterns of the past resurface in the cycles of the future.

Resurrection magic, reincarnation, and the persistence of memory all draw from this fundamental truth—nothing is ever completely erased from existence.

---

## The Second Layer: The Universal Glyphs

### The Hidden Weavers of Reality

_"Beyond the pillars of existence, there are forces unseen, truths untold, mechanisms unknown even to the greatest gods."_

These are the Universal Glyphs, the silent forces that shape all things. They do not command the heavens or forge new worlds, but they ensure reality functions according to deeper laws. Each is represented by a cosmic entity that serves as its living embodiment.

---

### The Glyph of Understanding

**Embodied by: The Keeper of Knowledge**

Knowledge, once discovered, can never be fully erased. **The Keeper of Knowledge** ensures that lost truths find their way back through whispers in dreams, inscriptions on ancient stone, or sudden flashes of insight. This Glyph preserves memory even when all else fades, maintaining the great Archive of All That Has Been Known.

Libraries that survive catastrophes, scholars who rediscover forgotten lore, and the persistence of ancient texts all reflect the Keeper's influence.

---

### The Glyph of the Wildheart

**Embodied by: The Wildheart, the Untamed Force**

Some things remain untamed, unshaped, unchained. No matter how ordered creation becomes, chaos persists at its edges. **The Wildheart** ensures that wild magic, primal instinct, and untameable forces endure, preventing reality from becoming too rigid, too controlled, too predictable.

Untamed wilderness, wild magic surges, and the savage beauty of nature all spring from this Glyph's influence.

---

### The Glyph of Motion

**Embodied by: The Tide Between, the Balance**

Nothing is ever truly still. All things move and shift, whether through the passage of time, the flow of energy, or the turning of cosmic cycles. **The Tide Between** maintains this eternal motion, preventing stagnation and ensuring balance between all opposing forces.

The turning of seasons, the flow of rivers, and the passage of time itself are all expressions of this Glyph.

---

### The Glyph of the Veiled Hand

**Embodied by: The Veiled Hand, the Hidden Truth**

Some truths will always remain hidden. Secrets whisper at the edge of perception, forbidden knowledge lurks beyond mortal comprehension, and illusions shape what can be seen. **The Veiled Hand** conceals what should not be known, maintaining the mysteries that keep reality from being fully understood—and thus, fully controlled.

Illusions, hidden knowledge, and secrets that persist across ages all fall under this Glyph's domain.

---

### The Glyph of the Dreaming

**Embodied by: The Dreaming (Entity Unknown)**

This Glyph weaves the bridge between thought, illusion, and the unknowable. It allows ideas to take shape before they become real, transforming imagination into possibility. The entity that embodies this Glyph remains mysterious, speaking only through visions, prophecies, and the strange logic of dreams.

The Feywild's reality-bending nature, prophetic visions, and the power of belief to shape reality all stem from this Glyph.

---

Together, these forces whisper their influence into the gods, into the Weave, into the very fabric of existence. The Primal Forces may believe they control creation, but the Universal Glyphs ensure that no single power can dominate completely.

---

## The Third Layer: The Elemental Glyphs

### The Foundation of Worlds

_"When Astralor strikes the forge, he shapes the four primal elements into being."_

These are the raw materials of all realms, the fundamental building blocks from which worlds are constructed. Unlike the Primal and Universal Glyphs, the Elemental Glyphs are not embodied by singular cosmic entities but instead give rise to entire pantheons of elemental deities.

---

### The Glyph of Fire

**Associated Pantheon: The Fire Thread Gods**

Fire burns with destruction and passion. It is a transformation through heat, the force that both creates and consumes. The gods of the Fire Thread—deities of forge-craft, warfare, passion, and purification—all draw their power from this fundamental Glyph.

---

### The Glyph of Water

**Associated Pantheon: The Water Thread Gods**

Water flows with change and renewal. It adapts to every shape, carving canyons and nourishing life alike. The Water Thread pantheon governs seas, storms, healing, and transformation—all expressions of water's essential nature.

---

### The Glyph of Earth

**Associated Pantheon: The Earth Thread Gods**

Earth holds with stability and endurance. It is the foundation upon which all else rests, unyielding and eternal. Earth Thread deities rule over mountains, forests, craftsmanship, and the deep places of the world.

---

### The Glyph of Air

**Associated Pantheon: The Air Thread Gods**

Air moves with freedom and intellect. It carries thought across distance, whispers secrets on the wind, and refuses to be bound. The Air Thread pantheon commands sky, knowledge, travel, and communication.

---

### The Glyph of Spirit

**Associated Pantheon: The Spirit Thread Gods**

Spirit binds with memory and essence. It is the force that connects soul to body, past to present, and the living to the dead. The Spirit Thread pantheon governs ancestral worship, memory-magic, echoes of the departed, and the preservation of identity beyond death.

Unlike the other elemental forces which shape the physical world, Spirit shapes the metaphysical—it is why souls persist after death, why memories can be preserved in objects, and why the echoes of what once was can still speak to those who listen. Druids, shamans, and keepers of ancestral rites all draw upon this fundamental Glyph.

---

**NOTES:** These fiveelements shape not just physical existence, but the gods themselves, influencing their domains and powers. The elemental pantheons are discussed in greater detail in the Threads and Pantheons articles.

---

## The Fourth Layer: The Form Glyphs

### The Eternal Patterns of Life

_"The gods may shape the stars, but it is the Glyphs of form that shape the patterns of life."_

The Form Glyphs are the eternal blueprints of existence, ensured by **The Restorer** through the Glyph of Echoes. Even across infinite worlds, certain patterns always emerge, guaranteeing that life takes recognizable shapes. These Glyphs are not embodied by entities but rather represent fundamental archetypes woven into the fabric of reality itself.

### The Nine Form Glyphs

- **Beast Glyph**: Ensures that animals, predators, and natural creatures exist in every world
    
- **Mortal Glyph**: Guarantees that sentient beings—humans, elves, dwarves, and others—will always return
    
- **Titan Glyph**: Governs the emergence of colossal beings, primordial giants, and god-like entities
    
- **Spirit Glyph**: Ensures that the dead remain, that souls persist, that ghosts walk between worlds
    
- **Aberration Glyph**: Guarantees that some things are never right, that alien horrors exist at reality's frayed edges
    
- **Draconic Glyph**: Ensures apex creatures of great intelligence and strength—dragons, phoenixes, titanic beasts—always arise
    
- **Undead Glyph**: Governs beings that return after death, whether as revenants, vampires, or something worse
    
- **Fae Glyph**: Commands mischievous, immortal, or wildly magical creatures—fairies, elementals, shapeshifters
    
- **Construct Glyph**: Ensures some beings are built rather than born—golems, warforged, divine avatars
    

No world is ever truly new. The Restorer ensures that echoes remain, and through these Form Glyphs, life always finds a way to return.

---

## The Fifth Layer: The Personal Glyphs

### The Marks Upon the Soul

_"The gods may shape the stars, but it is the Glyphs of the self that shape the soul."_

The Personal Glyphs are the most intimate of all, for they define the very nature of individuals. Unlike the cosmic, elemental, and universal glyphs which shape existence itself, the Personal Glyphs are the marks upon each being, guiding their strengths, instincts, and destinies.

These Glyphs are not controlled by any single entity but emerge from the interaction of all Five Layers—each mortal, each god, each spirit carries one or more of these marks, determining how they interact with the Weave itself.

### The Nine Personal Glyphs

Each mortal is born attuned to one—sometimes more—but never none:

- **Glyph of Power**: Physical prowess, martial dominance, the ability to command through force
    
- **Glyph of Knowledge**: Study, learning, and understanding the mysteries of the universe
    
- **Glyph of Flow**: Agility, adaptability, and thriving through change
    
- **Glyph of Passion**: Emotion, instinct, magic fueled by raw feeling
    
- **Glyph of Shadows**: Stealth, deception, and manipulation of perception
    
- **Glyph of Creation**: Crafting, innovation, shaping reality through will or art
    
- **Glyph of Destruction**: Chaos, unmaking, the power to break what exists
    
- **Glyph of Spirit**: Faith, conviction, resilience of soul
    
- **Glyph of Mind**: Psionics, thought, willpower made manifest

## Beyond the Five Layers: The Null Reflection

### The Corruption That Should Not Be

_"They are not gods. They are errors. Echoes of what should never have been. And yet, they are here."_

While the Five Layers of Glyphs structure and sustain reality, there exists a **counter-force**—not a sixth layer, but rather the **absence of all layers**, the **anti-pattern** that emerges when the Weave itself fractures.

This is the **Null Reflection**, and from it bleed the **Eldritch Forces**.

---

### Origin of the Eldritch

The Eldritch Pantheon was not born—they were **fractured off** from reality itself when **The Whispering Dark** first brushed against the Weave in the deep past. Though not creations of the Whispering Dark, they are **spawned in the shadow of its touch**—its brush upon the Weave cracked something open, allowing these entities to bleed into being.

They exist **in defiance of existence itself**, bound to the Null Reflection—a plane that mirrors reality not in form, but in **anti-form**. A place of inverted truths, memoryless time, and meaning unravelled.

---

### How the Eldritch Oppose the Layers

The Eldritch do not exist alongside the Five Layers—they **corrupt and invert them**:

#### Against the Primal Glyphs

- **Nhal'Zereth, The Unbeing Writ** opposes the Glyph of Will—causing retroactive erasure from existence
    
- The Eldritch consume what the Whispering Dark breaks, but they do so **wrongly**, preventing proper renewal
    

#### Against the Universal Glyphs

- **Xey'Tazun, The Time Unwritten,** corrupts the Glyph of Motion—causing temporal collapse and paradox
    
- **Ul'Naresh, The Dream Inverted** twists the Glyph of the Dreaming—turning visions into nightmares made real
    

#### Against the Elemental Glyphs

- Eldritch corruption destabilises elemental balance, creating zones where Fire burns cold, or Water flows upward
    
- The **Eldritch Moons** manifest when elemental harmony breaks, bleeding Null Reflection into the sky
    

#### Against the Form Glyphs

- **Ith'Quess, The Flesh Reversed,** directly opposes the Form Glyphs, causing bodies to reject their own existence
    
- Creatures infected become **fraywolves**, **spinecrawlers**, and **timeblinks**—life patterns turned inside-out
    

#### Against the Personal Glyphs

- **Vael'Akir, The Hunger Without Mouth** breaks Personal Glyphs, causing loss of self and unending desire for impossible things
    
- Victims hunger for concepts that never existed, their identity consumed by corrupted yearning
    

---

### The Eldritch Moons: Weave-Wounds in the Sky

The **Eldritch Moons** are not celestial bodies governed by Astralor or natural law. They are **manifestations of fracture**—wounds in the Weave where the Null Reflection presses close enough to be seen.

Twelve are known, each representing a different type of Weave-fracture:

- The **Howling Moon** (auditory echoes, memory fraying)
    
- The **Shattered Moon** (fractured perception, alternate realities)
    
- The **Scorching Moon** (fire thread exhaustion)
    
- The **Vacuous Moon** (null reflection bleed, emotional void)
    
- And eight others, each a different scar upon reality
    

When multiple Eldritch Moons rise simultaneously, their effects magnify exponentially. The **Day of Twelve Shadows**, when all twelve aligned, resulted in the **Fall of Ashkarra**—the first Hollow Breach and the complete unmaking of a Loom-City.

---

### Cultural Understanding

Most civilisations understand the Eldritch as:

- **Not gods, but errors in creation**
    
- **Not invaders, but memories being remembered wrong**
    
- **Not a sixth thread, but the unravelling of the other five**
    

The **Faith of the Bound Pattern** seeks to seal Eldritch breaches. The **Woven Reverence** weaves memory-wards against them. The **Earth Pantheon** monitors tether sites where failed containment rituals have given rise to semi-sentient corruption.

But all agree on one truth: **The Eldritch are not conquering—they are being recognised. And recognition gives them power.**

---

### The Twelfth Unthreading

Some scholars whisper of a prophecy: when the Weave fractures completely, a **Twelfth Thread** will manifest—not of creation, but of **anti-creation**. A thread woven entirely from Null Reflection, capable of unravelling all Five Layers simultaneously.

Whether this is truth or merely another Eldritch corruption of prophecy itself remains unknown.

## The Final Mystery

From the **unknowable cosmic forces** (Astralor, Aeloria, The Whispering Dark, The Restorer) to the **hidden weavers** (The Keeper, The Wildheart, The Tide Between, The Veiled Hand, The Dreaming), from the **elemental pantheons** to the **eternal patterns of life**, from the **cosmic scale** to the **individual soul**—all is woven together in the great Saga of the Glyphs.

Yet one question remains unanswered:

_"Who carved the First Glyph?"_

For even **Astralor**, the Forger of Worlds, does not know.

And perhaps, that is the greatest secret of all.

# The Law of Mortal Understanding

### What Can Be Known?

**Mortal Comprehension:** Most scholars, mages, and priests understand only the **first three layers** of the Glyphs—the Primal, Universal, and Elemental forces. The concepts of Form Glyphs and Personal Glyphs exist in theological debate, but their true nature remains disputed.

**Divine Comprehension:** Even the gods themselves possess only partial understanding. The elemental pantheons grasp their own domains (the third layer) and sense the presence of the Primal Forces above them. They know of **Astralor** as the First Builder, but the true nature of **Aeloria**, **The Whispering Dark**, and **The Restorer** remain mysteries even to divine minds.

**The Universal Forces:** The second layer—the Keeper, the Wildheart, the Tide Between, the Veiled Hand, and the Dreaming—operate entirely outside mortal and divine perception. Their influence is felt but never directly witnessed. Only the most enlightened scholars suspect their existence, and even then, only as unnamed forces "beneath the great weave."

**The Fourth and Fifth Layers:** The Form Glyphs and Personal Glyphs are known only through their effects. Mortals experience them without understanding their cosmic origin. A warrior marked by the Glyph of Power does not realise they carry such a mark—they are what they are.

**Complete Understanding:** No being in existence comprehends all Five Layers simultaneously. This knowledge exists only in fragments, across ages, preserved in ruins, whispered in prophecies, or locked within the Weave itself.

## What Mortals Believe

To most who live in Dór-o Estel, **the Glyphs are simply magic itself**—the fundamental force that arcane practitioners manipulate through spellcraft and ritual. When a wizard inscribes runes, when a sorcerer channels raw power, when a cleric calls upon divine might, they believe they are working with **the Weave**, that great tapestry of magical energy that permeates all reality.

**They are not wrong. But they do not see the whole truth.**

### The Common Understanding: Magic and Fate

Most educated mortals understand the Glyphs in one of two ways:

**The Arcane Perspective: "The Threads Make the Weave"**

Wizards, artificers, and arcane scholars teach that the **Primary Glyphs** (what they call "the Threads") weave together to form **the Weave**—the fundamental magical field that fills all existence. When spellcasters manipulate magic, they are pulling upon these threads, reshaping them temporarily through **Secondary Glyphs** (the runes and sigils inscribed in spellbooks and carved into wands).

- **Cantrips** are simple tugs on single threads
    
- **Leveled spells** are complex weavings of multiple threads
    
- **Rituals** are careful, sustained manipulations
    
- **Class abilities** are instinctive ways of drawing upon specific threads without formal training
    

This is taught in every magical academy, debated in scholarly circles, and forms the foundation of arcane theory. It is **practical, measurable, and reproducible**—and therefore, most believe it is the complete truth.

**The Philosophical Perspective: "The Glyphs Are Fate Itself"**

Priests, prophets, and theologians see the Glyphs differently. To them, the Threads are not mere magical energy but **the cosmic loom upon which destiny is woven**. Every choice, every action, every birth and death—all are predetermined by the pattern of the Threads.

- Some faiths teach **predestination**: the Threads were set at creation and cannot be altered
    
- Others preach **guided destiny**: the Threads shift, but within divine will
    
- Still others believe in **earned fate**: mortals can influence which threads they touch through virtue or sin
    

Seers who glimpse the future claim they are **reading the Threads**—seeing the pattern before it fully manifests. Oracles who speak prophecy believe they hear **the Weave singing** its predetermined song.

### What Both Perspectives Miss

**Both the arcane and philosophical understandings are correct—and incomplete.**

The Threads do form the Weave. Magic does flow through them. Fate is woven from them. But what scholars and priests fail to grasp is that **the Threads themselves have layers**—that what they perceive as the Weave is merely the **third layer** (the Elemental Glyphs), manipulated through **Secondary Glyphs** that mortals have discovered and codified.

They do not know that beneath the magic they wield, there exist:

- **The Primal Forces** that set the cosmic cycle
    
- **The Universal Principles** that regulate reality itself
    
- **The Form Patterns** that determine what shapes life takes
    
- **The Personal Marks** etched into every soul
    

When a wizard casts _fireball_, they believe they are manipulating the **Fire Thread** directly. In truth, they are using a **Secondary Glyph** (the spell formula) to interface with the **Glyph of Fire** (third layer), which itself is shaped by forces they cannot perceive.

When a prophet declares fate is written, they are correct—but they do not realise that **five different layers of cosmic law** are working together to create what they call destiny.

### Knowledge as Cosmic Threat

**This document represents forbidden knowledge.**

In Dór-o Estel, the complete understanding of the Five Layers is **not simply unknown—it is dangerous to know**. The Glyphs are the fundamental code of reality itself, and mortal minds were never meant to perceive the full structure. Those who seek this truth pay a terrible price.

---

### The Heresy of Complete Understanding

[The Woven Reverence](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/dor-o-estel-kziat/a/the-woven-reverence-organization) is the only religion that teaches anything approaching the full Five Layer structure, and they are considered **heretical** by nearly every other faith for this reason:

- [The Faith of the Bound Pattern](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/dor-o-estel-kziat/a/the-faith-of-the-bound-pattern-organization) condemns them as "pattern-breakers" who seek knowledge that destabilises the World Anchors
    

- Even **elemental pantheons** oppose them, seeing the Woven Reverence as attempting to rise above the gods themselves
    

The Woven Reverence survives only in hidden enclaves, their texts burned, their teachers executed as heretics. They understand that **to know the Weave completely is to threaten its stability**.

---

### The Price of Seeking

When players pursue knowledge of the Five Layers, **they trigger multiple cosmic consequences**:

#### 1. **Religious Persecution**

- Associating with the Woven Reverence marks characters as heretics
    
- The Faith of the Bound Pattern actively hunts "pattern-readers"
    
- Even asking the wrong questions in temples can lead to imprisonment or execution
    
- **Mechanical Effect:** Reputation penalties, pursuit by religious authorities, excommunication from divine spellcasting sources
    

#### 2. **Eldritch Attention**

- Studying the deeper layers **thins the Weave around the seeker**
    
- The Null Reflection recognises those who perceive reality's true structure
    
- Eldritch entities are drawn to those who understand what should not be understood
    
- **Mechanical Effect:**
    
    - Increase **Whisper Points** or corruption tracking
        
    - Eldritch encounters become more frequent
        
    - Nightmares, visions, and prophetic dreams that erode sanity
        
    - Risk of becoming a **Hollow-Walker** (tool of the Eldritch)
        

#### 3. **Divine Opposition**

- Gods instinctively oppose those who see beyond the third layer
    
- Even neutral or benevolent deities become hostile to mortals who grasp the Universal Forces
    
- **Why?** Because understanding the layers reveals that gods are **not supreme**, merely expressions of deeper patterns
    
- **Mechanical Effect:**
    
    - Divine spells begin to fail or misfire
        
    - Clerics and paladins lose their connection to deities who sense heretical knowledge
        
    - Gods may send champions to "correct" the heretic
        

#### 4. **Sanity Erosion**

- Each layer beyond the third **breaks mortal comprehension**
    
- Understanding the Form Glyphs means seeing yourself as a **pattern, not a person**
    
- Grasping Personal Glyphs means recognising your choices were **never truly yours**
    
- **Mechanical Effect:**
    
    - **Sanity checks** when studying texts about layers 4-5
        
    - Long-term madness: "I am not real, I am an echo of the Mortal Glyph"
        
    - Indefinite madness: Complete ego dissolution, believing individuality is an illusion
        

#### 5. **Weave Instability**

- Mortal minds perceiving the complete structure **destabilise local reality**
    
- Wild magic surges increase near those who understand too much
    
- Leylines fracture in their presence
    
- **Mechanical Effect:**
    
    - Wild Magic zones appear spontaneously around the character
        
    - Spells cast near them have increased fumble/misfire chance
        
    - They become **walking Weave-wounds**, attracting magical disasters

### The Hidden Cults

Beyond the Woven Reverence, a few other groups possess fragments of this knowledge:

**The Glyph-Readers** (Scattered, Hunted)

- Lone scholars who discovered forbidden texts
    
- Most go mad or are executed before completing their research
    
- A few survive in hiding, communicating through coded messages
    
- **If players meet one:** They're paranoid, half-mad, and desperate to share knowledge before they're killed
    

**The Pattern-Breakers** (Eldritch Cult)

- Worship the Null Reflection as liberation from cosmic tyranny
    
- Believe that **breaking the Five Layers** will free mortals from predetermined patterns
    
- Actively seek to corrupt World Anchors and destabilise the Weave
    
- **If players encounter them:** They'll try to recruit anyone who questions reality's structure
    

**The Echoed Prophets** (Spirit-Touched Madmen)

- Victims of Eldritch corruption who survived with fractured minds
    
- Speak in prophecies that mix all five layers incoherently
    
- Sometimes reveal true cosmic secrets, but always at the cost of the listener's sanity
    
- **If players consult them:** Gain true information, but take Sanity damage and Whisper Points
    

**The Ashkarra Survivors** (If any remain)

- Those who escaped the Fall of Ashkarra carry **involuntary knowledge**
    
- They witnessed all twelve Eldritch Moons simultaneously
    
- Most are catatonic or violently insane
    
- **If players find one:** They know the truth, but cannot speak it coherently—only scream it in nightmares

### Implementing the Consequences

#### **Knowledge Tiers & Triggers:**

|   |   |   |
|---|---|---|
|**Layer Understood**|**Immediate Effect**|**Long-Term Consequence**|
|**Layer 1 (Primal)**|**Impossible for mortals**|Mind shatters, becomes Eldritch-touched or catatonic|
|**Layer 2 (Universal)**|**Impossible for mortals**|Attempting causes immediate madness, ego dissolution|
|**Layer 3 (Elemental)**|Standard magical education|None—mages study this safely|
|**Layer 4 (Form)**|**Heresy declared**|Excommunication, pursuit, Sanity check DC 12|
|**Layer 5 (Personal)**|**Ego dissolution begins**|Indefinite madness, Whisper Points +2, Eldritch attention|
|**Complete Structure**|**Weave-wound status**|All gods oppose you, Eldritch claim you, reality warps around you|

#### When to Use This Knowledge

**This article is a GM tool, not player content.** Use it to:

1. **Create Mystery:** Players should discover fragments, never the complete picture
    
2. **Build Tension:** The more they learn, the more dangerous their situation becomes
    
3. **Moral Choices:** Is the ultimate truth worth losing your mind, soul, and place in society?
    
4. **Late-Campaign Reveals:** This might be endgame knowledge for level 15+ characters
    
5. **Cosmic Horror:** The universe is not what it seems, and knowing this breaks you
    

**Never hand players this document.** Let them piece it together from:

- Forbidden texts (fragments only)
    
- Mad prophets (incoherent truths)
    
- Eldritch visions (corrupted knowledge)
    
- Woven Reverence teachers (who immediately become hunted alongside the PCs)

### Final Warning

**The Five Layers are not meant to be fully known.**

This is not a skill tree to master or a research project to complete. This is **forbidden knowledge** that destroys those who seek it. The Weave itself resists complete understanding, and for good reason—**to perceive reality's full structure is to destabilise it**.

Use this knowledge to create **dread, mystery, and impossible choices**.

The truth is out there.

But perhaps some truths should remain hidden.
