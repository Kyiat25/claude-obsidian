# The Pendulum of Fate

## A Note from the Gambler-King's Ledger

_"The Weave does not always favour the wise. Sometimes, it favours the lucky. And sometimes, it watches you trip into your own grave for the third time that day."_

In FEARS, **Luck** is not just flavour. It is real, trackable, and volatile. It represents the **cosmic bias** of fortune and misfortune, tracking improbable streaks of natural rolls and converting them into tangible blessings or curses. Like fate itself, it resets with a breath—a single **[[Short Rest]]**.

---
## 🎲 What Triggers Luck

Luck changes when players roll with **unusual consistency**. It only counts **once per turn in combat**.

| Streak  | Condition                     | Result                    |
| ------- | ----------------------------- | ------------------------- |
| Cursed  | 3 Nat 1s before a Short Rest  | You gain **Cursed Luck**  |
| Blessed | 3 Nat 20s before a Short Rest | You gain **Blessed Luck** |

> Each additional **odd-numbered** roll (5th, 7th, etc.) of the same kind stacks and triggers the table **again**.

## 🔁 Reset & Management

- **All Luck effects reset on a Short Rest**.
- A player may only **benefit from one active Luck state** at a time (Blessed or Cursed), unless it states stacks.
- Rolling into both within the same rest cycle defaults to the **most recent effect**.

> _"The gods play dice. Sometimes, you get to keep them."_
