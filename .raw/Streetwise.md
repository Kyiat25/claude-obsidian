# The Unwritten Rules

> _“You don’t survive a city by knowing laws. You survive by knowing which alley gets you stabbed and which gets you fed.”_  
> — Liris “Three-Tooth” Varn, Queen of Beggars

---
## 🌆 What is Streetwise?

**Streetwise** is a skill representing your practical knowledge of **urban survival, social codes, underworld currents**, and the real rules that govern cities, camps, and frontier hubs.

It’s not about etiquette—it’s about instinct, experience, and reading the pulse of a place **before it kills you**.

Streetwise is used to:
- Detect deception in casual or criminal conversation
- Navigate unfamiliar settlements without drawing attention
- Spot ambushes or tailing agents
- Recall rumours, black market contacts, or coded territory signs

---
## 🎲 Streetwise Check Formula

**Streetwise Check** = `1d20 + Wisdom or Charisma Modifier + Proficiency (if trained)`

- Use **Wisdom** for observational savvy, spatial awareness, danger sense
- Use **Charisma** for blending in, reading people, con artistry

> Choose the appropriate modifier per context. GMs may set one as mandatory.

---

## 🧠 When to Roll Streetwise

|Situation|Use|
|---|---|
|You suspect a mugging or setup|Streetwise (Wisdom) to read danger|
|You want to find the black market in a new city|Streetwise (Charisma) to ask right people discreetly|
|You try to identify gang symbols or turf markers|Streetwise (Wisdom) to recall and decode|
|You listen for local rumors that matter|Streetwise (Charisma) + Insight or Memory assist|
|You attempt to disappear into a crowd|Streetwise vs Perception, or used with Stealth|

---

## 🎭 Streetwise vs Insight vs Investigation

|   |   |   |
|---|---|---|
|Skill|Focus|Scope|
|**Streetwise**|Gut instinct & social reading|Fast-moving, noisy environments|
|**Insight**|Emotional and motive analysis|Interpersonal dynamics|
|**Investigation**|Logical analysis of clues|Physical evidence & deduction|

> Streetwise spots the con **before it’s made**. Insight figures out **why it was made**. Investigation finds **how it was pulled off**.

---
## 🎒 Who Trains Streetwise?

Streetwise proficiency is common to:

- [[Rogue]], Scoundrels, [[Bard]], Urban Rangers
- Backgrounds like **[[Urchin]]**, **[[Fence]]**, **[[Informer]]**, **[[Courier]]**
- Anyone who’s lived long in **dense settlements or criminal culture**

It may also be taught via:

- Downtime immersion in unfamiliar city cultures
- Living among gangs, thieves’ guilds, smugglers, or informant networks

---
## 🕵️‍♂️ GM Guidance

Use Streetwise to:

- Short-circuit political intrigue through cultural fluency
- Expose city-based dangers early
- Let characters navigate unfamiliar or hostile districts without raw combat
- Replace overused Investigation/Insight checks in social-heated scenes

Streetwise should **reward attention to tone, cues, rhythm of place**, and reputation networks.

> It’s not how loud you speak. It’s who listens—and who pretends not to.
