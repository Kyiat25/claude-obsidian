# THE BURNING OF SYLVENMERE
## A Complete Chronicle of the Massacre That Changed the World

---

** #Event Name:** The Burning of Sylvenmere (also known as "The Second Burning," "The Grove Massacre," "The Night of Blessed Fire")  
**Date:** 487 FA, Month 4, Day 23  
**Location:** Sylvenmere Sacred Grove, Northern Kerythra Coast  
**Duration:** Approximately 6 hours (2 hours before dawn to 2 hours after sunrise)  
**Casualties:** 280-350 dead, 120-180 survivors  
**Perpetrators:** Corvessan Faith operatives, Velshara's Burner network, "private zealots"  
**Witnesses:** Survivors, Kerythran nomads (distant), Imperial sailors (offshore)

---

## TIMELINE OF TRAGEDY

### YEARS BEFORE: THE SEEDS OF DESTRUCTION

#### **12,000 Years Ago - Grove Founded**
- First trees planted by ancient wood elves
- Coastal rainforest selected for isolation and fertility
- 50+ World Trees begin their slow witness to history
- Community forms around memory-keeping traditions

#### **8,000 Years Ago - The Singing Roots Awakens**
- Mycelial network beneath grove achieves collective consciousness
- Forest spirit "The Singing Roots" (Root-Singer) emerges
- Becomes passive guardian of grove and guide to inhabitants
- Much weaker than Talanthar's Vaelthir but beloved by community

#### **484 FA - The Fatal Exposure**
- Kel Varyan Empire begins northern Kerythra trade expansion
- Imperial cartographers survey northern coast for safe harbors
- Sylvenmere's location enters official geographic records
- **First domino falls:** Isolation ends after 12,000 years

#### **485 FA - Regular Trade Contact Begins**
- Monthly merchant ships begin coastal route
- Sylvenmere trades kelp, herbs, healing services for tools and grain
- Community welcomes outsiders with traditional hospitality
- **Cultural naivety exposed:** No understanding of geopolitical dangers

#### **486 FA, Month 3 - First Imperial Contact**
**The Storm Shelter Incident:**
- Imperial merchant vessel *Silver Harvest* seeks refuge from storm
- Captain Theodrin Wavecrest and crew welcomed ashore
- Given food, shelter, medical care
- Shown sacred grove, taught about tree-ring methods
- **Intelligence windfall:** Full cultural briefing given freely
- Captain's report enters Imperial records (publicly accessible)

#### **486 FA, Month 6 - The Missionary Visit**
**Father Gregorian Ironwood (Corvessan Faith missionary):**
- Arrives on merchant vessel *Northern Star*
- Spends 5 days in intensive "theological discussions"
- Taught dendrochronological methods by Elder Loralyth
- Shown all 50+ ancient trees and their ages
- Given tour of memory-preservation techniques
- **Fatal error:** Cycle-Singers teach methods to verify their evidence
- Father Gregorian replicates their findings (confirms threat)

**Elder Loralyth's statement to him:**
> "See here, Father? This ring formed 12,400 years ago. Count them yourself—each year leaves a mark. And here, embedded in this ring from 11,500 years back, you can see the pollen from cultivated grain. Our ancestors were farming millennia before your Faith's creation narrative begins. Would you like to learn the Counting Song? It helps mark the rings accurately."

**Father Gregorian's classified report to Corvessan authorities:**
> "URGENT - THEOLOGICAL THREAT ASSESSMENT
> 
> Evidence is IRREFUTABLE and REPLICABLE. Population is COMPLETELY NAIVE. Site is VULNERABLE. NO SECURITY MEASURES. EXPOSURE INEVITABLE within 1-2 years unless action taken.
> 
> RECOMMENDATION: Immediate classified notification to theological security apparatus. Consider proactive measures to prevent evidence proliferation."

#### **486 FA, Month 9 - Corvessan Intelligence Analysis**
- Report reaches Corvessan Military Intelligence
- Confirms: Heretical evidence site, naive population, vulnerable location
- Surveillance operation authorized
- Agent "Markus the Trader" (The Wolf) embedded in merchant community

#### **487 FA, Month 1 - Velshara Receives Intelligence**
- Corvessan intelligence shares findings with wider suppressor network
- Velshara analyzes: "Perfect target—same evidence value as Talanthar, fraction of difficulty"
- Strategic assessment: "Priority target. Strike before they understand danger."
- Operation planning begins

#### **487 FA, Month 2-3 - Final Reconnaissance**
**"The Friendly Traders" Phase:**

"Markus the Trader" and team (actually Burner operatives) begin regular visits:

**Week 1-4 (Month 2):**
- Establish legitimate merchant cover
- Build rapport with community
- Trade generously (creating goodwill and trust)
- Ask "innocent" questions about grove layout

**Week 5-8 (Month 3, Weeks 1-4):**
- Map all evidence locations (which trees contain what data)
- Identify community leaders and priority targets
- Assess defensive capabilities (finding none)
- Chart evacuation routes (to be blocked)
- Build detailed population roster

**Week 9-12 (Month 3, final month):**
- Final intelligence gathering
- Confirm attack date (clear weather, moonless night)
- Position operatives
- Coordinate with naval support
- **Community suspects nothing** - "Such lovely people!"

**Star-Reader Vynnia's WARNING (Week 10, ignored):**
> "I don't trust them. The stars show ships bringing fire. These traders ask too many questions about our defenses. Why do they count our people? Why do they map every trail? Something is wrong."

**Circle of Roots' Response:**
> "Vynnia, you're being paranoid. These are honest merchants. Not everyone is a threat. We've survived 12,000 years through openness, not suspicion. Let's not ruin this beautiful new friendship with baseless fears."

**Loralyth:** "The ships worry me, Singer. More come each month. They ask too many questions."
