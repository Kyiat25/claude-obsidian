*Primary campaign continent, most developed setting*

**Size:** 22 million km²  
**Population:** ~40 million (most populous continent)  
**Tectonic Plate:** Central Pattern Plate  
**Climate Zones:** Temperate to subtropical (15°N to 45°N equivalent)  
**Thread Alignment:** Spirit (primary), Fire and Water (secondary)  
**Houses:** 2 of 5 Loom-Cities (Kel Varyan, Veltharyn)

**Strategic Importance:**
- Geographic and civilizational heart of known world
- Thread-system stability hub (40% of World Anchor control - 2 of 5 Loom-Cities)
- Continental trade networks nexus
- Diplomatic relationships center
- Cultural exchange crossroads
- Magical innovation convergence point

**Campaign Role:**
- Primary setting for most arcs
- Contains two Loom-Cities (diverse perspectives)
- Maximum political complexity
- Richest factional landscape
- Most developed infrastructure for player engagement

**Connection to Other Loom-Cities:**
- **Thar Vornhal** (Kerythra): Accessible via Dawnreach Expanse (2-4 weeks overland) or Coastal Circuit (4-6 weeks maritime)
- **Selveran** (Mirror Chain): Accessible via Leywake Route (3-5 weeks maritime)
- **Aeravae** (Iriveth Orre): Accessible via Faith Corridor (5-7 weeks maritime)

### **5. AERAVAE** - The City of Echoed Balance
*Air Thread Loom-City, IRIVETH ORRE*

**Location:** Iriveth Orre continent, built atop vast cenote-lake with floating islands  
**Population:** ~170,000 (city core) to ~650,000 (full domain)  
**Government:** Matrilineal monarchy - Queen of the Deep Mirror with ceremonial Mirror Marriages  
**Thread Affinity:** Air (Primary), Spirit (Secondary)  
**World Anchor:** The Echo-Cenote (hybrid - grafted into living World Tree roots)

**CRITICAL NOTE:** Aeravae is a LAKE/WATER-BASED city, NOT mountain-based!

**Seven-Level Vertical Structure:**
- **Level 1 - Sky Gardens (200-300 ft above water):** Floating platforms, nobility, astronomers
- **Level 2 - Floating Quarters (50-200 ft above):** Luxury barges, wealthy merchants
- **Level 3 - Canal Crown (water level):** Political/commercial heart, main canals
- **Level 4 - Ripple Quarters (10-30 ft below):** Semi-submerged neighborhoods
- **Level 5 - Submersion Districts (30-100 ft underwater):** Fully underwater, aquatic races
- **Level 6 - Echo Vaults (100-300 ft underwater):** Memory chambers, spiritual hermits
- **Level 7 - Root-Heart (300+ ft underwater):** World Anchor/World Tree fusion point

**Key Features & Locations:**
- **The Echo-Cenote (World Anchor)** - Perfectly circular pool, 300 ft diameter
  - Seven floating monoliths with Universal Glyphs
  - Memory Spiral at center
  - Hybrid system: Faith's artificial anchor grafted into living World Tree roots
  - Exists across multiple underwater levels
- **Floating Islands** - Connected by canals, underground rivers, waterlifts
- **Living Architecture** - Buildings constructed from:
  - Mirrorstone (reflects Thread colors)
  - Living coralwork (breathes)
  - Weave-bonded wood (adapts to Thread shifts)
  - Salt-laced brick (absorbs humidity and echoes)
- **Transportation** - Personal watercraft, canal-ferries, walking bridges, submersion shafts
- **Wind-bridges** - Solidified air connecting upper floating platforms
- **Storm Observatories** - Weather prediction and Thread monitoring (on upper floating levels)

**Access from Elarion:**
- **Primary:** Via Faith Corridor (5-7 weeks, challenging sub-arctic route)
- **Alternative:** Via aarakocra messenger service (10-14 days flight, limited cargo)
- **Communication:** Regular diplomatic missions (quarterly)

**Campaign Significance:**
- Arc 1B victim origin (murdered Threadbearer)
- Arc 2A philosophical debates (truth vs. stability)
- Arc 2B high elf perspective (persecution history)
- Prophetic consultation center
- Weather/Thread coordination
- UNIQUE: Only hybrid World Anchor/World Tree in existence

## LIVING WORLD MECHANICS

### **How Locations Interact with Player Choices**

**Every location has:**
1. **Current State** - Population, stability, acknowledgment rating
2. **Faction Influence** - Which groups control/influence
3. **Thread Alignment** - How Thread cycles affect it
4. **Connection Networks** - Which locations it influences/is influenced by
5. **Consequence Tracking** - How player actions change it

**Example: Kel Varyan**
- **Starting State:** Stable, acknowledgment rating 30%, Reality Stability 15%
- **Player reveals truth:** Acknowledgment rises to 60%, Stability rises to 35%, riots occur
- **Consequence Cascade:**
  - Nearby cities influenced through trade
  - Other Loom-Cities react to instability (requires weeks for news to travel)
  - Faith hierarchy responds across continent
  - Thread-system affected by World Anchor disruption
  - Next session quests generated from new state

---

### **Geographic Distance Matters**

**Information Spread Speed:**
- **Same City:** 1-2 days
- **River Network:** 1 week per 200 miles
- **Overland:** 2 weeks per 200 miles
- **Maritime:** 1 week per 300 nautical miles
- **Pattern-Space:** Instantaneous (for Threadbearers only)

**Player actions in Veltharyn:**
- Reach Kel Varyan in 2-3 weeks (overland within Elarion)
- Reach Thar Vornhal in 4-5 weeks (Dawnreach Expanse to Kerythra)
- Reach Selveran in 5-6 weeks (maritime to Mirror Chain)
- Reach Aeravae in 7-8 weeks (Faith Corridor to Iriveth Orre)
- But Loom-Cities coordinate through Pattern in hours (Threadbearers only)

---

### **Cultural Zones Create Friction**

**Fire-aligned (Veltharyn, dragonborn):**
- Value direct action, intensity, transformation
- Conflict with water-aligned bureaucracy
- Support Kethyr's methods more readily

**Water-aligned (Kel Varyan, imperial):**
- Value stability, flow, adaptation
- Oppose violent revelation
- Support suppression or controlled disclosure

**Earth-aligned (Thar Vornhal, dwarven):**
- Value evidence, law, permanence
- Neutral arbiter role
- Focus on verification

**Spirit-aligned (Selveran, elven):**
- Value memory, connection, truth
- Support Celeborn's mission (divided on methods)
- Pattern-space sensitivity

**Air-aligned (Aeravae, high elves):**
- Value philosophy, perspective, freedom
- Debate all positions
- Seek synthesis

---

### **No Location is Static**

**Every location tracks:**
- **Acknowledgment Rating** (0-100%: awareness of persecution)
- **Reality Stability** (0-100%: damage from truth-revelation)
- **Pattern-Touched Population** (number affected by forced testimony)
- **Faction Control** (which groups have influence)
- **Economic State** (trade disruption, prosperity)
- **Military Readiness** (response to threats)
- **Cultural Tension** (inter-racial/religious conflict)

**These change based on:**
- Player actions in that location
- Player actions in connected locations
- NPC faction actions
- Thread cycle effects
- Random events (natural disasters, political shifts)
- Time passage

---

### **Campaign Scope Flexibility**

**Elarion-Focused Campaign:**
- Use Kel Varyan, Veltharyn primarily
- Other Loom-Cities as distant coordinators (weeks of travel away)
- Reduce maritime travel requirements
- Concentrate political complexity
- 40-50 session arc achievable

**Multi-Continental Campaign:**
- Visit all five Loom-Cities (requires extensive maritime travel)
- Experience cultural diversity
- Show global consequences
- Require maritime/overland travel (weeks between locations)
- 60-80 session epic possible

**Region-Specific Campaign:**
- Focus on one area (e.g., Corvessa-Talanthar war)
- Use Loom-Cities as distant forces
- Smaller scope, deeper detail
- 20-30 session arc

**The world supports all three approaches while maintaining internal consistency.**

## CONCLUSION: A TRULY LIVING WORLD

This location index creates a **breathing, reactive campaign setting** where:

1. **Every location has purpose** - Not just backdrop, but active participant
2. **Geography creates real constraints** - Distance and culture matter
3. **Player choices ripple globally** - Actions in one city affect continents (with travel time delays)
4. **No predetermined outcomes** - World state shifts based on decisions
5. **Cultural complexity enriches** - Five Thread-affinities create authentic friction
6. **Scale adapts to table** - Can focus on Elarion or expand to global scope
7. **Consequences feel earned** - Tracking systems make impact visible
8. **World remembers** - NPCs and locations recall PC actions
9. **Mystery and exploration rewarded** - Unknown areas invite discovery
10. **Everything connects** - Thread-system creates global coherence (with realistic communication delays)

**This is not a static map. This is a living world that breathes, remembers, and responds.**
