# Glyph of Will – _Astralor’s Forge, the First Hammer_

> _“From the great void, Astralor struck the First Blow, and fire and stone were born.”_  
> — _Inscription at the Broken Anvil of the World-Forge_

---
## 🧱 Layer

**Layer:** Primal  
**Tier Rank:** 1 (First Tier, foundational)  
**Domain(s):** Creation, Cosmic Command, Order from Void  
**Opposing Glyph(s):** [[Glyph of Unbeing]], [[Glyph of Endings]]  
**Symbol:** An anvil cracked with light inside

---
## 🔷 Core Meaning

- The **Glyph of Will** is the **first cosmic assertion**—the divine necessity that **there must be something**.
- It is **not desire, nor intent, nor thought**—it is **inevitability**, the glyph that **struck the void** and from it carved the cosmos.
- This glyph **shapes all that is**: stars, worlds, souls, law, time. Its existence affirms reality.

> **Truth:** All that exists **must** exist, because Will has made it so.

---
### **The Manifestations of the Glyph of Will**

The **Glyph of Will does not simply exist—it echoes, manifesting throughout reality.**

- **The forging of a world is a whisper of the Hammer.**
- **The birth of a god is a flicker of its power.**
- **The shaping of a soul. A spark of invention. A child's first thought—each a fragment of the First Blow**

Even the gods, in their might, are **merely artisans shaping what this Glyph has provided.**

But **creation demands cost**, and that cost is **inevitably claimed by the Maw.**

_“To shape is to spend. To bring into being is to pay in silence, in self, and in flame.”_  
— _Fragment attributed to [[Tharamis]], found etched into slagstone in the ruined forge of [[Ashkarra]]_

---
## 🌀 Narrative Role

- This glyph is **the first glyph ever inscribed**, born from **Astralor’s divine hammer** before even the Weave fully formed.
- It appears in countless myths: the **[[Forging of the Ley Lines]]**, the **[[Binding of the World Anchor]]**, and **[[The Trial of the Null Forge]]**.
- **Gods do not create this glyph**—they wield it in times of genesis or final defense.
- **[[The Earth Pantheon]]** venerates it through **[[Vatharun]]’s stillness** and **[[Gaeldros]]’s unbreakable law**
- **[[The Fire Pantheon]]** honours it through **[[Soltharion]]’s shaping flame**.
- **[[Eldritch Forces]]** fears it most—it is the one glyph **they cannot erase**.

🛠️ **GM Tip:** If a player attempts to create something new with divine or planar significance, this glyph may resonate. The sound of the First Blow could echo in their dreams or leave a glyph-burn on nearby stone.

---

## ✨ Magical Influence

- The **Glyph of Will** is required to shape [[leylines]]. It is the glyph that causes **magic to take form** instead of dissipating into potential.
- All **binding, crafting, spell-forging, or world-altering rituals** draw unconsciously on this glyph.
- Spell types influenced:
    
    - **Conjuration** (bringing things into being)
    - **Transmutation** (redefining existing form)
    - **Abjuration** (reinforcing existence)
        
- High-tier artefacts may **anchor this glyph as a passive imprint**, allowing their power to persist through ages.

---

## 🧩 Divine Associations

- **[[Astralor]]** is the patron of this glyph, often called the **First Forger** or **Father of Will**.
- **[[Gaeldros]]** invokes it when declaring **divine laws** that bind gods and mortals alike.
- **[[Aeloria]]** uses its echo in the **Glyph of Threads**, ensuring fate flows from a place of fixed existence.
- **[[The Tide Between]]**, one of the Universal Forces, depends on the Glyph of Will to keep cycles anchored in reality.

---

## ☠️ Eldritch Corruption Risk

- Eldritch entities such as **[[Nhal’Zereth]]** (The Unbeing Writ) seek to **nullify** this glyph by unraveling its **cosmic memory**.
- **[[Mirag’Nael]]**, the False Author, may overwrite events shaped by Will—but **only temporarily**.
- When **the Glyph of Will is breached**, entire timelines may retroactively **never have existed**.
- Dangerous sites:
    - **[[Null Forges]]**, where failed attempts to inscribe this glyph caused **[[leyline haemorrhaging]]**
    - **[[Fractured Anchors]]**, where **Unbeing** has severed the foundation laid by Will.

---

## 🏛️ Places of Worship & Legacy

- The **[[Anvil Shrine of Tharamis]]** beneath [[07_Geography/02_Cities/Thar Vornhal]] is said to contain the **echo of the First Strike**.
- The **[[Bound Temple of Gaeldros]]** holds the **Stone of Origin**, carved using the Glyph of Will and never eroded.
- **[[Ashkarra]]’s [[World Anchor]]**, before its collapse, was said to hum with the **purest tone of Will** ever heard—until it broke under **triple Spirit Thread alignment**.

---

## 🧵 Fate & Glyphic Mechanics

- All **[[Secondary Glyphs]]** used by mortals **depend on the foundation of Will** to exist.
- **[[The Echoed Weaving]]** (chosen of the Weave) carries a **subglyph of Will** in their soul—an imprint visible only during **[[The Loom’s Test]]**.
- Even Eldritch-warped areas cannot fully **deny** the Glyph of Will—only obscure or reflect it in corrupted form.
