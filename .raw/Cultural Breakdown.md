# Complete Cultural Breakdown of Dór-o Estel by Geography

**[[Dór-o Estel]]** spans 7 major continental regions plus the vast [[Hollowdeep]] underground realm, with a total population of 120-125 million across 147.9 million km² of landmass. This breakdown includes ALL 51 distinct cultures from the established records.

---
## I. [[Elarion]] - "The Heart of Law and Loom"

**Size:** 22 million km² | **Population:** ~40 million (most populous) **Geography:** Temperate broadleaf forests, river valley floodplains, grain-producing plains, engineered wetlands and canals **Thread Alignment:** Spirit (primary), Earth and Water (secondary)

### The Loom-City Powers

#### **1. Kel Varyan Empire** (Water-Thread Loom-City)

- **Population:** ~8.5 million
- **Race Mix:** Humans (45%), Gnomes (25%), Elves (20%), Others (10%)
- **Government:** Imperial magocracy with Thread-bound succession
- **Geography:** Amethyst Crown palace, Vault of the Abyss, canal networks in river delta
- **Culture:** Dual-city arcane bureaucracy with prophetic governance
- **Inspiration:** Imperial Roman / Venetian / Mageocracy
- **Role:** First Water spirit site, center of magical law and imperial administration

#### **2. Thar Vornhal Judiciary** (Earth-Thread Loom-City)

- **Population:** ~4.2 million
- **Race Mix:** Dwarves (60%), Humans (25%), Gnomes (15%)
- **Government:** High judges selected through stone-echo trials
- **Geography:** Stone Tribunal, Deep Anvil, World Anchor chambers in mountain highlands
- **Culture:** Judicial theocracy enforcing divine law and glyph stability
- **Inspiration:** High Law Dwarven / Byzantine Roman legal traditions
- **Role:** Legal center, arbitration of inter-kingdom disputes, law enforcement

#### **3. Veltharyn Assembly** (Fire-Thread Loom-City)

- **Population:** ~3.8 million
- **Race Mix:** Dwarves (50%), Dragonborn (30%), Humans (20%)
- **Government:** Assembly of forge-masters and clan representatives
- **Geography:** Seven-tiered forge-mountain, highland mining complexes
- **Culture:** Democratic dwarven metallurgy with Incan-inspired mountain terraces
- **Inspiration:** Dwarven Highland / Incan-Mountain mix
- **Role:** Divine metal production, democratic decision-making, highland defense

### The Industrial and Trade Centers

#### **4. The Forgehost of Tarshem**

- **Population:** ~2.1 million
- **Race Mix:** Dwarves (70%), Humans (20%), Gnomes (10%)
- **Government:** Industrial guilds with steam-priest leadership
- **Geography:** River-powered industrial complexes, smoking foundries, gear-temples
- **Culture:** Proto-industrial dwarven society combining steam technology with divine forging
- **Inspiration:** Industrial Guild / Steam-priest dwarves
- **Role:** Advanced manufacturing, mechanical innovation, trade goods production

### The Reforged Territories

#### **5. Caradyn-Tal — The Reforged Kingdom**

- **Population:** ~800,000
- **Race Mix:** Humans (60%), Dwarves (20%), Mixed former slaves (20%)
- **Government:** Fortress-abbeys ruled by glyph-scarred ex-slaves bound to divine memory
- **Geography:** Northern Hollowdeep margin, reclaimed fortress-abbeys, terraced strongholds
- **Culture:** Tattoo-glyphs allow invocation of ancestral echoes during war or debate
- **Inspiration:** Post-Roman Celtic Lords × Mamluk Janissary Castes
- **Role:** Identity reformation through sacred scarring, memory-bound warrior culture

### The Mystical Territories

#### **6. Trinal Keys Sky-Temples**

- **Population:** ~450,000
- **Race Mix:** Aarakocra (60%), Air Genasi (25%), Humans (15%)
- **Government:** Solar ascetic hierarchy with sky-touched monks
- **Geography:** Mountain-top temple complexes south of main Elarion settlements
- **Culture:** Solar worship with aerial meditation and sky-bound asceticism
- **Inspiration:** Solar Ascetic / Aarakocra-like mountain monasteries
- **Role:** Spiritual guidance, aerial scouting, solar calendar maintenance
