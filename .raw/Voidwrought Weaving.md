# Voidwrought Weaving

_Style of Magic_

**"The stars are not silent. They whisper. They pull. They remember."**

Voidwrought Weaving is the style of magic shaped by **cosmic pressure**, **madness**, and the **curvature of reality**. It is not learned through study, but through exposure—when one's mind brushes against the stars that should not be, or glimpses the truths that break the horizon of understanding. Practiced by those touched by eldritch energies, haunted by the **[[Null Reflection]]**, or mutated by the **[[Eldritch Moons]]**, Voidwrought Weaving is power dragged from the brink of dissolution.

Unlike other styles, it distorts causality. Spells may happen **before they're cast**, or linger as **echoes** in time. The Weave itself recoils as it is twisted—pushed through gravity wells, memory scars, or unknowable geometries.

---

## Key Themes

- **Gravity and Pressure**: Spells mimic black holes, compression, or collapsing mass.
- **Madness and Insight**: Sanity is both cost and compass. The more you lose, the clearer the stars become.
- **Reality Warping**: The world bends—physically, mentally, or emotionally—when Voidwrought magic is cast.
- **Echoed Time**: Some spells manifest twice or loop unnaturally.

---

## Cultural Origins

### 🌌 **Luyarnhan Jaegers**

- Eldritch-hunters touched by the Moons. Weave void-magic into ammunition and armor.
- See magic as a war against unmaking.

### 🔮 **Eclipsed Scholars of the Null Tome**

- Study how the Weave breaks when faced with paradox.
- Use equations etched in starlight and incomprehensible glyphs.

### 🌬️ **Prophets of the Sinking Star**

- Channel void through dreams, blood, and divination.
- Often mad. Often right. Rarely both.

## Voidwrought Casting Features

- **Gravitic Twist**: You may reroll one damage die per spell that deals force, thunder, or psychic damage. The reroll applies gravitational acceleration.
- **Fractured Echo**: Once per long rest, cast a spell as if from a previous round—retroactively choosing its target or direction.
- **Void Cost**: When casting a Voidwrought spell, take 1d4 psychic damage per spell level (resistance negates).

---
## Echo Glyph Integration

Voidwrought Weaving tears at the Weave, leaving warped echoes behind:

- **Null Echo**: A failed spell still creates a 10-foot zone of gravity distortion for 1 round (difficult terrain, no concentration).
- **Warp Thread**: Spells cast within 10 feet of a previously cast Void spell gain +1 to attack rolls.
- **Starburn Trace**: Psychic spells create afterimages of thought; the next Insight check made nearby has advantage.

## Example Spells (Flavor Additions)

> These spells behave strangely when Voidwrought:

- **[[Misty Step]]**: Appears to leave a trail of static or reversed time.
- [[**Dispel Magic**]]: Unwinds a thread of reality like smoke.
- **[[Levitate]]**: Target's bones hum with unseen pressure.
- **[[Hold Person]]**: Locks the subject in a collapsed moment, like a memory stuck on repeat.

## Social Perception

- **Feared Universally**: Associated with madness, corruption, and star-plague.
- **Studied in Secret**: Only the desperate or fanatical study Voidwrought openly.
- **Monitored by the Faith**: [[The Faith of the Bound Pattern]] considers this style a warning sign of Weave corruption. Users are often watched, tested, or purged.

---
## Optional Mechanics

- **Fractured Mind ([[Flaw]] or [[Feat]])**: Gain advantage vs. being charmed, but disadvantage on Insight checks. Occasionally hear whispering moons.
- **Void-Tempered Focus**: A broken object or mirror that focuses spells better when damaged.
- **Eldritch Mark ([[Boon]])**: Once per day, a failed spell doesn't vanish—it lingers as ambient magic for a round and may be triggered by proximity.

---
## Design Notes

Voidwrought Weaving is not just spellcasting; it is magic's unraveling edge. Where Blood Magic digs inward and Glyph Binding roots itself in stability, Voidwrought pushes **beyond**, challenging the **very structure** of casting. It is dangerous, glorious, and utterly destabilizing.

Few who wield it remain unchanged.
