## 🧠 What Is a Fracture?

A **Fracture** is a mental or spiritual break that occurs when a character's **Sanity** fails under stress. It is the cost of witnessing horrors beyond mortal comprehension—most often from **Eldritch** forces, death trauma, or memory-shattering events.

Fractures lead to:

- Temporary or permanent psychological effects
- The acquisition of **Whisper Points**
- Unpredictable behaviours, hallucinations, compulsions, or memory loss

---
## ⚠️ When Do Fractures Occur?

|Trigger|Effect|
|---|---|
|Failing a Fracture Save|Roll on Fracture Table|
|Reaching Max Whisper Points|Automatically Fracture|
|Consecutive Failed Sanity Saves|Fracture Save at Disadvantage|
|Being exposed to Eldritch Truths|DM may call for Fracture even at full Sanity|

---
## 🧩 Whisper Points

Whisper Points are a mechanical measure of how close a character is to **mental instability or revelation**.

|   |   |
|---|---|
|Source|Gain Whisper Point?|
|Failing Sanity Save|Yes|
|Viewing an Eldritch Moon|Yes|
|Using Eldritch Magic or Glyphs|Maybe (DM discretion)|
|Recalling traumatic memory|Yes|

### 🧠 Whisper Point Cap = 1 + Sanity Modifier (min 1)

Once this cap is reached, **immediately roll a Fracture Save**.

---
## 🎲 Fracture Save

**DC = 13 + Whisper Points** at the time of the roll.

> Roll: `1d20 + Constitution Modifier + Sanity Modifier`

On success: Regain 1 Sanity and purge a Whisper Point. On failure: Gain a Fracture Condition.

## 💥 Fracture Table (Examples)

|   |   |
|---|---|
|d8|Fracture Effect|
|1|Catatonia (Cannot act until struck or healed)|
|2|Hallucination (Sees one ally as an enemy)|
|3|Compulsion (Must repeat last action until restrained or forced out)|
|4|Panic (Drops items and flees next round)|
|5|Fugue (Loses memory of the last 10 minutes; -2 to INT/WIS checks)|
|6|Weep Unseen (Loses all Focus; can’t gain Focus for 1 minute)|
|7|Awakened Insight (Gain random truth about enemy or Glyph—but take 1d6 psychic)|
|8|Eldritch Hunger (Must move toward unnatural source each turn until resisted)|

> DM may modify or assign custom Fracture effects based on narrative.

---
## 🧷 Optional Toggles

|   |   |
|---|---|
|Toggle Name|Description|
|**Residual Fracture**|Fracture effects linger for 1d4 hours even after Sanity stabilizes|
|**Compounding Whispers**|Each additional Whisper Point over cap gives disadvantage to Fracture Saves|
|**Silent Madness**|Player does not know exact Fracture effect—must roleplay symptomatically|
|**Glyph Feedback**|Using secondary Glyphs with high stress may add Whisper Points|

## 🛠 Integration Hooks

|   |   |
|---|---|
|System|Interaction|
|**Sanity/Fear**|Core system driver—Fractures are consequences of Sanity failure|
|**Focus**|Fractures drain or block Focus|
|**Whisper Engine**|Tied directly—Fracture is the primary danger of building Whisper|
|**Magic & Glyphs**|Eldritch or Unstable Glyphs may trigger Whisper gain or Fracture|
