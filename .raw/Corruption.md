# 🕳️ Eldritch Corruption
## How the Eldritch Infiltrate the Weave, the Mind, and the Flesh

> “They do not arrive. They are remembered by the space around them.”  
> — Onset warning carved at the threshold of the Hollow Vault

---

## 📖 What Is Corruption Progression?

Corruption Progression is the **escalating metaphysical rot** that occurs when mortals, environments, or magical structures are exposed to **[[Eldritch influence]]**—especially through contact with the **[[Null Reflection]]**, **[[Dead Magic Zones]]**, **[[Eldritch Carvings]]**, or **communes with [[Eldritch Forces]]**.

Unlike diseases or curses, **corruption is ontological**. It begins subtly—misaligned reflections, recursive glyphs, haunting dreams—and crescendos into **[[madness]], [[mutation]], or [[glyphic erasure]]**.

---
## ☣️ Why It Happens

Corruption occurs because Eldritch entities do not operate within the Weave—they **rupture it**.

Their presence introduces:
- **Recursive glyphs**
- **Broken logic or causality**
- **Dead or Wild Magic fields**
- **Reality-mirroring echoes**

These anomalies infect contact points like **flesh**, **magic**, or **soul**, birthing new anchors for their anti-truths to unfurl.

### ⚠️ Corruption Sources

- Using **[[Eldritch Carvings]]** (drawn, tattooed, or scarified)
- Entering **[[Eldritch Zones]]** (Dead Magic Fields, Arcane Rifts, Hollow Vault)
- Casting spells near **[[Eldritch Moons]]**
- Accepting truths or gifts from a member of **[[Eldritch Forces]]**
- Failing **[[Sanity Thresholds]]**
- Studying, touching, or attempting to bind a **[[Glyph of Unbeing]]**

---
## 🧠 When Corruption Progresses

Progression occurs through **thresholds**, **triggers**, or **ritual acts**:

| Event or Trigger                          | Effect                                                 |
| ----------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| 🩸 Carving a scarified [[Eldritch glyph]] | Save vs. 25d10 force damage or gain indefinite madness |
| 💀 Dying in an [[Eldritch Zone]]          | Mutation roll or glyphspawn transformation             |
| 🔮 Casting in a [[Dead Magic Zone]]       | Memory erosion or spell recursion                      |
| 🧪 Reaching 5+ Corruption Score           | Trigger a corruption threshold surge                   |
| 🌘 Dream-contact with an Eldritch         | Gain 1 Corruption, risk of madness                     |
| ✍️ Studying a corrupted glyph             | Roll for latent glyph-echo or mental instability       |

## 🎲 Optional: Effects by Tier (Expanded)

You could offer system hooks for each tier:

### Minor Corruption (5–9)

- **Disadvantage** on _Sanity Checks_ in Eldritch Zones
- 10% chance for spell _glyph feedback_

### Major Corruption (10–14)

- **+1 Madness Save DCs**
- _Illusions begin to speak_
- Touching a glyph can cause **Reality Bleed** (GM prompt)

### Eldritch Corruption (15–19)

- Gain **1 mutation** (roll on Mutation Table)
- **Cannot regain Focus** in Wild Zones
- Dreamwalking effects spread to party

### Terminal Corruption (20+)

- **Lose your Personal Glyph**
- Become a **Glyphspawn**, or enter **Echo-Bearer Phase**
- May only be healed through **Wish**, **True Resurrection**, or **sanity-forged glyphic ritual**

## 💡 DM Tips for Campaign Use

- Treat **Corruption Score** like milestone advancement—offer power at a cost.
- Use **Glyph Echoes** and warped environments to foreshadow madness.
- Allow glyphspawn mutations to offer power, at the price of instability.
- Let players **willingly sacrifice sanity**—it’s more terrifying that way.
