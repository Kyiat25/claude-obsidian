## THE MARSHLANDS (River-Keepers)

| **Aspect** | **Details** |
|---|---|
| **Total Population** | ~1 million |
| **Organization** | 12 independent families |
| **Function** | River passage control + maintenance |
| **Economic Model** | Passage taxes (5-12% of cargo) |
| **Annual Revenue** | ~375K-3.3M silver |
| **Best Navigators** | Superior river expertise unmatched |
| **Military Power** | Low (distributed family warriors) |
| **Political Influence** | Nearly invisible (deliberately) |
| **Strategic Value** | Essential infrastructure; invisible leverage |

1. **Wavebreak** (~85K) - Navigation, northern routes (8%)
2. **Deepwater** (~72K) - Poison-craft, dangerous routes (12%)
3. **Marshroot** (~78K) - Food/fishing, coastal (6% + provisions)
4. **Tideborn** (~70K) - Tide-reading, seasonal routes (10-15% variable)
5. **Shadowwater** (~65K) - Creature-hunting, deep waters (10%)
6. **Brightstone** (~62K) - Stone circles, ancient passages (5%)
7. **Silverrun** (~58K) - Agriculture, secondary routes (4%)
8. **Rushwood** (~55K) - Herbs/poisons, poison plants (9%)
9. **Fogmist** (~50K) - Smuggling, hidden routes (15% or trade)
10. **Moonlight** (~48K) - Prophecy, astronomical routes (4-10% variable)
11. **Blacktide** (~52K) - Ocean sailing, sea routes (7%)
12. **Willowmere** (~43K) - Mediation, peaceful routes (3%)

## THE THREADBEARER CRISIS

### What Happened
- Four of five Threadbearers assassinated 18 days ago
- Only Naleth Spiralbound (Spirit Thread) survived
- One World Anchor inverted, creating magical instability

### Immediate Effects
1. **Prophecy systems fail** - All prophecy-dependent civilizations in crisis
2. **Thread instabilities** - Ley-distortions, World Tree corruption, temporal anomalies
3. **Political chaos** - Authority questioned, powers repositioning
4. **Economic disruption** - Routes uncertain, insurance costs rising
5. **Refugee flows** - Displaced populations destabilizing regions

### Critical Vulnerabilities Exposed
- **Zhen-Lan**: Bureaucracy paralyzed without prophecy
- **Prophecy families**: Deepwater/Moonlight losing credibility
- **Kel Varyan**: Imperial authority weakened (prophecy was foundation)
- **Corvessa**: Seeing opportunity; planning seventh crusade
- **All powers**: Governing without precedent

## STRATEGIC CONNECTIONS

### Major Trade Routes
**The Ivora Corridor**: Corvessa wine → sea (Marshlands 8% tax)
**The Three Sisters**: Central hub at Kel Varyan (variable tax)
**The Dawnreach Passes**: Only land route to Kerythra (~800K tons annually)
**Maritime Routes**: Kel Varyan ↔ Selveran ↔ other continents (weather dependent)

### Threatened Flashpoints
**Corvessa-Talanthar Border**: Seventh crusade whispers; prophecy failure = no advance warning
**Velcran Expansion**: Blurring Marshlands boundaries; imperial authority weak
**Drow Incursions**: Southern leylines compromised; raids increasing
**Veltharyn Reform**: Caste system pressure; isolation debate ongoing

### Continental Relationships
**Kel Varyan-Corvessa**: Imperial overlord-vassal (tension rising)
**Corvessa-Talanthar**: Eschatological war (permanent conflict)
**Talanthar-Virelion**: Cautious peace (some support Corvessa ideologically)
**Deepstone-All**: Neutral merchants (trade with everyone)
**Lyrran-Kel Varyan**: Mutual benefit (navy protects merchants; merchants enrich empire)
**Veltharyn-All**: Isolation (self-sufficient, minimal engagement)
**Zhen-Lan-All**: Neutral mediators (authority declining)
