# The Paladin

**Divine Champions of Unwavering Conviction**

> *"The oath is not a promise to a god. It is a promise to everyone behind you."*

---

## Faith Made Flesh

The demon had broken three lines already. When she stepped forward, her allies didn't rally because the odds had changed. They rallied because she had. She planted her feet, raised her weapon, and the radiant light that erupted wasn't from a spell — it was from something older. The demon learned what it meant to stand between a Paladin and what she had sworn to protect.

You are the Paladin. Not a warrior who prays, but a divine force that happens to be wearing plate armour. Your **Divine Smite** is a declaration: you do not merely hit your enemies, you judge them. Your **Aura of Protection** means your allies are stronger simply because you are standing near them. Your **Lay on Hands** means your presence is the difference between survival and defeat.

In FEARS, the Paladin's Momentum interaction is defensive: **Righteous Stability** at 9th level means the first missed attack against you each turn doesn't cost Momentum when you're already ahead. You are the immovable object around which the battle organises itself.

---

## The Paladin's Essence

You are a tank, a healer, and a burst striker — often simultaneously. No class offers the same combination of reliable damage escalation, party-wide defensive aura, and targeted healing that the Paladin does. You are most powerful at the centre of a fight, absorbing pressure and redirecting it as radiant judgment.

You struggle at range and in fights that reward mobility over sustained presence. Play to your strengths: get in, stay in, protect the people behind you, and smite anything that threatens them.

The Paladin tracks:   
• Lay on Hands pool (5 × level)

• Channel Divinity uses (1–4 per rest)

 Recommended for players who enjoy protecting allies, dealing decisive burst damage, and embodying conviction.

## Class Features

### Hit Points

- **Hit Dice:** 1d10 per Paladin level
- **Hit Points at 1st Level:** 10 + your Constitution modifier
- **Hit Points at Higher Levels:** 1d10 (or 6) + your Constitution modifier per Paladin level after 1st

### Proficiencies

- **Armour:** All armour and shields
- **Weapons:** Simple weapons, martial weapons
- **Tools:** None
- **Saving Throws:** Wisdom, Charisma
- **Skills:** Choose two from Athletics, Insight, Intimidation, Medicine, Persuasion, Religion, and Resolve

### Starting Equipment

You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:

- (a) a martial weapon and a shield or (b) two martial weapons
- (a) five javelins or (b) any simple melee weapon
- (a) a priest's pack or (b) an explorer's pack
- Chain mail and a holy symbol

---

## Quick Build

To quickly create a Paladin, follow these steps:

1. **Ability Scores:** Make Strength your highest score, followed by Charisma.
2. **Lineage and Heritage:** Any combination works; Human, Aasimar, and Dragonborn are thematic but not required.
3. **Background:** Noble, Soldier, or any background with Persuasion or Religion.
4. **Combat Strategy:** Position between enemies and fragile allies; use Divine Smite on hits that matter; use Lay on Hands proactively rather than reactively when possible.

## Core Class Features

### Divine Sense (1st Level)

For 1 minute, you know the location of any Celestial, Fiend, or Undead within 60 feet (not behind total cover). You sense consecrated or desecrated areas within the same radius. Uses equal to your Proficiency Bonus + 1, regaining all uses after a long rest.

---

### Lay on Hands (1st Level)

You have a healing pool equal to **5 × your Paladin level**. As an action, touch a creature to restore HP from this pool. Alternatively, expend 5 points to cure one disease or neutralise one poison.

---

### Weapon Mastery (1st Level)

At 1st level, gain Weapon Mastery with **one martial weapon** of your choice. When you hit with that weapon, apply one Weapon Skill once per turn.

**Divine Synergy:** Weapon Skills stack with Divine Smite and other Paladin features. Apply a Weapon Skill and expend a spell slot for Divine Smite on the same hit.

Unlock new masteries after scoring critical hits: 2 crits for simple weapons, 3 for martial.

---

### Divine Smite (2nd Level)

When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, expend one Divine spell slot to deal radiant damage:

- Base: 2d8 for a 1st-circle slot, increasing by 1d8 per higher circle (maximum 5d8).
- Deal an additional 1d8 against Fiends or Undead.

---

### Martial Action (2nd Level)

Use a bonus action on each turn to perform one of the following: **Guard** (impose disadvantage on the next attack against you or an adjacent ally) or **Wind Up** (add extra damage equal to your Proficiency Bonus to your next hit with a heavy or versatile weapon).

---

### Fighting Style (2nd Level)

Choose one: Duelling, Great Weapon Fighting, Interception, Protection, Shield Master, or Sanctified Weapon (+1d4 damage against creatures opposed to your oath's tenets).

---

### Paladin Subclass (3rd Level, and 7th, 11th, 15th Level)

Choose a Sacred Oath such as **Oath of Devotion**, **Oath of Vengeance**, or **Oath of the Ancients**. Your oath grants features at 3rd, 7th, 11th, and 15th levels, as well as Oath Spells always prepared.

---

### Holy Precision (3rd Level)

Once per turn when you make a Called Shot, reduce the penalty by an amount equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum 1). With 16 Charisma (+3), a Called Shot to the head (normally −4) would only impose a −1 penalty.

---

### Multiattack (5th Level)

Make two attacks when you take the Attack action.

---

### Aura of Protection (6th Level)

When you or an ally within 10 feet makes a saving throw, add your Charisma modifier (minimum +1) to the roll. Requires consciousness to function.

---

### Aura of Courage (9th Level)

You and allies within 10 feet cannot be frightened. Requires consciousness to function.

---

### Righteous Stability (9th Level)

When you begin your turn at +2 or higher Momentum, the first attack that misses you before the start of your next turn does not reduce your Momentum. Your unwavering conviction shields not just your body but your tactical advantage.

You still lose Momentum normally from subsequent missed attacks, successful attacks, or other sources.

---

### Heroic Boon (10th Level)

Choose one:

**Divine Recovery:** When you heal a creature with Lay on Hands, that creature may spend one Hit Die and add their Constitution modifier — regaining that many additional HP. The target also gains temporary HP equal to your Charisma modifier.

**Radiant Strikes:** When you hit with a weapon attack, deal an extra 1d8 radiant damage. Fiends and Undead take 2d8 radiant instead. This bonus stacks with Divine Smite.

---

### Improved Divine Smite (11th Level)

All your melee weapon hits deal an additional 1d8 radiant damage automatically. This is always active and does not count as using your Divine Smite feature — you may still use regular Divine Smite once per turn in addition to this passive damage.

---

### Channel Divinity (Available from 2nd Level via Subclass)

Channel divine power to create magical effects. Begin with your subclass's Channel Divinity option at 3rd level. Gain 2 uses per rest at 13th level and 3 uses at 18th level.

**Ward of Faith (Reaction via Channel Divinity):** When an allied creature within 30 feet would lose Momentum, expend one use of Channel Divinity to reduce their Momentum loss by 1 (to a minimum of 0).

**Judgment (Bonus Action via Channel Divinity):** Your next weapon attack or spell attack this turn counts as a Called Shot, but you make the attack roll without penalty. Must attack before end of your turn or the use is wasted.

---

### Cleansing Touch (14th Level)

As an action, end one spell affecting yourself or a willing creature you touch. Uses equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum 1), regaining all uses after a long rest.

---

### Additional Fighting Style (15th Level)

Gain another Fighting Style option.

---

### Aura Improvements (17th Level)

The range of your Aura of Protection, Aura of Courage, and any subclass auras increases to **30 feet**.

---

### Epic Boon (20th Level)

Choose one:

**Aura of Salvation:** Activate for 10 minutes. Allies within 30 feet gain resistance to nonmagical damage, automatically succeed on death saves, and regain HP equal to your Charisma modifier at the start of their turns. Once per long rest.

**Avatar of Retribution:** Add your Charisma modifier to all weapon damage rolls. When you use Divine Smite, the damage increases by an additional 2d8 radiant damage.

---

## Last Stand Options

When standing at the precipice of death, a Paladin embodies their oath one final time.

**Rules for Last Stand:** Invoked when you would be reduced to 0 hit points, roll a death saving throw, or die outright. A free action requiring no reaction or consciousness. Once resolved, the character permanently dies.

---

### 1. Endless Mercy

*You subject yourself to a living hell, dissolving your soul into respite for your comrades.*

**Effect:**
- All allies within 100 feet gain the following for 5 minutes:
  - Regain HP equal to your character level at the end of each of your turns.
  - Cured of all poison and disease at the end of each of your turns.
- Allies gain +3 to one saving throw of your choice (Wisdom, Strength, or Constitution) for 5 minutes.

---

### 2. True Smite

*Your deity uses your body as a conduit for its full unbridled wrath, destroying your mortal vessel.*

**Effect:**
- Move up to your speed.
- Deliver a critical hit to one target within melee range.
- The target makes a Charisma saving throw (DC = 12 + PB + CHA modifier).
- On failure: banished to a plane of your choice for 1d12 + CHA modifier months.
- This saving throw cannot be bypassed by Legendary Resistance.

---

### 3. Turn Fate

*You clutch your holy symbol until it breaks skin, channelling divinity not against your foes, but against the future they would beckon.*

**Effect:**
- All enemies within 100 feet make a Wisdom saving throw (DC = 12 + PB + CHA modifier).
- On failure: turned (as by Turn Undead) for 1 minute. Evil-aligned creatures save with disadvantage.
- Turned enemies cannot escape the effect even if they take damage.
- This ability ignores line of effect.

| Last Stand Option | Effect | Best Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Endless Mercy** | Allies regain HP and cure poison/disease each turn; +3 to saves. | Healing and protecting the party. |
| **True Smite** | Critical hit + Charisma save or banished (ignores Legendary Resistance). | Removing a powerful enemy. |
| **Turn Fate** | All enemies within 100 feet turned for 1 minute (evil saves at disadvantage). | Neutralising hordes. |

## Class Reactions

Paladins have access to powerful reactions that protect allies and punish enemies through divine power. These reactions use your **Proficiency Bonus pool**, refreshing on short or long rest.

### Divine Intercession (2nd Level)

**Trigger:** An ally within 10 feet is targeted by an attack.
**Effect:** Force the attack to target you instead. Reduce the damage by PB + CHA modifier.

---

### Aegis of Oath (3rd Level)

**Trigger:** A creature within your Aura targets an ally.
**Effect:** The ally gains +PB to Evasion for that attack. If it still hits, the ally takes half damage and you take the other half as radiant damage.

The aura range (10 feet at 3rd, 15 feet at 6th, 30 feet at 18th+) determines the radius.

---

### Radiant Judgment (11th Level)

**Trigger:** You smite a creature with Divine Smite.
**Effect:** All hostile creatures of your choice within 10 feet of the target make a CON save (DC = 8 + PB + CHA) or take PBd6 radiant damage and are blinded until end of their next turn.
