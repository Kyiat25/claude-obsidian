## 🚨 CRITICAL TIMELINE INCONSISTENCY TO RESOLVE

### The Ashkarra Fall Date Problem

Your uploaded timeline and project knowledge contain **contradictory dates** for the Fall of Ashkarra:

| Source | Fall of Ashkarra Date | Years Ago |
|--------|----------------------|-----------|
| **Uploaded Timeline** | Year 0 (defining Fourth Age start) | 487 years ago |
| **Vashkora Atlas GM Reference** | Year 440 of the Fourth Age | 47 years ago |
| **Arc 2A (Prophet of Ashes)** | Year 336 of the Fourth Age | 151 years ago |

**This must be resolved before finalizing.** The uploaded timeline treats Year 0 as Ashkarra's fall, but project documents reference events IN the Fourth Age before Ashkarra's destruction.

**Recommendation:** If Ashkarra fell 47 years ago (Year 440), then:
- Fourth Age began ~487 years ago for a different reason
- Ashkarra's fall is recent enough for Kethyr to survive (he's ~250 years old)
- But Vashkora docs would need revision

If Ashkarra fell 487 years ago (Year 0), then:
- Kethyr Flameheart cannot be a living survivor (he's not elven)
- Arc 2A would need revision to make him a spirit, undead, or different concept
- All "47 years ago" references in Vashkora docs are wrong

**The Arc 2A "151 years" version** seems like a middle ground—Ashkarra fell in 336 A4, making the present day Year 487 = 151 years post-fall. This allows Kethyr (dragonborn, long-lived) to survive while keeping meaningful temporal distance.
