# 🌟 **Reputation**

### _Your Name is a Story the World Keeps Telling_

In _[[Dór-o Estel]]_, your actions weave into the memory of the world. Whether whispered in village inns or sung by street performers in metropolises, your reputation precedes you. Unlike **[[Wanted Score]]** (which tracks criminal notoriety) or **[[Disposition]]** (which governs NPC moods), **Reputation** represents the **narrative identity** others attach to your deeds—for better or worse.

This system allows players and GMs to track a character's public image, evolving reputation titles, and mechanical influence over social interaction, recognition, and opportunity.

---
## 🧠 **Key Distinctions**

|System|Tracks...|Source of Influence|Type|
|---|---|---|---|
|**Disposition**|Current attitude of a specific NPC|Roleplay, Charisma, local actions|Immediate/Local|
|**Wanted Score**|Criminal heat and pursuit level|Illegal or hostile acts|Legal/Global|
|**Reputation**|Public identity and cultural memory|Public deeds, myth, narrative|Narrative/Zone|

---
## 🧭 **Reputation Tiers**

Reputation is a fluid narrative tool. DMs and players can assign or evolve titles organically or via milestones. Each tier may have multiple interpretations (heroic, infamous, mysterious, etc.).

|Tier|General Recognition|Example Titles|Influence Scope|
|---|---|---|---|
|0|Unknown|"Wanderer", "No-One", "Outsider"|None|
|1|Local|"Woodswitch of Greerfield", "Thornfang Slayer"|Hamlet / Village|
|2|Regional|"Protector of the Vale", "Traitor of Ashkarra"|Towns and minor cities|
|3|National|"Fire-Walker", "Fleshbreaker", "Queen's Foe"|Cities, nobles, clergy|
|4|Global|"Living Legend", "The Hollow Herald"|Metropolis / Empire|

> Reputation is **not always accurate**. Titles may be earned truthfully, exaggerated, or completely misunderstood.

---
## 🏆 **Gaining Reputation**

Reputation increases when a deed becomes **public, repeated, or witnessed by key figures**.

### Common Triggers:

- Saving or destroying a settlement
- Killing a known threat (named monsters, commanders, nobles)
- Public duels, dramatic speeches, legendary escapes
- Creating a magical artifact or unlocking forbidden knowledge
- Being the subject of bardic song, priestly prophecy, or cultic rumor
- Earning faction rank, formal titles, or divine marks

### Optional Rule: Fame Points

DMs may assign abstract "Fame Points" per deed. Reaching thresholds (e.g., 3 FP = Tier 2) promotes the character up the scale.

---
## 🎭 **Reputation Titles**

Titles reflect how you are perceived. A character may have **different titles in different regions or among different factions**.

Examples:

- **Heroic**: Flame-Saver of Veltharyn, Saint of the Sewers, Binder of the Rift
- **Infamous**: Doomflame, The White-Eyed Butcher, Glyph-Cracked Prophet
- **Neutral/Complex**: Echo-Walker, The Threadmarked, Chosen of the Hollow Star

NPC reactions may differ based on alignment, faction, or region.

> A noble may treat you as a heretic while the peasants chant your name.

---
## 🎁 **Mechanical Benefits & Complications**

### Benefits of Fame:

- Advantage on **Persuasion** with admirers or aligned factions
- Discounts or free services in worshipful regions
- Easier access to courts, councils, and temples
- [[Black market]] contacts who want a piece of your legend
- Reputation-based **social initiative** in downtime scenes

### Risks of Infamy:

- Disadvantage on **Stealth** or **Deception** in known zones
- Harassment by zealots, fanatics, or bounty hunters
- Higher prices or outright denial of service
- Reputation imposters, false rumors, or forgeries
- Magical tracking by name or sigil (linked to myths)

---
## 🌍 **Regional Reputation and Faction Echoes**

Reputation scales **per region and per faction**.

- Your title in **[[07_Geography/02_Cities/Thar Vornhal]]** may be feared while **[[Aeravae]]** reveres you.
- **Spirit-aligned cults** may spread your name while **Earth Pantheon officials** hunt you.
- Faction may **rewrite your title** to align with their goals or propaganda.

DMs may create **Reputation Cards or Lore Slips** for each major faction/city to track PC perception.

---
## 🔄 **Changing or Redeeming Reputation**

Changing a reputation is hard—but not impossible:

- Disguise, Memory Magic, or Identity Transfer
- Public Deeds that contradict your myth
- Trial by Faith, Sacrificial Acts, or Divine Redemption
- Surrender and judgment before a Faction Tribunal

Even then, the **old stories may linger** in whispers.

> You may abandon your name, but the name may not abandon the world.

## 🏁 Final Note

Reputation is the memory of your legend in living form. It does not fade easily, nor can it be fully controlled. But in _FEARS_, your name is more than a line on a page—it is a force. Shape it. Fear it. Or burn it down.

> "They called me Doomflame in [[Varneth]]. Hero in [[Ashkarra]]. A ghost in [[07_Geography/02_Cities/Thar Vornhal]]. Let them argue. I remember who I was."
