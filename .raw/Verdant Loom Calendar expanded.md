# Verdant Loom Calendar (DM-Only Reference)

_Last updated: Celestialdawn 18, 487 A4_

The **Verdant Loom** is the official calendar of [[Dór-o Estel]], interweaving elemental Threads, seasonal cycles, leyline resonance, and mythic echoes of the [[World Trees]]. It governs civic life, ritual magic, and metaphysical rhythm in all [[Loom-Cities]], maintained by the [[Faith of the Bound Pattern]].

---

## 🧭 Current Date

- **Date**: Celestialdawn 18, Year 487 of the **4th Age**
- **Weekday**: **Threnday** (1st day of the 6-day week)
- **Season**: ❄️ **Deepveil** _(Winter)_

> 💠 **DM Insight — Auroratide**:  
> This is not a ceremonial gap. Auroratide marks a **Weave destabilization** phase. Wild magic zones fluctuate. Prophetic signals are scrambled. **Eldritch whispers** often bleed through in dreams. Threadbearers and Moonscribes may disappear or go silent. Use Auroratide to insert **unseen divine shifts**, **false prophecies**, or **threaded fractures**.

## 🔁 Weekdays (Thread Days)

Each 6-day week follows the elemental Threads.

| Day           | Domain |
| ------------- | ------ |
| **Threnday**  | Weave  |
| **Emberday**  | Fire   |
| **Stonehold** | Earth  |
| **Windfell**  | Air    |
| **Tidecall**  | Water  |
| **Veilrest**  | Spirit |

> 🧵 **DM Insight — Thread Day Influence**:  
> Each day exerts subtle metaphysical influence.  
> - Casting aligned to the day’s Thread gains small narrative bonuses.  
> - Cities schedule trials, births, executions, and rituals accordingly.  
> - **Auroratide’s days are Threadless**—no divine guidance, no Weave anchor.  
> Ritual failure, madness onset, or silent omens are common.

## 🌿 Mythic Seasonal Cycle

The Verdant Loom follows **5 mythic seasons**, each mapping to an elemental pattern and collective behavior.

|Season Name|Start Date|Elemental Theme|Description|
|---|---|---|---|
|🌱 Verdant Rise|Blossomaril 1|Earth|Renewal of land, memory planting, soil rites|
|🔥 Scorchpeak|Sunfirelia 1|Fire|Forging season, war blessings, sacred trial fires|
|🍂 Twilight Tithe|Harvestfall 1|Air|Offering and tribute, migration of wisdom|
|❄️ Deepveil|Frostspire 1|Water|Stillness, remembrance, **moonlit prophecy**|
|🌌 Threads Turn|Auroratide 1|Weave (Threadless)|**Loom unravels**, fate fractures, divine shifts|

> 🩸 **DM Insight — Deepveil & Eldritch Risk**:  
> Deepveil is the most dangerous prophetic season.  
> - **Memory-echoes** resurface.  
> - Eldritch Moons flicker faintly (even if not yet risen).  
> - Spirit echoes, dream-hauntings, and breach zones intensify.  
> Many Hollow Breaches trace their origin to Deepveil rituals gone wrong.

> 🧶 **Narrative Flag — Threads Turn**:  
> This is your cue for world events:  
> - **Loom-Keeper changes**,  
> - **City Thread realignments**,  
> - **first whispers of a new Eldritch Moon**,  
> - or **player-affecting fate rewrites** can be slotted here.  
> Threadless time is unmoored—**ideal for surreal sequences**, retroactive omens, or divine mysteries.
