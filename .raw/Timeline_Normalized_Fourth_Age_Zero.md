## MAJOR EVENTS (NORMALIZED TO FOURTH AGE START)

### Before Time (No Dates)
- First Weaving creates reality
- Four Universal Forces awaken
- Five Threads manifest
- Astralor and Aeloria sleep

### Loomless Time (Years ~-20,000 to ~-2,500)
- **Year ~-20,000**: Beginning of recorded history
- **Year ~-19,000**: First mortal civilizations emerge
- **Year ~-15,000**: Early pre-divine societies flourish
- **Year -12,400**: Deepforge civilization (archaeological evidence)
- **Year -11,800**: Silverwood civilization (archaeological evidence)
- **Year -8,600**: Tidemark civilization (archaeological evidence)
- **Year ~-5,000**: World Tree network naturally develops
- **Year ~-3,000**: Thread instability begins manifesting
- **Year ~-2,500**: First World Anchor attempted (First Age begins)

### First Age (Years ~-2,500 to ~-1,800)
- **Year -2,500**: First World Anchor construction begins
- **Year -2,480**: Loom-Cities concept developed
- **Year -2,400**: Five World Anchors planned
- **Year -2,300**: Veltharyn Anchor completed
- **Year -2,250**: Corvessa Anchor completed
- **Year -2,200**: Kel Varyan Anchor completed
- **Year -2,150**: Talanthar Anchor completed
- **Year -2,100**: Ashkarra Anchor construction begins
- **Year -2,000**: Thread stabilization achieved
- **Year -1,950**: Ashkarra Anchor completed
- **Year -1,900**: Loom-City network fully operational
- **Year -1,800**: First Age ends, Second Age begins

### Second Age (Years ~-1,800 to ~-1,100)
- **Year -1,800**: Golden era of magical advancement begins
- **Year -1,700**: Peak World Tree population (~150-200 trees)
- **Year -1,600**: First Eldritch Moon appears (Cindol, the Ashen Gate)
- **Year -1,550**: Second Eldritch Moon (Nyx, the Void's Edge)
- **Year -1,500**: Divine Emergence begins
- **Year -1,450**: Pantheons claim creation authority
- **Year -1,400**: First faith suppressions begin
- **Year -1,350**: Archaeological evidence systematically destroyed
- **Year -1,300**: Third Eldritch Moon (Threnix, Shattered Mirror)
- **Year -1,250**: Fourth Eldritch Moon (Vorthak, the Hungering Dark)
- **Year -1,200**: Multiple Thread collapses
- **Year -1,150**: Fifth Eldritch Moon (Ixilthar, the Writhing Spiral)
- **Year -1,100**: Second Age ends, Third Age begins

### Third Age (Years ~-1,100 to -95)
- **Year -1,100**: Recovery from Second Age disasters
- **Year -1,000**: Corvessa-Talanthar First Crusade begins
- **Year -950**: First Crusade ends (stalemate)
- **Year -900**: Sixth Eldritch Moon (Dreithan, the Hollow Witness)
- **Year -800**: Second Crusade begins
- **Year -780**: Second Crusade ends (Corvessa victory)
- **Year -750**: Third Crusade begins
- **Year -720**: Third Crusade ends (Talanthar victory)
- **Year -700**: Seventh Eldritch Moon (Yg'lothen, the Infinite Maw)
- **Year -600**: Fourth Crusade begins
- **Year -570**: Fourth Crusade ends (stalemate)
- **Year -500**: Eighth Eldritch Moon (Mor'gethil, the Bleeding Star)
- **Year -400**: Fifth Crusade begins
- **Year -385**: Fifth Crusade ends (Corvessa victory)
- **Year -300**: Ninth Eldritch Moon (Xarnithal, the Forgotten Dream)
- **Year -250**: Sixth Crusade begins
- **Year -230**: Sixth Crusade ends (Talanthar victory)
- **Year -200**: Tenth Eldritch Moon (Qu'theros, the Silent Scream)
- **Year -195**: **Kethyr Flameheart born**
- **Year -150**: Eleventh Eldritch Moon (Vel'drax, the Final Shape)
- **Year -105**: **Veshra Ashwalker born**
- **Year -100**: Seventh Crusade begins
- **Year -95**: **ASHKARRA FALLS** (The Catastrophe)

### The Unraveling (Years -95 to 0)
#### Phase 1: Absolute Chaos (Years -95 to -85)
- **Year -95**: Ashkarra's World Anchor destroyed
- **Year -95**: Third Age ends, quarter of population begins dying
- **Year -90**: Total governmental collapse
- **Year -85**: First Failed Anchor attempt begins (Selveran)

#### Phase 2: Failed Experiments (Years -85 to -55)
- **Year -85**: First Failed Anchor construction starts
- **Year -80**: Kethyr makes warlock pact (age 115)
- **Year -79**: First Failed Anchor explodes (40,000 dead)
- **Year -75**: Second Failed Anchor attempt begins (Thar Vornhal)
- **Year -75**: Veshra makes warlock pact (age 30)
- **Year -70**: Twelfth Eldritch Moon (Zar'gul, the Terminus Eye)
- **Year -68**: Second Failed Anchor fails (60,000 dead)
- **Year -55**: Third Failed Anchor attempt (Mirror Chain)
- **Year -55**: Third attempt sabotaged (10,000 dead)

#### Phase 3: Dark Stabilization (Years -55 to -20)
- **Year -55**: World accepts four-Anchor limitation
- **Year -50**: Magical rationing implemented
- **Year -40**: Life expectancy drops globally
- **Year -30**: Prophetic vision received
- **Year -20**: Cooperation finally achieved

#### Phase 4: Planning (Years -20 to -5)
- **Year -20**: All factions agree to work together
- **Year -15**: Aeravae site selected
- **Year -10**: Resources mobilized globally
- **Year -5**: Construction preparations complete

#### Phase 5: Construction (Years -5 to 0)
- **Year -5**: Aeravae construction begins
- **Year -3**: Main structure completed
- **Year -1**: Final attunements
- **Year 0**: **AERAVAE ACTIVATED - FOURTH AGE BEGINS**

### Fourth Age (Years 0 to 487+)
- **Year 0**: Echo-Cenote activates, Unraveling ends
- **Year 50**: First generation born in stability matures
- **Year 100**: Magical infrastructure partially restored
- **Year 200**: New political structures stabilize
- **Year 300**: Faith resurgence accelerates
- **Year 400**: Fourth Thread Cycle begins to fail
- **Year 430**: Fourth Thread Cycle collapses
- **Year 450**: Fifth Thread Cycle begins
- **Year 469**: Four Threadbearers assassinated (18 days before present)
- **Year 482**: Thornward Flats Disaster (Seventh Crusade finally ends)
- **Year 487**: **PRESENT DAY - CAMPAIGN START**
- **Year 487, Frostmarch 8**: Kethyr's "Sermon of Ashes"
