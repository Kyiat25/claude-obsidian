# The Shaman

**Conduits of Primal and Spiritual Power**

> *"The spirits don't leave a place cleanly. Where you have worked, the land holds memory. That is not a metaphor."*

---

## The Diplomat of the Unseen World

She didn't attack the monster. She placed three Totems at precise angles around it, activated the fourth, and waited. The monster attacked what looked like the weakest target — one of the Totems — and discovered that the weakest target was actually the centre of a web of ancestral power that made every blow it landed cost something. Shamans don't win fights by hitting hardest. They win by making the fight happen on their terms.

You are the Shaman. Your **Totems** are not decorations — they are the active architecture of every combat encounter. Your **Totemic Assault** is the spiritual hammer you bring down when the architecture is in place. Your **Sacred Focus** is the foundation that determines how you channel the chaos of primal spirits without being consumed.

In FEARS, your Momentum comes from spiritual attunement. **Totemic Rhythm** at 1st level: when you activate, summon, or move a Spirit Totem during combat, gain +1 Momentum. **Spirit-Touched Ground** at 7th level: being in a location you have spiritually marked also grants +1 Momentum at the start of your turn. You fight better in familiar ground, and you make everywhere familiar.

---

## The Shaman's Essence

You are a support-oriented battlefield controller who uses spirit totems to shape the encounter space. Your **Spirituality** subclass at 3rd level defines your specific flavour — curse-binding, spirit-warrior martial arts, wild animal bonds, or witch-doctor healing. **Greater Totem Spirits** at 11th level expand your repertoire dramatically.

You struggle in fights that destroy your Totems or require you to be highly mobile. You excel in encounters where you can place and maintain Totems and use the Spirit-Touched Ground feedback loop.

---

## Complexity Assessment: MODERATE (5/10)

The Shaman tracks:                                          
• Bound Totems (growing list, held in hand to use)      
• Pact Magic slots (Warlock-style, short rest recovery) 
• Spells Known (from Shaman spell list)                  
• Spirit-Touched locations (GM + player tracking)  
• Class Reaction pool (PB uses per short rest)         

Recommended for players who enjoy environmental control, spirit-based worldbuilding, and support-focused play.

## Class Features

### Hit Points

- **Hit Dice:** 1d8 per Shaman level
- **Hit Points at 1st Level:** 8 + your Constitution modifier
- **Hit Points at Higher Levels:** 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per Shaman level after 1st

### Proficiencies

- **Armour:** Light armour, shields
- **Weapons:** Simple weapons, blowguns, nets
- **Tools:** None
- **Saving Throws:** Wisdom, Focus
- **Skills:** Choose two from Animal Handling, Arcana, Gathering, Insight, Medicine, Nature, Performance, Religion, and Resolve

### Starting Equipment

You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:

- (a) a quarterstaff or (b) any simple weapon
- (a) a shortbow and 20 arrows or (b) 5 javelins
- (a) a priest's pack or (b) an explorer's pack
- Leather armour and one object to serve as your Totem

---

## Quick Build

To quickly create a Shaman, follow these steps:

1. **Ability Scores:** Make Wisdom your highest score, followed by Constitution.
2. **Lineage:** Any combination works; Orc, Goblin, and Tiefling are thematic.
3. **Background:** Outlander, Hermit, or any background with Nature or Religion.
4. **Combat Strategy:** Place Totems before or as soon as combat begins. Use Totemic Assault on high-priority targets. Position yourself in Spirit-Touched ground whenever possible for the Momentum bonus.

## Core Class Features

### Sacred Focus (1st Level)

You choose a Sacred Focus representing the core strength that enables you to channel spiritual power without being overwhelmed.

**Body:** Your HP maximum increases by 1 at 1st level and by 1 again each time you gain a Shaman level.

**Heart:** Whenever you make a saving throw to resist being charmed or frightened, add your Wisdom modifier (minimum +1) to the roll.

**Mind:** Whenever you would make an Intelligence-based check or saving throw, you can use your Wisdom modifier instead.

**Soul:** While not wearing armour or wielding a shield, your Evasion DC becomes 10 + DEX modifier + WIS modifier.

---

### Totems (1st Level)

You bind primal spirits into physical objects called Totems. A Totem can be any Tiny object (carved idol, animal bone, crystal). You must hold a Totem to use its abilities. You may activate one Totem's ability at a time unless otherwise specified.

**Totem Save DC** = 8 + PB + WIS modifier.

**Totemic Rhythm:** When you activate, summon, or move a Spirit Totem during combat, gain **+1 Momentum**.

You know a number of Totems as shown in the Bound Totems column. You learn additional Totems as you gain levels. See the Totem Catalogue below for available Totems.

---

### Totemic Assault (1st Level)

As an action while holding a Totem, choose one creature within 60 feet. The target makes a Charisma saving throw against your Totem Save DC. On a failed save, the creature takes necrotic damage equal to **1d10**. On a success, they take half damage.

Damage increases: 2d10 at 5th level, 3d10 at 11th level, 4d10 at 17th level.

---

### Primal Magic (2nd Level)

Your spell slots function like those of the Warlock class — a fixed level, shared pool, recovering on a short or long rest. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability.

- **Spell Save DC** = 8 + PB + WIS modifier
- **Spell Attack Modifier** = PB + WIS modifier

---

### Spirit Link (2nd Level)

As an action, focus your senses to detect creatures with spiritual or magical properties within 60 feet. Reveals the presence of spirits, active magical effects, and enchanted items. Automatically detects wild magic zones, arcane rifts, ley lines, and dead magic zones.

Uses equal to your Wisdom modifier per long rest.

---

### Spirituality (3rd Level, and 6th, 10th, 14th Level)

Choose a Spirituality defining your interaction with the spirit world: **Curse Binder**, **Spirit Warrior**, **Wild Heart**, or **Witch Doctor**. Grants features at 3rd, 6th, 10th, and 14th levels.

---

### Totemic Versatility (5th Level)

At the end of a long rest, replace one Totem you know with another Totem of your choice from the Totem list. Adapt to different challenges as you journey.

---

### Ancestral Precision (7th Level)

As a bonus action, choose one enemy within 60 feet. Your spirits reveal their weak points:

- You and allies have advantage on Called Shots against that target.
- Target's DR is reduced by your WIS modifier (minimum 1).
- When the target fails an Evasion roll, all allies within 30 feet gain +1 Momentum.
- If the target is within range of one of your active Totems, Called Shots deal an additional 2d6 spirit damage.
- When the target drops to 0 HP, the mark jumps to the nearest enemy within 30 feet.

Duration: 1 minute. Uses equal to your WIS modifier per long rest.

---

### Spirit-Touched Ground (7th Level)

The spirits do not leave a place cleanly. A location becomes **Spirit-Touched** when you activate or place a Spirit Totem there, cast a Primal Magic spell of 2nd level or higher there, use Totemic Assault there, or use Spiritbond Mastery or any Greater Totem Spirit ability there.

A location can accumulate up to **3 Spirit-Touch Marks**. Marks are permanent unless deliberately cleansed.

**Mechanical Effects (7th Level):** When you enter or begin your turn in a Spirit-Touched area, gain **+1 Momentum** (once per turn). Once per long rest while in a Spirit-Touched area, activate a Totem as a free action on your turn rather than a bonus action.

---

### Heroic Boon (10th Level)

Choose one:

**Totemic Conduit:** Activate two Totems simultaneously using the same action. All Totem ranges increase by 30 feet. When you use Totemic Assault, target one additional creature within range.

**Spirit Surge:** Once per short rest, when you activate a Totem, overcharge it — the Totem's numerical effects are maximised (maximum damage, healing, etc.). Additionally, once per short rest, activate all your active Totems simultaneously as an action affecting all valid targets within range.

---

### Totemic Resilience (10th Level)

Choose one type of bound Totem. While that Totem is active, gain one of the following based on the Totem's nature:

- **Guardian Totems (Bear, Ox, Turtle):** DR 1
- **Spirit Totems (Raven, Owl, Fox):** +2 to Evasion rolls
- **Ancestor Totems (Wolf, Eagle, Serpent):** Advantage on saves against being frightened or charmed

Change which Totem type grants this benefit during a long rest.

At 17th level: whenever you use Totemic Assault and a creature is in range of two of your Totems, gain advantage on the attack and temporary HP equal to your WIS modifier + Shaman level.

---

### Greater Totemic Bond (11th Level)

Bond with a **Greater Totem Spirit** (doesn't count against your Bound Totems total). This Greater Totem Spirit grants access to its spells at specific levels, each castable once per long rest without expending a spell slot.

When casting a Greater Totem Spirit spell, enhance its effect based on your Sacred Focus:
- **Body:** Gain temp HP equal to twice the spell's level.
- **Heart:** One ally within 10 feet of the spell's target gains a d6 Inspiration die.
- **Mind:** Reroll one damage or healing die; must use the new result.
- **Soul:** Creatures affected have disadvantage on saves against ongoing effects.

At 11th level, access 6th-level Greater Totem Spirit spells. At 13th, 15th, and 17th levels, access 7th, 8th, and 9th-level spells respectively.

When you use Totemic Versatility, you may also swap your current Greater Totem Spirit for another.

---

### Deepening Spirit (11th Level)

Spirit-Touched areas with 2+ marks gain additional properties:

**Tactical Presence:** Hostile creatures treat the ground within 10 feet of any of your active Spirit Totems as difficult terrain.

**Spiritual Impression:** Once per long rest per location, while standing within a 2+ mark area, ask the spirits a single yes/no question about events there within the past 7 days. Answer comes as sensation — warmth for yes, cold for no, pressure for uncertainty.

---

### Totemic Mastery (15th Level)

You can maintain one additional active Totem at the same time.

---

### Awakened Ground (15th Level)

In places you have Spirit-Touched three times, the ground is **Spiritually Awakened**. You always know the direction and approximate distance of any Awakened location. When a creature of CR 1+ (or character of 1st level+) enters an Awakened location while you are absent, you receive a faint impression of their presence. This does not wake you from sleep.

**Restored Power:** When you finish a short rest at an Awakened location, regain 1 expended spell slot of 3rd level or lower as the concentrated spiritual energy rethreads through you.

---

### Spiritbond Mastery (17th Level)

You can bond with a second Greater Totem Spirit. The two Greater Totem Spirits' spell lists combine, and when you cast a Greater Totem Spirit spell, you may apply the Sacred Focus enhancement from either spirit.

---

### Epic Boon (20th Level)

Choose one:

**Avatar of the Spirits:** As an action, enter an avatar state for 1 minute. While in this form: resistance to all damage; maintain one additional active Totem; teleport up to 30 feet at the start of each of your turns as part of your movement; Totemic Assault deals maximum damage. Once per long rest.

**Eternal Spirit:** When reduced to 0 HP but not killed outright, immediately restore half your HP maximum and activate any one of your Totems as a free action. Once per long rest. You no longer require food, drink, or sleep; immune to exhaustion; cannot be forcibly moved unless you allow it. When one of your active Totems is destroyed or deactivated, it reactivates automatically at the start of your next turn.

## Last Stand Options

When death comes for a Shaman, they invoke the full power of the spirits they serve.

**Rules for Last Stand:** Invoked when you would be reduced to 0 hit points, roll a death saving throw, or die outright. A free action requiring no reaction or consciousness. Once resolved, the character permanently dies.

---

### 1. Spiritstorm Manifestation

*The spirits you've bound rage free, transforming the battlefield into a swirling storm.*

**Effect:**
- Create a 40-foot radius Spiritstorm centred on yourself lasting 1 minute.
- Enemies make a CON save (DC = 10 + PB + WIS modifier): failure = 8d10 force damage + blinded for 1 minute; success = half damage, not blinded.
- At the start of each enemy's turn in the area: 2d10 damage (fire, cold, lightning, or necrotic — your choice).
- Your allies in the area gain resistance to all damage and advantage on saving throws.

---

### 2. Totemic Ascendance

*You become the spiritual anchor of your Totems, empowering your allies as you fall.*

**Effect:**
- Your active Totems remain on the battlefield and cannot be destroyed or deactivated for 5 minutes.
- All allies within 60 feet of your Totems gain: temp HP equal to 4 × your Shaman level; advantage on all attack rolls, saving throws, and ability checks while within Totem range; regain 1d10 HP at the start of their turn if within range of a Totem.
- Immediately activate all of your Totems as part of this action, affecting all valid creatures within range.

---

### 3. Ancestral Fury

*Your spirit merges with the ancestral warriors who came before you.*

**Effect:**
- For the next minute, manifest as a spectral warrior of pure spiritual energy.
- Become incorporeal; gain fly speed of 60 feet.
- Make a melee spell attack against any number of creatures within 30 feet as part of this action — each hit deals 6d10 radiant or necrotic damage (your choice).
- Enemies within 30 feet at the start of their turn make a WIS save (DC = 10 + PB + WIS modifier) or be frightened until end of their next turn.
- When the effect ends, unleash a final burst dealing 4d10 force damage to all creatures of your choice within 30 feet.

| Last Stand Option | Effect | Best Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Spiritstorm Manifestation** | 40-ft storm; 8d10 + blind enemies; ally resistance + advantage. | Controlling the battlefield. |
| **Totemic Ascendance** | Totems persist; allies gain temp HP, advantage, and healing each turn. | Sustaining the party. |
| **Ancestral Fury** | Spectral avatar; multi-target strikes; frighten enemies; final burst. | High damage and terror. |

## Class Reactions

Shamans have access to reactions that protect allies through spirit guardianship and totemic power. These reactions use your **Proficiency Bonus pool**, refreshing on short or long rest.

### Spirit Ward (2nd Level)

**Trigger:** An ally within 30 feet is hit.
**Effect:** Your Totem spirit shields them. Reduce damage by PB + WIS modifier, and the attacker makes a WIS save or be **Marked by Spirits** (next attack against them has advantage).

---

### Spirit Intercession (6th Level)

**Trigger:** An allied creature within 15 feet of you is hit by an attack.
**Effect:** Reduce the damage that creature takes by an amount equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum reduction of 1). Spirits interpose themselves between your ally and harm.

Uses equal to your Proficiency Bonus per short rest.

---

### Totemic Shield (5th Level)

**Trigger:** An ally within 30 feet is hit.
**Effect:** One of your Totems flares, granting resistance to the attack's damage type and temp HP equal to PB + WIS modifier. The attacker makes a WIS save (DC = 8 + PB + WIS) or be Marked by Spirits.

At 11th level, also grant the ally +2 AC until start of your next turn.
