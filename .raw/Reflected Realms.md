# ✧ Reflected Realms of the Weave
_“Every leyline casts a shadow. Every spell, a dream.”_

The **Reflected Realms** are not other planes in the traditional sense—they are **living reflections** of the Prime World, shaped by its ley lines, glyphs, and the breath of the Weave. Where the Prime World is bounded by structure and cycle, these mirrored realms are **unbound, reactive, and volatile**.

They represent what magic might become without balance: **the dream and the silence**, the **echo and the void**.

---

## 🌙 The Luminous Echo — The Dream of the Weave

The **Feywild**—known more accurately as the **[[Luminous Echo]]**—is the **Weave's dream-state**: a realm of untethered potential, emotion-bound creation, and recursive story. Magic here is fluid and breathes with intention, memory, and belief.

### Characteristics:
- **Time loops, stories repeat, memories gain mass**
- **Emotions shape reality**, fuelled by Personal Glyph resonance
- Ley lines exist as **mirrored exaggerations** of their Prime counterparts
- Contains **[[the Unstable Heart]]**, nexus of Wild Magic

### Laws of the Luminous Echo:
- [[Law of Reflection]]  
- [[Law of Belief]]  
- [[Law of Exaggeration]]  
- [[Law of Recurrence]]

This is a place where **fate is rewritten through whim**, and nothing is ever truly lost—only transformed.

---

## 🕳 The Umbral Veil — The Silence of the Weave

_“This is not death. This is what memory becomes when it is unloved.”_

Where the **Luminous Echo** is the Weave’s dream, the **Umbral Veil** is its grief. It is not simply a shadow of the Prime World—it is a record of what **could not be**, what **should not remain**, and what the Weave itself would **forget if it could**. This is the **anti-breath** of creation, the realm of entropy given form.

The Umbral Veil forms when **ley lines collapse** and Personal Glyphs are **frayed but not erased**. In this liminal death of meaning, time becomes a memory loop, identity becomes a burden, and even gods are mirrored as hollowed truths.

### Characteristics:

- **Time collapses inward**, becoming recursive, shattered, or stagnant
- **Magic decays** into glyph-fragments and spells that echo with unintended repetition
- **Memories live independently**, forming echo-beings or dreamless specters
- **The Hollow** lies at its heart—a dead glyph well, where Severance eats reality

---

### ☠️ Laws of the Umbral Veil

Like its luminous twin, the Umbral Veil operates on metaphysical laws—but these are **fraying rules**, not binding truths:

- **[[Law of Fracture]]**  
- **[[Law of Silence]]**  
- **[[Law of Reversal]]**  
- **[[Law of Recurrence]] (Inverted)**
