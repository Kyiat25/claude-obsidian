## 🧠 What Is This?

The **[[FEARS Character Sheet]]** is a complete reimagining of how you engage with your character—built for a world where reality twists, sanity shatters, and combat isn’t just about numbers.  
It introduces **seven new systems**—mental, narrative, and tactical—that shape **not just what your character does**, but **how they survive**.

This sheet:

- Tracks **mental integrity** alongside hit points.
- Makes **combat dynamic, precise, and brutal**.
- Lets you **bend fate itself**—but at a cost.

## 🧾 Sheet Sections Overview

### 🧠 Mental Resilience (NEW!)

- **[[Sanity]]** (SAN) Attribute– Your mind’s shield against horrors.
- **[[Focus]]** (FOC) Attribute – Your control in the face of chaos.
- **[[Corruption]] Score (CS)** – How far the Eldritch has infected you.
- **Mental State Trackers**: Stable → Frayed → Deranged → Broken.

### ⚔️ Dynamic Combat (DCS)

- **[[Evasions]]** (EV) – Roll to dodge incoming attacks instead of relying on AC.
- **[[Damage Reductions]]** (DR) – Each body part has its own armour rating.
- **[[Called shotss]]** – Aim for head, arms, legs, or torso with specific effects.
- **Combat Flow Tracker** – Visual step-by-step reference.

### 🛡️ Reactions & Tactical Play

- Use **free reactions** by **intentionally taking a hit** to counterattack, disarm, bash, or block.
- Shields are active tools, not just passive bonuses.
- Reaction options vary by class—look for synergies!

### 🎲 Narrative Manipulation

- **Whisper Points** (WP) – Spend to reshape events, retcon, or rewrite reality (but beware: corruption and memory distortion) via [[01_Rules/06_Optional/The Whisper Engine]].
- **[[Luck]] State** – Track streaks of 1s or 20s to gain **Blessed** or **Cursed Luck**.
- **[[Memory]], [[Occult]] & [[Streetwise]] Skills** – Survive glyphic dreams, haunted rituals, the criminal world and forbidden truths.

## 🎮 Quick Conversion Guide (5E to FEARS)

Already have a 5E character? Here’s what to add:

- Roll or calculate **[[Sanity]]** and **[[Focus]]** (like ability scores).
- Track **Evasion** instead of AC: `1d20 + DEX + PROF`.
- Replace AC bonus from armour with **DR per body part**.
- Add **Whisper Points**: `3 + Focus Mod + ½ Sanity Mod`.
- Begin tracking **Corruption Score**, **Mental State**, **Reaction Options**, and **Called Shot Effects**.

## 🎒 What to Bring to the Table

You’ll need:

- A **mental tracker** (for Sanity, Focus, Corruption)
- A **reaction reminder** (mark when you choose to forgo evasion)
- A **body map** for DR (head, torso, arms, legs)
- Your **Whisper Point pool** and how you’ve spent it
- A willingness to let your character **fail, fracture, and evolve**
