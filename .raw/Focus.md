# Focus: The Mind's Blade Against the Weave

## A Chronicle from the Tactician's Codex

_Steel cuts flesh. Fire scars the world. But Focus? Focus cuts through chaos._

Where others waver before the arcane storm or the scream of something that should not be, those with Focus remain. It is not merely mental endurance; it is mental control. Where Sanity is your shield against horror, Focus is the sword you wield in its face.

> _"The world frays at the edges. Your mind must not."_

---

## The Essence of Focus

Picture your will as a finely tuned instrument—capable of resisting illusions, channelling unstable magic, or slicing through distraction like a scalpel. **Focus** is not passive. It is **precision under pressure**.

It represents:

- **Cognitive clarity** in supernatural chaos
- **Concentration during glyphic overload**
- **Resistance to spell feedback, magical surges, and psychic assaults**

**Focus** is a core attribute like Strength or Intelligence. It reflects how **disciplined, stable, and tactically cantered** your mind is in high-risk situations.

---

## 🧠 **Focus Score**

- Starts between **8–20**, using rolled generation.
- Point buy = 10+ Class primary stat modifier/spellcasting modifier + con modifier.
- **Focus Modifier** = (Focus Score - 10) ÷ 2 (rounded down)
- Used in **Focus Checks**, **Focus Saves**, and as a buffer against **magical instability**, **concentration disruption**, and **Corruption escalation**

---
## 🎲 **When to Roll Focus**

Focus is tested when characters face:

- **Wild Magic** surges or unstable spellcasting
- **Concentration under stress** (injury, motion, overlapping effects)
- **Casting in [[Dead Magic Zones]] or [[Wild Magic Zones]]**
- **Defending against illusions, charm, or psychic domination**
- **Operating Glyphs of unstable or unknown nature**

> _"The glyph pulses. You hold it together. Barely."_

---
## 📉 **Focus Disruption & Failure**

Failing a Focus Save can cause:

- Spell collapse
- Glyph feedback (+1 Corruption Score)
- Wild surge event (GM-determined effect)
- Forced [[Sanity]] Save if exposure is Eldritch in origin

---

## ⚠️ **Focus Thresholds & Effects**

|**Focus Score**|**State**|**Effect**|
|---|---|---|
|13–20|Steady|No effect|
|9–12|Strained|Disadvantage on Focus saves under active spellcasting or chaos exposure|
|5–8|Fragmenting|Disadvantage on all Focus checks and saves; concentration breaks on damage|
|1–4|Splintered|Auto-fail on Wild Magic control; glyphs backlash on touch (DC 15 Sanity)|
|0|Collapsed|Cannot cast; must make Sanity Save or gain Corruption + Trauma|

## 🛠️ **Focus Recovery Options**

|**Recovery Method**|**Effect**|
|---|---|
|**Short Rest + Meditation**|Regain 1 Focus if no spellcasting during rest|
|**Long Rest**|Regain 1d4 Focus if completed in a stable magical zone|
|**Ritual Grounding**|Ritual involving your Personal Glyph stabilizes Focus (+2)|
|**Glyph Anchor**|Once per day, resist one Focus Save with advantage|

> **Save DC** = 8 + CR or Zone Rating + modifiers

|Event Type|Base DC|
|---|---|
|Chaotic Spell Discharge|10–12|

|Eldritch Spell Glyph|16–18|

---

## 🧩 **Interplay with Other Stats**

- **[[Sanity]]**: Failing Focus saves in Eldritch zones may trigger Sanity saves or Corruption gain.
- **[[Corruption]]**: Certain tiers of corruption impose Disadvantage on Focus saves.
- **Class Synergy**:
    - **Monks, Magi, Investigators, Psions**: Excel at Focus.
    - **Sorcerers & Warlocks**: May suffer more Focus failures due to internal magical instability.

---

## 🧠 At 0 Focus: Collapse of the Mental Core

The character becomes **[[Burned out]]**
- Cannot cast or concentrate
- Must roll Sanity Save vs. DC 18 or gain 1d4 Corruption Score
- Personal Glyph becomes unstable until Focus is restored

# 🔗 Focus and Corruption

> "When your hand fails, the glyph stutters. When your mind fails, the world breaks."

|Focus State|Corruption Effect|
|---|---|
|**Strained**|Failing a Focus Save in a Wild Zone = +1 Corruption|
|**Fragmenting**|Spellcasting in unstable glyph fields always triggers Disruption Table|
|**Splintered**|Auto +1 Corruption when in proximity to Eldritch glyph or anchor|
|**Collapsed**|Set CS to 15 or higher (GM discretion); Personal Glyph may fracture|

---
## 🎲 Focus Chain Reaction

1. **Spell cast in unstable zone** triggers **Focus Save**
2. **Fail** → roll on Disruption Table, gain +1 CS
3. If already at Major Corruption, Disruption escalates to mutation or glyph echo
4. Sanity Save may be required depending on glyph effect

---

## 🧠 Focus vs. Sanity vs. Corruption – How They Interlock

|   |   |   |   |   |
|---|---|---|---|---|
|Attribute|Role|Mechanically Defends Against|Triggers|Result of Failure|
|**Focus**|Mental clarity and resistance under pressure|Illusions, charm, psychic magic, magical collapse|Concentration disruption, Arcane Rifts, Wild Magic|Loss of spell control, confusion, spell feedback|

---
## 🔁 Gameplay Loop Summary (Focus-Centric)

1. **You cast a spell in a Dead or Wild Magic Zone**.
2. **Roll Focus Save** to control the chaos.
    - **Fail** = roll on the Disruption Table, possibly gain +1 Corruption Score.
3. If exposed to Eldritch influence, **Sanity Save** may follow.
    - **Fail** = gain additional Corruption.
4. As **Corruption Score** increases, Focus Saves become harder or penalized.
5. Dropping to **Collapsed Focus** forces a Sanity Save or triggers narrative loss.

## 🛠 Optional Focus System Improvements

- **High Focus scores (16+)** may buffer Sanity decay: ignore first Corruption from failed spell surge.
- Use **Corruption Tier DCs** to set Focus Save targets:
    - Minor Corruption area: DC 12
    - Major: DC 15
    - Eldritch: DC 18+
- **Sanity degradation** could cause Focus strain:
    
    > "When Sanity drops from Frayed → Unravelling, suffer Disadvantage on next Focus Save."
