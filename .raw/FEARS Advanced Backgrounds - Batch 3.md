# FEARS Advanced Backgrounds - Batch 3 (Complete)

## Overview

This batch covers the remaining gaps in FEARS backgrounds, including:

- **Magic-focused** (Apprentice Spellcaster, Alchemist, Rune Carver, Diviner, Thread Merchant)
- **Druidic/Nature** (Druidic Initiate, Beastmaster, Forester, Herbalist)
- **Urban Specialists** (Architect, Scribe, Lamplighter)
- **Social/Performance** (Herald, Minstrel)
- **Criminal Specialists** (Fence, Forger, Poisoner)
- **Working Class** (Miner, Fisher, Farmer, Skinner)
- **Specialized Religious** (Inquisitor, Pilgrim)
- **FEARS-Specific** (Pattern Keeper, Fracture Scout, Corruption Warden)

## MAGIC-FOCUSED BACKGROUNDS

### 1. APPRENTICE SPELLCASTER

_You studied at a formal magical institution, learning the structured approach to arcane arts._

#### Rank 1: Junior Apprentice

You've been accepted into a magical academy or taken as apprentice to a master wizard.

**Skill Proficiency:** Arcana, History  
**Tool Proficiency:** Calligrapher's supplies  
**Language:** One exotic language (Draconic, Celestial, Infernal, Primordial, Sylvan, or Abyssal)  
**Equipment:** Spellbook (blank), calligrapher's supplies, set of common robes, academic insignia, belt pouch with 10 gp

**Feature - Academic Access:** You have access to your academy's library and can:

- Research spells and magical phenomena (advantage on Arcana checks in your library)
- Borrow common spell scrolls for study (must return within 1 week)
- Attend lectures and demonstrations
- Seek advice from instructors on magical matters

**Holdings:**

- Shared dormitory room in academy
- Access to basic laboratory
- Mentor or supervising instructor
- Library card and restricted access to magical texts

**Progression:** Successfully cast a difficult spell, complete thesis on magical theory, or assist master with important research

---

#### Rank 2: Advanced Apprentice

You've proven your competence and earned greater trust.

**Holdings:**

- Private study room
- Small stipend for research (15 gp/month)
- Access to better laboratory equipment
- Permission to copy 2 spells per month from academy library
- Network of 5-8 fellow apprentices
- Can borrow uncommon spell scrolls

**Feature Expansion - Scholarly Research:** Your magical knowledge deepens:

- Can identify spell effects automatically (DC 15 or lower)
- Advantage on checks to understand new magical phenomena
- Can spend downtime researching to gain temporary expertise in specific magical topic
- Know locations of other magical libraries and academies
- Can recognize different magical traditions and schools

**Progression:** Publish magical research, discover new spell variation, or defend thesis before magical council

---

#### Rank 3: Journeyman Mage

You've completed your core studies and can practice independently.

**Holdings:**

- Personal tower room or sanctum
- Good stipend or teaching income (60 gp/month)

- 3-5 junior apprentices you instruct
- Access to restricted magical texts

**Feature Expansion - Magical Authority:** Your expertise is recognized:

- Can identify any spell being cast (as _counterspell_ identification)

#### Rank 4: Archmage / Master of the Arcane

**Rank 3 → 4:** 12-20 sessions, legendary accomplishment

### Downtime Integration

Each background should provide:

- **Income** appropriate to rank
- **Resources** players can leverage
- **Story hooks** from holdings and contacts
- **Challenges** to holdings that drive adventure

### Balance with Class Features

- Backgrounds provide **narrative power**, not combat power
- High-rank backgrounds open doors, don't replace class abilities
- Influence and resources are tools for creative problem-solving
- Some challenges become trivial (by design)
