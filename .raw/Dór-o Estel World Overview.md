# DM LORE OVERVIEW: DÓR-O ESTEL

---

## 🌌 WORLD STRUCTURE

**World Name:** [[Dór-o Estel]]  

**Cosmic Foundation:** Built upon the Weave, a metaphysical lattice shaped by elemental Threads (Earth, Fire, Air, Water, Spirit) and subtly governed by invisible forces like the Whispering Dark and the Form Glyphs.  

**Ages:** The world is in the **Fourth Age**, currently in the **8th Cycle**, with the canonical date being:

- **487-03-18** (6th Day of the 8th Cycle)
- **Month:** Celestialdawn
- **Thread Alignment:** Spirit (third consecutive alignment, known as "The Third Thread")

---
## 🌐 PLANAR & MAGICAL COSMOLOGY

- **Primary Glyphs**: Define reality, unknowable to mortals.
- **[[Secondary Glyphs]]**: Mortal access to magic. Used by scholars, priests, and sorcerers to manipulate the Weave.
- **[[Wild Magic Zones]]**: Areas where the Weave is frayed or torn, often appearing near corrupted leylines.
- **Leylines & Anchors**: Each Loom-City is built over a leyline nexus and protected by a World Anchor.

---
### 🐼 PANTHEONS & DIVINE STRUCTURE

**Elemental Pantheons:**

- **Fire:** Soltharion, Ignara
- **Earth:** Gaeldros, Ishara, Thamur
- **Air:** Thirian
- **Water:** Vaelora, Marethis, Nereus
- **Spirit:** Aelys, Nyzara

**Eldritch Pantheon:**

- Composed of Eight Being-level gods; rarely cooperate. Each has independent cults.
- Eldritch Moons (12 known) appear irregularly; their alignment triggers magical calamities.

**The Faith of the Bound Pattern:**

- Dominant religion linking all pantheons under a cosmological structure of the Weave.
- Five sacred centers, each led by a cult head from an elemental pantheon.

---
### ✨ RACES & RELIGIONS

- **Elves:** Woven Reverence, memory-based soul religion 
- **Dwarves:** Deep Law and the Stone-Song Codex 
- **Halflings:** Joy-Wild, rooted in laughter, stories, and death-defiance 
- **Orcs:** Ledger of the Scarred Line, honor through trials 
- **Dragonborn:** Burning Concord, caste and fire-god worship 
- **Drow:** The Ashen Web, absolute rule of [[Vel'Zyren]] 
- **Gnomes:** Borrow gods from other pantheons, highly magical
---
## 🏡 LOOM-CITIES

Each Loom-City contains:

- A **World Anchor**
- A ruling **Threadbearer** (changes with the Cycle)
- Districts attuned to the five Threads

**Current Active Loom-Cities:**

- **Kel Varyan** (Capital of an empire, Water legacy, arcane-political hub)
- **Thar Vornhal** (Judicial & Earth-aligned)
- **Aeravae** (Water-aligned, floating and underground)
- **Veltharyn** (Currently Fire-aligned, mountainous dwarf-dominant)
- **Selveran** (Skywood-connected, spiritual and elven influence)

**Fallen Loom-City:** 
- Ashkarra — destroyed by a triple Spirit Thread alignment, now an Eldritch breach site.

---
### ⚡ CURRENT ERA TENSIONS

- **Trust in the Weave is declining** due to multiple recent Cycle failures.
- **Spirit Thread dominance** causes prophetic instability and memory fractures.
- **Eldritch Moons** are appearing more frequently.
- **Inter-city politics** strained; some Loom-Cities contest Threadbearer legitimacy.
- **Underdark forces**, including the Drow and deeper Eldritch civilisations, grow in aggression.

# 🌏 CONTINENTS & MAJOR REGIONS

### 🌏 CONTINENTS

**1. Kerythra**

- **Size:** Largest continent
- **Biomes:** Mountains, ancient forests, tundra, rivers, plains, old deserts
- **Key Sites:** Thar Vornhal (Loom-City), several World Trees
- **Cultures:** Dwarves (Stone-Song Codex), humans, highland orcs, earth-aligned temples

**2. Iriveth Orre**

- **Type:** Island-continent
- **Biomes:** Jungle, aquifer-caverns, sacred springs
- **Key Sites:** Aeravae (Loom-City), underground water sanctums
- **Cultures:** Water-priests, spiritualist elves, tide-echo cults

**3. Mirror Chain**

- **Type:** Volcanic island arc
- **Biomes:** Volcanic highlands, coastal reefs, spiritual tide zones
- **Key Sites:** Selveran (Loom-City)
- **Cultures:** Mixed elven-human, spirit-weavers, volcanic seers

**4. Vashkora**

- **Type:** Storm-wracked landmass
- **Biomes:** Highlands, ruins, storm plains, abyssal coasts
- **Key Sites:** Ruins of Dragonborn dominance, hidden sanctums
- **Cultures:** Isolated Dragonborn clans, weather-bound mages, ruined empire fragments

**5. Elarion**

- **Type:** Southern expanse
- **Biomes:** Grasslands, salt flats, light jungle, volcanic ridges, world tree groves
- **Key Sites:** Kel Varyan (Loom-City), Veltharyn (Loom-City), sacred groves, corrupted World Tree, Halfling ranges
- **Cultures:** Halfling clans (Joy-Wild), wild elves, forest exiles, dwarves of Veltharyn, Kel Varyan imperials, spirit pilgrims
- **Notes:** Elarion houses multiple Loom-Cities and World Trees, serving as the heartland of the Faith’s power while also sheltering ancient elven traditions, Halfling sanctuaries, and Drow incursions through corrupted leyline roots.
