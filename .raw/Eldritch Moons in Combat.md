## 🌌 What Are Eldritch Moons?

Eldritch Moons are mysterious celestial anomalies that appear unpredictably across the skies of the world. Each one is a **manifestation of unknowable forces**, often tied to the **Whisper Engine**, **Fractures**, and forbidden knowledge. Their presence twists magic, alters sanity, and invokes latent truths.

Their appearance signals:

- Weave instability
- Sanity pressure and increased Whisper Points
- Mutation of spells, glyphs, and divine contact
- Strange boons, omens, or curses

---
## 📜 Known Eldritch Moons

There are **twelve known Eldritch Moons**, each with unique effects:

|Name|Effect (Examples)|
|---|---|
|Howling Moon|Increases Fracture DC by +2; all screams heard become distorted echoes|
|Shattered Moon|Glyphs misfire on doubles; memory magic is unreliable|
|Scorching Moon|Fire spells deal double damage but trigger Sanity Saves|
|Vacuous Moon|All Insight checks fail; lies become indistinguishable from truth|
|Glowering Moon|Enemies gain temporary HP each round while visible under its light|
|Glacial Moon|Movement halved; Fracture effects linger twice as long|
|Slumbering Moon|Long Rests require Sanity Save to complete|
|Yellow Crown|Dreams become prophecy; Prophetic characters gain false or inverted visions|
|Creeping Tarlight|All natural healing halved; poisons gain sentience|
|Krakenlight|Oceans whisper names; Fracture rolls summon hallucinated deep-sea events|
|Sanguine Eclipse|Blood spilled in moonlight grants +1 Whisper Point|
|Blood Moon of Rebirth|Dead monsters may rise as corrupted versions within 1 minute|

> DM may add more Moons as needed. These twelve are canon in most FEARS campaigns.

---
## 🧠 Moon Effects in Combat

|Effect Type|Rule Mechanic Impact|
|---|---|
|Sanity Modifier|Sanity Save DCs increase or are required more often|
|Whisper Generation|Actions may trigger Whisper Point gain|
|Magic Warping|Glyph effects shift, misfire, or amplify unpredictably|
|Environmental Twist|Terrain or lighting changes in response to the Moon|

Each Moon also has a **Surge Trigger**—specific event that calls upon its unique trait during combat.

---
## 🧷 Optional Toggles

|Toggle Name|Description|
|---|---|
|**Dual Moons**|Rare nights when two Eldritch Moons appear, compounding effects|
|**Moon Prophecy**|Characters marked by a Moon experience visions or compulsions|
|**Cycle of the Twelfth**|Every 12th full moon opens a portal or ritual site tied to Eldritch power|
|**Tide of Madness**|Moons affect all creatures—monsters may also Fracture or gain Whispers|
