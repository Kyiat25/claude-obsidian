# Pyrrithis, The Ashen Oracle

*"The fire does not lie. It only shows what you are too weak to accept."*

**Domains**: prophecy, visions, doomed knowledge. **Symbol**: a skull wreathed in smoke, with burning eyes. **Worshiped by**: seers, oracles, those cursed with knowledge of the end. **Allies**: [[Zerah]], Vaithis, Telys. **Enemies**: Nytheris, Kaelith, [[Soltharion]].

The god of truths no one wants to hear — his visions are never comforting, only fire, destruction, and the knowledge that all things burn in the end. Followers are feared and shunned, since hearing his words means knowing one's fate is sealed. Myth holds he was once a mortal seer gifted with sight by the gods, until he foresaw the day they would all fall and was burned alive to silence his voice — his whispers remain, forever warning those who dare listen.
