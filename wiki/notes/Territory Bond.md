# Territory Bond

[[Warden]]'s proposed narrative currency, also called **Guard Oath** (see [[Narrative Identity]]'s status note — this is a design proposal, not confirmed rules). Built by repeatedly defending a site, people, or path. The mechanical embodiment of the Warden's core pressure: an inability to abandon what they've claimed to defend.
