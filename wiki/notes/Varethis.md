# Varethis, The One Who Unravels

*"Not all destinies are meant to be completed. Some must be undone."*

**Domains**: the breaking of the Weave, forgotten names, the force that ends what should not exist. **Worshiped by**: those who erase, those who remove, those who bring endings.

Invoked when a name must be erased from history, when a story must be unwritten. Where [[Astralis]] ensures knowledge is forgotten, Varethis ensures destinies themselves can be unmade.
