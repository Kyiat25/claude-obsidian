# Dead Magic Zone

`status: excluded`. One of the four possible outcomes of a Critical Spell Hit on [[Unstable Magic Zones]] (the natural-1 Focus Check result). A cursed or collapsed area where magic simply does not work: all spells and cantrips automatically fail, magic items stop functioning, and portals/summoning/teleportation are impossible. The Weave itself is absent.

## Forcing magic anyway

Possible but severe. Requires a [[Sanity]] saving throw scaling by spell level (Cantrip DC 10; 1st-2nd DC 12-14; 3rd-4th DC 16-18, plus automatic exhaustion; 5th-6th DC 20-22; 7th-9th DC 24-28). Forcing a 3rd-level-or-higher spell causes 1-4 levels of exhaustion regardless of success. Each forced spell also adds a stack of **Mark of the Rift**, making the caster progressively easier for magical predators to track and hunt. Failure brings short-term madness plus additional exhaustion on top of the Mark stacking.
