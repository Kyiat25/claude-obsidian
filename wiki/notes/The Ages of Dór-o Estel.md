# The Ages of Dór-o Estel

The setting's five-epoch historical structure. Note: this page synthesizes a clean narrative-Ages source with a separate "Quick Reference" summary of a much larger (60,000+ word) master timeline document that was not read in full for this pass — treat the specific campaign-arc dates and character details below as a partial, lower-confidence layer on top of the Ages structure itself.

## The Loomless Time (pre-Age, mythic, duration unknowable)

Begins with **Astralor's Forge**, the first strike awakening Fire, Earth, Water, Air, and Spirit — the gods formless, proud, and unknowing, shaping the world through the **War of Elemental Becoming**. Soltharion (here also spelled Soltharis) rises to claim all change as his right, and the age ends with his sealing within the Sun by Pyrris and Vaeldros — the first divine punishment. The Weave stabilizes; memory becomes possible.

## The First Age — Discovery (~2700 years, 4500–1800 years ago)

Gods discover memory, consequence, glyphs, and the Weave's structure; mortal awareness slowly awakens. Ends with the **Trial and Exile of Vel'Zyren** by Gaeldros, severing her from all Threads — over the following 105 years she creates the Drow in secret.

## The Second Age — Heroes and Rebellion (700 years, 1800–1100 years ago)

Begins with the Unleashing of the Drow and divine fracturing; mortal heroes, glyph-binders, and divine challengers rise; the pantheons splinter. Vel'Zyren and Soltharion cults rise, Eldritch whispers begin, and the age ends with the **first Eldritch Moon** appearing in the sky.

## The Third Age — Collapse and Rebirth (1100 years, 1100–0 years ago)

Begins with the founding of [[The Faith of the Bound Pattern]] and the first Loom-Cities; civilization stabilizes under Threadbearers and World Anchors. The Dragonborn Empire rises under the Cult of Soltharion, capital [[Ashkarra]]; Eldritch Moons multiply, and Ashkarra endures three consecutive Spirit Thread alignments. Ends with the **Day of Twelve Shadows** (all Eldritch Moons aligning), [[Ashkarra]]'s fall, the First Hollow Breach, and the founding of [[Aeravae]] in the aftermath.

## The Fourth Age — Unraveling (487 years, ongoing — present day)

Begins with the Fifth Cycle under the Spirit Thread. Per this page's synthesis, the current cycle is only 18 days old with no new Threadbearers found; the age is defined by instability, faltering prophecy, and encroaching horror, its outcome genuinely unresolved — "either the Pattern holds, or unravels completely."

### The Unraveling period in detail (per the Master Timeline Quick Reference)

A separate, more granular timeline source dates Ashkarra's fall to **Year -95** and details a 95-year "Unraveling" period between the catastrophe and Aeravae's completion at **Year 0**: Phase 1, absolute chaos (a quarter of global population dies, magic fails unpredictably); Phase 2, three catastrophic failed attempts to build a replacement World Anchor (~110,000 combined deaths); Phase 3, "dark stabilization" around four overloaded, dimmed Anchors; Phase 4, desperate cross-faction cooperation under a forcing prophetic vision; Phase 5, Aeravae's five-year construction as a hybrid World Anchor/World Tree, activating at Year 0.

### Present-day statistics (Year 487, per the same source)

Four of five World Anchors currently sit dimmed (no living Threadbearer); only Aeravae's remains stable. Eldritch Moon frequency is reported at roughly 300% over baseline. Of an estimated 150–200 original World Trees, 73–75 are said to survive. This source also names specific campaign-arc material (Kethyr Flameheart, Veshra Ashwalker, warlock-pact-extended lifespans past the ~300-year Dragonborn norm, and five numbered campaign arcs converging in Year 487) that reads as GM campaign-prep content layered on top of the setting's baseline history — flagged here as such rather than treated as equally-weighted setting canon.
