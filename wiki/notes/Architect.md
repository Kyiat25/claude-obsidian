# Architect (Engineer) — Rank 1-4

*"You design and build the structures that define civilization."* Batch 3, Urban Specialist category. Source dual-names this "Architect / Engineer"; filed under the first name. Mundane construction and structural design — no overlap stated with [[Inventor]]'s magical Specialisation Upgrades or gadgetry.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Draftsman | Structural Assessment | Design an accepted structure, identify a flaw that prevents disaster, or complete a challenging build |
| 2 | Licensed Architect | Master Builder | Design a significant building, prevent structural failure, or innovate a new technique |
| 3 | Master Architect | Visionary Designer | Design a legendary building, revolutionize architecture, or save a city with an engineering solution |
| 4 | Grand Architect | Master of Construction | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Draftsman

**Skill Proficiency**: Investigation, Perception. **Tool Proficiency**: mason's tools, carpenter's tools. **Equipment**: mason's or carpenter's tools, measuring tools, 10 sheets of drafting paper, chalk, 15 gp.

**Feature — Structural Assessment**: assess a building's structural integrity; advantage on Investigation checks to find hidden architectural features; estimate construction costs and time; know basic engineering principles; identify load-bearing elements.

**Holdings**: a desk in an architect's office; access to building plans and references; drafting tools; a mentor architect.

## Rank 2: Licensed Architect

**Holdings**: a personal office; income from projects (40 gp/month); a complete set of quality drafting tools; a portfolio of completed projects; 3-5 workers you regularly employ; contacts with material suppliers; a license to practice architecture.

**Feature Expansion — Master Builder**: create detailed architectural plans for any standard structure; advantage on checks to oversee construction; identify weak points in any structure; know how to fortify or strengthen buildings; once per week, modify plans mid-construction without starting over.

## Rank 3: Master Architect

**Holdings**: an architectural firm with staff; major income (120 gp/month); multiple ongoing projects; 10-15 workers and junior architects; a regional reputation; contracts with nobility and government; your buildings are landmarks.

**Feature Expansion — Visionary Designer**: design complex structures (fortresses, cathedrals, palaces); identify and fix any structural problem; advantage on urban-planning checks; once per month, identify the perfect solution to an "impossible" building challenge; your designs are studied by other architects.

## Rank 4: Grand Architect

**Holdings**: multiple firms across cities; excellent income (400 gp/month); dozens of ongoing projects; 50+ workers and architects under you; a royal appointment as chief architect; your buildings define the age; monuments bear your name.

**Feature — Master of Construction**: design anything within fantasy physics (floating towers, impossible bridges); automatic success on architectural checks (DC 25 or lower); once per year, design a structure that becomes a Wonder of the Age; your presence improves any construction project; see flaws in any building instantly. Kingdoms compete for your services, your techniques become standard practice, and you may incorporate magical elements into your designs.
