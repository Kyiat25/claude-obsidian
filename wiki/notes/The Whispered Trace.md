# The Whispered Trace

*"It was just a shadow. But now it's a voice. And now it's yours."*

**Type**: Memory/Haunting Thought. **Origin**: appears near collapsed libraries, failed prophecy temples, or graveyards of gods. **Behavior**: begins as a stray thought, then repeats something you didn't mean to think — eventually takes on your voice, then speaks independently. **Effect**: steals internal monologue, making victims doubt which thoughts are their own. **Echo power**: if it convinces a victim to act, it may possess them for a single moment, performing a glyph or word they would never willingly speak.

An **Echo** — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a manifestation of an erased or forgotten idea.
