# Consequence Engine

The design principle behind FEARS' claim that "every roll, every decision carries weight." Named in the source as the **Consequence Engine**, though it reads as a philosophy statement rather than a distinct mechanical subsystem (unlike [[Whisper Points]] or [[FEARS Combat System]], no discrete resource or roll is attached to it).

## Stated principles

- Critical failures generate narrative opportunities rather than dead-end "you fail" outcomes
- Success is framed as "a story waiting to unfold," not just a pass/fail number
- Player creativity is described as "the most powerful stat on your character sheet"

## Editorial note

This page exists mainly to capture FEARS' stated design values, since they recur as justification across the other mechanics ([[FEARS Combat System]]'s Active Defense, [[Whisper Points]]' narrative reshaping, [[Unstable Magic Zones]]' critical-hit variance). If a mechanics source later formalizes this into an actual subsystem (e.g. a complication table), update this page from `developing` to `mature`.
