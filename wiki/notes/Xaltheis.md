# Xaltheis, The Hungering Foam

*"The tide takes and takes. And still, it is not full."*

**Domains**: devouring waves, the water that consumes, the unquenchable ocean. **Symbol**: a wave curling inward, swallowing itself. **Worshiped by**: feared by sailors, recognised by those who watch the erosion of land. **Rivals**: [[Syralis]], [[Kaemor]], [[Marethis]]. **Allies**: [[Voraxis]], Rythos, Morgathis.

The hunger of the ocean — the tide that eats away at land, why shorelines vanish over time. Settlers near the sea leave offerings to appease him; legends blame lost island kingdoms on tribute unpaid.
