# Beneath-the-Face

*"There was something trying to be you. It almost succeeded."*

**Origin**: a fragment of a mortal erased by [[Nhal'Zereth]] but *almost* remembered by someone. **Appearance**: a person-shaped outline with no features, half-formed voice, eyes that are always almost yours. **Effect**: attaches to those who mourn the forgotten, causing dreams of conversations that never happened and memories of events no one else recalls. **Risk**: if one grieves for it too long, it replaces the mourner's face and begins using their body to insert itself into history.

An **Unfinished** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a being that tried to form and failed but still persists.
