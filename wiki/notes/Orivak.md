# Orivak, The Rune-Bound Sentinel

**Domain**: leyline guardianship, runes, magical equilibrium. **Symbol**: a standing stone marked with glowing runes. **Worshiped by**: druids, geomancers, rune-keepers, those who police arcane excess.

Oversees the flow of primal energy through the earth and binds the [[Leylines]] into stability — born at the first leyline crossing, standing there ever since; when the stones sing, it is Orivak breathing. The most active defender of the Lock, and one of the four gods (with [[Vatharun]], [[Thamur]], and Gaeldros) responsible for [[The Hollow Thread]]'s seal — his specific contribution was binding nearby leylines with anti-collapse runes designed to resist conceptual collapse.
