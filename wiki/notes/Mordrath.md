# Mordrath, The Black Flame

*"One day, the last ember will die. And then, all will be dark."*

**Domains**: death, entropy, finality. **Symbol**: a dying ember in skeletal hands. **Worshiped by**: necromancers, prophets of doom, fatalists. **Enemies**: [[Ignis]] and others.

Doesn't destroy for destruction's sake — destroys because nothing should last forever. Doesn't oppose fire, is simply its inevitable end: waits patiently, feeds on wasted flames, moves unnoticed, since even gods don't wish to acknowledge Fire itself will one day end. Framed as [[The Whispering Dark]]'s patient ally within Fire's own pantheon — every empire burns out, every rebellion fades to ash, every star eventually flickers and dies, and Mordrath doesn't need to fight for a victory already written. [[The Restorer]] offers the counterpoint: fire has always passed from hand to hand across ages, and when the last fire dies, a new hand will find the embers.
