# Streetwise

One of three knowledge skills (with [[Memory]] and [[Occult]]). Lets a character navigate cities, gangs, and political hotspots; find black markets, identify turf, spot setups. Explicitly named as one input to [[Disposition]] — can shift an NPC's attitude from Suspicious to Indifferent, especially while undercover.

## Formula and distinction from Insight/Investigation (from a dedicated `Streetwise.md` source)

**Streetwise Check** = `1d20 + (WIS or CHA, contextual) + Proficiency` — Wisdom for danger-sense and spatial awareness, Charisma for blending in and reading people. Explicitly distinguished from its two nearest-neighbor skills: *"Streetwise spots the con before it's made. Insight figures out why it was made. Investigation finds how it was pulled off."* Common among [[Rogue]], [[Bard]], Urban Rangers, and backgrounds like Urchin/Fence/Informer/Courier.

## Provenance note

Named (without definition) in [[Translation Matrix]]'s summary of [[Welcome to FEARS]] as one of the new skills the 5E-to-FEARS conversion adds. [[A World that Watches]] is the first source to define it — same gap-fill pattern as [[Sanity]] and [[Focus]].
