# Geographical Zones

`status: excluded`. A settlement-tier system formalizing how far a character's notoriety spreads, how fast news travels, and how law responds — driven by [[Wanted Score]], [[Disposition]], and [[Reputation]] together.

## The five tiers

**Hamlet** (~10-15 mile reach, Suspicious default disposition, word-of-mouth only, local elders/custom as law) → **Village** (~20-40 miles, messengers/merchants) → **Town** (~60-80 miles, notices and criers, a Captain/Watch/Council) → **City** (~150-200 miles, fast print/network/magical spread, Guard Bureau/Courts) → **Metropolis** (variable, weeks of travel; instant spread via international relays, spells, or elite informants; Secret Police/State Bureaus). A crime in a Village might reach nearby Towns in a week; a City crime can reach a Metropolis in three days or less.

## Wanted Score integration

Score 1-2 ("Known Troublemaker") only matters in Hamlet/Village; 3-4 ("Lawbreaker") draws regional bounty hunters across Town/Region; 5-6 ("Criminal, Dead or Alive") means guards recognize the character on sight across City/Nation with posted rewards; 7+ ("Enemy of the State") triggers instant detention and elite response at Metropolis/International scale.
