# Vashkora — the Emberbound Continent

The southern continent of Dór-o Estel (~18 million km², population ~22 million), homeland of the fallen Loom-City [[Ashkarra]] and site of the [[Ashkarra|First Hollow Breach]] 47 years ago. Its name, from the Dragonborn imperial tongue "Vash-Khor-Ra" ("The Land That Remembers Fire"), captures both its volcanic geography and the fact that the land itself seems to carry traumatic memory of the catastrophe — travelers report Vashkora "remembers wrong": history repeats incorrectly, prophecies invert, and the past won't stay buried. The Faith of the Bound Pattern classifies the continent as an **Anomalous Root Zone**, where Thread-aligned magic misfires and even resurrection sometimes produces unexpected results — clerics describe their connection to their gods as "refracted, as if passing through broken glass." Zero of the five Loom-Cities remain active here (Ashkarra was the only one). Currency runs on universally-valued memory-glass shards.

## Legacy of the fall

The Ashkarra crater, the **Wyrmglass Flats**, is now a miles-wide expanse of molten, mirror-like fused earth generating hallucination storms and living reflections. Vashkora is also where all Twelve [[Eldritch Moons]] first manifested in observable reality during Ashkarra's destruction — whether they caused the fall or were summoned by it remains one of the Third Age's great unresolved mysteries. Of the continent's original ten [[World Trees]], six survive; three were destroyed and one — **the Root That Forgot Fire** — is the only actively corrupted World Tree on the planet's surface, inverting the Weave and nesting unknown entities in its heartwood, classified a Category 5 Existential Threat requiring constant Faith observation. See also [[Islands of Eternal Green]], a chain of sacred oases on Vashkora's coast burned five days before Ashkarra's own fall.

## Other named powers

**Thiravarn**, the Sky-Kingdom capital (~340,000, a volcanic-plateau city under dual Sultan/Sky-King leadership, Fire/Air Thread affinity); the **Mirror Empire**, an aggressively expansionist obsidian, reflection-based civilization ruled by Empress Mordessa; and various desert Sultanates with complex trade networks — none deeply detailed in this pass.

## Character

Described as "beautiful catastrophe": ancient fire-aligned civilizations decaying in real time, Gothic mirror palaces beside bone-singing desert wastes, perfect memory preservation coexisting with identity erasure. Vashkora hosts both the most sophisticated magical research in Dór-o Estel and the highest risk of a second Hollow Breach — "the continent that destroyed itself once might do so again, and take the world with it."
