# Valthos, The Keeper of the Final Thread

*"No fate is certain until the final moment. You will not know what your last choice truly means until it is already made."*

**Domains**: the last choice, the moment before death, the decision that changes everything. **Symbol**: a thread pulled taut, ready to break. **Worshiped by**: warriors who seek to defy prophecy, those who believe fate is decided in the final moment. **Allies**: [[Vaithis]], [[Nytheris]], [[Astralis]]. **Enemies**: [[Astria]], [[Aelys]], [[Vel'Zyren]].

Does not control fate — he is the moment just before it is decided. Worshiped by warriors who believe no prophecy is final until the battle is won or lost.
