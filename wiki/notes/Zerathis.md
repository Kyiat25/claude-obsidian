# Zerathis, The Silver Oath

*"Words spoken upon the water are never forgotten."*

**Domains**: oaths, unbreakable promises, the ties that water seals. **Symbol**: two hands clasped beneath flowing water. **Worshiped by**: those who swear upon the sea, lovers who bind their fates, warriors who vow vengeance. **Rivals**: [[Vaedoris]], Varashti, Syralis. **Allies**: [[Tylos]], Ithrien, Marethis.

Unlike [[Vaedoris]], who punishes oathbreakers, Zerathis only ensures what is sworn upon water cannot be undone. Wedding vows are often sworn beside rivers for this reason.
