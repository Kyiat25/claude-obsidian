# Jorvethis, The Maw Beneath

**Domains**: the terror below, deep-sea predators, unseen hunters.

## The Day the Web Forgot

Jorvethis waited in drowned cisterns while [[Vel'Zyren]] dreamed, stilling the sea into a perfect mirror, and touched the first lie she ever told — a lie even her own priestesses dare not name. She screamed without sound; eight priestesses now each carry one-eighth of that memory, and none can dream the full truth without choking on salt and silk. This haunts her precisely because he saw what she buried deepest and proved she could forget. Since that day she never stops weaving, even in sleep, knowing he waits — patient as the sea — for her to forget again.

## Campaign use

A possible hook, "The Truth Beneath," lets Jorvethis offer to reveal what he saw when Vel'Zyren forgot — knowledge that could shatter Drow faith in her, but might break reality too. Players must weigh whether the truth is worth the cost of releasing it.
