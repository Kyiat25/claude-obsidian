# Cyrnith, The Frost-Wind Hunter

*"I do not hate you. I simply do not care if you live."*

**Domains**: glacial winds, the icy breath of death, snowstorms that swallow. **Symbol**: a pale blue wolf's head, its breath turning to ice. **Worshiped by**: those who live in frozen lands, ice-walkers, hunters. **Allies**: [[Howlar]], [[Vaelora]]. **Enemies**: [[Soltharion]], [[Thalmar]].

Not cruel — simply indifferent. Does not kill out of malice, only ensures the strong survive. Nomadic ice-hunters carve his sigil into their weapons.
