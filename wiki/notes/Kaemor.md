# Kaemor, The Harbour's Keeper

*"The tide will return. The waves will rise. But there must always be something that stands against them."*

**Domains**: safe harbours, sea barriers, the line between civilisation and the deep. **Symbol**: a lighthouse standing unbroken against crashing waves. **Worshiped by**: coastal rulers, shipwrights, harbour workers, sailors. **Rivals**: [[Vaelora]], Nyxthys, [[Voraxis]].

Doesn't rule water, holds it back — where the Abyss hungers, he stands between it and the world above; the silent guardian ensuring not all is lost to the sea. Before a port is built, his name is etched into its first stone; harbor towns hold an annual "Silent Vigil," standing at the shore watching for those who never returned. A city that builds too close to the water believing itself untouchable will fall — Kaemor only holds back what is deserved.
