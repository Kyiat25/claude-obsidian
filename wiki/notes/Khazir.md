# Khazir, The Breath of the Dunes

*"The desert does not kill. It only watches to see who is strong enough to survive."*

**Domains**: dunes, sandstorms, the wind that whispers through ruins. **Symbol**: a twisting trail of sand forming an eye. **Worshiped by**: nomads, desert traders, those who live by the shifting sands. **Allies**: [[Zephir]], Howlar. **Enemies**: [[Keldros]], Tahris.

Does not rage — he erodes. The shifting paths, the mirage that disappears when reached for. To honor him is to never trust the path before you.
