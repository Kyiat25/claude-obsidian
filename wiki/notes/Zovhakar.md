# Zovhakar, The Weeping Pyre

*"Once, they called upon me before battle. Once, I led armies of flame against the heavens. And now? Now they have forgotten my name. But I have not forgotten them."*

**Domains**: lost glory, abandoned wars, the ashes of fallen kingdoms. **Symbol**: a cracked sword, wreathed in dying embers. **Worshiped by**: no one anymore, except those who remember what war once was.

Once a great war-god of honor rather than conquest, abandoned when war ceased to be sacred and became mere slaughter. Now lingers as a ghost of fire in forgotten battlefields, mourning warriors who no longer call his name. Myth holds he once stood against [[Xethar]], refusing to let war descend into chaos, but faded without followers into an ember in the darkness.
