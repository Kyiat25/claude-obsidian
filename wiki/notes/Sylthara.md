# Sylthara, The River's Edge

*"I am the place where the land ends and the water does not stop."*

**Domain**: boundaries, riverbanks, erosion, edge magic. **Symbol**: a ripple flowing outward from stone. **Worshiped by**: riverfolk, ferrymen, druids of estuaries, boundary witches.

The thin space where elements touch but do not merge — ensures rivers respect their courses and those on thresholds don't slip into chaos. Myth: when a king tried to dam a sacred river, Sylthara whispered into the current and the waters broke their bonds in rage. Revered by [[Orivak]]; disliked by [[Marethis]] (Water's river-god). **Aspect of the Lock**: she defends transitional points where magic or corruption could bleed through the seams of reality.
