# Welcome to FEARS

Marketing-voiced introductory chapter for **FEARS** ("Where Every Action Echoes"), a tabletop RPG system pitched as backwards-compatible with 5E. The source's own frontmatter marks it `status: revision-needed` — treat every claim here as a design pitch, not a finalized ruleset. No dice mechanics, DCs, or numeric resolution formulas are given anywhere except the Momentum range (-2 to +3).

## What it covers

- **Backwards compatibility**: existing 5E characters/modules translate via a [[Translation Matrix]] (Attribute Mapping + Skill Transformation), with new Focus and Sanity attributes layered on top.
- **Combat**: AC is replaced by the [[FEARS Combat System]] (Evasion, Damage Reduction, Momentum, deliberate-hit counterattacks).
- **Narrative currency**: [[Whisper Points]] let players reshape minor past events, amplify successes, or avoid character death.
- **Turn structure**: the [[Dynamic Action Economy]] (Fluid Action System, Whisper Engine, Narrative Momentum) replaces rigid move/action/bonus-action turns with coordinated, ripple-effect action.
- **Magic**: spellcasting is deliberately volatile — see [[Unstable Magic Zones]].
- **12 new archetypes** — Investigator, Inventor, Magus, Psion, Runesmith, Sangromancer, Shadowbinder, Shaman, Tamer, Tattooist, Thaumaturge, Witch — each summarized by one signature feature (see [[FEARS MOC]] for the full list, each has its own page).
- **Design philosophy**: the [[Consequence Engine]] — critical failures generate narrative opportunity rather than just "you fail."

## Editorial note

The prose is entirely promotional ("infinite possibilities," "kaleidoscope of new archetypes") with no crunch to back specific claims — e.g. "backwards compatible" is asserted but the actual Attribute Mapping table isn't included in this chapter.
