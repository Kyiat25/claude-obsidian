# Velmara, The Obsidian Fang

*"You were warned not to climb too high. You were warned not to drink where the shadows gather."*

**Domain**: predation, stone-born dangers, mountain perils. **Symbol**: a jagged obsidian tooth. **Worshiped by**: ambush predators, hunters, mountain tribes, rogue beastkin.

The hidden danger of Earth — natural, inevitable, merciless, but not evil, simply necessary. Sleeps beneath mountain ranges, rising only when mortals grow too bold in believing the land belongs to them. Ally to [[Garrun]]; feared by halflings; called "the Night's Bite" by underdark dwellers. **Aspect of the Lock**: a fail-safe predator — if too much is disturbed, she awakens as the land's first killer.
