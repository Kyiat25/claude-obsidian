# Ormara, The Frozen Tear

*"The snow does not fall in rage. The ice does not freeze in hate. Winter is not cruel—it is simply quiet."*

> [!note] Naming collision with an Earth Pantheon deity
> The Earth Pantheon separately names an "Ormara, The Crystal Sage" — a preserver of forbidden memory. Both are documented as they appear in their respective source files, not treated as the same entity; domains and myths are unrelated. See [[Ormara (Earth)]] for that page.

**Domains**: frozen beauty, the stillness of ice, the cold that does not burn. **Symbol**: a single, delicate snowflake suspended in midair. **Worshiped by**: poets, mourners, those who find peace in silence. **Rivals**: [[Aelthys]], [[Voraxis]], [[Marethis]]. **Allies**: [[Ythara]], Iskorel, Vithras.

The gentleness of winter — not the rage of the storm or the bite of the cold, but the world made still and silent. Some say she is winter's sorrow, the frozen grief of the world.
