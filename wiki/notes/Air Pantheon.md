# Air Pantheon

Per [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]'s Pantheon Vulnerabilities, a **moderate**-vulnerability pantheon — its prophetic visions are susceptible to temporal manipulation.

## Named deities

**Astria, the Falcon of Fate** — omens arrive backward, a symptom of [[Xey'Tazun]]'s corruption; prophecy becomes vulnerable to temporal corruption generally.

**Zephir, the Trickster Wind** — illusions take on independent life, a symptom of [[Esh'Tarah]]'s corruption; reflections disobey their source.

**Thirian** — named once, in contrast to Zephir and Astria: where Zephir ignores the Eldritch threat and Astria merely observes it, Thirian actively fights. No further detail given.

## Strategy against the Eldritch

Variable and disunified — some Air gods fight (Thirian), some observe (Astria), some ignore the threat entirely (Zephir). No coordinated response exists within this pantheon, unlike any of the other four.
