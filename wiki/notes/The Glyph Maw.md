# The Glyph Maw

**Anchor**: a leyline node where a god's final spell collapsed and rewrote itself infinitely. **Appearance**: a floating spiral of nullified glyphs. **Effect**: consumes active spells, causing feedback loops and spell erasure. **Unique**: can swallow glyphs directly from a caster's mind, removing their knowledge of how to cast a specific spell.

A **Tether** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, an entity bound to a corrupted leyline or glyph point.
