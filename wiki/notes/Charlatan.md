# Charlatan — Rank 1-4 (Modified from PHB)

*"You're a master of deception, living by your wits and charm."* Tier 2 (Specialized).

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Con Artist | False Identity | Pull off a major con, impersonate someone important, or establish a long-con that pays off big |
| 2 | Skilled Deceiver | Master of Disguise | Con someone of significant power/wealth, establish a major fraud operation, or make an identity so convincing it becomes "real" |
| 3 | Master Con Artist | The Long Con | Execute a legendary con, fool an entire organization, or create an alternate identity that gains real power |
| 4 | Criminal Mastermind | Master of Facades | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Con Artist

**Skill Proficiency**: Deception, Sleight of Hand. **Tool Proficiency**: disguise kit, forgery kit. **Equipment**: fine clothes, disguise kit, tools of your con (weighted dice, marked cards, or a signet ring), 15 gp.

**Feature — False Identity**: a second identity, complete with documentation, disguises, and backstory; you can adopt other personas with proper preparation.

**Favorite Schemes** (choose or roll d6): cheat at gambling, shave coins or forge documents, insinuate yourself into people's lives, sell fake miracles or cures, sell fake magic items or relics, impersonate officials or nobility.

**Holdings**: 2-3 multiple identities; a safe house under a false name; a contact who can create forged documents.

## Rank 2: Skilled Deceiver

**Holdings**: 3-5 safe houses under different names; a network of 5-8 accomplices; a forger who creates excellent documents (DC 20 to detect); small ongoing-con income (25 gp/month).

**Feature Expansion — Master of Disguise**: you can create a believable identity in 1 hour; advantage on Deception checks when using a false identity; you can impersonate specific individuals if you've studied them (1 week minimum).

## Rank 3: Master Con Artist

**Holdings**: a legitimate business as a front (worth 500+ gp); 5-7 personas with complete backstories; a network of 15-20 accomplices and patsies; income from multiple cons (100 gp/month); fake titles and documentation (nobility, clergy, military, etc.).

**Feature Expansion — The Long Con**: once per month, execute a major con netting 500-2,000 gp; you can impersonate any type of professional or noble with proper setup; you can create believable organizations or businesses (fake merchant houses, noble families, etc.).

## Rank 4: Criminal Mastermind

**Holdings**: multiple legitimate businesses and properties; dozens of identities across cities and social circles; a network of 50+ accomplices across the continent; major income from sophisticated frauds (500 gp/month); some alternate identities hold real political or social power.

**Feature — Master of Facades**: you can maintain 3-5 completely separate lives simultaneously; automatic success on Deception checks against non-expert targets; you can forge any document (undetectable by mundane means); once per month, execute a scheme that changes the political landscape, bankrupts an organization, or positions an alter-ego for major power. Several of your false identities have become so established they're accepted as real by society, granting you multiple spheres of influence under different names.
