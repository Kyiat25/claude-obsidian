# Called Shot Subclass Features

> **Scope note.** This is the first appearance of a subclass system anywhere in the FEARS cluster — every subclass named below is known *only* through its Called Shot interaction. No other subclass content (spell lists, other features, level progression) has been ingested. Treat this as a narrow window into a larger unconfirmed system, not a subclass reference.

Called Shot penalty modifiers and special interactions, by class/subclass:

| Class / Subclass | Head | Arms | Legs | Special |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [[Rogue]] | −3 | −1 | −1 | Sneak Attack applies to called shots and ignores DR; penalty reduced 2 when qualifying for Sneak Attack |
| Rogue: Assassin | 0 vs. surprised | 0 vs. surprised | 0 vs. surprised | No penalty vs. surprised targets; crits vs. surprised impose the effect without a save; ignores all DR on surprised targets, first turn only |
| [[Monk]] | −3 | −1 | −1 | Pressure Point Strikes: Head→Blinded, Arms→Disadvantage on attacks, Legs→cannot Dash |
| Fighter: Battle Master | variable | variable | variable | Spend a Superiority Die to reduce the penalty by the die result |
| Fighter: Champion | −4 | −2 | −2 | Crits on called shots impose the effect with the save at disadvantage |
| [[Barbarian]] (Raging) | −5 | 0 | 0 | No penalty on arm/leg called shots while Raging |
| Ranger: Hunter's Mark | −3 | −1 | −1 | Reduces target DR by Wisdom modifier; marked target's detection roll is at disadvantage |
| Ranger: Favoured Enemy | −2 | 0 | 0 | Stacks with Hunter's Mark |
| Ranger: Mark + Favoured combo | 0 | 0 | 0 | Penalty-free; DR reduced by Wisdom mod +1; +1 damage die |
| Ranger: Gloom Stalker | −2 | 0 | 0 | Round 1: no penalties; called shots in dim light/darkness impose disadvantage on the target's save |
| Paladin: Oath of Vengeance | −2 | −1 | −1 | Vow of Enmity: one attack/round vs. the marked target ignores penalties entirely |
| Paladin: Oath of Conquest | −3 | −1 | −1 | Leg shots force a WIS save or restrain, instead of just speed reduction |
| Bard: College of Swords | −2 | −1 | −1 | No penalty during a Blade Flourish |
| Hexblade Warlock | −3 vs. cursed | 0 vs. cursed | 0 vs. cursed | Reduced 2 vs. a cursed target; adds necrotic damage = Charisma modifier |
| Bladesinger Wizard | −2 | −1 | −1 | While in Bladesong, called shots add bonus damage = spell slot level |
