# Archivists of the Shattered Loom

In-fiction authorial voice credited for [[Four Threads of the Mind]] ("A Whispered Primer from the Archivists of the Shattered Loom"). A second narrator persona distinct from [[Daldir Lunadreamer]] (credited for [[Welcome to FEARS]]) — suggests FEARS sourcebooks are written as in-world documents attributed to different fictional authors/factions rather than a single consistent voice. "Shattered Loom" plausibly connects to [[The Weave]] (loom/weave imagery), though no source states this explicitly. No other biographical or organizational detail given.
