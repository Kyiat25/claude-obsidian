# Syvaris, The Skybound Sage

*"The wind does not lead you home. The wind does not lead you anywhere. The wind simply moves, and you must choose whether to follow."*

**Domains**: wind-current navigators, hidden airborne paths, the flight of knowledge. **Symbol**: a silver cloud shaped like an open book. **Worshiped by**: sky-nomads, airship pilots, seekers of the hidden winds. **Rivals**: [[Astria]], Vaelora, Aelys.

A god of motion, not guidance — he does not show the way, only ensures there is always a way to be found. Scholars revere him as keeper of lost wisdom, but he never answers questions directly.
