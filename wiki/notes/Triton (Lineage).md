# Triton

*"We were the ocean before the ocean had a name. Then Wind touched Water, and we opened our eyes."*

**Origin myth — The First Breath**: Tritons are not a created race in the usual sense — they *are* the ocean becoming self-aware, born from the first wave when Wind touched Water, a literal synthesis of the Water and Air elemental strands, with Spirit joining later as inherited "memory." No god made them; the gods *covenanted* with them afterward, once they already existed — see [[The Tide Covenant]]. This origin places Tritons structurally closer to primordial/elemental cosmology than to any single pantheon's creation account, distinguishing them from [[Sea Elf (Lineage)]], who are elves who *chose* the sea rather than an elemental people born of it.

**Physical**: mature ~15, live ~200 years; Medium, 5–6½ ft., 30 ft. walk/swim speed.

## Traits

- **Amphibious**: breathe air and water without penalty
- **Guardian of the Depths**: cold resistance
- **Emissary of the Sea**: one-way communication with aquatic beasts
- **Control Air and Water**: innate CHA-based *shape water* cantrip, unlocking *fog cloud* at 3rd level and *gust of wind* at 5th
- **Current-Reader**: Perception proficiency + reliable detection of underwater currents/disturbances
- **Oath-Keeper's Intuition**: always know when someone lies about a promise made to you
- **Languages**: Common + Primordial (Aquan dialect)

**Mental**: Focus +1 / Sanity +0.

**DCS Integration**: Dodge +1 / Endure +0 / Deflect +1; DR +0. Full underwater combat dominance — advantage against non-swimming opponents, no penalties of any kind underwater.

## Corruption: The Stilled Current

Corruption enters through stagnation, blocked flow, or broken oaths — a people defined by movement and covenant are most vulnerable to stillness and betrayal. At Corruption 10+: Current-Lock, Oath-Static, Depth-Fear. **Fractured Covenant table** (1d6): False Tide, Current-Silence (Control Air and Water disabled), Oath-Weight (Sanity save or −1d4), Depth-Cascade, Eldritch Undertow (+1 Whisper Point, Oath-Keeper's Intuition inverted — false readings), or Current Holds (Corruption −1).

## Subraces (depth-caste)

| Subrace | ASI | Signature features |
|---|---|---|
| **Deep-Dwelling / Pressure-Born** (elite nobles, commanders) | STR+1, CON+1, CHA+1 | Pressure Resistance; innate *command* → *hold person* (leveled); Abyssal Vision 120 ft. |
| **Mid-Current / Flow-Walkers** (the majority — merchants, artisans, soldiers) | DEX+1, INT+1, CHA+1 | Trade-Current Blessing; innate *gust* → *expeditious retreat* → *misty step* (leveled); Current-Dancer |
| **Surface-Singer / Voice Between** (diplomats, land liaisons) | CON+1, WIS+1, CHA+1 | Air-Walker's Adaptation; innate *guidance* → *comprehend languages* → *calm emotions* (leveled); Diplomatic Training |

Deep-Dwelling castes form the Coral Dominion's ruling stratum; Mid-Current is the demographic majority; Surface-Singers are the caste most often encountered on land, serving as ambassadors between Triton society and land-bound peoples.
