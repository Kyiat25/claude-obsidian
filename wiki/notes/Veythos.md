# Veythos, The Drowned Ferryman

*"The sea does not return its dead. But I do."*

**Domains**: the souls of the drowned, passage between life and death, the unclaimed dead of water. **Symbol**: a lone boat drifting on a silent sea. **Worshiped by**: sailors, those who fear drowning, priests who tend to the lost. **Rivals**: [[Tzhor]], [[Astralis]], Morgathis. **Allies**: Eryndal, Lyrithis, Selmira.

Unlike [[Tzhor]], who keeps the drowned within his kingdom, Veythos guides those never meant to be lost at sea onward — carrying those Water has swallowed but Spirit has not yet embraced.
