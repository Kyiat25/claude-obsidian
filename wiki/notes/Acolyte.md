# Acolyte — Rank 1-4 (Modified from PHB)

*"You served in a temple or religious organization, though the gods are dead."* Tier 1 (Core). The "gods are dead" clause is a notable, setting-specific twist on the vanilla PHB Acolyte — worth cross-checking against [[Welcome to FEARS]] and [[A World that Watches]] for the fuller theological picture, not yet reconciled here.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Novitiate | Shelter of the Faithful | Complete a religious pilgrimage, perform a miracle, or convert a significant number of followers |
| 2 | Ordained Priest | Keeper of Lore | Establish a new shrine, perform a significant ceremony, or uncover lost religious knowledge |
| 3 | High Priest | Divine Authority | Build a new temple, broker peace between warring factions, or perform an unprecedented religious act |
| 4 | Arch-Priest/Religious Leader | Voice of Faith | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Novitiate

**Skill Proficiency**: Insight, Religion. **Language Proficiency**: two of choice. **Equipment**: holy symbol, prayer book or prayer wheel, 5 sticks of incense, vestments, common clothes, 15 gp.

**Feature — Shelter of the Faithful**: you and your companions receive free healing and care at temples, shrines, and religious communities of your faith; those who share your religion support you at a modest lifestyle.

**Holdings**: cell in a temple dormitory; access to the temple library (Religion research); a mentor priest or higher-ranking acolyte.

## Rank 2: Ordained Priest

**Holdings**: private quarters in temple; small stipend (10 gp/month); a congregation of 20-30 faithful; access to temple vaults (can borrow non-magical religious items).

**Feature Expansion — Keeper of Lore**: you can research religious and historical matters in any temple or shrine in days rather than weeks. You can identify religious symbols and their meanings, blessed or cursed items (DC 15 Religion check), and locations of holy or unholy significance.

## Rank 3: High Priest

**Holdings**: suite of rooms in temple; leadership of a temple with 100-200 congregants; monthly tithe income (50 gp); network of 5-10 priests across the region; authority to perform all religious ceremonies.

**Feature Expansion — Divine Authority**: once per week, call upon your congregation to aid you (donate money, shelter fugitives, provide information, etc.). You can grant blessings (mechanical benefit: inspiration to believers) and excommunicate believers (a social penalty in religious communities).

## Rank 4: Arch-Priest/Religious Leader

**Holdings**: a cathedral or major temple complex; leadership over 500+ faithful; network of 20+ priests and 10+ temples; major tithe/donation income (200 gp/month); political influence in religious matters.

**Feature — Voice of Faith**: you can summon religious councils to make doctrinal decisions; your decrees are followed by thousands of faithful; once per month, call for mass action (crusade, pilgrimage, charitable drive) mobilizing hundreds; you have the ear of rulers on religious matters. Shelter of the Faithful now extends to healing serious injuries and diseases, and providing resources for major undertakings.
