# Tavron, The Keeper of Crossroads

*"At every turning point, I wait. I do not judge your step—but once chosen, it cannot be unchosen."*

**Domain**: sacred crossroads, mortal choice, branching fates. **Symbol**: a four-way road with a stone eye at the center. **Worshiped by**: pilgrims, oath-takers, prophecy seekers.

The divine embodiment of irreversible choice — manifests at crossroads of purpose, where the next step determines fate. Myth: a woman knelt at a crossroads and begged for both roads; Tavron gave her neither. He once offered a god three roads; the god chose none, and was erased from memory. Respected by [[Gaeldros]], since his judgments often follow the decisions Tavron watches; feared by [[Vel'Zyren]], who cannot bind those who choose truly. **Aspect of the Lock**: guards the metaphysical crossroads near the leylines, where mortal action might affect the seal whether they know it or not.
