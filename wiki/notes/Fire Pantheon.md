# Fire Pantheon

Per [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]'s Pantheon Vulnerabilities, a **high**-vulnerability pantheon — its ambition-driven pursuit of knowledge and power leads directly to forbidden truths.

## Named deities

**Vaeldros, the Ash-Warden** — purging flames leave backwards flesh, a symptom of [[Ith'Quess]]'s corruption; cannot cleanse what doesn't remember burning.

**Pyrris, the Ashen Oracle** — illusions leave echoes of longing, a symptom of [[Vael'Akir]]'s corruption; shadow and smoke become corrupted.

**Zerah, the Ember-Speaker** — prophetic logic breaking, a symptom of [[Zhurr'Kael]]'s corruption; prophecy-threads terminate in impossible forks, and the future contradicts itself.

## Strategy against the Eldritch

Purging with flame, destroying evidence, killing the infected before corruption spreads — "burn it before it spreads."
