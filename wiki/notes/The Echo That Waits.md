# The Echo That Waits

*"I dreamed of the end. I woke before it came. But now I see it again—exactly as I dreamed it. And I know I never woke at all."*

**Origin**: a thought-form that bloomed at the edge of the Lantern Below, believed to have crossed into reality during the first breach of [[The Hollow Thread]]. **Behavior**: appears in mirrored surfaces, dream-reflections, and the eyes of those who see the future too clearly — it never speaks, only reflects. **Effect**: grants visions of futures that never happened, yet begin to manifest; prophets and seers who contact it lose all memory of the vision, but reality does not. **Danger**: not a liar — a memory of a future that should not exist. When its vision comes true, the present begins to fracture, reshaping to match the dream it remembered.

A **Dream-Born** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, tied directly to the Hollow Thread's first breach.
