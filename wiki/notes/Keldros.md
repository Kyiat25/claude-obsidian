# Keldros, The Unyielding Shield

*"I do not fall. I do not yield. I do not forget. If you make a vow in my name, the world itself will see it done."*

**Domain**: defense, resilience, sacred oaths. **Symbol**: a stone shield inscribed with an unbroken line. **Worshiped by**: paladins, wardens, oathbound guardians, dwarven oathkeepers.

The one who guards not just people but promises — invoked at weddings, treaties, and by warriors who swear not to retreat. Myth: when a mortal broke his oath on sacred stone, Keldros turned his flesh to gravel, scattering him across the hills. Ally of [[Gaeldros]] (law) and [[Sarnok]] (territorial defence). **Aspect of the Lock**: he guards the oaths that bind the gods, and watches the seals holding back what lies beneath.
