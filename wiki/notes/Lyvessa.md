# Lyvessa, The Jewel of the Deep

*"The ocean is the greatest thief in history. And I decide what it keeps."*

**Domains**: sunken treasure, lost ships, the ocean's hoarded gold. **Symbol**: a golden coin sinking beneath the waves. **Worshiped by**: treasure hunters, ship captains, deep-sea divers. **Rivals**: [[Vaelora]], [[Jorvethis]], [[Voraxis]], [[Syralis]], Quoras. **Allies**: [[Marethis]], Selmira, Nyssara, Morgathis.

Governs the treasures the sea has taken — not out of greed, but because the ocean is owed a debt that must always be paid. She does not share what she has taken, and never loses what is hers.
