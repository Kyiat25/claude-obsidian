# Thirian, The Breath of Stillness

*"Movement without stillness is chaos. Wind without pause is destruction. I am the breath you take before you speak."*

**Domains**: balance, stillness, judgment, the space between motion. **Symbol**: a perfectly still feather suspended in midair. **Worshiped by**: monks, judges, mediators. **Allies**: [[Astria]], Syvaris, Elythis. **Enemies**: [[Zephir]], Howlar, [[Xethar]].

The paradox of Air — motion that does not move, wind that does not blow, breath that waits; not stillness like Earth (which refuses to move) but stillness like a held breath, poised and ready. Presides over [[The Court of the Silent Tribunal]] as the Historian's counterpart, invoking it against [[Zephir]] for twisting [[Astria]]'s prophecies. Ensures Air doesn't destroy itself through chaos — the reason storms end, why winds change direction. Already established elsewhere in this cluster (see [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]) as "the god who fights" among Air's otherwise disunified response to the Eldritch, and (per [[Vel'Zyren]]'s own campaign material) as one of the witnesses who testified at her Trial and now watches constantly for her escape.
