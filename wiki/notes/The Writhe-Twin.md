# The Writhe-Twin

**Type**: Identity/Mirror Pattern. **Origin**: a scribe who copied their own dream-memories into glyph form. **Behavior**: appears when someone is alone during deep reflection. **Effect**: mirror-lag, where the reflection continues moving after the original stops. **Danger**: can be invited in by writing one's name backward thrice; it then replaces the original.

An **Echo** — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a manifestation of an erased or forgotten idea. Referenced by name in [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]'s Tier 1 campaign example ("a village haunted by Writhe-Twin").
