# Hideouts

Secret sanctuaries for rest, recovery, and planning once a character's name becomes "too loud." Can be built urban, wilderness, or mobile; can be staffed with guards, traps, or informants; can store [[Black Market]] goods or stage heists; can be networked to extend influence or evasion range.

## Risk

Discovery risk scales directly with [[Wanted Score]] — disloyal staff, persistent bounty hunters, and magical tracking glyphs are named as specific threats.
