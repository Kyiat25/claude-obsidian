# Sythros, The Dream That Was Never Dreamed

*"Every choice eliminates another. Every path walked means another was abandoned. I remember them all."*

**Domains**: forgotten choices, the roads never taken, the what-ifs of the Weave. **Symbol**: a branching path with no clear end. **Worshiped by**: philosophers, diviners, those who regret their past. **Allies**: [[Nytheris]], [[Vaithis]], [[Astralis]]. **Enemies**: [[Aelys]], [[Vel'Zyren]], [[Astria]].

The voice of what could have been, heard in dreams and regrets. His presence does not change fate, but reminds mortals that no destiny is absolute.
