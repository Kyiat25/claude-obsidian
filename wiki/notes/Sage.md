# Sage — Rank 1-4 (Modified from PHB)

*"You're a scholar, researcher, or expert in esoteric knowledge."* Tier 2 (Specialized).

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Student | Researcher | Publish a significant work, make an important discovery, or solve a mystery that's baffled scholars |
| 2 | Scholar | Expert Knowledge | Become the foremost expert on a subject, discover lost knowledge, or teach at a prestigious institution |
| 3 | Master Scholar | Loremaster | Make a groundbreaking discovery, write a definitive text, or found an academy |
| 4 | Archscholar | Living Legend of Learning | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Student

**Skill Proficiency**: Arcana, History. **Language Proficiency**: two of choice. **Equipment**: bottle of ink, quill, small knife, a letter from a dead colleague with an unanswered question, common clothes, 10 gp.

**Feature — Researcher**: if you don't know something, you know where to find it (libraries, scriptoria, universities, sages) — gaining access might require bribes, favors, or quests.

**Specialty** (choose or roll d8): Alchemist, Astronomer, Discredited academic, Librarian, Professor, Researcher, Wizard's apprentice, Scribe.

**Holdings**: library access at your institution; a small collection of books (worth 50 gp); academic contacts in your field.

## Rank 2: Scholar

**Holdings**: an office or study at an academic institution; modest stipend (20 gp/month); a growing library (worth 200 gp); network of 5-8 fellow scholars; access to restricted library sections.

**Feature Expansion — Expert Knowledge**: advantage on Intelligence checks related to your specialty; research time cut in half; you can deduce information about items, creatures, or phenomena in your field with 1 hour of study (DC 15 Intelligence check).

## Rank 3: Master Scholar

**Holdings**: a private study with an extensive library (worth 1,000 gp); good stipend (75 gp/month); 3-5 research assistants; academic contacts across the continent; invitations to symposia and councils.

**Feature Expansion — Loremaster**: you can answer nearly any question in your field of expertise; you're consulted by nobles and organizations on important matters; you can publish works that influence scholarly thought; access to secret or forbidden knowledge.

## Rank 4: Archscholar

**Holdings**: an academy or major library named after you; excellent income from publications and consulting (200 gp/month); a staff of 10+ researchers and assistants; international academic reputation; influence over academic institutions.

**Feature — Living Legend of Learning**: automatic success on Intelligence checks in your field (DC 20 or lower); you can identify any magical effect, creature, or phenomenon with 10 minutes of study; universities and libraries grant you unrestricted access; once per month, call upon the collective knowledge of scholars worldwide (gain the answer to any question that has a known answer). You can also fund and direct research projects that shape intellectual progress across the continent.
