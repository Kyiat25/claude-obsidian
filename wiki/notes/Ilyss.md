# Ilyss, The Keeper of Unfinished Stories

*"Not every story is meant to be finished. Not every name should be remembered."*

**Domains**: lost histories, unfinished fates, stories that were never told. **Symbol**: a book with half its pages missing. **Worshiped by**: historians, storytellers, those who believe some truths are best left forgotten. **Allies**: [[Nyzara]], [[Astralis]], [[Xelthar]]. **Enemies**: [[Vaithis]], [[Zeythis]], [[Vel'Zyren]].

The spirit of half-truths and abandoned fates — does not deceive, she simply does not reveal everything. Books with missing endings are said to be taken by her.
