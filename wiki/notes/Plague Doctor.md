# Plague Doctor — Rank 1-4 (New, Grim Hollow-inspired)

*"You treat the sick and dying in a world of disease and corruption."* Tier 4 (Niche, Grim Hollow-inspired dark fantasy).

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Physician's Assistant | Disease Knowledge | Successfully treat a serious outbreak, or discover treatment for a rare disease |
| 2 | Physician | Healer's Touch | Save someone important from certain death, or contain a major outbreak single-handedly |
| 3 | Master Physician | Master Healer | Develop a breakthrough treatment, save a royal from mysterious illness, or write a standard medical text |
| 4 | Surgeon General | Miracle Worker | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Physician's Assistant

**Skill Proficiency**: Medicine, Nature. **Tool Proficiency**: herbalism kit, poisoner's kit. **Equipment**: healer's kit, herbalism kit, common clothes, a physician's mask, leather gloves, 15 gp.

**Feature — Disease Knowledge**: advantage on Medicine checks to identify diseases; you can create basic remedies (healing equivalent to herbalism kit uses); you know the symptoms of common diseases and poisons.

**Holdings**: an apprenticeship with an experienced physician; access to medical supplies; a small collection of herbs and ingredients.

## Rank 2: Physician

**Holdings**: an office or clinic; income from wealthy patients (20 gp/month); comprehensive medical supplies; 1-2 assistants; a growing reputation in your city.

**Feature Expansion — Healer's Touch**: you can stabilize dying patients automatically (no check needed); advantage on checks to treat disease and poison; you can perform minor surgeries; you know how to quarantine and contain outbreaks.

## Rank 3: Master Physician

**Holdings**: an established practice or hospital; major income from wealthy patrons (80 gp/month); a team of assistants and nurses; an extensive laboratory and supply stores; clients among nobility; access to rare medical texts.

**Feature Expansion — Master Healer**: you can cure diseases that others cannot, within reason; you can create powerful antitoxins and remedies; patients recover faster under your care (half recovery time); you can identify and treat curses masquerading as illness.

## Rank 4: Surgeon General

**Holdings**: a hospital or medical college bearing your name; excellent income (250 gp/month); a staff of 20+ medical professionals; research facilities; political influence on public health matters; your medical practices are adopted widely.

**Feature — Miracle Worker**: you can treat nearly any mundane disease or poison; once per month, save someone from death (even after failing all death saves, if the body is intact); you can create medical wonders (prosthetics, organ transplants using Renaissance-era techniques); your presence stops plague spread (morale effect plus practical containment). Rulers seek your counsel on health crises, your texts are studied across the continent, other physicians journey to learn from you, and you can identify supernatural diseases and know experts who might help.
