# Xerathis, The Wind-Walker

*"The wind carries blades. The wind moves unseen. The wind is already behind you."*

**Domains**: stealth, the unseen wind, the assassin's breath. **Symbol**: a single shadowed footprint, fading into mist. **Worshiped by**: assassins, spies, monks who move like the wind. **Allies**: [[Sylphine]], Vaethis, Elythis. **Enemies**: [[Astria]], [[Thalmar]], Keldros.

The master of silence — where the wind does not move, he has already passed. Assassins carve his sigil into their weapons before a kill.
