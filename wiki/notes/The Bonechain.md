# The Bonechain

**Anchor**: a failed binding spell for a war god's soul. **Appearance**: a spectral chain of bone and glyphs, linked to nothing. **Effect**: binds mortals and beasts into itself. **Key threat**: cannot be cut — severed links simply rewrap around the closest soul.

A **Tether** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, an entity bound to a corrupted leyline or glyph point.
