# Vatharun, The Stone-Father

*"Once, I turned beneath the stars. The mountains rose in protest. The leylines screamed. I have not moved since. But should I rise again, it will not be to shape the world—but to bury what survives it."*

**Domain**: stability, unshaken endurance, the First Mountain, the leylines. **Symbol**: a great mountain with cracks of light running through it. **Worshiped by**: dwarves, architects, geomancers, leyline scholars. **Allies**: [[Gaeldros]] (Law), [[Thamur]] (Secrecy), [[Orivak]] (Leyline Guardian).

The leylines are literally his bones — to damage one is to wound the world itself. He has moved only twice in recorded history: once in the primordial past (the mountains rose in protest, the leylines screamed), and once during the [[The Fractured Pillar|Fractured Pillar]] event, folding mountains over [[The Hollow Thread]]'s seal. He has sworn never to move again except at the true end. Some believe he will move a final time only when the Lock breaks entirely.
