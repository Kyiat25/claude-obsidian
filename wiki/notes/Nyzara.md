# Nyzara, The Moon That Watches

*"The stars are distant. The sun is cruel. But the moon watches, and she never forgets."*

**Domains**: protection, memory, the celestial guardian of the Weave. **Symbol**: a full moon with an endless thread spiralling around it. **Worshiped by**: healers, storytellers, those who believe in fate's guidance. **Rivals**: [[Vel'Zyren]], Nytheris, Xelthar.

The mother of the Weave, the first thread spun into the void — observes and records rather than commanding or dictating fate, ensuring knowledge is not lost. Sometimes dreams of memories returning unasked, believing them mistakes; they are actually [[The Restorer]]'s work, unrecognized by her. Already established elsewhere in this cluster (see [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]) as a memory guardian who maintains "productive forgetting," and (per [[Vel'Zyren]]'s own material) as possibly the conceptual opposite or "reflected twin" of Vel'Zyren — she weeps during mirror rites and cannot bear to hear Vel'Zyren's name spoken near sacred memory wells, a connection neither goddess has ever confirmed.

> [!note] One of Aeloria's unknowing threads
> Per [[Spirit Pantheon]]'s framing, [[Aeloria]] wove the entire Spirit Pantheon into being as her final act of visible presence after erasing herself from memory — Nyzara, like the other Spirit gods, believes she shapes memory independently, without knowing she is one of Aeloria's own threads.
