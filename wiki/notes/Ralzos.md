# Ralzos, The Infernal Blade

*"The blade must be wielded with purpose. The fire must burn with precision. War without control is ruin."*

**Domains**: war, mastery, strength, discipline. **Symbol**: a perfectly crafted sword, glowing with heat. **Worshiped by**: elite warriors, weapon-masters, those who refine combat into an art. **Allies**: [[Vaeldros]], [[Tharamis]]. **Enemies**: [[Xethar]] (wildfire and recklessness), [[Vel'Zyren]].

Represents war not as chaos but as discipline — the perfect strike, the refined warrior, mastery as an ideal. Where Xethar fights with reckless fury, Ralzos fights with the precision of a master forging a blade. Myth holds he once tempered Xethar's fury, until he realized some fires could never be controlled.
