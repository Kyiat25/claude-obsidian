# Eldritch Moons in Combat

`.raw/Eldritch Moons in Combat.md`'s own frontmatter reads `status: excluded` (with only a bare `template` tag, the weakest provenance signal of the three sources marked this way so far). **Corrected by explicit user instruction**, the same correction applied to [[Spell Critical]]: this content is confirmed canon, not excluded. [[The Dynamic Combat System (Excluded Draft)]] was *not* reclassified by this correction and remains treated as excluded — these are source-specific corrections, not a blanket policy change.

## What are Eldritch Moons?

Celestial anomalies tied to the **Whisper Engine**, **Fractures**, and forbidden knowledge — twisting magic, altering sanity, and invoking latent truths when they appear. Signals: Weave instability, Sanity pressure and increased Whisper Points, mutated spells/glyphs/divine contact, strange boons/omens/curses.

## Twelve named Moons

| Name | Effect (examples) |
|---|---|
| Howling Moon | +2 Fracture DC; screams become distorted echoes |
| Shattered Moon | Glyphs misfire on doubles; memory magic unreliable |
| Scorching Moon | Fire spells deal double damage but trigger Sanity Saves |
| Vacuous Moon | Insight checks fail; lies indistinguishable from truth |
| Glowering Moon | Enemies gain temporary HP each round while visible under its light |
| Glacial Moon | Movement halved; Fracture effects linger twice as long |
| Slumbering Moon | Long Rests require a Sanity Save to complete |
| Yellow Crown | Dreams become prophecy; Prophetic characters get false/inverted visions |
| Creeping Tarlight | Natural healing halved; poisons gain sentience |
| Krakenlight | Oceans whisper names; Fracture rolls summon hallucinated deep-sea events |
| Sanguine Eclipse | Blood spilled in moonlight grants +1 Whisper Point |
| Blood Moon of Rebirth | Dead monsters may rise as corrupted versions within 1 minute |

"These twelve are canon in most FEARS campaigns" per the source; the GM may add more.

## Combat effects and hooks

Each Moon has a Surge Trigger that calls on its unique trait during combat. Four effect categories: Sanity Modifier (Sanity Save DCs increase or trigger more often), Whisper Generation (actions may trigger Whisper Point gain), Magic Warping (glyph effects shift/misfire/amplify), Environmental Twist (terrain/lighting changes). Optional toggles: Dual Moons (two active at once, compounding), Moon Prophecy (marked characters get visions/compulsions), Cycle of the Twelfth (every 12th full moon opens a portal/ritual site), Tide of Madness (monsters also affected, not just PCs).

Integration hooks name four systems: [[Whisper Engine]] (Moons accelerate Whisper Checks, surge risk, "Moon Resonance traits"), Fracture System (Fracture effects escalate under specific Moons), Magic & Glyphs (warps casting/glyph behavior — same Echo Glyph content as [[Spell Critical]]), Sanity Mechanics (strong Sanity pressure from exposure/dreams/resonance).

## Second confirmed Fracture sighting

Alongside [[Spell Critical]], this is the second confirmed source referencing a **Fracture** mechanic by name — "Fracture DC," "Fracture System," "Fracture rolls," "Fracture effects." Both sources are now confirmed canon, so a real Fracture subsystem is established to exist, though its full rules still aren't defined by either. `Fracture Whispers.md`, still uningested, remains the source expected to supply the actual mechanic.

## Whisper Engine as a check-based mechanic

[[Whisper Engine]]'s open question — is it the accounting mechanism for [[Whisper Points]], a separate expansion, or unrelated — is not resolved here, but this source's confirmed mention of "Whisper Checks" as something Moons "accelerate" is a real new data point: Whisper Engine involves rollable checks, not just a passive point pool.

Moon Resonance (trait), Whisper Check, Moon Prophecy, Cycle of the Twelfth, Tide of Madness — confirmed to exist but not detailed enough for dedicated pages yet. Not filed as separate stub pages per the no-thin-filler-pages convention; listed here for searchability.
