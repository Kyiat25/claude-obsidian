# Entertainer — Rank 1-4 (Modified from PHB)

*"You thrive in performance, living to capture an audience."* Tier 2 (Specialized).

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Street Performer | By Popular Demand | Perform at a major venue, gain patronage of someone important, or develop a signature act that becomes famous |
| 2 | Recognized Performer | Local Celebrity | Perform for royalty, create a legendary performance, or train under a master |
| 3 | Famous Performer | Star Power | Become the most famous performer in your art, innovate your art form, or establish a tradition that outlives you |
| 4 | Legendary Artist | Living Legend | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Street Performer

**Skill Proficiency**: Acrobatics, Performance. **Tool Proficiency**: disguise kit, one musical instrument. **Equipment**: musical instrument, a favor from an admirer (love letter, lock of hair, or trinket), costume, 15 gp.

**Feature — By Popular Demand**: you can find a place to perform (inn, tavern, circus) receiving modest lodging and food in exchange for nightly performances.

**Entertainer Routines** (choose or roll d10): Actor, Dancer, Fire-eater, Jester, Juggler, Instrumentalist, Poet, Singer, Storyteller, Tumbler.

**Holdings**: a regular performance spot; a small following of 10-20 fans; a contact in the local entertainment scene.

## Rank 2: Recognized Performer

**Holdings**: performances at established venues; income (20 gp/month); a growing fanbase (50-100 people); invitations to private parties; a network of performer friends in major cities.

**Feature Expansion — Local Celebrity**: recognized in major settlements in your region; you can gain audience with minor nobility and wealthy merchants; fans provide information and small favors; advantage on Performance checks in your region.

## Rank 3: Famous Performer

**Holdings**: high performance fees; major income (100 gp/month); a large, devoted fanbase (500+); patronage from a wealthy noble or merchant; you can fill theaters or large venues; a network of fellow famous performers.

**Feature Expansion — Star Power**: recognized across the nation; you can request audience with most nobility; your endorsement carries weight (products, establishments, causes); you can organize benefit performances raising 1,000+ gp for causes.

## Rank 4: Legendary Artist

**Holdings**: a theater, opera house, or performance hall named after you; massive income from performances, endorsements, and patronage (400 gp/month); thousands of devoted fans across the continent; political influence through fame; your works are studied and emulated.

**Feature — Living Legend**: automatic success on Performance checks for your specialty; you can meet with anyone (even rulers) based on fame; your performances are cultural events that affect society; once per month, organize a performance that impacts politics, culture, or public opinion on a major scale. You can also command fees of 500+ gp per private performance, your autobiography becomes a bestseller, academies study your techniques, and artists across the continent claim to be your students.
