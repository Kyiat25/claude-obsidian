# Kaelith, The Emberborn Knight

*"A warrior's flame does not flicker. It does not waver. It burns with purpose."*

**Domains**: war, strength, justice. **Symbol**: a flaming shield with a broken sword. **Worshiped by**: warriors, knights, avengers. **Rival**: Vexith (sees his endless-rebirth cycle as a mockery of a warrior's duty). **Enemy**: Mordrath, for whose fire should never be allowed to go out.

Unlike [[Soltharion]] and [[Vaeldros]], Kaelith does not fear knowledge and sees [[Zerah]]'s wisdom as a weapon rather than a threat. Fights neither for empire nor rebellion, but because warriors must fight, because some wars must be won. Myth: his flames were once dimmed by betrayal, but he reforged himself in the fire of his own trial, proving true strength is never broken. Rose, per one account, as the "reformed echo" counterpart to [[Kael'Thar]]'s fall — the two haven't spoken in divine council for twelve cycles, but any time a warrior executes a surrendering foe or a court lights a pyre too quickly, Kael'Thar is said to stir.
