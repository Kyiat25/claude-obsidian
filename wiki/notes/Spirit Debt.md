# Spirit Debt

[[Witch]]'s proposed narrative currency (see [[Narrative Identity]]'s status note — this is a design proposal, not confirmed rules). Built when binding, compelling, borrowing from, or betraying spirits. Spirits "remember coercion, they make demands, and curses often travel farther than intended" — the mechanical embodiment of the Witch's core narrative pressure.
