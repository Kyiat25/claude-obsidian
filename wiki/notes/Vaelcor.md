# Vaelcor, The Blade-Forged

*"To defend is not weakness. To stand between the innocent and destruction is the greatest honor."*

**Domains**: protection, guardianship, defensive warfare. **Symbol**: a shield wreathed in slow-burning fire. **Worshiped by**: defenders, protectors, warrior-monks, castle wardens. **Allies**: Kaelith, [[Kaldris]], Pyrris. **Enemies**: [[Xethar]], Xalros, Nytheris.

Fire as protection — the slow, steady flame that holds the line against darkness. To his followers, defense is the greatest test of strength, not weakness. His temples are watchtowers, his priests are knights. Myth holds he was once a war-god like Xethar, but rejected conquest, choosing to wield his fire only in defence of others.
