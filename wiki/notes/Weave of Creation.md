# Weave of Creation

Self-described as "the Definitive Cosmic Structure of Ley Lines, Reflections, and Universal Balance" — "the Grand Synthesis." The single most systematized leyline-classification document in this cluster, restating and substantially deepening [[Weave and Magic]]'s five-layer cosmology.

## The Weave as the lifeblood of reality

Every world, plane, and dimension is connected through [[Leylines]] originating from [[Dór-o Estel]], the Creation World. Without leylines, magic ceases to function and reality deteriorates into nothingness. The oldest and most sacred leylines are said to be carved from the bones of **Vatharun**, the First Mountain and Earth's foundation god — see [[Earth Pantheon]]. The Earth Pantheon does not move these leylines; they *hold* them, ensuring the world doesn't collapse from within.

## The five Universal Forces

Named here with slightly different framing than [[Universal Glyphs]]' presentation, but the same five entities: Keeper of Knowledge, The Wildheart, Tide Between, The Veiled Hand, The Lantern Below — regulators, not creators, ensuring the Weave neither unravels nor stagnates.

## Leyline types (by scale)

Minor, Major, Trunk (the backbone, connected to World Anchors), Corrupted, and Dead — see [[Leylines]] for the full classification and how it maps onto [[Three Pillars of Magical Flow]]'s stability-based typing.

## The Nine Form Glyphs

The fullest version of the Form Glyph roster in this cluster — nine entries rather than [[How the Five Layers Shape the Creation World]]'s original five. Full table on [[Form Glyphs]].

## The Abyssal Anchors: external threats to the Weave

Distinct from the internal Eldritch corruption documented in [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]], this source introduces two competing cosmic threats to leyline control:

- **The Chained Depths (Hells)** — the Law of Domination; binds leylines into rigid structures, preventing magic from evolving freely. If it wins, magic becomes a bureaucratic system requiring absolute control.
- **The Howling Maw (Abyss)** — the Force of Unmaking; seeks to shatter leylines, making magic completely unstable. If it wins, magic becomes chaotic and uncontrollable, consuming everything in its wake.
- **The Tide Between** prevents either side from achieving full control, keeping magic in constant, fragile balance.

## Planar reflections

Restates [[Luminous Echo]] and [[Umbral Veil]]'s leyline-mirroring mechanics with additional flavor text (this is the primary source both of those pages draw the bulk of their detail from).

## The birth of the leylines

When [[Astralor]] struck his hammer upon the void, he created not just matter but motion, energy, and flow — four forces determined the structure of the first leylines simultaneously: the Strike of Will (the Glyph of Will) set the first Trunk Leyline (the Origin Flow) into motion; the Loom of Threads (Glyph of Threads) wove leylines into structured paths; the First Collapse (Glyph of Endings) broke leylines into weaker conduits; the Restorer's Breath (Glyph of Echoes) ensured faded leylines could reform.
