# Neldros, The Rootfather

*"I do not speak. I do not move. I do not command. But I remember."*

**Domain**: trees, ancient groves. **Worshiped by**: none formally — a spirit rather than a worshiped god.

The spirit of all great trees, ancient and eternal — does not interfere, but has witnessed every war, every kingdom, every mortal life pass beneath his branches. When a tree lives long enough, it becomes part of him, its memories absorbed into his endless, slow thoughts.

**Myth — The Tree That Watched a Thousand Kings**: a tree older than any throne has seen rulers come and go, armies rise and fall. It does not judge or mourn — only remembers. When the last kingdom falls, it will still be there.

> [!note] Distinct from World Anchors and Thread System's Earth Pantheon Neldros
> A deity also named Neldros is mentioned in passing on [[World Trees]] as an Earth Pantheon figure alongside Vatharun. Treated as the same figure given the shared name and domain (trees/roots), not a contradiction.
