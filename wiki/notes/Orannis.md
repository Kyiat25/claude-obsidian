# Orannis, The Cyclekeeper

*"The river does not stop. The tide does not fail. The rain does not forget. I am the reason why."*

**Domains**: the water cycle, rainfall, renewal, rivers that never cease. **Symbol**: a droplet falling into a whirlpool, endlessly spiraling. **Worshiped by**: farmers, sailors, those who live by rivers and rain. **Rivals**: [[Soltharion]] (the Droughtmaker), Xethar.

Doesn't rule water, ensures it *moves* — without him rain would never fall, rivers would never reach the ocean, tides would remain still. Farmers offer water to the sky before planting, hoping for his blessing; when a river dries, it's said Orannis has turned away, waiting for mortals to remember their respect.
