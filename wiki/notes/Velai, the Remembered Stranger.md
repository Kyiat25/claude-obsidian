# Velai, the Remembered Stranger

**Origin**: a noblewoman who erased her own name using the Glyph of Unbeing. **Behavior**: appears in photographs, portraits, and reflected dreams. **Effect**: begins as an unplaceable familiarity, gradually becomes a trusted memory. **Final phase**: if enough people recall her, she physically manifests, replacing someone beloved.

A **Reflected** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a mirror-entity born from corrupted identity.
