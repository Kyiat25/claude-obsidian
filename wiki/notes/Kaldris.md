# Kaldris, The Hearthmother

*"The greatest fire is not found on the battlefield, nor in the forges of kings. It is found in the hearth, in the warmth of family, in the love that outlasts even war."*

**Domains**: hearth, protection, family, sacred fire. **Symbol**: a golden flame cradled in a hand. **Worshiped by**: mothers, healers, those who seek warmth and protection. **Allies**: [[Ignis]], Pyrris, Kaelith. **Enemies**: Xalros, [[Xethar]], [[The Whispering Dark]].

Fire as home and safety rather than conquest — her flame protects and unites rather than destroys. Myth holds she sheltered the first mortals while the gods waged war in the heavens, keeping the fire burning on the mortal plane so the world would not be swallowed by the dark. Her temples are homes, her altars are fireplaces.
