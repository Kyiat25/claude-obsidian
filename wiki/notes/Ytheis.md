# Ytheis, The Echo That Remains

*"You have lived this before. You will live it again. No matter the choice, the road always returns."*

**Domains**: repeating destinies, cycles of fate, the unbroken thread. **Symbol**: a spiral etched into stone, looping endlessly. **Worshiped by**: seers who believe in reincarnation, those who experience déjà vu. **Allies**: [[Vaithis]], [[Nytheris]], [[Telys]]. **Enemies**: [[Astralis]], Varethis, [[Kaelor]].

Not a god of fate, but of repetition — the echo of choices already made. Those who sense they have lived their life before are said to hear Ytheis in their dreams.
