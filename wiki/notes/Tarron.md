# Tarron, The Crackling Sky

*"The sky does not rage without reason. When I speak, it is not a warning—it is a promise."*

**Domains**: thunder omens, the first sign of war, divine wrath foretold. **Symbol**: a black storm cloud, split by a single bolt of lightning. **Worshiped by**: warriors, storm-seers, warlords, generals who believe in prophecy. **Allies**: [[Veydras]], [[Tahris]], [[Zephir]]. **Enemies**: [[Soltharion]], Keldros, [[Nereus]].

The first thunderclap before the storm — does not bring war, but declares when it is inevitable. Even kings hesitate at his voice.
