# Vorendral, The Forgotten King

**Domain**: the buried dead, unmarked graves, lost kings. **Symbol**: a crumbling throne half-buried in earth. **Worshiped by**: tomb-chroniclers, lost soul cults, mourners of erased legacies.

Rules over souls not claimed by [[Torvax]] — those forgotten by time or purposefully erased. His throne, beneath the tombs, is made from the memories no one speaks of anymore; he does not seek worship, only to be remembered. Linked to [[Karthas]]: where Karthas buries empires, Vorendral buries their rulers. Attributed with the warning quote about [[The Hollow Thread]]: "To speak of the Hollow Thread is to tie your own name to its fray. And the Earth does not suffer loose ends."
