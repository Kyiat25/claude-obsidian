# Varthos, The Nameless One

*"They were worshiped once. Their names were known. Their temples stood tall. And now? Dust, whispers, echoes in the Weave."*

> [!note] Tier labeled inconsistently in source
> The source file lists Varthos under the "Demigods & Spirits" heading but labels his own entry "(Lesser God)" — an internal inconsistency, not resolved here.

**Domains**: forgotten gods, divine obsolescence, the echoes of worship. **Symbol**: a broken crown, half-buried in sand. **Worshiped by**: the last faithful of dying religions, those who seek knowledge of erased gods, cults that attempt to resurrect lost divinities. **Allies**: [[Astralis]], [[Oriveth]], [[Vel'Zyren]]. **Enemies**: [[Aelys]], [[Vaithis]], [[Nytheris]].

The god of lost deities — those who once held power but were abandoned, erased, or devoured by time. Does not seek worship or mourn the forgotten; he simply ensures their remnants persist in the Weave. When a god dies, its last prayers drift to Varthos.
