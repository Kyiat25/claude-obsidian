# Slumbering Moon

*"Do not wake them. Do not dream of them. Do not remember where the dream ends."*

First seen 3rd Age, 877 TA, as an unseasonable harvest-sleep. Felt behind eyelids, not seen with the eyes.

## Nature

The veil between waking and sleeping thins; the world becomes dream-adjacent. [[Ul'Naresh]], The Dream Inverted, breathes into the world — a time when to dream is to open a door.

## World effects

**Dreamveil Manifestation**: sleep runs deeper but is less restful; shared dreams become common in high-magic areas; sleep spells gain unpredictable traits. **False Awakening Waves**: waking up becomes unreliable — a person may rise, speak, and act for hours entirely within the dream-layer. Each long rest carries a 10% chance per dreamer of occurring entirely within [[The Null Reflection]].

**Monster effects**: Dream-Born spirits (Ulzen, Marrowbell, the Quilt-Worm) activate; creatures with legendary actions gain a "dream action" usable only against sleeping or hallucinating opponents; NPCs and beasts mutter shared phrases. There's a 5% nightly chance a creature dreamed by the party manifests for 1d6 hours, often hostile, warped, or incomplete.

## Madness

Short-term: confusion between dream and real memories. Long-term: belief one is currently dreaming. Indefinite: the compulsion to "wake up" by killing or destroying something.

## Glyph interactions and resistance

Dream glyphs (prophecy, sleep-magic, fate-threads) oscillate or spontaneously duplicate; Spirit glyphs guiding souls may redirect toward Null Reflection doorways; teleportation and plane shift have a 15% chance of routing through a dreamscape shard, causing brief misplacement. The Echoed Mother becomes overwhelmed, weaving rebirths from dreams rather than truth; [[Vaelora]] is said to weep black foam under this moon, remembering the ocean's first sleep; the Restbringer retreats entirely, and some who die "dream their way back" no longer resembling who they were.

## Campaign use

Inception-style dream delving; the emergence of a new Dream-Born spirit; half the world believing it never actually woke up.
