# Dwarf

*"What is built must last. What lasts must be true."*

**Origin myth**: per the Stone-Song Codex, dwarves were constructed, not sculpted, by the Forgebound (divine artisans who built the world's foundations) — endurance from bedrock, will from molten veins, strength from buried ore. Codified in [[The Deep Law]]'s six oaths (Stone, Forge, Clan, Silence, Echo, Final). Dwarves acknowledge but don't worship the Forgebound — "gods may forget you, but the mountains will remember."

**Physical**: mature by 50, fully "adult" at 100, live 350–450 years; Medium, 4–5 ft. but dense (150–250 lb), 25 ft. speed.

## Traits

- **Darkvision** 60 ft.
- **Dwarven Resilience**: advantage + resistance vs. poison
- **Dwarven Endurance**: +1 DR all locations; advantage vs. prone/forced movement
- **Stonecunning**: double proficiency bonus on History checks about stonework origin
- **Tool Proficiency**: one artisan's tool set of choice
- **Languages**: Common + Dwarvish

**Mental**: Focus +0 / Sanity +1 (bred-in stubbornness resists despair/manipulation).

**DCS Integration**: Dodge −1 / Endure +1 / Deflect +0; DR +2 (stacks with armor). Advantage on Rally, higher casualty threshold to trigger Breaking Point.

## Corruption: The Tainted Foundation

Advantage vs. Corruption from mundane sources (curses, mortal rituals); **disadvantage** vs. geological sources — Eldritch energies from deep earth, corrupted leylines, things sealed beneath mountains (direct tie to [[The Hollow Thread]]). At Corruption 10+: Stone-Deafness (Stonecunning fails), Foundation Crack (−1 DR), Oath-Weight. **Fractured Foundation table** (1d6): False Memory, Ancestral Static (Dwarven Resilience disabled), Foundation Tremor (1d4 psychic + prone disadvantage), Weight of Failure (Sanity save or −1d4), Eldritch Resonance (+1 Whisper Point, Stonecunning reveals something disturbing), or Deep Law Holds (Corruption −1).

## Cultural touchstones

Living Locks — dwarves who voluntarily seal themselves in corrupted chambers to guard what should not wake, directly tied to [[The Hollow Thread]] and the **Silence Chain** enacted after Ashkarra's fall. Respects [[Gaeldros]] (law/oaths) and [[Thamur]] (sealing/containment) without worshipping the Faith of the Bound Pattern outright.
