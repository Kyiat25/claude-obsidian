# Ulthar, The Unwritten Force

*"The Weave is not a tool. It is not a law. It is power, pure and unbound."*

**Domains**: the Weave's raw power, uncontrolled magic, the force behind all things. **Symbol**: a chaotic mass of shifting glyphs, never forming a single pattern. **Worshiped by**: sorcerers, mages who fear the Weave's true force. **Allies**: Zeythis, [[Aelys]], Tyralis. **Enemies**: Astralis, [[Nyzara]], Oriveth.

Not a being with intention — the raw force of the Weave itself, neither creation nor destruction. Where Aelys ensures magic remains structured, Ulthar simply exists, untamed and unstoppable; to touch his energy is to risk being consumed by the fabric of existence itself. Per [[Vel'Zyren]]'s own material, she was fascinated by the raw power he represents and sought to harness it, though he neither helps nor hinders her.
