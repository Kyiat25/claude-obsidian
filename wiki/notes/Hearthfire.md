# Hearthfire, The Keeper of Warm Nights

*"Fire is not just for war. Fire is for home. Fire is for those who gather around it, share stories, and remember why they are alive."*

**Domains**: home, hearth, celebration. **Symbol**: a small cooking fire, always glowing. **Worshiped by**: halflings, innkeepers, families, traveling merchants. **Enemies**: [[Mordrath]] (Fire's death), [[Xethar]] (destruction).

Unlike [[Soltharion]] (fire as control) or Xethar (fire as destruction), Hearthfire sees fire as warmth, safety, and storytelling. Halflings believe the first fire was made not for war but for a gathering, a meal, a song; his temples are homes, his priests are storytellers, his rituals are feasts and laughter. Unlike most Fire gods he demands no tribute — only that his fire never goes out. A hearth gone cold in a halfling home is considered a terrible omen, a sign the house has lost its soul.
