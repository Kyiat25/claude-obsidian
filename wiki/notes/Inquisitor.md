# Inquisitor — Rank 1-4

*"You investigate and root out heresy, corruption, and supernatural threats to faith."* Batch 3, Specialized Religious category. Broader mandate than [[Witch Hunter]] (Batch 2), which is vampire-adjacent dark-magic-specific — Inquisitor covers heresy, church corruption, and Corruption Score itself, with an explicit mechanical tie to [[Corruption]] this batch's Witch Hunter never had.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Junior Inquisitor | Inquisitorial Authority | Expose a heretic, uncover a cult, or prove your investigative skills |
| 2 | Full Inquisitor | Hunter of Heresy | Break a major cult network, identify corruption in church hierarchy, or survive assassination by heretics |
| 3 | Grand Inquisitor | Hammer of Faith | Destroy an ancient cult, purge major corruption from the church, or uncover demonic infiltration |
| 4 | Supreme Inquisitor | Purifier Supreme | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Junior Inquisitor

**Skill Proficiency**: Insight, Investigation. **Language**: one of choice. **Equipment**: a holy symbol, manacles, a book of heresy records, an inquisitor's badge, traveler's clothes, 15 gp.

**Feature — Inquisitorial Authority**: question people about religious matters, who must answer truthfully or face consequences; advantage on Insight checks to detect lies about faith; detain suspects temporarily; access church records on heretics and cults; other religious authorities recognize your position.

**Holdings**: quarters in a temple or church; a church stipend (10 gp/month); access to church resources; a mentor inquisitor; authority to investigate within your region.

## Rank 2: Full Inquisitor

**Holdings**: a personal office in the church; a salary (30 gp/month); authority across the diocese; 2-3 guards who assist you; you can call on local authorities for help; access to church prisons; torture implements, if your order uses them.

**Feature Expansion — Hunter of Heresy**: cast *zone of truth* once per long rest; advantage on all checks investigating religious crimes; identify individuals with a high [[Corruption]] score; once per week, detain anyone suspected of heresy; your interrogations are frighteningly effective.

## Rank 3: Grand Inquisitor

**Holdings**: an inquisitorial headquarters; a major salary (90 gp/month); authority across multiple regions; 10-15 junior inquisitors under you; you can requisition military support; access to the church's most secret records; your word can condemn or absolve.

**Feature Expansion — Hammer of Faith**: sense corrupted individuals within 60 ft; advantage on saves against Corruption; excommunicate individuals, cutting them off from church support; once per month, organize a heresy purge in a town; your investigations are thorough and feared.

## Rank 4: Supreme Inquisitor

**Holdings**: a cathedral dedicated to the inquisition; an excellent salary (200 gp/month); authority across an entire nation; 40+ inquisitors in your organization; you can command church military forces; your judgments are final; secret archives under your control.

**Feature — Purifier Supreme**: immune to Corruption; sense any corrupted individual within 1 mile; once per year, organize a region-wide purge; your presence terrifies heretics and cultists; perform exorcisms and remove curses as a high-level cleric. Even bishops fear your authority, cults flee regions where you operate, and you may have a divine mandate from the faith itself.
