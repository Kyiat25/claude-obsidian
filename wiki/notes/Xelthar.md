# Xelthar, The Whisper Beyond Time

*"There are things that should not be known. But if you listen, I will tell you."*

**Domains**: forbidden visions, prophetic madness, the cost of knowledge. **Symbol**: a shifting eye that never stays the same. **Worshiped by**: oracles, seers, those who glimpse too much. **Allies**: Zeythis, Nyxaris (Air), [[Astralis]]. **Enemies**: [[Nyzara]], [[Aelys]], [[Vaithis]].

The god of visions that break the mind — doesn't deceive, reveals, but some knowledge should never be known. Prophets who listen often go mad, cursed with truths too vast to comprehend; his priests aren't scholars but the ones who write warnings in places no one should ever find.
