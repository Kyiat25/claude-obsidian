# Xethar, The Wildfire

*"No king owns the flame. No god commands the inferno. Fire does not bow. It rises. It burns."*

**Domains**: tempest, war, chaos, freedom. **Symbol**: a wildfire consuming a city. **Worshiped by**: rebels, warlords, those who reject rule. **Enemies**: [[Soltharion]], [[Vaeldros]], [[Tharamis]].

The enemy of all rulers — believes [[Ignis]] and [[Soltharion]] corrupted Fire into a tool for kings and tyrants, and leads an eternal rebellion against order. Burns down temples of Soltharion, spreads influence among the oppressed, and respects [[Zerah]]'s knowledge, believing that revealing Fire's true history will crumble its rulers. His eternal war against [[Soltharion]] — the Empire and the Wildfire, the Cycle of Flame and Ash — is described as fire's most fundamental conflict: every Fire-worshiping king claims Soltharion's favor, every uprising claims Xethar's blessing, and no kingdom that rules by Fire has ever lasted, since Fire's nature is always to destroy itself. The Dragonborn's lost empire and the Dwarves' shattered kingdom are both cited as casualties of this cycle. [[Air Pantheon|Air]]'s Zephir constantly undermines Soltharion's empire in Xethar's name; [[Water Pantheon|Water]] fears Xethar's fire most of all, since storms rage when this war ignites.
