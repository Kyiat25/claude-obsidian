# Tylos, The River-Seeker

*"Follow the water, and you will never be lost."*

**Domains**: rivers as paths, destiny shaped by water, journeys that never end. **Symbol**: a winding river splitting in two directions. **Worshiped by**: wanderers, travelers, lost souls seeking direction. **Rivals**: [[Kelphor]], Orynthos, Rythos. **Allies**: [[Marethis]], Ithrien, Zerathis.

Unlike [[Kelphor]], who embodies wandering without destination, Tylos ensures even the lost have a path to follow — the reason lost ships find their way home. Those who refuse to follow the water's signs are forever lost.
