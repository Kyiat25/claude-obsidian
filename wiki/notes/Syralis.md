# Syralis, The Silver Current

*"Fortune moves like the tide—it flows, it shifts, and it never stays with one man for too long."*

**Domains**: oceanic trade, the movement of wealth, commerce at sea. **Symbol**: a silver coin resting on a wave. **Worshiped by**: merchants, traders, navigators, ship captains. **Rivals**: [[Lyvessa]], [[Voraxis]], [[Jorvethis]]. **Allies**: [[Kaemor]], [[Marethis]], Selmira, [[Ithraval]].

Ensures wealth remains in motion, never hoarded, always exchanged — he does not decide who is rich or poor, only whose fortune the ocean allows to flow. Merchants toss a coin into the water before each voyage to secure his favor.
