# Corruption Warden — Rank 1-4

*"You monitor and contain the spread of Eldritch corruption."* Batch 3, FEARS-Specific category. The single most direct background-to-hub-mechanic tie across all three batches — this background's entire premise is built on the existing [[Corruption]] score, and its Rank 1 feature explicitly states it requires understanding "the relationship between Corruption and [[Whisper Engine]]," the first source anywhere in this vault to connect those two concepts directly.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Corruption Watcher | Corruption Sense | Identify a corrupted individual before they harm others, help someone resist Corruption, or survive Corruption exposure yourself |
| 2 | Quarantine Warden | Corruption Expert | Contain a Corruption outbreak, prevent a corrupted individual from spreading taint, or develop a helpful treatment |
| 3 | Senior Warden | Corruption Containment | Stop a Corruption epidemic, save a corrupted individual thought lost, or uncover a Corruption cult |
| 4 | Grand Warden | Master of the Taint | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Corruption Watcher

**Skill Proficiency**: Insight, Medicine. **Language**: Deep Speech or another exotic language. **Equipment**: a holy symbol or protective amulet, a journal for recording corruption cases, silver-plated manacles, a healer's kit, common clothes, 10 gp.

**Feature — Corruption Sense**: advantage on Insight checks to detect corrupted behavior; recognize signs of advanced Corruption (physical mutations, mental changes); know quarantine and observation procedures; understand the relationship between [[Corruption]] and [[Whisper Engine]]; provide basic counsel to early-stage corrupted individuals.

**Holdings**: quarters in a church or government facility; a basic stipend (10 gp/month); access to Corruption records; a mentor warden; authority to question suspected corrupted individuals.

## Rank 2: Quarantine Warden

**Holdings**: an office with a secure observation room; a salary (30 gp/month); authority to quarantine individuals; protective equipment against Corruption; access to Corruption treatment facilities; 2-3 guards who assist you; records of all local Corruption cases.

**Feature Expansion — Corruption Expert**: assess someone's Corruption level accurately, within 1 tier; advantage on saves against gaining Corruption; identify the source of Corruption (what exposure caused it); once per week, help someone resist Corruption progression (advantage on their check); know signs of an impending Corruption crisis.

## Rank 3: Senior Warden

**Holdings**: a quarantine facility under your command; a major salary (80 gp/month); authority across the region; 10-15 junior wardens; you can requisition military for containment; access to dangerous Eldritch research; a network monitoring Corruption across the area.

**Feature Expansion — Corruption Containment**: resistance to the effects of Corruption; perform rituals slowing Corruption progression; advantage on all checks related to Corruption; once per month, contain a major Corruption event, preventing spread; sense concentrations of Corruption within 1 mile; know the relationship between Corruption and [[Eldritch Moons in Combat|Eldritch Moons]].

## Rank 4: Grand Warden

**Holdings**: a national Corruption monitoring network; an excellent salary (200 gp/month); authority across the nation; 40+ wardens in your organization; quarantine facilities and treatment centers; access to forbidden Corruption research; you advise rulers on Corruption policy.

**Feature — Master of the Taint**: immune to gaining Corruption, though the DM may override this for truly extreme events; sense corrupted individuals within a 5-mile radius; once per year, cure someone of Corruption, reducing it by 2 tiers; identify Eldritch entities by their Corruption signature; may be able to track Corruption to its source. Rulers depend on your Corruption reports, your treatments save countless lives, and you may understand why the gods' death released Corruption in the first place.
