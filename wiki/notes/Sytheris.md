# Sytheris, The Lingering Choice

*"You have chosen. But do you wonder what could have been?"*

**Domains**: lost destinies, fate's regrets, the weight of what-ifs. **Symbol**: a branching path that twists back into itself. **Worshiped by**: those haunted by past choices, those who believe they were meant for another life. **Allies**: [[Vaithis]], [[Elyon]], [[Telys]]. **Enemies**: [[Nytheris]], [[Ytheis]], [[Kaelor]].

Governs the roads never taken. Does not change or break fate; she simply ensures mortals and gods alike never forget what they did not choose.
