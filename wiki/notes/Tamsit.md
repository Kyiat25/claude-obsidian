# Tamsit, The Recording Hand

**Domains**: writing, records, the written-down, ink's modest honesty. **Symbol**: a quill laid across a closed ledger.

The positive act of writing, held — junior to [[Nyzara]] (memory) and opposite Earth's Thamur (erasure), with one sharp doctrine that keeps him humble and keeps him hired: a record is not the truth, a record is a witness — it says only "someone wrote this then," and that, honestly kept, is worth more than truth-claims. Scribes touch his sign before signing anything; his standing, cheerful feud with the storyteller-god Wendrel (the written version against the living one) plays out as the whole tension of the setting's historiography, fought between two minor gods over what both insist is a small professional difference.
