# Grave Keeper — Rank 1-4 (New, Gothic Horror)

*"You tend to the dead and keep watch over burial grounds."* Tier 4 (Niche, gothic horror).

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Undertaker's Assistant | Last Rites | Properly inter someone important, or prevent a corpse from rising |
| 2 | Grave Keeper | Guardian of the Dead | Defeat a powerful undead creature, or prevent a mass raising |
| 3 | Master Grave Keeper | Shepherd of Souls | Consecrate a mass grave after a massacre, or destroy a necromancer's operation |
| 4 | Death's Warden | Bulwark Against Death | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Undertaker's Assistant

**Skill Proficiency**: Medicine, Religion. **Tool Proficiency**: mason's tools. **Equipment**: shovel, holy symbol, jar of salt, bell, book of funerary rites, common clothes, 10 gp.

**Feature — Last Rites**: you can perform funerary ceremonies that give the deceased peaceful rest; advantage on Religion checks related to burial customs and undead; you know the signs of undead activity.

**Holdings**: a small cottage near a graveyard; access to the graveyard and crypt; basic undertaking tools.

## Rank 2: Grave Keeper

**Holdings**: a keeper's house on graveyard grounds; modest income from burial fees (15 gp/month); a complete set of undertaking tools; access to blessed ground; a network of other grave keepers.

**Feature Expansion — Guardian of the Dead**: you can sense undead within the graveyard (30 ft radius); advantage on attacks against undead in your graveyard; you can create holy water (1 vial/day); you know if a grave has been disturbed.

## Rank 3: Master Grave Keeper

**Holdings**: a large keeper's house; good income from donations and services (60 gp/month); 3-5 assistants; authority over cemetery operations; access to old burial records; a blessed or silver weapon from the church.

**Feature Expansion — Shepherd of Souls**: you can perform rituals that prevent undead rising (consecration lasting 1 month); advantage on all checks related to undead; you can speak with spirits in your graveyard (as *speak with dead*); undead will not willingly enter your graveyard.

## Rank 4: Death's Warden

**Holdings**: authority over all graveyards in the region; major income (150 gp/month); a staff of 20+ grave keepers; a fortress-like cemetery with ancient crypts; a repository of death records going back centuries; holy relics that protect against undead.

**Feature — Bulwark Against Death**: immune to fear from undead; you can turn undead as a cleric of your level; once per month, perform a ritual that prevents ALL undead activity in a 1-mile radius for 1 week; you can identify any undead creature and know its weaknesses. Rulers seek your counsel on necromancy and undead threats, other grave keepers come to train under you, your presence blesses the land (undead can't rise naturally), and legends say death itself respects you.
