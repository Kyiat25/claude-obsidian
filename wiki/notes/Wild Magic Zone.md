# Wild Magic Zone

`status: excluded`. One of the four possible outcomes of a Critical Spell Hit on [[Unstable Magic Zones]] (the Focus-Check-failure result). A place where magic behaves unpredictably rather than reliably — every spell or magical class ability requires a **[[Focus]] Saving Throw** (`1d20 + Focus modifier`, DC typically 15-20+).

## Resolution

Failure triggers a Wild Magic Surge; a natural 1 rolls two surges that may combine; success lets the spell work normally but with strange cosmetic effects; a natural 20 grants advantage on saves and Focus rolls for 1 minute. Each failure raises the zone's DC by +1 until a success or the caster exits — cumulative risk the longer a caster stays. Failing the Focus Save also forces a Concentration Check even on spells that normally wouldn't require one.
