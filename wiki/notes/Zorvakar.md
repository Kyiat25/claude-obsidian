# Zorvakar, The Deep Hoarder

**Domain**: greed, buried wealth, the curse of gold. **Symbol**: a spiral of gold sinking into stone. **Worshiped by**: greedy kings, ambitious dwarves, cursed prospectors.

The whisper beneath the mountain that draws men to ruin through obsession — he does not hoard gold, he *is* the gold, and he chooses who will never leave it. Dwarves believe those who mine without purpose risk awakening him.
