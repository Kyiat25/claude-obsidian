# Primal Glyphs

The immutable, first-Law Glyphs — the core laws of reality, existing before even the gods, embodied one each by the four Primal Forces already documented in depth on their own pages. Cataloged here as a single reference table rather than four separate pages, since each glyph's content lives entirely on its bearer's existing entity page.

| Glyph | Bearer | Cosmic influence |
|---|---|---|
| **Glyph of Will** | [[Astralor]] | Governs the force of creation itself; leylines emerge through it, binding reality to Astralor's Forge |
| **Glyph of Threads** | [[Aeloria]] | Represents Aeloria's Loom; dictates how leylines and fate weave through reality |
| **The Glyph of Endings** | [[The Whispering Dark]] | Determines the decay of leylines and concepts, ensuring entropy |
| **The Glyph of Echoes** | [[The Restorer]] | Ensures leylines and lost things never fully vanish, and can be reforged |

These glyphs are immutable — mortals and deities can manipulate leylines, but not rewrite the fundamental laws the Primal Glyphs express. [[Weave and Magic]] and [[Weave of Creation]] both restate this exact four-glyph structure with no material difference from [[How the Five Layers Shape the Creation World]]'s original framing.
