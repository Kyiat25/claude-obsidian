# Poisoner (Assassin) — Rank 1-4

*"You're trained in the art of death through poison and silent killing."* Batch 3, Criminal Specialist category. Source dual-names this "Poisoner / Assassin"; filed under the first name. Notably the only background across all three batches that scales all the way to "immunity to all poisons" and offers to literally kill on behalf of the player character as a stated capstone feature.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Apprentice Poisoner | Toxic Knowledge | Successfully poison a target, survive being poisoned yourself, or create a new poison formula |
| 2 | Professional Assassin | Death's Touch | Complete a legendary assassination, kill a magically protected target, or become preferred assassin for a powerful organization |
| 3 | Master Assassin | Silent Death | Kill someone thought unkillable, survive an assassination attempt by a rival, or refuse a contract from a god |
| 4 | Death's Shadow | Master of Murder | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Apprentice Poisoner

**Skill Proficiency**: Medicine, Stealth. **Tool Proficiency**: poisoner's kit. **Equipment**: poisoner's kit, 3 vials of basic poison, dark clothes, a dagger, 15 gp.

**Feature — Toxic Knowledge**: identify poisons by sight or smell; advantage on checks to resist poison; apply poison to weapons without risk to yourself; know how to acquire or create basic poisons; estimate the time until a poison takes effect.

**Holdings**: a hidden laboratory; a supply of basic poisons and antidotes; a mentor or guild contact; knowledge of poison suppliers.

## Rank 2: Professional Assassin

**Holdings**: a safe house with a hidden lab; income from contracts (45 gp/month average); an arsenal of poisons (contact, ingested, inhaled); a network of informants providing intelligence; quality weapons and equipment; access to rare poisons through contacts; several successful contracts completed.

**Feature Expansion — Death's Touch**: create poisons of various types and effects; advantage on Stealth checks for assassination approaches; analyze how someone died (identify poison or method); once per week, create a poison that leaves no trace; your poison attacks deal extra damage (1d6).

## Rank 3: Master Assassin

**Holdings**: multiple safe houses across cities; major income (150 gp/month); a laboratory with rare ingredients; a network of 20+ agents and informants; you can create any poison given time; several identities and covers; contracts from nobility and criminal lords.

**Feature Expansion — Silent Death**: create poisons that perfectly mimic natural death; poison targets in seemingly impossible ways; advantage on all checks related to assassination; once per month, poison a target without being anywhere near them, via agents and a complex plan; immune to all poisons; identify magical protections against assassination.

## Rank 4: Death's Shadow

**Holdings**: a network of safe houses across the continent; excellent income (400 gp/month when working); you can create impossible poisons; an organization of 30+ assassins under your command; clients including rulers and gods' servants; your very name causes fear; legend says you've never failed.

**Feature — Master of Murder**: create a poison that kills anything, within reason, DM discretion; once per year, create a poison bypassing magical protections; kill without touching, being seen, or leaving evidence; automatic success on Stealth checks for assassination approach (DC 25 or lower); your presence is death — targets you've marked sometimes die of "natural causes." Kingdoms fear you, other assassins worship or fear you, and you can demand any price for your services.
