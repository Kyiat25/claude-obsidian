# Nereus, The Tide-King

*"You may leave the sea. But the sea will never leave you."*

**Domains**: the tides, fate, the eternal pull of the ocean. **Symbol**: a spiral wave endlessly looping back upon itself. **Worshiped by**: seafarers, those who believe in destiny. **Rivals**: [[Soltharion]], Mordrath. **Allies**: [[Selmira]] (moon-tides), Marethis.

Inevitability itself — proof the ocean will always return. Does not rage or destroy, but will not be denied; ensures the balance of rise and fall is never broken. Sailors believe defying the tides is defying fate; a ship vanishing without a storm is said to have been "called home" by Nereus.

**Myth — Fire's War Against Water**: when [[Soltharion]] tried to boil the oceans, Water did not fight back — it waited. Lakes turned to steam, rivers dried to dust, but Fire cannot last; when its hunger spent itself, Water returned first a trickle, then a torrent. Fire claimed victory; Water didn't need to. In a second confrontation, Nereus and [[Vaelora]] pulled Soltharion into the abyss rather than fighting him in the open, nearly drowning him for the first time in his existence — he escaped, forever changed, and Fire never ruled the world alone again.
