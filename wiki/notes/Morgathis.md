# Morgathis, The Leviathan of Fear

*"You do not know what lies below. And that is why you are afraid."*

**Domains**: the unknown terror of the deep, paranoia upon the water, the fear of what is below. **Symbol**: a single ripple in the water, with nothing visible beneath it. **Worshiped by**: feared by all, placated by few. **Rivals**: [[Vaelora]], [[Jorvethis]], Rythos. **Allies**: Nyxthys, [[Voraxis]], Veylith.

Does not hunt or kill — he simply reminds you that something might. His power is fear itself, freely supplied by mortals staring at still water and wondering what watches them.
