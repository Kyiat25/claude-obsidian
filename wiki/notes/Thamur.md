# Thamur, The Guardian Below

*"Some things should never be known. Some truths should never be spoken. I will ensure that they are not. I do not seal the Vault because I fear it. I seal it because I remember what waits. And I would rather silence the world than let it speak that name again."*

**Domain**: buried knowledge, hidden depths, the truth that must remain sealed. **Symbol**: a sealed stone door with no key. **Worshiped by**: keepers of ancient secrets, tomb-guardians, silent monks. **Allies**: [[Orivak]] (leylines), [[Mourndrel]] (the Warden of the Sealed).

Chief architect of [[The Hollow Thread]]'s seal — see that page for the full four-phase sealing ritual. His temples have no doors, no records, no statues, only silence; those who uncover too much simply find themselves erased. **Myth — The Name That Was Erased**: a god once spoke a name that should never have been spoken; Thamur erased him, his history, his voice, his very existence, so completely that not even the other gods remember him now. He once felt [[The Restorer]]'s touch in the deep stone and built a tomb around it — then forgot what he buried.
