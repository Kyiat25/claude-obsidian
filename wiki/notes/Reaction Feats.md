# Reaction Feats

The fourth independent, non-overlapping feat-system sighting in this wiki, after [[Called Shot Feats]] (14), [[Evasion Feats]] (4), and [[Weapon Mastery Feats]] (2). Four disjoint feat lists from four independent sources is strong evidence FEARS has a genuinely broad feat system spanning every major combat subsystem, not a mechanic invented once and never revisited.

| Feat | Effect |
|---|---|
| Quick Reflexes | Gain +1 reaction per round, usable only for Disarm, Counterattack, or Shield Reactions |
| Expert Blocker | Add Proficiency Bonus to contested rolls when using Shield Deflect or Shield Bash |
