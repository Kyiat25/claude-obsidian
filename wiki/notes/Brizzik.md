# Brizzik, The Mad Flame

*"Mistakes are just successes you haven't understood yet! Now stand back—this might explode!"*

**Domains**: innovation, chaos, discovery. **Symbol**: a fireball encased in gears, mid-explosion. **Worshiped by**: gnomish inventors, alchemists, reckless scientists. **Enemies**: [[Vaeldros]] (order), [[Tharamis]] (controlled craftsmanship).

Represents Fire's chaotic, unpredictable side — never part of the High Flame, but "accidentally" self-invented when a Divine Experiment went wrong. Thrives on failed experiments and accidental breakthroughs; Gnomes love him, but no one can predict when his blessings will work or explode. To Gnomes, every failed invention and blown-up lab is a form of prayer to Brizzik — the greatest cultural honor is making something explode, then doing it bigger.
