# Dverrik, The Gemlord

*"A diamond is worth nothing in the dark. But the moment it is seen, men will kill for it."*

**Domain**: precious stones, hoarded wealth. Ensures gems remain rare and valuable — doesn't curse the greedy, simply ensures their greed consumes them. Some say every gemstone has its own fate, and it's Dverrik who decides where it will rest.

**Myth — The Ruby That Killed a Hundred Men**: a ruby has never remained with the same owner for long; every man who has held it has died. And still, they chase it.
