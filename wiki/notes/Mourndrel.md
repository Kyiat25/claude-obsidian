# Mourndrel, The Warden of the Sealed

*"You do not want to know what is buried beneath you. You do not want to unearth what I have locked away. And if you do, I will bury you with it."*

Guardian of all things that must never be freed. Does not punish those who seek knowledge — simply ensures some knowledge cannot be found. When a ruin is sealed, a name erased from history, a god forgotten, it is his work. Some believe he guards the corruption beneath the [[Leylines|leylines]] specifically; some say even [[Vel'Zyren]] fears to step near his domain; some say he was once a god who sealed himself away so the world would not remember him.

**Myth — The City That Disappeared**: a kingdom sought the forbidden and dug too deep. Mourndrel didn't destroy or punish them — he simply sealed their entire city beneath the land, where it's said to still stand, empty, waiting, untouched by time.
