# Universal Glyphs

Second-Law Glyphs that don't shape the Creation World directly but keep whatever emerges from it coherent — regulators of the Weave rather than creators or destroyers. Each is personified as a named "Universal Force."

| Glyph / Force | Cosmic influence |
|---|---|
| **The Glyph of Understanding** (Keeper of Knowledge) | Records lessons across cycles so mistakes don't repeat; prevents lost leylines from being fully erased |
| **The Glyph of Life** (The Wildheart) | Prevents ley lines and reality from becoming too rigid, maintaining magic's wild potential |
| **The Glyph of Motion** (Tide Between) | Governs cyclical balance, ensuring leylines shift rather than stagnate |
| **The Glyph of Shadows** (The Veiled Hand) | Hides certain leylines and forbidden truths, ensuring some magic remains unknowable |
| **The Glyph of Dreaming** (The Lantern Below) | Seeds new leylines through vision, prophecy, or subconscious creation — paths imagined before they're real |

None of these five forces directly interfere in mortal affairs, but their presence ensures no single power can dominate magic entirely. [[Veiled Star]] is explicitly linked to (but distinct from) this layer — described as "the reason those Glyphs do not destroy those who bear them," particularly Shadows/Veiled Hand, Dreaming/Lantern, and Understanding.
