# Aasimar

*"You are not the light. You are the shadow it casts when truth hides its face."*

**Origin myth — The Star-Marked**: per the Oath of the Veiled Star, Aasimar aren't celestial-blessed but born of divine *restraint* — the [[Veiled Star]] is a pre-cosmic force older than the gods that refused to speak too soon, never woven or worshipped, watching for the moment intervention becomes necessary. Aasimar are that restraint made flesh: not divine champions but "divine restraint," meant to act when prophecy screams too loud or the Eldritch breach and no one else can. Cross-references [[Veiled Star]] (a `confidence: low`, not-upgraded-to-canon existing wiki page — worth checking for a naming/concept overlap in a future pass).

**Physical**: matures ~20 (calling often arrives later — 30s/50s+), lives 160–180 years; Medium, 5–6½ ft., 30 ft. speed; unsettling rather than beautiful (glowing eyes, shifting skin patterns, wrong-moving shadows, constellation-shaped Star-Marks).

## Traits

- **Darkvision** 60 ft.
- **Celestial Resistance**: resistance to necrotic and radiant damage
- **Healing Hands**: action, touch heals HP = your level, 1/long rest
- **Light Bearer**: knows *light* cantrip (CHA)
- **Veiled Purpose** *(cultural)*: Insight proficiency, 1/long rest double proficiency bonus on an Insight check
- **Languages**: Common + Celestial

**Mental**: Focus +0 / Sanity +1 (celestial spark shields the mind from prophetic/fragmentary revelation).

**DCS Integration**: Dodge +0 / Endure +1 / Deflect +0; DR +0. Advantage vs. fear, +1 to Breaking Point DCs — but more vulnerable to mental breaks once Corruption reaches them through prophecy.

## Corruption: The Screaming Star

Eldritch Moons specifically target Aasimar, trying to turn veiled truth into truth too pure to survive. At Corruption 10+: Vision-Bleed, Star-Burn (mark becomes painful), Clarity-Madness. **Veiled Fracture table** (1d6): False Prophecy, Star-Wound (Healing Hands disabled), Purpose-Void (1d4 psychic + lose Insight), Weight of Clarity (Sanity save or −1d4), Eldritch Touch (+1 Whisper Point, Veiled Purpose backfires), or Star Holds (Corruption −1).
