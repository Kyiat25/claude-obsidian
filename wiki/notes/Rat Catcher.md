# Rat Catcher — Rank 1-4 (Urban Pest Controller)

*"In a grim world, you deal with the vermin—both literal and metaphorical."* Tier 3 (Dark Fantasy). Fulfills the "Rat Catcher" slot [[FEARS Advanced Backgrounds]] flagged as not-yet-developed after Batch 1. The most mundane-sounding premise in either batch, but scales to genuinely significant urban-underworld authority by Rank 4.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Vermin Handler | Urban Vermin | Clear a major infestation, discover something important in the sewers, or survive an encounter with giant rats or ratmen |
| 2 | Master Rat Catcher | Sewer Expertise | Discover a major criminal operation through your work, clear an "impossible" infestation, or save a district from plague-carrying vermin |
| 3 | Chief Rat Catcher | Lord of the Underside | Prevent a plague, expose a major criminal operation, or defeat a ratman warren threatening the city |
| 4 | Urban Undermarshal | Master of the Deep Dark | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Vermin Handler

**Skill Proficiency**: Animal Handling, Stealth. **Tool Proficiency**: poisoner's kit. **Equipment**: 6 rat traps, a cage for holding rats, thick leather gloves, a long pole with a hook, common clothes, 5 gp.

**Feature — Urban Vermin**: navigate sewers and the undercity; advantage on checks to track or catch rats, insects, and small vermin; know where vermin infestations occur; use rats to gather information (follow them to find hidden places).

**Holdings**: a small cart for carrying equipment; contacts among other rat catchers; knowledge of sewer systems.

## Rank 2: Master Rat Catcher

**Holdings**: a workshop/storage for equipment; income from contracts (15 gp/month); a network of 5-8 contacts in the undercity; quality equipment (better traps, stronger poisons); trained rat-catching dogs or ferrets; contracts with wealthy households and businesses.

**Feature Expansion — Sewer Expertise**: know hidden passages through any city; identify and track larger threats (wererats, criminals); advantage on saves against disease; train rats or other vermin with basic commands; your methods clear infestations faster.

## Rank 3: Chief Rat Catcher

**Holdings**: a headquarters with storage and kennels; good income (60 gp/month); a team of 10-15 rat catchers; contracts with the city government; authority over vermin control in your jurisdiction; an information network — you learn everything in the sewers.

**Feature Expansion — Lord of the Underside**: navigate any sewer, tunnel, or hidden passage; command swarms of rats (simple obedience); once per week, your network discovers important information; immune to most diseases; criminals and monsters in the sewers respect/fear you.

## Rank 4: Urban Undermarshal

**Holdings**: a large organization of rat catchers and urban rangers (40+); excellent income (150 gp/month); every sewer and tunnel in multiple cities mapped; contracts with multiple city governments; authority recognized even by the underworld; your methods are adopted universally.

**Feature — Master of the Deep Dark**: navigate any urban underground system automatically; control entire vermin swarms (spies, defenders, or attackers); once per month, your network can expose any secret in the city; immune to all diseases and poisons; urban monsters (wererats, ghouls, etc.) negotiate rather than fight. You know every secret the sewers hold, criminal organizations pay you "insurance," and some cities make you official Undermarshal.
