# Veydras, The Thunder-King

*"You will kneel before me, or I will strike you from the sky."*

**Domains**: unchallenged storms, sky-dominion, lightning's wrath. **Symbol**: a floating, empty crown, shaped from lightning. **Worshiped by**: storm-priests, rulers who fear the sky's judgment. **Enemies**: [[Soltharion]], Keldros, [[Astria]].

Does not serve — commands. Doesn't strike without reason, but when he does nothing stands against him; the storm made god, the sky's answer to those who think they rule the land. Unlike Zephir he doesn't whisper, and unlike Tarron he doesn't warn — he simply unleashes wrath and nothing is left standing. Prayed to in fear, worshiped out of respect, feared by all who build too high.
