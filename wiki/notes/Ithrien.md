# Ithrien, The Tidemother's Blessing

*"Not all who travel the water are taken by it. Some are carried home."*

**Domains**: safe passage, luck upon the water, the gentle flow of the sea. **Symbol**: a cresting wave that never breaks. **Worshiped by**: sailors, fishermen, those who hope for calm waters. **Rivals**: [[Zorveth]], [[Voraxis]], Kelphor. **Allies**: [[Tylos]], Selmira, Kaemor.

The rare mercy of the sea — she does not prevent storms or stop waves, but ensures those who respect the water are not abandoned. Unlike [[Zorveth]] or [[Voraxis]], she demands only humility, not suffering.
