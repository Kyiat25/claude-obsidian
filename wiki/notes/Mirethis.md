# Mirethis, The Tainted Flow

*"You do not see it at first. You drink, you bathe, you build. But in time, you will understand. And by then, it will be too late."*

**Domains**: polluted water, slow rot, disease spread by rivers. **Symbol**: a cup of water with dark tendrils spreading within. **Worshiped by**: feared by healers, riverfolk, those who rely on clean water. **Rivals**: [[Istriel]], [[Itheris]], [[Marethis]]. **Allies**: [[Othrys]], Yltheris, Quoras.

Does not strike suddenly — he spreads. The reason once-thriving rivers turn black. Some say he was once a river god cursed to drink from his own poisoned waters, and now ensures others suffer as he did.
