# Disposition

An NPC's starting attitude toward the player character: **Hostile, Suspicious, Indifferent, Friendly,** or **Helpful**. Affects shopkeeper prices, guard behavior, combat strategy, and quest availability — the hub stat that every other social/mental system in [[A World that Watches]] feeds into.

## Shaped by

- [[Wanted Score]]
- [[Reputation]]
- [[Sanity]] and general mental state
- [[Streetwise]] / Focus (reading and reacting to the environment)
- Recent actions (rescue vs. betrayal, aid vs. threat)

Described as never fixed — shifts with every encounter. No numeric scale or shift-per-action table is given.
