# Vathryn, the Hollowed Child

**Origin**: the grief of a dozen mothers who lost stillborn children under the Slumbering Moon. **Appearance**: a small child of stitched rags and bone with a too-perfect voice. **Effect**: haunts nurseries, replacing dead infants with dream-shells. **Final form**: if allowed to "grow up," becomes a vessel of glyph-unraveling magic.

An **Unfinished** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a being that tried to form but failed.
