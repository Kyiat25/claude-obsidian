# Marrowbell

**Origin**: a bone-forged temple bell cast for a forgotten dream-god. **Behavior**: cannot be seen, only heard. **Effect**: causes joint inversion and phantom dislocations, with rhythmic contractions. **Danger**: its tone crosses realms, resonating across leylines.

A **Dream-Born** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a nightmare that has breached into waking life.
