# Shalsor, the Many-Limbed Echo

*"He mirrored a god once. Now he reflects everyone, just a little bit wrong."*

**Origin**: a mirror-priest who tried to glimpse [[The Whispering Dark]] and shattered into endless refractions. **Appearance**: a humanoid with mismatched limbs, eyes, and voices — all drawn from people it's reflected. **Effect**: whispers truths only the mirrored would know; can mimic voice, posture, and spells, but imperfectly. **Behavior**: speaks as "we," denies individuality, sometimes appears in mirrored armor or glyph-forged windows.

A **Reflected** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a mirror-entity spawned from corrupted identity.
