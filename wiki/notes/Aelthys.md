# Aelthys, The Storm Siren

*"You believe you can sail me? You believe you can command me? Then come, sailor—raise your sails, and let us see."*

**Domains**: tempests, hurricanes, water's rage. **Symbol**: a jagged, spiraling storm over a churning sea. **Worshiped by**: stormcallers, desperate sailors, warriors who embrace chaos. **Rivals**: [[Veydras]], Marethis. **Allies**: [[Voraxis]], Zorveth.

Doesn't punish or protect — simply is. Doesn't rage from anger, but because she cannot be anything else; when a ship vanishes into the storm, it's not because she willed it, but because the sea doesn't care for those who cannot master it. No temples exist to her, only stories of those who faced her and survived.
