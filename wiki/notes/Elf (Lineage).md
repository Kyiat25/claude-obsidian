# Elf

*"We were not made. We arrived when the Pattern required us. And when it changes again, we shall return."*

**Origin myth**: per [[The Woven Reverence]], elves are not divine creations but cosmic harmonics — woven into the Weave before the gods understood the threads they now claim to command, emerging when the Pattern required balance and memory. Elves don't worship the Threadweavers (primordial forces maintaining the tapestry), they *harmonize* with them.

> [!note] Overlap with three standalone lineage files
> This source (`Elf_Lineage_Rewritten.md`) lists Drow, Sea Elf, and Shadar-kai as elf **subraces** with brief entries. All three also exist as separate, much longer standalone lineage files (`Drow_Lineage_Rewritten.md`, `Sea_Elf_Lineage.md`, `Shadar-kai_Lineage.md`) — see [[Drow (Lineage)]], [[Sea Elf (Lineage)]], [[Shadar-kai (Lineage)]] for the fuller, likely-authoritative treatment. Not resolved as a contradiction (the brief subrace entries here are consistent with, not contradictory to, the standalone versions), but the standalone files should be treated as primary.

**Physical**: mature ~20, culturally "adult" at 100, live 750–1,000 years; Medium, 5½–6½ ft., 30 ft. speed.

## Traits

- **Darkvision** 60 ft.
- **Keen Senses**: Perception proficiency
- **Fey Ancestry**: advantage vs. charm; immune to magical sleep
- **Trance**: 4-hour meditation = a full long rest; remain semiconscious/wakeable, ancestral-memory dreams
- **Graceful Evasion**: +1 Dodge Evasion
- **Elven Weapon Training**: longsword, shortsword, shortbow, longbow
- **Languages**: Common + Elvish

**Mental**: Focus +0 / Sanity +0 — no biological mental edge; resilience is *learned* over centuries, not innate (a young elf is as vulnerable as anyone).

**DCS Integration**: Dodge +1 / Endure +0 / Deflect +0; DR +0 — avoidance-based, not durability-based. Advantage on Rally defending long-term-important sites; disadvantage on short-term tactical Rally.

## Corruption: The Frayed Thread

No special resistance; **disadvantage** vs. Corruption from memory-related sources (false memories, corrupted ancestral echoes, attacks through shared cultural memory) — the same Trance pathway that grants wisdom becomes a corruption conduit. **Disturbed Trance table** (1d6, rolled at Corruption 10+ during Trance): False Memory, Ancestral Static (gain exhaustion instead of removing it), Echo Bleed (1d4 psychic, learn info about a nearby creature), Pattern Fracture (Sanity save or −1d4), Whisper Contact (+1 Whisper Point, next Perception auto-fails), or Harmonious Rest (Corruption −1).
