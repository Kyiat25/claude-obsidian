# Seize the Moment

A universal PC reaction that mirrors [[Enemy Rally]] but puts control (and risk) in the players' hands — the mechanical answer to "what do PCs do when the enemy has seized rhythm."

## Mechanics

- **Type**: Reaction, once per round, only **one PC** may attempt it (simultaneous declarations require the players to agree who goes).
- **Trigger**: start of Round 2+, before that round's initiative is determined.
- **Range**: 60 ft — must see or hear allies.
- **Cost**: consumes the reaction (no opportunity attacks, Shield, etc. that round).
- **Check**: `1d20 + Focus modifier` vs. `DC = 10 + Proficiency Bonus + allies currently at negative Momentum`.

| Level | PB | 0 negative | 1 negative | 2 negative | 3+ negative |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1-4 | +2 | 12 | 13 | 14 | 15+ |
| 5-8 | +3 | 13 | 14 | 15 | 16+ |
| 9-12 | +4 | 14 | 15 | 16 | 17+ |
| 13-16 | +5 | 15 | 16 | 17 | 18+ |
| 17-20 | +6 | 16 | 17 | 18 | 19+ |

The DC scales against the party's own state — reliable when things are fine, genuinely hard exactly when the party needs it most.

## Outcomes

| Result | Effect |
|---|---|
| Success | Side acts first regardless of Momentum average; +1 MS; allies within 60 ft get +1 on their first attack |
| Failure | −2 Momentum (floor −2); side acts second; Disadvantage on your first attack |
| Nat 1 | You and allies within 60 ft lose −2 MS (floor −2); side acts second; first enemy to act gets Advantage on their first attack against you |
| Nat 20 | Side acts first; you and allies within 60 ft reset to 0 Momentum; allies get Advantage on their first attack; the first enemy to act has Disadvantage |

A character already at the −2 floor simply can't be pushed lower by a failed attempt. If a table uses the optional Swing Guardrail, Seize the Moment's Momentum changes apply at the initiative step, exempt from the normal ±2/turn cap — a collective morale shift, not an individual action.

## Why Focus, not Charisma

Deliberate design choice: "the battle cry of a tactically brilliant Investigator or a disciplined Monk counts as much as the booming voice of a Paladin. Mental discipline under pressure is what leadership in FEARS measures." Reinforces Focus's role across the cluster — Whisper Points, Enemy Rally, and now this all key off Focus rather than Charisma for "leadership"-flavored checks.

## Optional variants

- **Bloodied Desperation**: +2 to the roll if you're at or below 50% HP, but a failure's Momentum loss (floor −2) hits every ally in range, not just you.
- **Legendary Heroism** (level 17+): once per long rest, use Seize the Moment even if your reaction is already spent.

## Class hooks

| Class | Interaction |
|---|---|
| [[Paladin]] | High Focus + auras make them natural leaders for this roll |
| [[Bard]] | Jack of All Trades adds half PB to the check; Bardic Inspiration helps allies capitalize on the first-attack bonus |
| [[Investigator]] | High Focus and battlefield-reading make timing this a strength |
| [[Warlock]] | Focus-based; a real choice against holding the reaction for other features |
| [[Fighter]] / [[Barbarian]] | Lower Focus means higher risk, but a landed success feels earned |
| [[Rogue]] | Competes with Uncanny Dodge/Sneak Attack windows for the same reaction — a genuine sacrifice |
| [[Monk]] | Competes with Deflect Missiles/Patient Defense; high risk at low Focus |
| [[Ranger]] / [[Druid]] | Moderate Focus; effective mid-level pack leaders |
