# Soldier — Rank 1-4 (Modified from PHB)

*"You served in an organized military force, whether imperial legion, city guard, or mercenary company."* Tier 1 (Core).

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Recruit | Military Rank | Distinguish yourself in battle or complete a major military operation |
| 2 | Veteran | Tactical Mind | Lead a successful military operation or save your unit from disaster |
| 3 | Officer | Rally the Troops | Win a major battle, develop a new tactic adopted by your organization, or thwart an enemy intelligence operation |
| 4 | Commander | Strategic Genius | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Recruit

**Skill Proficiency**: Athletics, Intimidation. **Tool Proficiency**: one gaming set, vehicles (land). **Equipment**: insignia of rank, trophy from a fallen enemy, bone dice, common clothes, 10 gp.

**Feature — Military Rank**: soldiers of your organization recognize your authority; you can invoke rank to gain temporary control of soldiers (if your rank is high enough) or requisition simple equipment for temporary use.

**Holdings**: bunk in a garrison barracks; mess hall access and basic rations; a contact — one veteran who served with you.

## Rank 2: Veteran

**Holdings**: private quarters in garrison; armory access (borrow standard equipment); small stipend (5 gp/month when not deployed); network of 3-5 fellow soldiers who will aid you.

**Feature Expansion — Tactical Mind**: spending 1 minute observing a fortification or defensive position identifies its weak points, optimal approach routes, and estimated garrison strength (±20%).

## Rank 3: Officer

**Holdings**: officer's quarters; command over a squad (8-12 soldiers, use Guard statistics); monthly stipend (20 gp); access to military intelligence networks; right to requisition supplies for your squad.

**Feature Expansion — Rally the Troops**: once per long rest, spend 10 minutes delivering a rousing speech — all allies who hear it gain temporary hit points equal to your level + CHA mod and advantage on their next saving throw against being frightened.

## Rank 4: Commander

**Holdings**: command suite with personal staff; command over a company (80-120 soldiers); major stipend (100 gp/month); influence with military leadership; access to restricted military documents and war councils.

**Feature — Strategic Genius**: once per long rest, call upon military resources — request a squad of elite soldiers (Knights or Veterans) for one mission, gain intelligence reports on any known military force, or requisition military-grade equipment (siege weapons, warhorses, etc.). Rally the Troops now affects up to 50 people and grants advantage on attack rolls for 1 minute.
