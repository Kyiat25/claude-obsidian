# Spy — Rank 1-4 (Intelligence Operative)

*"You gather secrets and information for those who pay—or for causes you believe in."* Tier 1 (Essential). Fulfills the "Spy" slot [[FEARS Advanced Backgrounds]] flagged as not-yet-developed after Batch 1.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Informant | Ear to the Ground | Deliver crucial intelligence, recruit an agent, or successfully maintain deep cover |
| 2 | Field Agent | Professional Spy | Complete a major intelligence operation, run your own spy network, or prevent a significant threat through intelligence work |
| 3 | Spymaster | Master of Shadows | Prevent or cause a war through intelligence, recruit a mole in enemy leadership, or build a network rivaling national organizations |
| 4 | Intelligence Director | Eyes Everywhere | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Informant

**Skill Proficiency**: Deception, Stealth. **Tool Proficiency**: disguise kit, one gaming set or musical instrument. **Language**: one of choice. **Equipment**: disguise kit, fine clothes, common clothes, an invisible ink pen, a small steel mirror, 15 gp.

**Feature — Ear to the Ground**: learn common rumors within 1 hour in any settlement; advantage on Investigation checks to gather information; know how to leave and find dead drops; identify other spies (DC 15 Insight check).

**Specialty** (choose your intelligence focus): Military intelligence, Political intrigue, Criminal intelligence, Economic espionage, Counter-intelligence, Foreign intelligence.

**Holdings**: 2-3 safe houses; a handler or contact who gives you assignments; a simple code for encrypted messages.

## Rank 2: Field Agent

**Holdings**: a network of 8-12 informants; income from operations (30 gp/month); multiple cover identities with documentation; 5-6 safe houses across the region; access to the organization's resources (forged documents, disguises, etc.); encrypted communication methods.

**Feature Expansion — Professional Spy**: create a convincing cover identity in 24 hours; advantage on Deception checks when using a cover identity; identify surveillance (both being followed and placed surveillance); know basic codes and ciphers automatically; once per week, "disappear" completely (untraceable for 48 hours).

## Rank 3: Spymaster

**Holdings**: a network of 30-40 agents and informants; major income (100 gp/month); safe houses and dead drops across multiple regions; access to high-level intelligence; 5-8 field agents who report directly to you; sophisticated encryption and communication systems; blackmail material on important figures.

**Feature Expansion — Master of Shadows**: know major events before they become public; arrange for information to be leaked or suppressed; once per week, organize a major intelligence operation; agents in most important organizations; identify intelligence operations by others.

## Rank 4: Intelligence Director

**Holdings**: a massive spy network (100+ agents); excellent income (300 gp/month); agents in every major organization and government; a hidden headquarters with extensive files; direct access to national leadership; control over counter-intelligence operations; archives of blackmail, secrets, and intelligence going back decades.

**Feature — Eyes Everywhere**: know all major political, military, and criminal developments in your region; once per month, mobilize your network for an operation affecting national security; identify any spy or agent operating in your territory; your intelligence assessments are treated as gospel by leadership; you can "arrange" for nearly anything given time and resources. Governments fear your knowledge, you have leverage over most important figures, other intelligence services try to recruit or eliminate you, and your network will outlast you as an institution.
