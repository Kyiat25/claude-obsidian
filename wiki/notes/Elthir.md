# Elthir, The Flickering Thread

*"Fate does not always speak in clear words. Sometimes, it flickers, uncertain. Follow it at your own risk."*

**Domains**: fate's messengers, the unstable path, the Weave's hidden patterns. **Symbol**: a silver thread that fades in and out of sight. **Worshiped by**: oracles, those who seek divine messages, lost travelers. **Allies**: [[Vaithis]], [[Nytheris]], [[Astria]]. **Enemies**: [[Aelys]], Keldros, [[Astralis]].

Not a god but a messenger — a flickering thread that appears only when fate is uncertain, the reason oracles sometimes see contradictory visions. Carries visions between gods and mortals, but sometimes changes their meaning.
