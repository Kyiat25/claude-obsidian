# The Inkwept Gate

*"You opened a door no one ever closed."*

**Anchor**: a portal ritual that succeeded — but no one recalls opening it; it's just always been there. **Behavior**: a standing gate made of dripping letters, held open by syntax with no origin language. **Effect**: anyone passing through emerges altered, either missing pieces of self (voice, memory, colour) or carrying impossible spells. **Echo risk**: may summon Dream-Born or Reflected Spirits who believe they came from "the other side." **Evolves into**: a [[The Null Reflection]] breach capable of infecting planar glyphs and leylines far beyond its original anchor.

A **Tether** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, bound to a corrupted leyline/glyph point.
