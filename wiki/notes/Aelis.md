# Aelis, The Breath Between Worlds

*"I have seen what will be. I have seen what cannot be. I have seen what should never be. And I cannot tell them apart."*

**Domains**: prophecy, madness, divine inspiration, the boundary between truth and delusion. **Symbol**: a spiraling wind carrying fragments of broken mirrors, each reflecting a different future. **Worshiped by**: oracles who walk the edge of sanity, mad prophets, artists touched by divine madness. **Allies**: [[Astria]] (complicated), [[Zerah]], Nyxaris. **Enemies**: [[Thirian]], Vaeldros, [[Soltharion]].

Rides the breath between prophecy and madness — sees all possible futures simultaneously, unable to distinguish which are true, where [[Astria]] sees fate with clarity. Once worked alongside Astria before shattering under the weight of seeing every possible ending at once. Her prophecies are always true but never clear, arriving in fragments and riddles understood only after they come to pass. Her priests, "the Unraveled," have sacrificed sanity for sight.
