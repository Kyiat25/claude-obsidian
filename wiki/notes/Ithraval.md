# Ithraval, The River's Bargain

*"Water flows freely. But civilization demands order. The river may wander, but the canal has purpose."*

**Domains**: irrigation, engineered waterways, the civilization of rivers. **Symbol**: a river splitting into two controlled channels. **Worshiped by**: farmers, engineers, merchants who rely on river trade, rulers of river-bound cities. **Rivals**: Kelphor, Orynthos, [[Voraxis]]. **Allies**: Syralis, [[Kaemor]], [[Marethis]], [[Orannis]].

Where [[Marethis]] ensures rivers flow freely, Ithraval ensures water is used — controlled for irrigation, trade, and city-building. He does not fight the river; he bargains with it, shaping its path to serve mortal needs without ever fully conquering it.
