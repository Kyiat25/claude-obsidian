# Transformation Systems

*"Power demands a price. The question is whether you pay it in coin, corruption, or your very soul."* A Grim Hollow-inspired FEARS adaptation covering permanent, fundamental changes to a character's nature — becoming vampire, werewolf, angel, or something stranger. Unlike [[Corruption]] (always a looming threat), transformations are **optional chosen paths** granting immense power at terrible cost. Each has 4 progressive stages with escalating power and drawbacks; some transformations replace Terminal Corruption outcomes entirely, others exist independently of the Corruption system; all permanently alter how the character interacts with society once begun. Only the opening structure and catalog of paths were read this pass (of 6,812 lines); each path's full per-stage mechanical detail was not transcribed.

## The nine transformation paths

1. **Seraph** (Angelic Ascension)
2. **Vampirism** (the Blood Curse)
3. **Lycanthropy** (the Beast Curse) — with sub-variants
4. **Lich** (the Undying Scholar) — built around phylactery mechanics core to all its stages
5. **Fiendish Transformation** (Demonic Corruption) — top stage (Demon Prince/Archdevil) is NPC-only
6. **Fey Transformation** (the Wyld Calling)
7. **Aberrant Horror** (Eldritch Apotheosis)
8. **Spectre** (Unfinished Business)
9. **Giant Transformation** (Primordial Heritage)

Each path includes its own Philosophy, Corruption Relationship, staged progression, Thematic Integration with Dór-o Estel, and Risk/Reward Summary sections — a consistent per-path template across all nine.
