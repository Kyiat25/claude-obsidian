# Zorveth, The Maelstrom Lord

*"You cannot calm me. You cannot tame me. You cannot outrun me."*

**Domains**: uncontrollable oceanic wrath, storms that destroy without purpose, chaos of the waves. **Symbol**: a spiralling vortex consuming ships and land alike. **Worshiped by**: feared by all, whispered about by stormcallers. **Rivals**: [[Kaemor]], Rhaldris, Veydras. **Allies**: [[Aelthys]], [[Voraxis]], Rythos.

Unlike [[Aelthys]], who embodies storms' natural fury, Zorveth is chaos itself — unpredictable and unrelenting, serving no god and following no pattern. When he and Aelthys rage together, no ship survives.
