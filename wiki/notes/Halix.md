# Halix, The White Border

**Domains**: salt — preservation, wages, the sealed treaty-meal, the line where sea meets stone. **Symbol**: a white line on grey rock.

Both the Earth and Water Pantheons claim Halix; neither presses the claim — the one *deliberate* shared spirit in the pantheon system, the benign twin of the [[Xilthos]] naming-collision situation, held up by both priesthoods as proof they can share when they choose to (and cited by neither when they don't). Bread and salt shared across a threshold deepens the setting's frith/guest-right custom from guest to friend; salt cast on a sealed bargain "cures" it like a hide. Coastal salterns keep his one feast: the first pan of the season, given back to the sea and the stone in equal halves.
