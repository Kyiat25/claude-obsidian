# Vaithis, The Wandering Star

*"There is always another way. You need only have the courage to seek it."*

**Domains**: cosmic balance, the Weave's forked roads, fate's flexibility. **Symbol**: a distant star flickering between two positions. **Worshiped by**: wanderers, those who seek to escape fate. **Allies**: Nytheris, Telys, [[Nyzara]]. **Enemies**: [[Astria]], Kaelor, [[Vel'Zyren]].

Ensures no prophecy is unbreakable, not through destruction but through choice — doesn't defy or enforce fate, only illuminates paths beyond what's foretold. Unlike Nytheris, who breaks fate outright, Vaithis helps mortals and gods find a way around it.
