# The Ink-Root

**Anchor**: a shattered Weave codex beneath a collapsed library. **Appearance**: black vines etched with shifting glyphs. **Effect**: alters the meaning of written language — changing contracts, spellbooks, and names. **Evolution**: becomes a glyph-mass that rewrites spells in real time.

A **Tether** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, an entity bound to a corrupted leyline or glyph point rather than roaming freely.
