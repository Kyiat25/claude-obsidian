# Sea Elf

*"The ocean remembers everything. We remember the ocean."* — Thessala Memory-Deep, Elder Current-Singer

**Origin myth — The Diving / The Sundering**: per the Abyssal Hymn (see [[The Deepwater Communion]]), 10,000 years ago during the Second Age's God-Tide, elvenkind split — some stayed with the groves and the Pattern (see [[Elf (Lineage)]]), others dove into the ocean rather than be claimed by the new gods, becoming the ocean's sworn librarians and historians. Physically, Sea Elves are almost identical to land elves (same build, ears, features) with exactly four adaptations — gill slits, slit pupils, finger/toe webbing, nictitating membranes — a deliberate authorial choice making the separation *more* tragic, not less: they look like siblings who chose different paths. This is the standalone, fuller treatment of Sea Elf — [[Elf (Lineage)]] also lists it as a brief subrace entry; treat this page as primary.

**Physical**: mature ~100 (mentally "adult" at 150), live 750+ years (some Memory-Keepers 1,000+); Medium, 5'4"–6'2", 30 ft. walk/swim speed.

## Traits

- **Amphibious**: breathe air and water, no penalty in either (best after weekly ocean immersion)
- **Superior Darkvision** 120 ft.
- **Keen Senses**: Perception proficiency
- **Fey Ancestry**: advantage vs. charm, immune to magical sleep
- **Trance**: 4-hr Memory-Drift meditation = long rest, reliving fragments of oceanic history
- **Memory of the Deep**: History proficiency; DC 15 INT (History) in Trance reveals historical visions
- **Current-Reader**: advantage on underwater Survival navigation/tracking; auto-detects water-disturbing hidden creatures within 30 ft.
- **Child of the Sea**: one-way communication with aquatic beasts, who trust you instinctively
- **Languages**: Common + Elvish + Aquan + one extra

**Mental**: Focus **+2** (tied for the highest documented lineage Focus bonus) / Sanity +0 — but vulnerable to **Memory-Sickness**, a culturally-recognized condition from carrying millennia of grief (early: joylessness/solitude; advanced: emotional detachment; terminal: withdrawal, coral-merging, or diving deep and never returning).

**DCS Integration**: Dodge +2 / Endure +0 / Deflect +1; DR +0. Full underwater combat supremacy (no penalties, advantage vs. non-swimmers, automatic ambush detection). Advantage on Rally defending archives/sacred sites; disadvantage when archives are destroyed.

## Corruption: The Memory-Fracture

Corruption enters through fractured memory, stolen knowledge, or forgotten oaths. At Corruption 10+: Memory-Static (Current-Songs jumbled), Archive-Strain, Name-Weight. **Fractured Archive table** (1d6): False Memory, Song-Silence, Archive-Burn (Memory of the Deep disabled), Name-Cascade (Sanity save or −1d4 + grief), Eldritch Whisper (+1 Whisper Point, next INT check at disadvantage), or Memory Holds (Corruption −1).

## Relationship to land elves

The central axis of Sea Elf psychology: recognition and grief in the same instant. A d6 personal-connection table is provided in the source for determining a given Sea Elf's tie to a land-elf NPC/PC: Severed Family, Maintained Contact, First Contact, Old Grudge, Romantic Echo, or Prophecy-Bound.
