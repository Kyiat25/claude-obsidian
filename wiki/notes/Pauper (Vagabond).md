# Pauper (Vagabond) — Rank 1-4 (from Grim Hollow)

*"You're an outcast who has taken to the open road."* Tier 3 (Grim Hollow-inspired dark fantasy). The source notes a separate "Pauper (Beggar)" variant is "already covered in project files" — not part of this source and not yet located elsewhere in this vault.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Outcast | Friendly Face | Survive a major storm on the road or meaningfully participate in a town's internal disputes |
| 2 | Drifter | Road Wisdom | Save the life of one of your contacts or deliver an important message that prevents disaster |
| 3 | Seeker | Wanderer's Network | Play a major role in the downfall of a prominent person, or become known for your role in a historical event |
| 4 | Wanderer | The Walking Chronicle | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Outcast

**Skill Proficiency**: Insight, Survival. **Tool Proficiency**: one gaming set. **Equipment**: walking stick, old wool cloak, iron pot, worn blanket, ragged clothing, 5 cp.

**Feature — Friendly Face**: spending 1 hour in a town and making a DC 10 Wisdom (Insight) check lets you track down an ally from a previous visit — the ally begins friendly and will answer questions about the town, its services, and inhabitants.

**Holdings**: a tarnished compass; a posting from a town seeking able-bodied workers after a disaster.

## Rank 2: Drifter

**Holdings**: news from far parts of your region (not public knowledge); a letter or package from a contact to deliver to another town; a network of 5-8 contacts across the region who offer shelter.

**Feature Expansion — Road Wisdom**: advantage on Survival checks to find food, water, and shelter; you can identify dangerous areas or warn of natural hazards; you know safe routes between towns.

## Rank 3: Seeker

**Holdings**: a lightweight tent and bedroll; the ability to send messages across long distances through your network; a network of 15-20 contacts across multiple regions; a reputation as a reliable messenger/problem-solver.

**Feature Expansion — Wanderer's Network**: you can find shelter in nearly any town; you hear rumors and news from across the continent; you can arrange meetings between distant parties; you know multiple routes between any two major settlements.

## Rank 4: Wanderer

**Holdings**: a respected or feared reputation in any settlement you visit; information about major events across Etharis before it reaches most places; a network of 50+ contacts across the continent; invitations from settlements that want your counsel.

**Feature — The Walking Chronicle**: you automatically know general information about any major settlement; you can find contacts and allies in even remote locations; your stories and advice are sought by leaders; once per month, your arrival in a settlement can trigger a major event (inspire rebellion, warn of danger, expose corruption). Bards sing songs of your travels, and some towns have standing offers of hospitality hoping you'll visit.
