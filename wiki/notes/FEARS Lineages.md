# FEARS Lineages

*Lineage = what you ARE (biology + mystical nature). Heritage = how you were RAISED (culture). Background = what you've DONE (personal history). This hub catalogs the Lineage layer only.*

Per the framing document [[FEARS_Corrected_Lineage_Analysis]], FEARS's 19 lineage source files each follow an identical mechanical shape: an origin myth, a Traits list, a Focus/Sanity modifier pair, a DCS Evasion Profile with a flat Natural DR, a named Corruption vulnerability with a 1d6 table (Corruption 10+, rolled on long rest, option 6 always a Corruption −1 "holds" result), and a subraces/variants table.

## Cross-lineage narrative threads

- **Faith of the Gnawing Crown**: Bugbear, Goblin, and Hobgoblin share a discarded-divine-Fragment origin myth. Faith source `The Broken Crown and the Cracked Fang` not yet ingested.
- **The Ashen Accord**: Half-Elf and Tiefling both forge identity through choice rather than birthright.
- **Elf/Drow/Sea-Elf/Shadar-kai duplication**: [[Elf (Lineage)]] briefly lists all three as subraces; each also has a fuller standalone page. Standalone pages are treated as authoritative.
- **The Glintsing gap**: [[Halfling (Lineage)]] carries a detailed, unreciprocated grievance narrative against Drow (the Glintsing massacre, the Second Echo silent-hunter ritual). [[Drow (Lineage)]] has no corresponding acknowledgment yet — flagged as an open gap.
