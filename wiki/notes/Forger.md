# Forger (Counterfeiter) — Rank 1-4

*"You create false documents, currency, and identities."* Batch 3, Criminal Specialist category. Source dual-names this "Forger / Counterfeiter"; filed under the first name. Narrower and more document/currency-focused than [[Charlatan]] (which covers whole false personas and long cons) — a Forger makes the paper trail, a Charlatan runs the whole performance.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Amateur Forger | Basic Forgery | Create a forgery passing official scrutiny, establish a lasting false identity, or copy a difficult document |
| 2 | Skilled Forger | Expert Counterfeiter | Forge a royal document, create a perfect counterfeit of a valuable item, or become the region's best forger |
| 3 | Master Forger | Artistic Deception | Forge an artifact's appearance, create a counterfeit that crashes an economy, or work directly for a government |
| 4 | Grandmaster of Falsification | Perfect Forgery | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Amateur Forger

**Skill Proficiency**: Deception, Investigation. **Tool Proficiency**: forgery kit, calligrapher's supplies. **Equipment**: forgery kit, calligrapher's supplies, 5 sheets of quality parchment, sample documents, 10 gp.

**Feature — Basic Forgery**: forge simple documents (letters, basic contracts); advantage on checks to spot forgeries; know how to age documents to look authentic; copy handwriting with practice; create believable but false identities.

**Holdings**: a hidden workshop; a collection of seals and samples; customers seeking false papers; a contact in the criminal underworld.

## Rank 2: Skilled Forger

**Holdings**: a secret workshop with quality equipment; income from forgeries (35 gp/month); an extensive collection of seals, samples, and materials; a network of 8-12 clients; you can forge official documents; quality inks and parchments; multiple false identities for yourself.

**Feature Expansion — Expert Counterfeiter**: forge complex documents (noble credentials, bank notes, etc.); create counterfeit currency (DC 18 to detect); your forgeries require DC 20+ Investigation to detect; forge magical signatures with time and samples; once per week, create a rush forgery in 1 hour.

## Rank 3: Master Forger

**Holdings**: multiple workshops across cities; major income (100 gp/month); a complete collection of official seals, possibly stolen or copied; you can forge any non-magical document; 3-5 apprentices learning the art; wealthy clients including criminals and spies; your work has influenced major events.

**Feature Expansion — Artistic Deception**: forge any mundane document or item; your currency is indistinguishable from real (DC 25 to detect); forge a magical item's non-magical appearance; once per month, create a forgery of a legendary item or document; advantage on all Deception checks using your work.

## Rank 4: Grandmaster of Falsification

**Holdings**: a network of workshops across the continent; excellent income (300 gp/month); you can create perfect copies of anything; an organization of 20+ forgers; governments secretly employ you; your counterfeits are used as genuine by those who know; your collection includes copies of legendary items.

**Feature — Perfect Forgery**: forge anything, including a magical item's appearance, but perfectly; your currency is identical to real, undetectable without magic; once per year, create a forgery so perfect it temporarily gains the original's properties, DM discretion; create false magical auras on items; your work has passed divine inspection. Nations fear your ability to destabilize currency, spymasters compete for your services, and museums unknowingly display your work.
