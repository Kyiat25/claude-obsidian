# Aelys, The Weave-Keeper

*"Magic is a tapestry. If one thread is pulled, the whole world may unravel."*

**Domains**: magic's stability, the Weave's structure, divine order. **Symbol**: a silver thread woven into an endless spiral. **Worshiped by**: mages, scholars, priests of balance. **Rivals**: Nytheris, Zeythis, [[Vel'Zyren]].

Does not create magic, ensures it remains stable — without her the Weave would fray and reality would collapse. Serves as the Scribe on [[The Court of the Silent Tribunal]], recording intention and outcome in mirrored glyphs written into memory itself. Already established elsewhere in this cluster (see [[The Hollow Thread]]) as the guardian most vulnerable to [[Nhal'Zereth]], whose erasure directly unmakes her work, and (per [[Vel'Zyren]]'s own material) as her eternal opposition — Aelys actively destroys Vel'Zyren's surface cults, knowing privately that Vel'Zyren was at least partially right about the Weave's flaws.

> [!note] One of Aeloria's unknowing threads
> Per [[Spirit Pantheon]]'s framing, Aelys's "Thread-Keeper" role is described as unconsciously channeling [[Aeloria]]'s actual weaving — when Aelys "recognizes" something as woven into the Pattern, she is detecting Aeloria's work without ever knowing it.
