# Rhyzel, The Cascading One

*"I do not stop. I do not rest. I do not turn back."*

**Domains**: waterfalls, the unceasing flow of water, movement that cannot be halted. **Symbol**: a silver stream of water falling endlessly. **Worshiped by**: travelers, messengers, those who fear stagnation. **Rivals**: [[Othrys]], Yltheris, Dymora. **Allies**: [[Marethis]], Selmira, Istriel.

The direct opposite of [[Othrys]]: motion embodied, rejecting stagnation entirely. Waterfalls are considered his temples. If a river suddenly changes course, it is said Rhyzel willed it.
