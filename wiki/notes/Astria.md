# Astria, The Falcon of Fate

*"Fate is not a choice—it is a road. And I do not care if you refuse to see it."*

**Domains**: prophecy, divine will, the sky's omens. **Symbol**: a falcon soaring with three eyes, each seeing a different future. **Worshiped by**: oracles, rulers, scholars who study omens. **Rivals**: [[Zephir]], Vaethis, [[The Whispering Dark]].

The one force of Air that believes in order — not a law of kings or a rule of gods, but the unseen, inevitable command of the wind. Serves as the Historian on [[The Court of the Silent Tribunal]], interpreting omens and cosmic precedent for divine trials. Already established elsewhere in this cluster (see [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]) as having omens that arrive backward, a symptom of [[Xey'Tazun]]'s corruption.

**The Trial of the Whispered Lie**: Astria genuinely foresaw a coming Eldritch Moon and tried to warn her siblings, but [[Zephir]] intercepted and twisted her words mid-flight into a half-lie, causing a proto-Loom-City's collapse. She fell into silence out of shame afterward — her omens have arrived backwards, sideways, or in fragments ever since, as though the Weave no longer fully trusts her.
