# Ithrial, The Ghost Wave

*"It should not be there. And yet, it is."*

**Domains**: phantom waves, unnatural ocean movements, the unseen touch of the sea. **Symbol**: a single ripple in a calm ocean, with no source. **Worshiped by**: sailors, deep-sea voyagers, those who experience the unexplainable. **Rivals**: [[Nyssara]], [[Vaelora]], Eryndal. **Allies**: Nyxthys, Morgathis, Rythos.

The wave that comes from nowhere — why ships suddenly lurch for no reason, why calm waters can feel like something just passed beneath. Does not hunt or take; he only reminds sailors the sea is never truly still.
