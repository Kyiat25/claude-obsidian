# Sarnok, The Blooded Stone

*"You fight for land you do not own. You fight for power you cannot hold. You fight because you think you will live forever. You will not."*

**Domain**: warfare over territory, defence of home, sacred ground. **Symbol**: a blood-marked shield carved into rock. **Worshiped by**: borderfolk, hill clans, tribal defenders, dwarves in siege.

Blesses those who fight to defend their land, not those who conquer for its own sake. Relation to [[Gaeldros]]: where Gaeldros judges law, Sarnok enforces it through mortal hands.

**Myth — The Kingdom That Could Not Fall**: a warlord sacrificed hundreds seeking Sarnok's favor and received nothing; a farmer raised a wooden shield to protect his home, and Sarnok stood beside him. The warlord was forgotten; the farmer's land still stands.
