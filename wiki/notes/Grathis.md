# Grathis, The Smelter's Hand

*"True fire does not consume. It refines. The impurities are not destroyed. They are revealed."*

**Domain**: metal purity, alloycraft, sacred forges. **Symbol**: a crucible pouring silver into black stone. **Worshiped by**: metallurgists, alchemists, battleforgers, inventors.

The refiner, not the forger — governs what makes metal worthy, the process of purifying, balancing, and blending. Myth: refused to let the gods forge weapons in his sacred forge until each sacrificed something impure from themselves; once quenched a blade that could cut lies, then sealed it away, for the world wasn't ready. Close to [[Durnath]] and [[Rhoris]]; wary of Fire's pantheon, especially [[Xethar]], whose chaos ruins metal before it's ready. **Aspect of the Lock**: he is the last heat that tempers the divine chains holding back what lies beneath — his work cannot crack or warp, or all is lost.
