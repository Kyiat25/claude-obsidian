# Sarvion, The Unlooked-For Rain

*"You did not expect me. You did not think I would come. And yet, here I am."*

**Domains**: the first rain after drought, unexpected blessings, renewal. **Symbol**: a single raindrop falling onto parched earth. **Worshiped by**: farmers, wanderers, those who live in uncertainty. **Rivals**: [[Yltheris]], Torvath. **Allies**: Luthan, Istriel, Selmira.

Not a savior — simply the rain that may or may not arrive in time. His worship is less devotion than hope: farmers pray to him not because he answers reliably, but because he sometimes does.
