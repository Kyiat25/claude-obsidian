# The Lantern's Croon

*"It was a lullaby. Then it was a warning. Now it is a scream wrapped in a whisper."*

**Type**: Song/Forgotten Guidance. **Origin**: believed to be a fragment of the Dreaming Lantern, the lost Spirit guide of death and dreams. **Behavior**: manifests as a lullaby heard underwater or in dreamspace, often during a full or eclipsed moon. **Effect**: those who hear it begin seeing children they don't remember, doors that don't exist, paths that lead nowhere. **Echo power**: sung aloud during a sleep ritual, it can open a doorway into [[The Null Reflection]] — dreamwalkers may not return.

An **Echo** — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a manifestation of an erased or forgotten idea rather than an independently-born entity.
