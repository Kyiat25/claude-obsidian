# Kallur's Knot

*"The ward was perfect. That's the problem."*

**Anchor**: a magical protection ward so perfectly constructed it turned inward and sealed itself eternally. **Behavior**: a floating glyph-ring spinning endlessly, never breaking or decaying. **Effect**: all spells cast within its radius automatically succeed — but without mortal control; glyphs change mid-casting, intentions replaced with universal laws. **Truth**: the ghost of a perfect spell, now consuming other magic to stay flawless. **Evolves into**: a Weave-sapping vortex that may merge with [[Ulthar]]'s raw Weave energy, becoming an unintentional power source or a cataclysmic collapse.

A **Tether** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, bound to a corrupted leyline/glyph point.
