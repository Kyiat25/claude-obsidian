# The Echo of the Empty Name

**Type**: Forgotten Language/Glyphic Pattern. **Origin**: a corrupted ritual invoking the Glyph of Names within a Dead Magic Zone. **Behavior**: instills a compulsion to name the unnameable. **Effect**: glyph bleed — scrolls and runes change meaning. **Power**: speaking the true "Empty Name" erases the speaker from all written records within a 1-mile radius.

An **Echo** — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a manifestation of an erased or forgotten idea.
