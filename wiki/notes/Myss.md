# Myss, The Whistling Wind

*"You think you hear your friend's voice? You think you hear a whisper calling your name? No, you hear me."*

**Domains**: the unpredictable wind, mirth, illusionary sounds. **Symbol**: a laughing mask, half in shadow. **Worshiped by**: tricksters, illusionists, children who play with the wind. **Allies**: [[Zephir]], [[Syvaris]], Orris. **Enemies**: [[Astria]], Vaelora, [[Sylphine]].

The playful wind, the trickster's breath — his voice is in every rustling leaf. Neither kind nor cruel, he simply enjoys the chaos of confusion.
