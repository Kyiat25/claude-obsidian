# Xilthos, The Spark of Rebellion

*"The first fire is not the one that burns the palace—it is the one that burns in the hearts of those who will set it alight."*

**Domains**: freedom, revolution, the destruction of tyranny. **Symbol**: a broken chain wreathed in flame. **Worshiped by**: rebels, escaped slaves, freedom fighters. **Allies**: [[Zephir]], Lysara, Vaethis. **Enemies**: [[Soltharion]], [[Vaeldros]], Pyrris.

The whisper before the riot — followers set the first fire without waiting for permission or approval. Myth holds he was once a servant of Soltharion before turning against his master, stealing his fire, and using it to burn the first palace to the ground.

> [!contradiction] Claimed by two pantheons
> `.raw/The Air Pantheon.md` separately names a Lesser God also called "Xilthos, The Whisper Before the Riot" — domains of rebellion and the first act of defiance, allied with [[Zephir]] and Vaethis, enemy of Soltharion and Astria. The overlap is too exact to be coincidental naming (same epithet, same core myth-beat, same ally/enemy shape) but the two sources disagree on his element: this page's Fire source gives him a fire-theft origin story tied to [[Soltharion]]; the Air source gives no origin myth and files him purely as a wind/whisper concept. Documented as an unresolved cross-pantheon contradiction rather than merged or duplicated.
