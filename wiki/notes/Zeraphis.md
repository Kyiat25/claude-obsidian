# Zeraphis, The Hurricane Lord

*"You see me coming. You pray. You prepare. It will not save you."*

**Domains**: hurricanes, typhoons, monsoons, storms that devour cities. **Symbol**: a vortex swallowing a city. **Worshiped by**: coastal peoples, storm-priests, those who prepare for catastrophe. **Rivals**: [[Howlar]], [[Nereus]], [[Veydras]].

Unlike Howlar's wild storms, Zeraphis is patient, deliberate, and unrelenting — not chaotic but inevitable. His followers worship him not to be spared, but to be ready.
