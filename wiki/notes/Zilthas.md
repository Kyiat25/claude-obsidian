# Zilthas, The Fallen Ashlord

*"You thought Fire was a god? Fire was a mistake."* — [[Vel'Zyren]], upon Zilthas's death

Before the Drow turned from the light and cast aside the gods of the sun, they worshiped one flame: Zilthas, the only Fire god who dared enter the Shadow Below, believing even the deepest night could be burned away. He promised the Drow light in the darkness, a kingdom carved in flame beneath the world.

[[Vel'Zyren]] destroyed him — whispering into his flames until his light turned to shadow and his fire burned cold, teaching the Drow that Fire was weakness and true darkness could never be burned. His temples were torn down, his name erased, his embers left to fade in silence — see [[The Fall of the Drow]] for the fuller conversion event this was part of. Some embers still remain: whispers persist of fire-wielding exiles among the Drow who still seek his lost flame, said to smolder in secret places, waiting to rise again.
