# Reputation

One of the named inputs to [[Disposition]] — framed as the qualitative "how you're remembered" axis (heroic, cruel, notorious) distinct from [[Wanted Score]]'s purely criminal/legal tracking. The source names it but gives no separate mechanic, scale, or example beyond that three-word list, so it currently reads as more of a narrative label than an independently tracked number.
