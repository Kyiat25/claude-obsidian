# Lyrithis, The Ever-Drowned

*"You think they are gone? They are not gone. The ocean does not lose. It only keeps."*

**Domains**: forgotten names, lost memories, the ghosts of the deep. **Symbol**: a single, faded footprint in the sand, washing away. **Worshiped by**: those who seek the truth of the past, those who have lost something to the sea. **Rivals**: [[Vaelora]], [[Tzhor]], Morgathis. **Allies**: Eryndal, [[Varashti]], [[Rymora]].

The spirit of all who were forgotten — those who drowned with no name, whose graves were lost beneath the tide. Not malevolent nor kind; he only ensures some things remain unknown. Also named in `.raw/Pantheon_Perspectives_on_Godhood.md` as one of Water's figures on the nature of godhood — see [[Water Pantheon]] for that cross-reference.
