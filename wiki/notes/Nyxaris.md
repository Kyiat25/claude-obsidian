# Nyxaris, The Maddening Breath

*"Not all voices on the wind are real. Not all words are meant to be heard. But once you listen, you can never unhear them."*

**Domains**: madness, hallucinations, the wind that whispers lies. **Symbol**: a shifting mask, impossible to focus on. **Worshiped by**: the insane, oracles, cursed wanderers, prophets of doom. **Allies**: [[Howlar]], Xerathis, Saelis. **Enemies**: [[Astria]], [[Aelys]], Vaelora.

Does not lie — he simply ensures you never know what is real. His wind carries forgotten words, truths that should have been erased, echoes of lost gods and dead voices.
