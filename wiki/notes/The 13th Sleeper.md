# The 13th Sleeper

*"You never had a 13th guest. So why is there someone in that bed?"*

**Origin**: emerged in an isolated monastery after thirteen monks dreamed of the same unknown figure sleeping among them. **Appearance**: always horizontal, never seen standing; wrapped in a traveler's shroud; breaths leave black frost. **Effect**: induces "sleep identity collapse" — victims forget who they are upon waking, replacing their self with imagined roles (child, god, prisoner). **Echo power**: if more than 12 people sleep nearby, the 13th Sleeper may fully awaken and choose a new host identity.

A **Dream-Born** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, aligned with [[Ul'Naresh]].
