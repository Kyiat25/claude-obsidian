# Vaedoris, The Oath of the Tide

*"The sea remembers. It always remembers. And it does not forget a broken oath."*

**Domains**: shipwrecks, safe return, the last promise. **Symbol**: a knotted rope, half-submerged in water. **Worshiped by**: sailors, oath-bound warriors. **Rivals**: [[Tzhor]], Nyxthys. **Allies**: Eryndal, [[Marethis]], Selmira.

Does not create promises — he enforces them. Sailors carve their names into their ships to bind themselves to the sea; those who break their word upon the water find their ships cursed and their names erased from history. Does not demand worship, only remembrance.
