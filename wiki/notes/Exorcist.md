# Exorcist — Rank 1-4 (Banisher of Possessing Spirits)

*"You drive out malevolent spirits that possess the living."* Tier 3 (Dark Fantasy). Fulfills the "Exorcist" slot [[FEARS Advanced Backgrounds]] flagged as not-yet-developed after Batch 1.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Acolyte Exorcist | Sense Possession | Successfully perform your first exorcism, save someone from possession, or banish a malevolent spirit |
| 2 | Licensed Exorcist | Rite of Cleansing | Exorcise a powerful demon, save someone important from possession, or survive an exorcism gone wrong |
| 3 | Master Exorcist | Spiritual Warrior | Exorcise an entity that possessed multiple people, prevent a mass possession, or banish an arch-demon |
| 4 | Grand Exorcist | Bulwark of Souls | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Acolyte Exorcist

**Skill Proficiency**: Insight, Religion. **Tool Proficiency**: holy symbol, herbalism kit. **Equipment**: a holy symbol, 5 vials of holy water, iron chains, a prayer book, common clothes, 10 gp.

**Feature — Sense Possession**: advantage on Insight checks to detect possession; identify signs of spiritual attachment or haunting; know rituals for minor spiritual cleansing; understand types of possessing entities.

**Holdings**: a mentor exorcist; access to church resources; basic exorcism tools.

## Rank 2: Licensed Exorcist

**Holdings**: income from exorcisms and spiritual cleansing (25 gp/month); quality exorcism tools (blessed chains, holy implements); a network of 5-8 church contacts; access to blessed ground for dangerous exorcisms; a reputation in the religious community.

**Feature Expansion — Rite of Cleansing**: perform exorcism rituals (contested Wisdom check against the possessing entity); advantage on saves against possession yourself; create holy water (1 vial/day); possessed individuals usually can't harm you during exorcism; know the weaknesses of common possessing spirits.

## Rank 3: Master Exorcist

**Holdings**: major income from the church and desperate families (80 gp/month); a sanctified chamber for dangerous exorcisms; holy relics and powerful religious artifacts; a team of 3-5 assistant exorcists; church authority to perform any exorcism; a network across the region; your successes are documented and studied.

**Feature Expansion — Spiritual Warrior**: resistance to necrotic and psychic damage; force possessing spirits to reveal themselves; once per week, perform a major exorcism that drives out even powerful entities; create wards against possession; possessed individuals trust you instinctively.

## Rank 4: Grand Exorcist

**Holdings**: a cathedral sanctum for exorcisms; excellent income (200 gp/month); a team of 20+ exorcists under your training; a collection of holy relics; authority across religious organizations; your methods are taught universally; you're consulted on theological and spiritual matters.

**Feature — Bulwark of Souls**: immune to possession; perform exorcisms that others cannot; once per month, permanently banish an entity, even powerful demons; your presence disrupts possession nearby; consecrate entire areas against spiritual threats. Churches call upon you for impossible cases, demons know and fear your name, your writings on spiritual warfare are canonical, and upon death your soul is protected — it cannot be captured or damned.
