# Ith'Quess, The Flesh Reversed

*"Skin is a lie. Bones are betrayal."*

**Domain**: the body believing it should not be. **Symbol**: an ouroboros inside out. **Truth**: causes flesh to remember backwards — skin becomes memory, bones forget purpose. **Mythic tie**: whispers from beneath [[The Hollow Thread|the Hollow Vault]]. **Known incident**: the Cradle of Rejection — a site where all life sloughs into unrecognizable forms.

## How it bleeds into reality

- **Form Glyphs**: Beast, Titan, and Aberration glyphs melt and reorganize; glyphmages find patterns turning inside-out; healing magic causes tissue to invert.
- **Fire Pantheon**: [[Fire Pantheon|Vaeldros]]'s purging flames leave backwards flesh; burned corpses scream in silent voices; fire can't cleanse what doesn't remember burning.

## Corruptions

- **Beasts — Spinecrawlers**: nervous systems inverted, brains on the outside; animals grow organs not meant for them; predators become prey to their own bodies.
- **Monsters**: fleshwarped horrors reflect those who killed them, taking on the patchwork anatomy of memory — bodies remember every wound they've inflicted.
- **Mortals — Flesh Inversion**: bones on the outside, organs rearranged; wizards studying Form magic suffer morphic collapse; thoughts reshaped by body errors (mind follows flesh).

## Preferred manifestation zones

Necromantic rifts, transmutation failure sites, battlefields with mass casualties, medical facilities or plague sites.

## Warning signs

Animals born with inverted anatomy; healing spells cause swelling or mutations; corpses don't decompose — they reorganize; nightmares of your own body betraying you.
