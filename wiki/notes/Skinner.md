# Skinner (Tanner) — Rank 1-4

*"You process animal hides into leather and other materials."* Batch 3, Working Class category. Source dual-names this "Skinner / Tanner"; filed under the first name.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Tannery Worker | Hide Knowledge | Produce exceptional leather, skin a rare creature, or master a difficult tanning technique |
| 2 | Journeyman Tanner | Master Tanner | Tan a hide from a legendary creature, supply a major armorer, or develop a new tanning process |
| 3 | Master Skinner | Legendary Leather | Tan dragon hide, supply an entire army with leather armor, or discover a method for tanning unstable magical hides |
| 4 | Grand Master of Hides | Master of All Hides | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Tannery Worker

**Skill Proficiency**: Nature, Survival. **Tool Proficiency**: leatherworker's tools. **Equipment**: leatherworker's tools, a skinning knife, a leather apron, work clothes, 10 gp.

**Feature — Hide Knowledge**: identify an animal by its hide; know how to properly skin animals without damaging the hide; advantage on checks to assess leather quality; preserve hides for later tanning; know the value of different pelts.

**Holdings**: a work station in a tannery; modest wages; access to tanning chemicals and equipment; fellow tanners as contacts.

## Rank 2: Journeyman Tanner

**Holdings**: a small tannery or workshop; income from leather sales (25 gp/month); complete tanning equipment; regular customers (leatherworkers, armorers); a supply of common hides; contacts with hunters and butchers; you can produce various leather types.

**Feature Expansion — Master Tanner**: tan exotic hides (giant, monster, etc.); your leather is 25% stronger than normal; advantage on all leatherworking checks; identify leather's origin and quality instantly; once per month, produce exceptional leather for magic item crafting or premium sale.

## Rank 3: Master Skinner

**Holdings**: a large tannery with multiple vats; major income (80 gp/month); 5-8 workers under you; supply contracts with armorers and merchants; you can process rare and exotic hides; a network of hunters supplying you; your leather is sought after by craftspeople.

**Feature Expansion — Legendary Leather**: tan any hide, including from magical creatures; your leather is used in magical armor, sought by enchanters; advantage on all hide/leather checks; once per month, produce masterwork leather (armor made from it gains +1 AC); identify any leather item's origin and construction.

## Rank 4: Grand Master of Hides

**Holdings**: multiple tanneries; excellent income (200 gp/month); 30+ workers; exclusive contracts with nobility and military; you can process any hide in existence; your leather is used in legendary armor; museums display examples of your work.

**Feature — Master of All Hides**: tan any material into leather, including impossible ones; your leather has magical resistance properties; once per year, create leather retaining a creature's magical properties (fire resistance from dragon hide, etc.); automatic success on leatherworking checks (DC 25 or lower); identify and extract value from any hide. Adventurers bring you rare creature hides, your techniques preserve hides thought impossible to tan, and you may have tanned hides from celestial or infernal beings.
