# Whisper Engine

First mentioned in [[Welcome to FEARS]] as the subsystem that "tracks and amplifies player agency," folded into the [[Dynamic Action Economy]] description without its own page. [[A World that Watches]] links to it as `01_Rules/06_Optional/The Whisper Engine` — an internal path from the original source vault confirming it's documented there as a standalone **optional rule**, not a core mechanic bundled inside the action economy as the first source implied.

## Open question — largely resolved (from Fracture Whispers)

Is the Whisper Engine the accounting mechanism for [[Whisper Points]], a separate optional expansion of it, or an unrelated system that happens to share the "Whisper" name? [[Fracture Whispers]]'s Integration Hooks table states it plainly: the Whisper Engine is "tied directly" to Whisper Points, and "Fracture is the primary danger of building Whisper." Combined with [[Eldritch Moons in Combat]]'s earlier "Whisper Checks" data point, this is treated as resolved in favor of the first option — the Whisper Engine is the accounting/tracking mechanism for the Whisper Points pool (including its instability-meter side, see [[Whisper Points]]) — though no source spells out the mechanical detail of how a "Whisper Check" is actually rolled.
