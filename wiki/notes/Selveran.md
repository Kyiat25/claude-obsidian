# Selveran — The Living City

Spirit-Thread [[Loom-Cities|Loom-City]]. "Selveran is not built. It grows." Combined population ~387,000, occupying ~2,400 sq km of the ancient **Vaeltrun Forest**: the **Skywood Bastion** (~156,000, a multi-tiered tree-city 100-380 ft up in the canopy) and the **Rootbound Settlements** (~231,000, ground-level clearings — Thornwall, Redholt, Marrowfen, Greybend, plus minor settlements), unified under the Living Concord.

> [!note] Two accounts of this city
> An earlier gazetteer entry (`.raw/Dór-o Estel.md`) described a much larger (~3.8 million) city under a "Circle of Remembrance" governed by prophetic dreams. The deep-dive campaign source below instead describes the Council of Threadways bicameral system and a far smaller population. Treat the material below as the more detailed and current canon; the Circle of Remembrance material may survive as older spiritual tradition predating the current Council.

## Founding: the Split Seed of Vaelora

During the First Silence (an early Eldritch darkness that consumed an entire civilization), the goddess **Vaelora** wept and planted her final tear-wrapped seed at the heart of the devastation. It grew in both directions at once — roots spreading into what became the Rootbound Settlements, branches rising into what became the Skywood Bastion — blessed by Aelys to "remember both earth and sky." Selveran voluntarily allied with the Kel Varyan Empire 190 years ago via the Oath of the Second Loom, and now exists as a protected autonomy rather than a subject territory. The **Verdant Spiral** World Anchor sits at the heart of the city where the Vaeltrun trees' leylines converge.

## Government: the Council of Threadways

A bicameral system meeting at **Mir's Crown**, whose central Echochime only sounds when both chambers speak in unison. The **Rootward Assembly** (grounded, traditionalist — 4 Principal Rootwarders including **Maevis Thornweaver** of Thornwall and **Dulgan Claywright** of Redholt, plus 7 elected Hearthvoices) handles defense, resources, and traditional law. The **Boughsinger Hall** (elevated, adaptive — 4 High Boughsingers including **Aelynn Starweaver** and **Veren Memorykeeper**, plus 7 Threadcallers) handles magical regulation, diplomacy, and innovation. Bills need both chambers' seals to become law. The highest office, the **Spiral Seat**, can only be held by two people in accord: the **Oathroot** (currently **Aldrich Stonebound**, human, 54) and the **Starbranch** (currently **Silviana Windwright**, elf, 132) — if they can't reach consensus, the seat sits empty until the next trial. **Threadbearer Kaelen Vorthak** (half-elf, 67) holds a permanent advisory seat in both chambers.

## Secret: the Knotted Root

Beneath the Verdant Spiral lies a pre-community chamber known to only three living people (Threadbearer Kaelen Vorthak, 340-year-old Elder Spiral Keeper Morin Deeproot, and Master Archivist Lyra Voidward), holding **Vaelora's Memory Fragment** (a crystallized divine memory revealing the founding involved far more divine intervention than publicly known), a dormant **Glyph of Union** (a Primary Glyph capable of permanently harmonizing — or catastrophically unraveling — all five Threads), the **Original Compact** with the forest's original consciousness (with secret ongoing obligations), and a complete **Thread Convergence Map** of the region. Separately, Selveran secretly harbors ~50 drow refugees from the Hollowdeep who have renounced their former culture and integrated quietly into the community.
