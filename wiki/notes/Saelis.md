# Saelis, The Echo of the Lost

*"Names fade. Histories crumble. But the wind remembers."*

**Domains**: forgotten souls, the winds that carry lost voices, the breath of the vanished. **Symbol**: a broken name etched into stone, half-worn away. **Worshiped by**: mourners, ghost-priests, those who search for the forgotten. **Allies**: [[Sylphine]], Nysara, Aetheris. **Enemies**: Vaelora, [[The Whispering Dark]].

Carries lost names and remembers those the world has forgotten. Priests mark the names of the dead on banners and release them into the wind. He does not always answer — and sometimes his voice is not alone.
