# Sethir, The Guardian of Broken Weaves

*"Some things can be fixed. Some must remain broken."*

**Domains**: repairing fate, fixing magic, healing what was lost. **Symbol**: a golden thread, half-mended into a broken tapestry. **Worshiped by**: those who seek to restore lost magic, healers of broken souls. **Allies**: [[Aelys]], [[Nyzara]], Valthos. **Enemies**: [[Zeythis]], [[Tyralis]], [[Nytheris]].

Not a god of creation — a spirit that ensures what can be fixed is fixed. His presence is felt when an unstable spell suddenly stabilizes.
