# Elyon, The Shattered Path

*"Some fates do not happen as intended. But that does not mean they are gone."*

**Domains**: fragments of fate, echoes of lost prophecies, splintered timelines. **Symbol**: a broken thread drifting in the wind. **Worshiped by**: those who feel their fate slipping away, those haunted by past choices. **Allies**: [[Nytheris]], [[Vaithis]], [[Ytheis]]. **Enemies**: [[Kaelor]], [[Astralis]], [[Oriveth]].

Governs the remnants of lost fates — destinies that should have happened but did not. His presence is felt in déjà vu, the eerie sense an event was meant to go differently.
