# The Faceless Choir

**Type**: Sound/Memory. **Origin**: a collapsed Spirit temple holding the Glyph of Mourning. **Behavior**: sings forgotten liturgies; each listener hears a different harmony based on their greatest regret. **Effect**: shared dreams of forgotten worship, compulsive humming. **Manifestation**: if 13 or more people hum together, it becomes audible to the deaf and manifests as shadows with stitched mouths.

An **Echo** — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a manifestation of an erased or forgotten idea rather than an independently-born entity.
