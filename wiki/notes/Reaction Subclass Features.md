# Reaction Subclass Features

The second appearance of FEARS's subclass system in this wiki (after [[Called Shot Subclass Features]]), known only through this narrow lens — how specific subclasses modify [[Tactical Reactions]] and [[Shields|Shield Reactions]]. No general subclass reference exists yet; this table is scoped to reaction interactions only, not a full subclass writeup.

| Class / Subclass | Feature | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| [[Fighter]] (Battle Master) | Precision Reaction | Spend a Superiority Die to add the result to a Counterattack or Disarm attempt |
| [[Fighter]] (Champion) | Indomitable Defence | Advantage on contested rolls for Shield Deflect, Bash, and Disarm |
| [[Barbarian]] (Path of the Bear) | Totemic Resistance | Gain resistance to the triggering attack's damage type when performing a Tactical Reaction |
| [[Barbarian]] (Path of the Berserker) | Reckless Counter | While Raging, Counterattacks via forgoing Evasion don't gain advantage but use Reckless Attack rules instead |
| [[Rogue]] | Opportunistic Retaliation | When using Uncanny Dodge alongside a Counterattack, halve incoming damage before retaliating |
| [[Paladin]] (Oath of the Crown) | Shield of Resolve | Shield Block provides +5 DR instead of +3 DR |
| [[Paladin]] (Oath of Conquest) | Frightening Bash | Shield Bash forces a Wisdom save (DC = 8 + PB + CHA mod) or the attacker is Frightened until the end of your next turn |
| [[Ranger]] | Hunter's Precision | Counterattacks against Hunter's Mark targets deal bonus damage equal to Wisdom modifier |
| [[Ranger]] (Favoured Enemy) | Favoured Disarm | Disarm attempts against favoured enemies automatically succeed on a contested roll of 15 or higher |

Barbarian's Level 7 Primal Defiance (reduce a Momentum loss by 1 and gain +1 MS as a reaction) is a base-class reaction feature, not subclass-gated — already documented on [[Momentum]] and [[Barbarian]], not repeated here as a new subclass row.
