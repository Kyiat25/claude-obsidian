# Thalmar, The Sky-Warrior

*"You fight for kings. I fight because it is what I am."*

**Domains**: war, strength, the storm as a battlefield. **Symbol**: a sword made of storm clouds, flashing with lightning. **Worshiped by**: skyborne warriors, aerial cavalry, those who fight under the open sky. **Rivals**: Keldros, [[Xethar]], [[Soltharion]].

War without allegiance — the storm that does not choose sides, fighting for no cause but battle itself; his favor is given only to those who fight freely, without chains.
