# Burned out

Terminal state reached at **0 [[Focus]]**: a character cannot cast or concentrate. The Focus-track counterpart to [[Broken]] (Sanity's 0-point terminal state). Unlike [[Broken]], no source connects this state to the [[Mental State]] tracker's four stages (Stable/Frayed/Deranged/Broken) — it may be a separate, Focus-specific terminal condition rather than a stage on that shared tracker.
