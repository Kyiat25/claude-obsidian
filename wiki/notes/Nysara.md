# Nysara, The Silent Chill

*"The cold is patient. The frost does not rage—it simply waits."*

> [!note] Near-homophone, not a collision
> [[Water Pantheon]] separately names a "Nyssara, The Veil of Mists" (fog and illusion) — a distinct spelling, distinct domain, and distinct source file. Not treated as the same entity; flagged here only because the names are easy to confuse aloud.

**Domains**: the first snow, the silent freeze, the death that comes softly. **Symbol**: a frozen tear suspended in air. **Worshiped by**: frost-mages, mountain druids, those who seek quiet deaths. **Allies**: [[Cyrnith]], [[Sylphine]]. **Enemies**: Xalros, [[Soltharion]].

The frost that creeps in the night, the silence after the last breath. Worshiped by those who see death not as an enemy, but as a peaceful end.
