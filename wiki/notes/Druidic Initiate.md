# Druidic Initiate — Rank 1-4

*"You've been inducted into a circle of druids who guard nature's balance."* Batch 3, Druidic/Nature category. Unrelated mechanically to the [[Druid]] class (no Wild Shape, no spell slots below Rank 2) — this is circle membership and social standing, not the class's shapeshifting kit, though a Druid-class character with this background would be a very natural fit narratively.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Aspirant | Circle Membership | Protect a sacred grove, commune successfully with a nature spirit, or complete a druidic pilgrimage |
| 2 | Circle Member | Nature's Guardian | Defeat a threat to nature, restore blighted land, or earn a title from circle elders |
| 3 | Grove Keeper | Nature's Champion | Defend a grove from major corruption, restore a destroyed ecosystem, or commune with an ancient primordial force |
| 4 | Archdruid | Voice of the Wild | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Aspirant

**Skill Proficiency**: Nature, Survival. **Tool Proficiency**: herbalism kit. **Language**: Druidic. **Equipment**: herbalism kit, a wooden staff, a sprig of mistletoe, leaf-woven clothing, 10 gp.

**Feature — Circle Membership**: identify other druids and nature guardians; know sacred grove locations in your region; receive shelter and assistance from circle members; understand basic druidic rites and traditions; animals are less likely to attack you unprovoked.

**Holdings**: a tent or shelter in a sacred grove; access to circle gatherings; a mentor druid; knowledge of local nature spirits.

## Rank 2: Circle Member

**Holdings**: a personal grove site or tree dwelling; a stipend from the circle (15 gp/month in trade goods); herbalism supplies and natural materials; a network of 8-12 circle druids; authority to perform druidic rites; an animal companion or familiar from the grove.

**Feature Expansion — Nature's Guardian**: cast *speak with animals* once per long rest, no components; advantage on weather-prediction checks; identify all plants and their properties in your region; know safe paths through dangerous terrain; once per week, ask a nature spirit for guidance.

## Rank 3: Grove Keeper

**Holdings**: a sacred grove under your guardianship; good income from the circle and offerings (60 gp/month); 3-5 junior druids assisting you; a network across multiple circles; authority over the grove's territory; a rare plants and seeds collection; a relationship with powerful nature spirits.

**Feature Expansion — Nature's Champion**: cast *speak with plants* once per long rest; bless the grove (designated creatures gain advantage on checks within it for 24 hours); nature spirits warn you of intrusion; command local animals to assist or scout; your grove has magical properties (healing, protection, etc.).

## Rank 4: Archdruid

**Holdings**: multiple sacred groves under your protection; excellent income (150 gp/month); a circle of 20+ druids who respect your authority; influence over nature across the entire region; a legendary, never-violated grove; you can call upon ancient treants and powerful nature spirits; a voting position on the greater druidic council.

**Feature — Voice of the Wild**: cast *commune with nature* at will; once per month, perform a ritual influencing an entire ecosystem; permanently *awaken* trees and plants once per month; nature spirits obey your requests; animals will not harm you. Other circles seek your counsel, you can sense disturbances across vast distances, legends say you can assume any natural form, and you may have achieved a timeless body.
