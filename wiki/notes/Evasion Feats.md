# Evasion Feats

> **Scope note.** Second independent sighting of FEARS's feat system (after [[Called Shot Feats]]), this time through the Evasion lens. Still only a narrow window — no general feat reference exists.

| Feat | Effect |
|---|---|
| Agility Master | +2 to all Evasion rolls; always halve AoE damage on a failed roll |
| Medium Armour Evasion Expert | Add Proficiency Bonus to Evasion rolls in medium armor; halve non-magical AoE on failure |
| Heavy Armour Evasion Specialist | Add Proficiency Bonus to Evasion rolls in heavy armor; reduce melee damage on a failed Brace |
| Shield Mastery: Evasion Defender | Add half Proficiency Bonus to Evasion rolls while wielding a shield; gain a reaction after successful defences |

No overlap with [[Called Shot Feats]]' 14 entries — the two lists are disjoint, reinforcing that this is a real, broader feat system rather than a single-mechanic gimmick.
