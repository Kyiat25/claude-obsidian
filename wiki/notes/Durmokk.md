# Durmokk, The Stone-Bound Oath

*"The dead do not sleep until their oaths are kept. We are bound to the living, waiting for debts unpaid."*

**Domains**: ancestral spirits, unfulfilled oaths, the duty of the bloodline. **Symbol**: a stone tablet, cracked but unbroken. **Worshiped by**: dwarves who seek their ancestors' guidance, those who fear breaking family traditions. **Allies**: [[Kaelor]], [[Aelys]], [[Nyzara]]. **Enemies**: [[Xyvaris]], [[Nytheris]], [[Oriveth]].

Ensures no promise made in a dwarven bloodline is forgotten. Does not punish those who fail their oaths — he simply waits, reminding them their ancestors are watching.
