# Pyrris, The Ashen Oracle

*"Fire shows you the truth. It just doesn't show you the whole truth."*

**Domains**: trickery, twilight, secrets. **Symbol**: a flame burning behind a mask. **Worshiped by**: illusionists, assassins, spies. **Enemies**: [[Vaeldros]], [[Soltharion]], [[Tharamis]].

Doesn't reveal knowledge but distorts it, ensuring no one can tell truth from deception; hides worshipers behind illusions to keep cults unseen; mocks [[Soltharion]]'s law by proving all truths can be burned away. Doesn't need to win, only needs no one else to win either. Temples are places of binding oaths, where warriors swear eternal brotherhood — myth holds Pyrris was the first to swear upon fire, an oath so powerful even [[Ignis]] himself was bound by it. Already established elsewhere in this cluster (see [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]) as having illusions that leave "echoes of longing," an early symptom of [[Vael'Akir]]'s corruption.
