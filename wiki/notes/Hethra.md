# Hethra, Keeper of the Long Evening

**Domains**: old age, elders, the last working years, dignity at the far end. **Symbol**: a lamp trimmed low but burning clean.

Holds the span between vitality's gods and Varethis's cut that no other office claimed — not dying, but *lasting*: the elder's craft-knowledge, the seat by the fire earned rather than offered, the right to be consulted. Her rite is the handing-down: tools, recipes, and grudges formally passed before the end, witnessed, so nothing dies untransferred. Shares the late watches with [[Dovenna]] and shares nearly everything else with [[Nyzara]].
