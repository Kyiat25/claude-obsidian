# Bloodied and Bruised

Enemy-only (see [[Bloodied Breaking Points Rally]]'s status note). Triggers at **50% HP or below**, after the [[Injury System]] roll per [[Combat Flow]]'s sequencing.

## The four effects (all apply simultaneously)

| Effect | What it does |
|---|---|
| Frailty | A tactical weakness is exposed (e.g. −2 Evasion, disadvantage on saves, damage vulnerability) |
| Strength | Desperation resilience emerges (e.g. +2 damage, resistance, temp HP) |
| Ability | A once-per-combat triggered move activates |
| Death Throes | An explosive finale on death (area damage, curse, transformation) |

Design note from the source: effects should match creature and narrative context — a cornered wolf gains Pack Tactics (Strength) but exposes a wounded flank (Frailty); a dying demon explodes in hellfire (Death Throes).

## Auto Rally trigger

When an enemy **leader** (or the highest-CR enemy if no leader) becomes Bloodied, they **must** immediately attempt a Rally check (no choice) — range 120 ft, affects all enemy allies in range. `1d20 + Focus mod` vs DC 15 (or current casualty-tier DC if higher). See [[Enemy Rally]] for the full outcome table. Legendary leaders may auto-succeed once per combat.

## Legendary Bloodied Phase 2

Instead of standard Bloodied effects, a Legendary enemy leader enters **Legendary Phase 2** with new/enhanced abilities.
