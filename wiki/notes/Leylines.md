# Leylines

Thin streams of arcane energy stretching across the world, linking key locations together — the framework of the Weave, ensuring magic can be accessed predictably everywhere. The first and most commonly understood of the [[Three Pillars of Magical Flow]].

## Function

Constant and always present, forming the foundation for spellcasting and stability. Mages near leylines experience improved efficiency, faster casting, and increased power. They connect major magical sites into intersecting web-like structures, and vary enormously in strength — some faint and nearly inert, others powerful enough to tear reality apart.

## Types

| Type | Properties | Effect on magic |
|---|---|---|
| **Stable Ley Lines** | Balanced, controlled flow | Predictable magic, reliable spellcasting |
| **Unstable Ley Lines** | Weak or chaotic flow | Wild magic surges, unpredictable spell effects |
| **Ley Nexus Points** | Intersections of multiple leylines | Extreme magical amplification, sometimes dangerous |
| **Dead Ley Zones** | Broken or severed leylines | Magic weakened or completely absent |

Separately, [[Weave of Creation]] categorizes them by scale rather than stability: **Minor** (weak, unstable currents), **Major** (stronger, structured currents), **Trunk** (the backbone of magical energy, connected to World Anchors), **Corrupted** (twisted by external forces such as [[The Whispering Dark]] or magical disasters), and **Dead** (ceased functioning, leaving magical voids). Both classification schemes are treated as compatible lenses on the same phenomenon rather than a contradiction — one describes behavior, the other describes scale/role.

## Origin

Per [[Weave of Creation]]'s creation myth: when [[Astralor]] struck his hammer upon the void, he created not just matter but motion, energy, and flow — the Weave of Magic wasn't placed, it was forged into existence, and through it the first leylines emerged as raw conduits of power. The oldest and most sacred leylines are said to be carved from the bones of Vatharun, the First Mountain and Earth's foundation god — see [[Earth Pantheon]].

## Relationship to the other Pillars

Leylines feed into [[Wells of Power]], allowing magic to be stored and stabilized; [[Astral Conduits]] pull energy from leylines and Wells to move magic across dimensions. When a leyline fractures, it may give birth to a new Well or an unstable Conduit; if a Conduit collapses, it can sever leyline connections across entire regions.

## Stakes

[[The Hollow Thread]] is, at its core, a single corrupted leyline — the entire apocalypse scenario documented on that page begins with this exact infrastructure turning against itself.
