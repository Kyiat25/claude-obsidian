# Gorvath, The Blood-Thread of Memory

*"A warrior who is remembered never truly dies. And I remember all who have fallen."*

**Domains**: ancestral memory, warriors' legends, bloodlines that carry fate. **Symbol**: a red thread, woven through a cracked war-banner. **Worshiped by**: orcish warlords, shamans, warriors who wish to leave a legacy. **Allies**: [[Nyzara]], [[Vaithis]], [[Xyvaris]]. **Enemies**: [[Astralis]], [[Vel'Zyren]], [[Oriveth]].

Binds warriors to their ancestors — the unbroken chain of battle, written in blood and steel. Ensures no warrior's name is lost unless they die a coward. Orcish culture's dedicated Spirit deity of warrior legacy.
