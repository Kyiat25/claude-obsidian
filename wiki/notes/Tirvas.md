# Tirvas, The Laughing Farmer

*"A well-fed man does not go to war. A man who sees his fields full does not burn the fields of another."*

**Domain**: bountiful harvests, plentiful crops. Ensures those who work the land with care are rewarded — doesn't punish or curse, but withholds from those who take without care. Farmers invoke him at planting season, bakers whisper his name kneading dough.

**Myth — The Feast That Never Ends**: a village has never known hunger, its fields always blooming; it's said Tirvas once sat at their table, and in return their harvest never failed.
