# Erona, The Hearthmother

*"Stone warms when you are near it. Fire glows brighter when laughter fills the hall. This is the true temple."*

**Domain**: family, hearth, shelter, domestic order. **Symbol**: a stylised stone hearth with a flame cradled in its centre. **Worshiped by**: halflings, homesteaders, dwarven matriarchs, those who guard kin and hearth.

Embodies the quiet strength of the home — not in walls or roofs, but in the warmth that survives within them. Myth: when a family was forced to flee across frozen stone, she walked with them, and where she stepped, fire followed. Works closely with [[Dalvora]]. **Aspect of the Lock**: Erona is what the Lock protects — the lives that continue because the world has not yet broken.
