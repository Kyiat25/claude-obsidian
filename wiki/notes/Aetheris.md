# Aetheris, The Whispering Veil

*"The sky does not exist without the sea. The sea does not move without the wind."*

**Domains**: mist, hidden paths, the unseen current. **Symbol**: a crescent moon reflected in mist. **Worshiped by**: sailors, storm-seers, fog-walkers. **Rivals**: [[Veydras]], [[Nereus]], Zeraphis.

The wind that guides ships but also conceals dangers beneath the waves — neither ally nor enemy to Water, sometimes carrying its will, sometimes drowning its children. Does not answer prayers; he merely moves as he wills.
