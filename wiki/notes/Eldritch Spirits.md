# Eldritch Spirits

Not deities, but **sentient symptoms** of the Eldritch infection: fragments of greater gods, independent echoes of forgotten truths, unrecorded and unintended but terrifying. They grow stronger when Eldritch Moons rise, [[The Null Reflection]] draws close, dreams are shared, or magic falters (Wild or Rift zones).

## Classification

| Category | Definition | Named examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Echoes** | Manifestations of erased/forgotten ideas | [[The Faceless Choir]], [[The Writhe-Twin]], [[The Echo of the Empty Name]] |
| **Unfinished** | Beings that tried to form but failed | [[Vathryn, the Hollowed Child]], [[The Shuddering Cradle]], [[Mother-Shell Elessh]] |
| **Reflected** | Mirror-entities from corrupted identity | [[Eshari's Mirror]], [[The Pane-Creep]], [[Velai, the Remembered Stranger]] |
| **Dream-Born** | Nightmares breaching into waking life | [[Ulzen, the Sleepless Host]], [[The Quilt-Worm]], [[Marrowbell]] |
| **Tethers** | Bound to corrupted leyline/glyph points | [[The Ink-Root]], [[The Glyph Maw]], [[The Bonechain]] |

## Tier system

| Tier | Echo | Unfinished | Reflected | Dream-Born | Tether |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Minor** | Whisper | Lingerer | Glimmer | Whisper | Dormant |
| **Moderate** | Shadow | Interloper | Double | Tide | Active |
| **Major** | Voice | Architect | Replacement | Host | Evolved |

Each of the 15 named entities above is presumed to start at "Minor"/base tier per its category unless a GM escalates it through the table above — the source gives the tier-name progression generically, not per-entity.
