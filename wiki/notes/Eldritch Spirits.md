# Eldritch Spirits

Not deities, but **sentient symptoms** of the Eldritch infection: fragments of greater gods, independent echoes of forgotten truths, unrecorded and unintended but terrifying. They grow stronger when Eldritch Moons rise, [[The Null Reflection]] draws close, dreams are shared, or magic falters (Wild or Rift zones).

## Classification (29 named entities across 5 categories)

A second, closely-related source (`Eldritch Forces.md`) restates this same five-category system and adds 14 more named spirits not present in the original `Guide to the Eldritch_Forces.md` roster — folded in below rather than treated as a separate list, since the classification and tier language are identical between the two sources.

| Category | Definition | Named examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Echoes** | Manifestations of erased/forgotten ideas | [[The Faceless Choir]], [[The Writhe-Twin]], [[The Echo of the Empty Name]], [[The Lantern's Croon]], [[The Forgotten Note]], [[The Whispered Trace]] |
| **Unfinished** | Beings that tried to form but failed | [[Vathryn, the Hollowed Child]], [[The Shuddering Cradle]], [[Mother-Shell Elessh]], [[The Spine-Womb]], [[Beneath-the-Face]] |
| **Reflected** | Mirror-entities from corrupted identity | [[Eshari's Mirror]], [[The Pane-Creep]], [[Velai, the Remembered Stranger]], [[Shalsor]], [[The Silver Veil]] |
| **Dream-Born** | Nightmares breaching into waking life | [[Ulzen, the Sleepless Host]], [[The Quilt-Worm]], [[Marrowbell]], [[The 13th Sleeper]], [[The Ink-Flooder]], [[Fareth]], [[The Echo That Waits]] |
| **Tethers** | Bound to corrupted leyline/glyph points | [[The Ink-Root]], [[The Glyph Maw]], [[The Bonechain]], [[Sethil]], [[The Inkwept Gate]], [[Kallur's Knot]] |

## Tier system

| Tier | Echo | Unfinished | Reflected | Dream-Born | Tether |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Minor** | Whisper | Lingerer | Glimmer | Whisper | Dormant |
| **Moderate** | Shadow | Interloper | Double | Tide | Active |
| **Major** | Voice | Architect | Replacement | Host | Evolved |

Each named entity is presumed to start at "Minor"/base tier per its category unless a GM escalates it through the table above — the source gives the tier-name progression generically, not per-entity.
