# Mother-Shell Elessh

**Origin**: a failed ascension ritual in a fertility cult during the Blood Moon. **Appearance**: a towering form of cracked clay wombs, weeping amniotic ichor. **Effect**: spreads pregnancy dreams; victims birth writhing song-beasts. **Goal**: to replace existing birth-gods in mortal memory.

An **Unfinished** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a being that tried to form but failed.
