# Atherion, The Weigher of Souls

*"You believe that death is the end. It is not. You believe that fate decides your worth. It does not. You believe that I will be merciful. I will not."*

Stands at the gateway between life and judgment. He does not decide if a soul passes on — that is [[Torvax]]'s role — he only weighs what they have done and become. Light souls rise untouched by regret; heavy souls sink under their own burdens; empty souls, forgotten even by themselves, are taken by [[Vorendral]] instead.

**Myth — The Day the Scales Broke**: a mortal tried to cheat Atherion's scales by carving false deeds into history and erasing his sins. When judgment came, the scales did not move. Atherion spoke once: "You have made yourself nothing. And so, nothing is what you shall become." The mortal was erased — not punished, not rewarded, simply gone.
