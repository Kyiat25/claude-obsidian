# Ul'Naresh, The Dream Inverted

*"You dreamed it wrong. Now you must live it that way."*

**Domain**: dreams infecting reality with their internal logic. **Symbol**: an eye that blinks inward. **Truth**: once dreamt, reality is permanently altered. **Mythic tie**: the Fall of the Dreaming Lantern — once a Spirit god. **Known manifestation**: the 7th Sleep of Kings, where rulers awoke believing they never were.

## How it bleeds into reality

- **Spirit Pantheon**: Nyzara's dreams ripple wrong; she hears songs from throats that don't exist; the moon reflects impossible constellations.
- **Water Pantheon**: [[Water Pantheon|Vaelora]]'s abyss projects nightmares into the tides — dreamtraps (spaces that don't exist but still drown), the ocean dreaming itself into new forms.

## Corruptions

- **Beasts**: sleepwalk into new forms, packs forget they're predators; **Dream-bears** feed on their own cubs without realizing.
- **Monsters**: appear only in shared dreams, leave physical wounds when awoken, exist between sleep and wake.
- **Mortals — Inversion Sleep**: wake states feel like dreams, dreams gain permanence; cast magic while unconscious; reshape surroundings through sleep-thought; eventually can't tell which state is real.

## Preferred manifestation zones

Wild Magic Zones, Astral Gates, sites of mass trauma, anywhere people sleep in groups.

## Warning signs

Shared nightmares across entire towns; dreams becoming prophetic; sleep-casting becoming common; reality feeling increasingly dreamlike.

| Tier (Levels) | Scale |

## Role in the Hollow Thread

Ul'Naresh plants false memories of [[The Hollow Thread]]'s seal being elsewhere, infecting the dreams of anyone researching it — the source is explicit that this could cut either way (misdirecting guardians, or guiding release-seekers to the true location).
