# Luthiris, The Tear of the Tides

*"You weep for them. But your grief will not bring them home."*

**Domains**: the ocean's mourning, the weeping of the waves, those lost to the sea. **Symbol**: a single droplet, suspended above still water. **Worshiped by**: sailors' widows, those who lost loved ones to the sea, poets and mourners. **Rivals**: [[Tzhor]], Nyxthys, Vaedoris. **Allies**: [[Eryndal]], Selmira, Ilyra.

The quiet grief that lingers after the storm has passed. She does not save the lost or take revenge — she only weeps, believed by some to be the ocean's own sorrow for what it has taken and cannot return.
