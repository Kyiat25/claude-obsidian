# Tharamis, The Forge-Father

*"A wasted flame is an insult to the forge. Use it well, or do not burn at all."*

**Domains**: forge, knowledge, craft. **Symbol**: a molten hammer striking an anvil. **Worshiped by**: blacksmiths, architects, builders. **Enemies**: [[Xethar]], Pyrris.

Sees [[Xethar]] as a reckless child throwing fire without purpose, believing Fire must build rather than merely destroy. Does not love [[Soltharion]]'s laws but prefers order over chaos, believing his rule may be necessary to keep Fire productive; supplies weapons to the Fire Wars without fighting them himself. In Dwarven myth he gave dwarves the First Forge and taught them the secrets of metal — to dwarves, fire is not freely given but a contract earned through labor, and a forge left untended grows cold just as a blade misused shatters. Among humans he is the silent watcher of every smith's hammer.
