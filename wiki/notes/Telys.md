# Telys, The Dream That Speaks

*"You will dream. You will see. And you will never know if it was truly real."*

**Domains**: dream prophecy, fate seen in sleep, the subconscious guide. **Symbol**: a silver crescent, with a closed eye in its center. **Worshiped by**: seers, those who receive divine messages in dreams, those haunted by visions. **Allies**: [[Vaithis]], [[Nyzara]], Elthir. **Enemies**: Nytheris, Xelthar, Oriveth.

Ensures some destinies are revealed only in dreams — his visions can be truth or deception, guidance or manipulation. To hear his whisper is to see the future but never know if it's certain; some believe him a guide, others a deceiver, and no one truly knows which.
