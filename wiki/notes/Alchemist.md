# Alchemist — Rank 1-4

*"You transform mundane materials into extraordinary substances through chemical mastery."* Batch 3, Magic-Focused category.

> [!note] Not the same as the Apothecary class
> This background covers general alchemical crafting (potions, acids, antitoxins). [[Apothecary]] is a full spellcasting class with its own Reagent-driven Concoctions resource — no mechanical overlap is stated in either source, but a character could plausibly be both.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Novice Alchemist | Alchemical Intuition | Create a particularly effective potion, survive a laboratory accident, or discover a useful reagent source |
| 2 | Practicing Alchemist | Master Chemist | Develop a new formula, supply an expedition with critical potions, or save lives with a rare antidote |
| 3 | Master Alchemist | Alchemical Mastery | Create a legendary potion, revolutionize the field, or synthesize a thought-impossible substance |
| 4 | Alchemical Genius | Transmutation Master | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Novice Alchemist

**Skill Proficiency**: Arcana, Nature. **Tool Proficiency**: alchemist's supplies, herbalism kit. **Equipment**: alchemist's supplies, herbalism kit, 3 empty vials, a leather apron, 15 gp.

**Feature — Alchemical Intuition**: identify common poisons and potions by sight/smell; advantage on checks to create basic alchemical items (acid, alchemist's fire, antitoxin); harvest alchemical reagents from natural sources (double materials from foraging); know safe handling procedures.

**Holdings**: a small shared workspace; basic equipment and glassware; a supply of common reagents; a contact with ingredient suppliers.

## Rank 2: Practicing Alchemist

**Holdings**: a personal laboratory, small but well-equipped; income from potions and reagents (25 gp/month); a substantial reagent supply; a network of 5-7 ingredient suppliers; a collection of 10-15 formulas; regular customers.

**Feature Expansion — Master Chemist**: create alchemical items at half normal cost; identify any common/uncommon potion or poison automatically; once per week, create an enhanced alchemical item (double duration or effect); know antidotes for most poisons; safely handle and neutralize dangerous chemicals.

## Rank 3: Master Alchemist

**Holdings**: a full laboratory with rare equipment (worth 1,200 gp); major income (90 gp/month); a comprehensive reagent collection (worth 800 gp); a network of suppliers across regions; 3-5 apprentices; a published formula book others use; contracts with wealthy clients.

**Feature Expansion — Alchemical Mastery**: create rare alchemical items given time and materials; your potions are 50% more effective; advantage on saves against poison and disease; once per week, brew an emergency potion in 1 hour; create custom alchemical items at DM discretion.

## Rank 4: Alchemical Genius

**Holdings**: multiple laboratories across cities; excellent income (250 gp/month); a warehouse of rare reagents and materials; an organization of suppliers and apprentices (20+); your formulas are industry standard; consulting contracts with governments and wealthy merchants.

**Feature — Transmutation Master**: create any non-magical alchemical item given time; immune to poison and disease; once per month, create an alchemical substance with a magical effect (DM approval); analyze and reverse-engineer any alchemical substance; your antidotes work on magical poisons. Alchemists across the land study your texts, rulers stockpile your creations for war, and some whisper you've achieved true transmutation.
