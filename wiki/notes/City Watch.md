# City Watch — Rank 1-4 (Law Enforcement)

*"You enforce the law in civilization's heart, where crime never sleeps."* Tier 2 (Specialized). Fulfills the "City Watch" slot [[FEARS Advanced Backgrounds]] flagged as not-yet-developed after Batch 1 — the lawful counterpart to [[Criminal]] and [[Bounty Hunter]].

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Watchman | Legal Authority | Make a significant arrest, prevent a major crime, or earn commendation for bravery |
| 2 | Sergeant | Street Wisdom | Solve a major case, break up a criminal organization, or save the city from a significant threat |
| 3 | Captain | Law's Shield | Become Chief of Watch, eliminate corruption in the Watch, or solve a case that saves the city |
| 4 | High Commander/Watch Chief | Guardian of Order | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Watchman

**Skill Proficiency**: Athletics, Insight. **Tool Proficiency**: one gaming set. **Equipment**: a badge or symbol of office, manacles, a wooden club, a horn, a uniform, 10 gp.

**Feature — Legal Authority**: you can arrest criminals and search for evidence; citizens must comply with lawful orders; you can requisition aid from other watchmen; you know the law and legal procedures.

**Holdings**: a bunk in the watch house; standard watch equipment (club, horn, lantern); a partner or small patrol group; access to watch resources.

## Rank 2: Sergeant

**Holdings**: private quarters in the watch house; income (25 gp/month); command of 5-8 watchmen; better equipment (sword, breastplate, badge of rank); a network of informants; authority to approve minor legal matters.

**Feature Expansion — Street Wisdom**: advantage on Investigation checks within your city; identify criminals by sight (known offenders); know which areas are dangerous and when; your squad provides backup within 10 minutes when called; you can authorize searches and seizures.

## Rank 3: Captain

**Holdings**: a captain's office and quarters; major income (70 gp/month); command of 40-60 watchmen; authority over a city district; a network of 20+ informants and contacts; you can requisition city resources; political connections.

**Feature Expansion — Law's Shield**: organize raids and large-scale operations; authority to investigate anyone within limits of law; once per week, call in favors from other districts or organizations; your word carries weight in legal matters; you can assign guards for protection details.

## Rank 4: High Commander/Watch Chief

**Holdings**: a headquarters office; excellent income (200 gp/month); command of 200+ watchmen; authority over all law enforcement in the city; a seat on the city council or direct access to the ruler; an intelligence network across the city; you can authorize use of military force.

**Feature — Guardian of Order**: mobilize the entire watch for operations; once per month, declare martial law in a crisis; your legal judgments are rarely questioned; you can pardon minor crimes or reduce sentences; you can deputize citizens for emergencies. Criminals fear your name, your career becomes a model for law enforcement, other cities seek your counsel on policing, and you've built a watch force that will outlast you.
