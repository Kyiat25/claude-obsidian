# Elythis, The Starlit Wanderer

*"You think the stars lead you. They do not. They are distant, unmoving, watching. It is the wind beneath them that carries you forward."*

**Domains**: celestial winds, the movement of the sky, the shift of storms. **Symbol**: a constellation map woven into wind currents. **Worshiped by**: star-navigators, explorers who follow shifting air patterns. **Rivals**: [[Astria]], [[Aelys]], [[Soltharion]].

Does not control the stars — that belongs to Spirit — but ensures no mortal reaches them unchanged. Not fate, only motion: those who worship her believe to stay in one place is to die.
