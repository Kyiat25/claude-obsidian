# Zephir, The Trickster Wind

*"Kings write laws. Gods forge fate. I laugh at them all."*

**Domains**: trickery, rebellion, fate's defiance, chaos on the wind. **Symbol**: a swirling gust shaped like a laughing face, shifting in form. **Worshiped by**: rogues, anarchists, gamblers, rebels, free spirits. **Allies**: [[Xilthos]], Vaethis, Orris, Myss. **Enemies**: [[Soltharion]], [[Astria]], Keldros.

Doesn't just break rules — ensures they never last; whispers to complacent kings, opportunistic thieves, and freedom-seeking lovers. His temples don't stand still; they move, hidden meeting places for those who refuse to be ruled. Already established elsewhere in this cluster (see [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]) as having illusions that take on independent life, a symptom of [[Esh'Tarah]]'s corruption.

**The Judgment of Zephir** — see [[The Court of the Silent Tribunal]]'s Trial of the Whispered Lie for the full account: he intercepted [[Astria]]'s genuine warning of a coming Eldritch Moon and twisted it into a half-lie that caused a proto-Loom-City to collapse under self-fulfilling delusion. [[Thirian]] invoked the Silent Tribunal, and Zephir was cursed to speak only in illusions and riddles ever since, his true voice scattered into the upper sky.
