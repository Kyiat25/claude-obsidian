# Investigator (Background) — Rank 1-4 (Solver of Mysteries)

*"You uncover truth through keen observation and relentless investigation."* Tier 2 (Specialized).

> [!note] Naming collision with the Investigator class — flagged, not silently resolved
> [[FEARS Advanced Backgrounds]]'s own "not-yet-developed" list, written after Batch 1, explicitly called this out in advance: "Investigator (as a background, not the [[Investigator]] class)." Now that it has arrived, the collision is real — a character could plausibly be an [[Investigator]]-class detective who *also* has this background, or a completely different class using this background as flavor (a Rogue running a detective agency, for instance). This page is disambiguated with "(Background)" in its filename since Obsidian/the filesystem can't have two files both named `Investigator.md`. No mechanical interaction between the class and the background is stated anywhere in either source.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Private Investigator | Eye for Detail | Solve a difficult case, catch a criminal who evaded authorities, or gain a reputation for discretion |
| 2 | Established Detective | Deductive Reasoning | Solve a high-profile case, expose a major conspiracy, or catch a serial criminal |
| 3 | Master Investigator | Solve the Unsolvable | Solve a legendary cold case, bring down a major criminal organization, or write a text on investigative methods |
| 4 | Legendary Detective | Nothing Escapes You | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Private Investigator

**Skill Proficiency**: Insight, Investigation. **Tool Proficiency**: disguise kit or thieves' tools. **Equipment**: a magnifying glass, a notebook and pencil, a case file folder, common clothes, 15 gp.

**Feature — Eye for Detail**: advantage on Investigation checks to find clues; reconstruct events from evidence (DC 15 Investigation check); know how to interview witnesses effectively; identify when someone is hiding something.

**Specialty** (choose your focus): Murder investigations, Missing persons, Insurance fraud, Infidelity cases, Theft and burglary, Blackmail cases.

**Holdings**: a small office, often your residence; basic investigation tools; 2-3 clients; a contact in the city watch or guards.

## Rank 2: Established Detective

**Holdings**: proper office space; income from cases (30 gp/month); a filing cabinet full of solved cases; a network of 8-12 informants; better equipment (disguises, lockpicks, etc.); contacts in various organizations; a growing reputation for solving "impossible" cases.

**Feature Expansion — Deductive Reasoning**: advantage on Intelligence checks when solving mysteries; recognize patterns and connections quickly; once per day, ask the DM for a crucial clue you might have missed; tail suspects without being noticed (advantage on Stealth).

## Rank 3: Master Investigator

**Holdings**: a well-appointed office with an assistant; major income from high-profile cases (100 gp/month); extensive files on criminals and mysteries; a network of 25+ contacts and informants; access to restricted information through connections; clients including nobility and wealthy merchants; published cases that others study.

**Feature Expansion — Solve the Unsolvable**: reconstruct entire crimes from minimal evidence; advantage on all mental ability checks related to investigation; once per week, have a breakthrough that solves a major mystery; identify criminals by method; your testimony is trusted in legal proceedings.

## Rank 4: Legendary Detective

**Holdings**: a detective agency with multiple investigators working for you; excellent income (250 gp/month); archives of thousands of solved cases; a network of 50+ informants across the region; influence with law enforcement and legal systems; your methods are taught in academies; international reputation.

**Feature — Nothing Escapes You**: automatic success on Investigation checks (DC 20 or lower); solve nearly any mystery given time and evidence; once per month, expose a hidden truth (conspiracy, criminal mastermind, hidden plot); your presence makes criminals nervous; identify lies automatically. Rulers hire you for the most sensitive cases, other investigators consult you, criminals fear you more than the law, and your methods revolutionize investigative work.
