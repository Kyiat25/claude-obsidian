# Ythera, The Ashen Bride

*"I swore to wait by the fire until they returned. I did not realize that the fire would burn out first."*

**Domains**: eternal love, waiting, the ashes of broken promises. **Symbol**: a candle burned down to its final flicker. **Worshiped by**: lovers waiting for their beloved to return, widows, those who refuse to move on.

Once a goddess of love and devotion, prayed to for reunions and safe returns — but those who prayed to her never came back. Her temples are abandoned now, her name spoken only by those still waiting for something that will never return. Myth holds she waited at the edge of the world for her lover to return from war; he never did, and she waits still, a goddess of patience turned into a goddess of sorrow.
