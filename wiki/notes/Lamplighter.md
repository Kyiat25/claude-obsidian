# Lamplighter (Night Watchman) — Rank 1-4

*"You guard the streets when darkness falls."* Batch 3, Urban Specialist category. Source dual-names this "Lamplighter / Night Watchman"; filed under the first name. An informal, observation-based nighttime presence rather than [[City Watch]]'s formal arrest authority — no legal power is granted at any rank.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Lamplighter | Night Eyes | Stop crime through timely observation, maintain lamps through a major event, or identify a criminal pattern |
| 2 | Senior Lamplighter | Guardian of Night | Foil a major crime, become an unofficial informant network leader, or save someone important during rounds |
| 3 | Night Captain | Master of Darkness | Prevent disaster through vigilance, break a criminal network through observation, or be appointed master of night operations |
| 4 | Master of the Night | Lord of Darkness | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Lamplighter

**Skill Proficiency**: Perception, Stealth. **Tool Proficiency**: tinker's tools. **Equipment**: tinker's tools, a lamp lighter's pole, a lantern, 3 flasks of oil, dark clothes, 10 gp.

**Feature — Night Eyes**: advantage on Perception checks in dim light or darkness; know your city's street layout intimately; navigate the city in complete darkness without getting lost; notice suspicious activity others miss; know all alleys and shortcuts.

**Holdings**: a small room near the lamp depot; a maintained route (30-50 lamps); basic repair tools; fellow lamplighters as contacts.

## Rank 2: Senior Lamplighter

**Holdings**: better lodgings; a salary (15 gp/month); authority over 10-15 lamps and 3-5 junior lamplighters; quality tools and equipment; a reputation with the night watch; you know every late-night establishment in your district.

**Feature Expansion — Guardian of Night**: advantage on Insight checks to detect suspicious behavior; request assistance from the night watch; know who operates in the streets at night; once per week, be in exactly the right place at the right time (DM lets you witness an important event); your presence deters petty crime.

## Rank 3: Night Captain

**Holdings**: a comfortable home; a good salary (50 gp/month); authority over all lamplighters in your district (20-30); informal authority over night watch patrols; a network of night workers reporting to you; you can command emergency lighting changes; respected by the guard captain.

**Feature Expansion — Master of Darkness**: sense disturbances in your district (major crimes or incidents); advantage on all night navigation/perception checks; organize a night watch response to threats; once per month, illuminate or darken the entire district; your word carries weight with both criminals and law enforcement.

## Rank 4: Master of the Night

**Holdings**: a fine house; an excellent salary (120 gp/month); command of all night operations in the city (100+ workers); integration with the city guard; a network of informants across every district; authority to implement emergency measures; lighting-system innovations you developed.

**Feature — Lord of Darkness**: automatically aware of major nighttime incidents in your city; navigate any urban environment in darkness perfectly; once per week, organize a city-wide response to a threat; criminals fear your knowledge of night activities; declare and enforce curfew. The city council consults you on public safety, and some say you have supernatural awareness of the city at night.
