# Timeless Keepers — The Last Wardens of Fate

*"They do not bargain. They do not judge. They are not kind, nor cruel. They are simply… Keepers. If you see one, it is because you have tampered with fate too much."* — Master Chronicler Tivalis, the Silent Archive

Cosmic-neutral, non-sentient enforcers of anti-paradox balance — not gods, not mortals, not aligned with any conventional faction. Triggered to appear by excessive use of the [[Whisper Engine]], Forbidden Whispers, or accumulating reality contradictions. They do not act unless absolutely necessary, ignoring small changes and individual lives entirely — but once someone bends fate beyond its limits, they arrive not as executioners but as **correctors**. Their origin is unknown even in-world: some scholars believe they were created at the dawn of existence to maintain fate's balance; others whisper they are all that remains of those erased by [[The Whispering Dark]], now doomed to prevent others sharing that fate.

## The Bargains

When a Timeless Keeper manifests, it offers an escalating series of three bargains before resorting to erasure: **"Be Forgotten"** (the character remains and keeps all abilities, but is erased from every NPC's memory), **"Lose What Was Never Meant to Be"** (the GM permanently removes a spell, ability, memory, or companion of the Keeper's choosing), and **"Offer Another in Your Place"** (the player must choose an NPC or ally to vanish completely instead). Refusing all three bargains means [[The Whispering Dark]] takes the character — full erasure from existence.
