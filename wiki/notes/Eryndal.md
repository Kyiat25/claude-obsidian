# Eryndal, The One Who Listens

*"No one else hears you. But I do."*

**Domains**: the last words of sailors, lost voices beneath the waves. **Symbol**: a single candle floating on the waves. **Worshiped by**: sailors before their final journey, families who lost loved ones at sea. **Rivals**: [[Tzhor]], Morgathis. **Allies**: [[Vaedoris]], Nyssara.

Does not judge — only ensures no final words are lost. The god sailors speak to in the moment they believe they will die, not to be saved, but to be heard.
