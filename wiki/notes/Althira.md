# Althira, The Whirlwind Mind

*"You must create faster than you can think! Only the mind that outpaces itself can reach true genius!"*

**Domains**: inspiration, sudden genius, unstable innovation. **Symbol**: a wind-blown blueprint, half-formed. **Worshiped by**: gnomes, inventors, mages who experiment recklessly. **Allies**: [[Zephir]], Tyralis, Pyrris. **Enemies**: [[Aelys]], Keldros, Kaelor.

The spirit of chaotic genius — inspiration that comes in a flash, too fast to grasp. The reason inventors wake with ideas they cannot fully remember.
