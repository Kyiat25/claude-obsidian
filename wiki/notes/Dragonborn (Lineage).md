# Dragonborn

*"We are not dragons. We are their shadow. We are not fire. We are its consequence."*

**Origin myth**: seared into being by [[Soltharion]] (the Sun Tyrant) in a final act of defiance against annihilation by the Weave, colliding with [[Xethar]] (the Wildfire) — "not born but tempered, not blessed but branded." [[Keldros]], [[Vatharun]], [[Aelys]], and [[Ignis]] each contributed a gift (discipline, duty, memory, the building flame) without being worshipped for it — gods as architects who finished their work and moved on. Homeland: the fallen Dragonborn empire of Ashkarra, scattered after its Collapse (see [[The Burning Concord]] for the surviving faith/caste structure).

**Physical**: matures by 15 (age of caste confirmation), lives 80–100 years (150–250 for the Flame-Tested); Medium, 6–7 ft., 30 ft. speed, scaled.

## Traits

- **Draconic Ancestry**: choose a dragon type (Black/Blue/Brass/Bronze/Copper/Gold/Green/Red/Silver/White) — sets breath weapon damage type and shape (line or cone)
- **Breath Weapon**: action, DC 8+CON+PB, 2d6 (3d6 at 6th, 4d6 at 11th, 5d6 at 16th) damage on a failed save (half on success); recharges on short/long rest
- **Damage Resistance**: to your ancestry's damage type
- **Draconic Presence** *(cultural)*: Intimidation proficiency, doubled proficiency bonus when invoking caste authority
- **Branded Purpose**: Caste-Loyal (artisan tool or martial weapon proficiency) or Caste-Broken (Deception or Survival)
- **Languages**: Common + Draconic

**Mental**: Focus +1 (bred-in discipline) / Sanity +0.

**DCS Integration**: Dodge +0 / Endure +1 / Deflect +1; natural DR +2 (plus an extra +2 DR against your ancestry's own damage type). Advantage on Rally when led by a superior, disadvantage when leaderless.

## Corruption: The Fractured Brand

Corruption entering through broken hierarchy, violated oaths, or shattered order hits catastrophically. At Corruption 10+: Brand-Flicker (wrong scale colors), Purpose-Strain (WIS saves to follow orders), Caste-Doubt. **Fractured Brand table** (1d6): False Caste, Brand-Burn (breath weapon disabled until long rest), Structure-Void (1d4 psychic + lose a skill proficiency), Weight of Chaos (Sanity save or −1d4), Eldritch Touch (+1 Whisper Point, next Intimidation auto-fails), or Flame Resists (Corruption −1).

## Relations

Respects dwarven structure/craft-discipline; distrusts elves ("remember, but do not act"); fascinated by human chaos-that-endures; kinship with orcs over trial-focused strength; baffled by halflings functioning without structure. The Faith of the Bound Pattern is viewed with suspicion by orthodox Dragonborn as antithetical to divine order, though many scattered Dragonborn have adopted it since the Collapse.
