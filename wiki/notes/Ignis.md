# Ignis, The Eternal Flame

*"I was First. I will always be First. This war is an illusion, and I do not indulge illusions."*

**Domains**: war, forge, ambition. **Symbol**: a burning anvil with an unbroken flame. **Worshiped by**: kings, conquerors, blacksmiths. **Enemies**: [[Xethar]], [[Soltharion]] (quietly), Mordrath.

The great and undying forge fueling all progress — prayed to not for mercy but for strength, for the will to build, rule, and impose order on chaos. Fire is not truly first among the elements (Earth, Water, and Air all preceded it), but Ignis burned paths through the wild Weave, making way for order and civilization, and the world came to believe Fire was first anyway. He knows the truth privately and refuses to acknowledge it, hoping it's never spoken aloud. He lets [[Soltharion]], [[Xethar]], and [[Vaeldros]] fight among themselves as distractions, tolerates [[Tharamis]] (creation is necessary for conquest), and seeks no allies, only servants — watching [[Soltharion]] carefully, knowing his "son" will one day try to take his throne.
