# Weapon Skills

The technique library behind [[Weapon Mastery]]. Each skill has an Action version; some add a Fighter-only Bonus Action and/or Reaction variant.

## Swords

- **Arcing Slash** (Greatsword) — half damage to a second target within 5 ft; full damage at +3 Momentum.
- **Grinding Halt** (Greatsword, Reaction) — resist forced movement with a bonus roll.
- **Break Flow** (Greatsword) — strip 1 Momentum from the target; +1d6 force damage at +3 Momentum. Fighter Reaction: trigger whenever a creature gains Momentum from any source.
- **Lock Blades** (Longsword/Rapier, Reaction) — parry attempt via attack roll; success negates the attack and grants +2 to your next Evasion.
- **Short Draw** (Longsword/Scimitar) — pommel strike, no damage, grants advantage on the next attack against the target.
- **Bloody Wound** (Scimitar/Dagger) — 1d4 ongoing bleed (CON save ends); 2d4 + 1 Momentum drain at +3 Momentum.
- **Main Gauche** (Rapier + dagger, Reaction) — add PB to Evasion against the triggering attack.

## Axes

- **Crushing Blow** (Battleaxe/Maul) — permanently reduces target's DR by 1 (2 at +3 Momentum) until armor repaired.
- **Cleave** (Battleaxe) — half damage to a second target within 5 ft; full at +3 Momentum.
- **Hamstring** (Battleaxe) — CON save or speed halved; escalates at +2/+3 Momentum (no Dash, then ¾ reduction + Disadvantage on DEX saves). Cross-weapon: suits any slashing/piercing weapon at GM discretion.
- **Disarming Parry** (Glaive/Spear, Reaction) — attack roll to disarm an attacker.
- **Trip** (Glaive/Halberd/War Pick) — STR save or prone, vs. Large or smaller.

## Polearms

- **Pin** (Spear) — STR save or speed 0; at +3 Momentum, target also loses 1 Momentum (unpreventable).
- **Rebuff** (Halberd) — push up to 10 ft on hit.
- **Charge** (Lance) — requires 20 ft running start; +1d12 piercing, STR save or prone; advantage on all damage at +3 Momentum.
- **Repel Charge** (Lance, Reaction) — auto-crit against a charging attacker, STR save or prone (+ dismount).

## Bludgeons

- **Bruising Blow** (Mace/Warhammer) — CON save or can't use DEX mod on Dodge until healed/treated.
- **Ribshatter** (Mace/Morningstar) — CON save or stunned (longer if damage ≥ target's CON score).
- **Crushing Blow** (Maul) — as Battleaxe.
- **Hurling Impact** (Maul) — STR save or pushed 15 ft.
- **Piercing Point** (War Pick) — ignores all DR; crits on 19-20 at +3 Momentum.
- **Sundering Strike** (Warhammer) — damages a wielded weapon (disadvantage on attacks with it); a second hit destroys it.
- **Shatter Guard** (Warhammer) — DR −2 until your next turn, scaling to −3/−4 and extending to two attacks at +2/+3 Momentum. Doesn't stack with Crushing Blow — use the greater. Cross-weapon: suits bludgeoning/heavy weapons.

## Daggers & short blades

- **Bleed** (Dagger) — as Scimitar's Bloody Wound.
- **Pinning Point** (Dagger) — pins a Large-or-smaller creature to a surface; STR save each turn to escape.
- **Concealed Blade** (Dagger) — Sleight of Hand vs. Perception to hide the weapon; advantage vs. surprised targets or before their first turn.

## Ranged weapons

- **Pinning Shot** (Bow) — halves movement; strips the Reaction at +3 Momentum.
- **Trick Shot** (Bow) — roll twice, lower result still hitting is an automatic crit.
- **Piercing Bolt** (Crossbow) — ignores all DR; crits on 19-20 at +3 Momentum.
- **Shrapnel Shot** (Heavy Crossbow) — destroys an object for an AoE burst (DEX save for damage, or CON save for blinded).

## Monk weapons

- **Lunge** (Bō) — attack + swap positions with the target.
- **Vault** (Bō) — double long-jump distance, with an attack on landing adjacent.
- **Flow Strike** (Monk & Bender, unarmed) — 5 ft move without provoking; regain 1 Technique Point at +3 Momentum. See [[Weapon Mastery]].
- **Butterfly Kick** (Unarmed) — free Dodge Evasion roll against the next attack, no reaction spent.
- **Gentle Rebuke** (Unarmed, Reaction) — a non-damaging reaction attack that throws the target 10 ft prone instead.

## Flails & whips

- **Chain Garrote** (Flail) — grapple around the throat: target can't speak/breathe, Disadvantage on attacks vs. you; suffocates if surprised.
- **Shield Snare** (Flail) — ignores shield DR/Evasion bonus; STR save or the shield is pulled away.
- **Bullwhip** (Whip) — vs. beasts/low-INT targets, WIS save or Frightened.
- **Snare** (Whip) — STR save to disarm, or restrain the wielder if targeting their weapon.

## Hammers

- **Dazing Blow** (Light Hammer, ranged) — CON save or Incapacitated until next turn.
- **Sunder Joints** (Light Hammer) — vs. medium/heavy armor, reduces movement by 10 ft (non-cumulative) instead of dealing damage.
