# Diplomat — Rank 1-4 (Political Envoy)

*"You navigate the treacherous waters of politics and international relations."* Tier 2 (Specialized). Fulfills the "Diplomat" slot [[FEARS Advanced Backgrounds]] flagged as not-yet-developed after Batch 1.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Attaché | Diplomatic Immunity | Negotiate a minor treaty, prevent a diplomatic incident, or establish an important relationship |
| 2 | Ambassador's Aide | Diplomatic Arts | Negotiate a significant agreement, prevent a war, or establish a profitable trade relationship |
| 3 | Ambassador | Voice of the Nation | Negotiate a major treaty, prevent a war, or broker a landscape-changing alliance |
| 4 | Chancellor/High Ambassador | Master Diplomat | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Attaché

**Skill Proficiency**: Insight, Persuasion. **Language**: two of choice. **Equipment**: fine clothes, diplomatic papers, the seal of your nation/lord, a writing kit, 20 gp.

**Feature — Diplomatic Immunity**: cannot usually be arrested or detained in foreign lands; entitled to meetings with officials; access to embassies and consulates; your correspondence is (theoretically) protected.

**Specialty** (choose your focus): Trade negotiations, Military alliances, Cultural exchange, Peace negotiations, Espionage, Border disputes.

**Holdings**: quarters in an embassy or consulate; access to diplomatic resources; a mentor (senior diplomat); official credentials.

## Rank 2: Ambassador's Aide

**Holdings**: private quarters; income (30 gp/month); authority to negotiate on the ambassador's behalf; a network of contacts in 3-5 foreign courts; access to intelligence reports; an official seal for correspondence.

**Feature Expansion — Diplomatic Arts**: advantage on Persuasion checks with nobility and officials; identify political factions and loyalties; know proper etiquette for most cultures; arrange meetings with important figures; your recommendations influence policy.

## Rank 3: Ambassador

**Holdings**: an embassy or ambassadorial residence; major income (100 gp/month); a staff of 15-20 (clerks, guards, servants); authority to negotiate treaties; a network of contacts across multiple nations; access to state secrets; a diplomatic pouch for secure communication.

**Feature Expansion — Voice of the Nation**: speak for your nation in official capacity; once per week, arrange significant political action (a meeting between leaders, a trade agreement, a military alliance); rarely-violated immunity in foreign lands; your assessments shape foreign policy; you can grant asylum or safe passage.

## Rank 4: Chancellor/High Ambassador

**Holdings**: a grand embassy or palace residence; excellent income (300 gp/month); multiple embassies under your authority; a staff of 50+; direct access to your nation's ruler; an intelligence network spanning multiple nations; authority to commit your nation to agreements.

**Feature — Master Diplomat**: automatic success on Persuasion checks (DC 20 or lower) in diplomatic contexts; once per month, broker an agreement affecting multiple nations; organize summits and conferences; your mediation ends conflicts; rulers seek your counsel on foreign affairs. Peace treaties are named after you, your memoirs become required reading for diplomats, multiple nations seek you as a neutral arbiter, and you've prevented wars through sheer diplomacy.
