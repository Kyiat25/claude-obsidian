# Vael'Akir, The Hunger Without Mouth

*"You want what was never yours. You always did."*

**Domain**: abstract hunger for things that don't exist. **Symbol**: a gaping void within a circle of fangs that never touch. **Truth**: to be touched is to yearn endlessly for something never real. **Mythic tie**: the Shadow War — suspected devourer of lost demigods. **Effect**: victims cannot name what they desire but will destroy to find it.

## How it bleeds into reality

- **Fire Pantheon**: [[Fire Pantheon|Pyrris]]'s illusions leave echoes of longing; desire for nonexistent things is one of the first signs of touch; ambition becomes literal hunger.
- **Spirit Pantheon**: the Echoed Mother's rebirth domain is hijacked — souls reincarnate with needs they never had, seeking concepts instead of sustenance.

## Corruptions

- **Beasts — Gorgebeasts**: endlessly feed without growth, stomachs never fill, tongues devour thought — hunt things that don't exist.
- **Monsters**: mimics shift to metaphorical forms, mimicking longing instead of objects — become desire given teeth.
- **Mortals — Desire Sickness**: hungering for truths, people, and places that never existed; lovers vanish, cities turn to sand, meals taste like memory; eventually consume themselves seeking the impossible.

## Preferred manifestation zones

Arcane Rifts, sites of great tragedy or loss, abandoned temples, places where dreams were shattered.

## Warning signs

Compulsive searching for unnamed things; markets selling impossible goods; people starving despite eating; dreams of perfect satisfaction that leave you emptier.
