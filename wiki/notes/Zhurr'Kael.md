# Zhurr'Kael, The Logic That Devours

*"One plus one is fire. Two plus two is hunger. Three plus three is your mother's face turning to salt."*

**Domain**: rational thought consuming itself through paradox. **Symbol**: a triangle where every corner denies the other two. **Truth**: converts belief into contradiction. **Mythic tie**: the Cult of Iron Geometry — philosophers who tore minds open through rational worship. **Effect**: temples become impossible shapes, logic eats meaning.

## How it bleeds into reality

- **Spirit Pantheon**: Aelys finds mathematics that multiply into symbols; glyphs change meaning based on who reads them; knowledge becomes a self-referential trap.
- **Fire Pantheon**: [[Fire Pantheon|Zerah]]'s prophetic logic breaks; prophecy-threads terminate in impossible forks; the future contradicts itself.

## Corruptions

- **Beasts**: act with structured ritual but no purpose; herds walk geometric spirals until death, following logic that leads nowhere.
- **Monsters**: begin uttering equations; when solved, the solver loses their identity — mathematics made flesh.
- **Mortals — Thought Loops**: thinking themselves into collapse; construct philosophies that erase believers; logic becomes a virus in the mind, able to prove anything and therefore nothing.

## Preferred manifestation zones

Wild Magic Zones, universities and libraries, philosopher's towers, sites of intense intellectual debate.

## Warning signs

Mathematics stops working correctly; logical arguments lead to impossible conclusions; buildings with paradoxical architecture; people caught in verbal loops.

## Unwitting agent: Thalanis the Void-Scholar

Per [[Arc 4C - The Eldritch Rewriter|Campaign Arc 4C]], a wizard named Thalanis is infected with Zhurr'Kael corruption and genuinely cannot perceive it — his "upgraded mathematics" are Zhurr'Kael thought-loops, and his happy, transformed followers are caught in the same self-consuming logic mistaken for enlightenment.
