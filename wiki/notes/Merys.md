# Merys, The Whispering Thought

*"You knew once. You will know again. But only when it is no longer useful to you."*

**Domains**: forgotten genius, lost discoveries, the cost of knowledge. **Symbol**: a half-written manuscript, its letters shifting. **Worshiped by**: gnomes, wizards, scholars obsessed with uncovering lost secrets. **Allies**: [[Oriveth]], [[Zeythis]], [[Thyvael]]. **Enemies**: [[Aelys]], [[Nyzara]], [[Kaelor]].

Governs the knowledge that is always out of reach — the discovery lost before it could be written, the spell that worked once but never again. Gnomes curse her when their best ideas are forgotten.
