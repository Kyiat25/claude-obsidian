# Farmer (Yeoman) — Rank 1-4

*"You work the land and provide food for civilization."* Batch 3, Working Class category. Source dual-names this "Farmer / Yeoman"; filed under the first name.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Farm Laborer | Agricultural Knowledge | Save a harvest from disaster, learn an advanced technique, or inherit/purchase a small plot of land |
| 2 | Yeoman Farmer | Master Farmer | Expand your farm significantly, develop an improved crop variety, or become the leading farmer in your community |
| 3 | Prosperous Landowner | Agricultural Expertise | Establish an agricultural innovation, survive a famine year, or become a voice for the farming community with nobility |
| 4 | Agricultural Lord | Master of the Harvest | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Farm Laborer

**Skill Proficiency**: Animal Handling, Nature. **Tool Proficiency**: vehicles (land). **Equipment**: farming tools (hoe, sickle, etc.), a cart, work clothes, 5 gp.

**Feature — Agricultural Knowledge**: know planting and harvest seasons for common crops; judge soil quality and know what grows well; advantage on Animal Handling checks with farm animals; predict weather for the next 24 hours; care for crops and livestock.

**Holdings**: a room in a farmhouse or barn; modest wages or food/shelter; access to farm equipment; fellow workers as contacts.

## Rank 2: Yeoman Farmer

**Holdings**: a small farm (5-10 acres); modest income from crops (15 gp/month); a farmhouse and barn; basic livestock (chickens, pig, cow); farming equipment; you can feed yourself and family comfortably; respect in the farming community.

**Feature Expansion — Master Farmer**: advantage on Nature checks related to agriculture; your crops yield 25% more than average; know how to manage land sustainably; identify plant diseases and pests; once per season, produce an exceptional harvest (50% increase).

## Rank 3: Prosperous Landowner

**Holdings**: a large farm (50-100 acres); good income (60 gp/month); multiple buildings and facilities; substantial livestock; 5-10 farm workers; regular market surplus to sell; influence in the farming community; savings to survive bad years.

**Feature Expansion — Agricultural Expertise**: your harvests are consistently excellent; advise other farmers, spreading your techniques; advantage on all farming/land-management checks; once per year, produce a legendary harvest (double normal yield, exceptional quality); identify causes of agricultural problems.

## Rank 4: Agricultural Lord

**Holdings**: multiple farms totaling hundreds of acres; excellent income (200 gp/month); dozens of farm workers and tenant farmers; advanced equipment and facilities; control over regional food supply; economic and political influence; your methods are adopted widely.

**Feature — Master of the Harvest**: your lands never fail — always a good harvest; once per year, produce a miraculous harvest feeding an entire region; speak with plants (supernatural awareness of the land's health); your presence improves soil and crop health; weather seems to favor your fields. Nobles depend on your food supply, your techniques feed nations, and druids respect your harmony with the land.
