# Garrun, The Beastlord

*"To be prey is to learn fear. To be predator is to learn restraint. All beasts must know both."*

**Domain**: primal strength, the wild hunt, predator and prey. **Symbol**: a stag skull entwined with roots and fangs. **Worshiped by**: hunters, beast tamers, rangers, barbarian warbands, tribal survivalists.

The raw instinct of the wild — the balance of predator and prey, ensuring neither overreaches. Myth: he once hunted a beast that could never die, and when he caught it, he let it go, to remind himself that even the strongest must show mercy. Ally of [[Thyssir]] (spirit of beasts), respected by [[Ishara]]. **Aspect of the Lock**: he is the teeth and muscle enforcing the land's natural balance — if he fails, ecosystems collapse and corruption may spread.
