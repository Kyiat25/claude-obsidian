# Personal Glyphs

Fifth-Law Glyphs — each mortal, god, or entity carries one, determining not *what* power they have but *how* they channel it and interact with the fate they're given.

| Glyph | Represents | Manifestation |
|---|---|---|
| **Glyph of Power** | Strength, dominance, physicality | Warriors, resilience, wielding raw might through leylines |
| **Glyph of Knowledge** | Intellect, memory, study | Learning, magical study, understanding leyline secrets |
| **Glyph of Flow** | Balance, movement, adaptation | Agility, change, shifting through the Weave's currents |
| **Glyph of Passion** | Emotion, desire, instinct | Wild power, sorcery fueled by raw emotional force |
| **Glyph of Shadows** | Deception, trickery, illusion | Manipulating perception, hiding within the Weave |
| **Glyph of Creation** | Art, invention, change | Forging, innovating, shaping reality through craft or will |
| **Glyph of Destruction** | Chaos, unmaking, death | Unraveling or unmaking existence through entropy or force |
| **Glyph of Spirit** | Faith, willpower, inner strength | Connection to divine forces, the afterlife, higher cosmic energies |
| **Glyph of Mind** | Thought, telepathy, psionics | Mental resilience, telepathic power, will-based magical control |

## How they shift across planar reflections

The same nine glyphs manifest completely differently depending on which of the Triadic Layers of Reality a bearer stands in:

| Glyph | Material Plane | [[Luminous Echo]] (Feywild) | [[Umbral Veil]] (Shadowfell) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Power | Physical strength | Reshape surroundings through sheer dominance | A fading echo of former might, drawn from ancient battles |
| Knowledge | Study, memory | Record/decipher the Feywild's chaotic leylines | Hoards fragmented, incomplete knowledge |
| Flow | Adaptation | Drift through time loops, change form at will | Slow, cyclical, repetitive motion |
| Passion | Emotional fuel | Direct emotion-to-magic (a laugh creates light) | Muted, dulled to a whisper |
| Shadows | Illusion | Vanish, alter one's own story, rewrite past events | Merge with real, detached spectral echoes |
| Creation | Craft | Craft living constructs, sculpt entire worlds from thought | Requires immense willpower to force any change |
| Destruction | Entropy | Unmaking and reshaping, dissolving forests into raw magic | Erosion — unravels things simply by existing |
| Spirit | Divine connection | Commune with echoes of past Weave-walkers | One of the strongest here — communes with or binds the trapped dead |
| Mind | Mental control | Illusions indistinguishable from truth | Muddled, sluggish, prone to decay |

The pattern is consistent across both reflections: the Feywild ([[Luminous Echo]]) amplifies and exaggerates every glyph into raw creative potential, while the Shadowfell ([[Umbral Veil]]) mutes, fragments, or chains every glyph to the past. Neither reflection *empowers* the way the Material Plane does — Feywild empowers through excess, Shadowfell burdens through persistence.
