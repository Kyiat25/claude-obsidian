# Lysara, The Muse of Fire

*"The world is cold, empty, meaningless. But fire brings light, colour, beauty. It gives life."*

**Domains**: passion, art, music, inspiration. **Symbol**: a swirling flame in the shape of a dancer. **Worshiped by**: bards, painters, lovers, dreamers. **Allies**: [[Zephir]], [[Zerah]], Telys. **Enemies**: [[Vaeldros]], Xalros, [[The Whispering Dark]].

Fire as joy, passion, and the creative force burning in all things — the reason mortals create, the inspiration turning sparks into masterpieces; her flame doesn't destroy, it ignites the soul. Her temples are theaters, her priests are performers. Myth holds she was once a nearly-forgotten lesser goddess until she gifted fire to the first artist, ensuring creation would never fade from the world.
