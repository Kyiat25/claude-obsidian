# Glacial Moon

*"There is no motion in its light. Only the certainty that nothing will ever move again."*

First seen 3rd Age, 1025 TA, freezing a Loom-City harbor overnight. A chilling anomaly — a pale moon so cold it silences stars.

## Nature

Stillness that transcends weather, magic, and thought. Known as "The Stillstone Above" or "Anti-Motion Sigil" — possibly the work of [[Ith'Quess]], or a failed attempt to stop time entirely.

## World effects

**Temporal Arrest**: time flows at half-speed in moonlight (a narrative effect); rivers, winds, and falling leaves slow to near-still; fires flicker without warmth. NPCs become emotionally dulled, losing motivation and speaking in monotone; conversations lose urgency, with delayed or partial responses.

**Monster effects**: constructs, undead, and oozes become hyper-stable (immune to prone/restrained); living creatures must make a DC 13 Con save each dawn or gain Frostbind, and at 3 stacks of Frostbind become paralyzed until magically thawed.

## Madness

Short-term: belief that you're slowing down. Long-term: paranoia that emotions and memories are being frozen. Indefinite: refusal to act — becoming a passive observer.

## Campaign use

A town frozen mid-moment, in perfect stasis, unaging and undying; a seer's prophecies cycle endlessly; a Tether Spirit awakens beneath a ruined temple.
