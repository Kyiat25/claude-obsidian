# Theroneth, The Deep Made Radiant

*"Vaelora hides the deep. Nyxthys is the deep. I am the reason you go anyway."*

**Domains**: the beauty of the deep, light in dark water, coral and glow, wonder as a lure. **Symbol**: a spiral of pale light descending into blue-black. **Worshiped by**: deep-divers, reef-tenders, glass-artists, the incorrigibly curious.

[[Vaelora]]'s true opposite number: both know what is down there, but Vaelora says it should not be found, Theroneth says it should be seen — the deep's secrecy and its glory are one argument with two goddesses. Sailors' wisdom holds her the most dangerous of the gentle gods: nothing needs to bait a hook when Theroneth herself is the bait. Reef-villages hold the Descent of Lamps once a year, sinking weighted lanterns along the drop-off, "returning light to the one who lends it." Divers who have seen the glow-gardens are forbidden to describe them fully — one detail per telling, so the deep is never fully spent.
