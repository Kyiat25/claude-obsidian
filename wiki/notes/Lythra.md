# Lythra, The Keeper of Lies

*"Not all who tell prophecy serve fate. Some of us serve something else."*

**Domains**: false omens, lies that become truth, the deception of the Weave. **Symbol**: a mask with one side cracked, hiding a hollow void. **Worshiped by**: con artists, false prophets, illusionists who claim divine knowledge. **Allies**: [[Vel'Zyren]], [[Xelthar]], Nyxaris. **Enemies**: [[Aelys]], [[Vaithis]], [[Nyzara]].

Does not lie — she simply ensures others believe the wrong things. The reason no prophecy is completely reliable.
