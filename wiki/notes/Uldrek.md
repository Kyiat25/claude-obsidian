# Uldrek, The World-Breaker

**Domain**: tectonic upheaval, mountain formation, shifting plates. **Symbol**: a broken peak with lava pouring from its center. **Worshiped by**: earthshapers, seismic priests, survivalist orders.

The primal force that reshapes landmasses — not through rage but necessity, cracking the crust to bleed pressure and forging continents and calderas. Myth holds he broke the world so it would not shatter outright. Relation to [[Vatharun]]: the hand of change for the one who does not move — Vatharun is stillness, Uldrek is controlled motion.
