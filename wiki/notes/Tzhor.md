# Tzhor, The Drowned King

*"You are mine now. You are not forgotten. You are not saved. You are simply mine."*

**Domains**: death at sea, souls of the drowned. **Symbol**: a broken ship, its mast sinking beneath the waves. Does not weep for the dead, does not punish, does not reward — simply keeps what he is given.

**The God Beneath the Tide**: in the deep-trenches west of [[Aeravae]], Tzhor once followed a tide with no source and found, in a silence that screamed, a shape in the dark — a god not born of this world, weeping from a wound that bled upward. It didn't speak; it turned away. Ever since, the tides in that place — sailors call it the Eld Sea — are wrong, and even the Drowned King is uneasy about what he's been asked to keep.
