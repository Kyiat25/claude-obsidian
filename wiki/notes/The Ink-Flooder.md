# The Ink-Flooder

*"You dreamed you were writing. But what you wrote is now inside your skin."*

**Origin**: formed from failed dream transcription — Spirit scribes tried to record prophetic dreams, but the glyphs turned inward. **Appearance**: a tide of black glyph-ink shaped like a hooded, faceless scribe, dripping language from its fingers. **Effect**: causes "dreamwriting possession" — victims wake with glyphs tattooed on their skin, carrying spells they can't remember casting. **Echo power**: in high glyph-density zones, it can rewrite nearby scrolls, spellbooks, or contracts in dream-script.

A **Dream-Born** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, aligned with [[Ul'Naresh]].
