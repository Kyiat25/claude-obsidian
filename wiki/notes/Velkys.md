# Velkys, The Silent Deep

*"There is nothing down here. You are alone. And yet… you are not."*

**Domains**: the crushing weight of the deep, the silence below, the pressure of the ocean's abyss. **Symbol**: a dark, featureless void, deeper than any eye can see. **Worshiped by**: deep-sea explorers, those who dive too far, those who hear the silence. **Rivals**: [[Vaelora]], Nyxthys, Morgathis. **Allies**: [[Jorvethis]], Nyssara, Ithrial.

Does not move or speak — he only ensures the deepest places of the world remain forever untouched. Some say if you listen too closely to the ocean's silence, you will hear him breathing.
