# Myzael, The Whispering Current

*"I am the hand upon your shoulder, the breath that carries your sails, the voice that whispers 'this way' when all paths seem lost."*

**Domains**: steady breezes, airborne guidance, the soft winds of summer. **Symbol**: a single white feather drifting through the air. **Worshiped by**: sailors, shepherds, sky-mages. **Allies**: [[Syvaris]], [[Elythis]]. **Enemies**: Zeraphis, Howlar.

The gentle wind that guides ships without turbulence. If she turns her back on you, you drift endlessly, never finding home again.
