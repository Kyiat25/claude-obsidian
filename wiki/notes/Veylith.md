# Veylith, The Weeping Sky

*"It does not stop. It does not relent. It only falls."*

**Domains**: unceasing rain, the storm that does not end, water's slow suffocation. **Symbol**: a raincloud with no sky above it, only more rain. **Worshiped by**: feared by farmers, appeased by stormcallers. **Rivals**: Selmira, Luthan, Yltheris. **Allies**: [[Aelthys]], [[Voraxis]], Rythos.

Does not rage like Aelthys or erase like Voraxis — she simply does not leave. Some say when she weeps, it is for something the ocean lost, and she will never stop mourning.
