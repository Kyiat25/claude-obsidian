# Luck

A fate mechanic with an actual trigger condition (rare in this source set — most FEARS/Dór-o Estel mechanics have none):

- Three natural 1s before a short rest → **Cursed**
- Three natural 20s → **Blessed**

Effects can turn NPC attitudes unexpectedly, empower or punish the next social/combat action, trigger divine/fey/infernal attention, or randomly modify [[Disposition]].

## The Weave (from Four Threads of the Mind)

Luck is framed as [[The Weave]]'s mechanism — "the Weave's last joke, or its moment of mercy," triggered only "if the dice are watching." Being Blessed while [[Corruption|Corrupted]] draws the Weave's specific attention.
