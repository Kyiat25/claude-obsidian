# Luck

A fate mechanic with an actual trigger condition (rare in this source set — most FEARS/Dór-o Estel mechanics have none):

- Three natural 1s before a short rest → **Cursed**
- Three natural 20s → **Blessed**

Effects can turn NPC attitudes unexpectedly, empower or punish the next social/combat action, trigger divine/fey/infernal attention, or randomly modify [[Disposition]].

## The Weave (from Four Threads of the Mind)

Luck is framed as [[The Weave]]'s mechanism — "the Weave's last joke, or its moment of mercy," triggered only "if the dice are watching." Being Blessed while [[Corruption|Corrupted]] draws the Weave's specific attention.

## The full d8 tables (from a dedicated `Luck.md` player-guide source)

A previously-untracked standalone source gives Cursed/Blessed Luck concrete d8 effect tables, all resetting on a Short Rest, with only one active state at a time. **Cursed Luck** ranges from Expanded Critical Failure (1s and 2s crit-fail, stacks) and Ancestral Break (racial abilities lost until next rest) to Weakened Strikes (damage dice one step smaller, but crit range widens to 19-20). **Blessed Luck** ranges from Critical Ascension (19-20 crit) and Healing Wind (a Proficiency × 1d4 healing pool) to Fey-Touched Gift (a rest-time gift from a Celestial/Fey/Elemental/Devil, "no bargains, warnings may accompany it"). Each additional odd-numbered streak (5th, 7th nat 1/20) re-triggers the table. Cross-system interactions: a Nat 1 while [[Focus]] is Fragmenting/Splintered triggers Cursed Luck instantly; certain Cursed results force a [[Sanity]] Save (DC 13); Cursed Luck in an Eldritch zone can auto-add [[Corruption]]; and being Deranged or Broken on the [[Mental State]] tracker grants advantage (or double-roll-and-choose) on Luck-table rolls.
