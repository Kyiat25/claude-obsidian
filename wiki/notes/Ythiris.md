# Ythiris, The Drowned Pestilence

*"It does not matter how strong you are, how well you build, how carefully you drink. If the water wills it, you will fall."*

**Domains**: waterborne disease, the ocean's sickness, plague carried by the tide. **Symbol**: a skeletal fish, rotting from the inside. **Worshiped by**: feared by sailors, avoided by healers, whispered about in coastal cities. **Rivals**: [[Itheris]], Selmira, Rhaldris. **Allies**: [[Mirethis]], Veylith, Lyrithis.

Does not choose who lives and who dies — he only ensures water is never truly safe. Some say he is not truly a spirit, but the ocean's own defense against those who take too much from it.
