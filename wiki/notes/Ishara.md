# Ishara, The Verdant Queen

*"You build. You carve. You reshape. And yet, the moment you turn away, I will take it all back."*

**Domain**: wild growth, nature that will not yield, reclamation. **Symbol**: a tree growing over a ruined temple. **Worshiped by**: druids, survivalists, those who live in untamed places. **Rivals**: [[Dalvora]] (agriculture), Fire (destruction), civilization itself. **Allies**: Vaelun (wild forests), [[Thyssir]] (beasts).

**Myth**: a city built walls, paved roads, burned the trees it stood on — and when its people left, she took it all back so completely that no one remembers where the city stood. Those who build in the wild leave offerings to her, lest she reclaim what they've made; some believe that when the Lock breaks, she will be the first to reclaim the ruins of the world.
