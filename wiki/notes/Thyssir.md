# Thyssir, The Keeper of Beasts

*"The lion does not beg. The wolf does not regret. The deer does not defy fate. Learn from them."*

**Domain**: wild fauna, the balance of predator and prey. Ensures the natural order of the hunt remains intact — doesn't stop predators from killing or prey from fleeing, simply ensures balance. When a species becomes too strong, he weakens it; when a species nears extinction, he strengthens it.

**Myth — The Predator That Would Not Die**: a beast defied death and became too strong. Thyssir did not kill it — instead he gave its prey the knowledge to fight back, and balance was restored.
