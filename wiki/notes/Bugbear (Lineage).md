# Bugbear

*"The gods cast us into shadow. So we became the thing that hunts in darkness—and taught fear itself to pray."*

**Origin myth**: per the Faith of the Gnawing Crown's Fangborn Path, Bugbears are the shadow of fear and blood-memory incarnate — when the divine Fragment (see [[Goblin (Lineage)]]'s shared origin) was cast out, part of it remembered the hunt, predator and prey, the moment between stalk and strike. Murruth (goddess, ancestral collective, or the Hunt itself) lives in blood-memory; every hunt is theology in motion. Sibling lineage to [[Goblin (Lineage)]] and [[Hobgoblin (Lineage)]] under the same Gnawing Crown mythos — see [[The Broken Crown and the Cracked Fang]] for the full faith.

**Physical**: mature by 12, adult by 16, live ~80 years; Medium (barely — 6½–8 ft.), 30 ft. speed, massive fur-covered frame with long arms.

## Traits

- **Darkvision** 60 ft.
- **Long-Limbed**: +5 ft. reach on melee attacks
- **Powerful Build**: counts one size larger for carrying capacity
- **Sneaky**: Stealth proficiency despite size
- **Surprise Attack**: +2d6 damage (3d6 at 5th, 4d6 at 11th, 5d6 at 17th) on a surprised target's first hit
- **Fang-Memory**: advantage on Intimidation; 1/long rest lock eyes with a creature within 30 ft. — WIS save or frightened 1 minute
- **Languages**: Common + Goblin (spoken as a growl — "Fang-Tongue" for sacred matters)

**Mental**: Focus +0 / Sanity +1 (comfort with darkness/violence as familiar, not foreign).

**DCS Integration**: Dodge +1 / Endure +1 / Deflect +0; DR +1. Advantage on Rally in combat; disadvantage in non-violent social situations.

## Corruption: The Hunting Madness

Corruption enters through sadistic cruelty, dishonored hunts, or killing without ritual. At Corruption 10+: Stalk-Sickness, Prey-Confusion (allies look like prey), Ritual-Loss. **Maddened Fang table** (1d6): False Hunt, Shadow-Blindness (Stealth disabled), Fang Rejection (1d4 psychic + lose Long-Limbed), Weight of Bloodlust (Sanity save or −1d4), Eldritch Prey (+1 Whisper Point, Surprise Attack auto-fails), or Hunt Holds (Corruption −1).
