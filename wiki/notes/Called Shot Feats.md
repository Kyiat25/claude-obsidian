# Called Shot Feats

> **Scope note.** This is the first appearance of a feat system anywhere in the FEARS cluster — every feat below is known *only* through its Called Shot interaction. No other feat content has been ingested.

| Feat | Prerequisite | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Sharpshooter | Ranged weapon proficiency | −2 to ranged Called Shot penalties; crits impose disadvantage on the save; ignores long-range penalties on called shots |
| Great Weapon Master | Heavy melee weapon proficiency | Combine with Power Attack (−5/+10); crits deal +1 weapon die and auto-fail the save; a called-shot kill grants a bonus-action called shot |
| Crossbow Expert | Crossbow proficiency | Ignore the penalty on the first attack each turn; called shots bypass 1 DR at the location |
| Martial Adept | none | Pinpoint Strike: spend a Superiority Die to reduce the penalty; Nerve Strike: spend one after a hit to impose disadvantage on the target's next attack |
| Sentinel | none | Sentinel reaction attacks may be Called Shots; a leg shot that zeroes speed keeps it at 0 until your next turn regardless of other effects |
| Mobile | none | Move 10 ft without provoking after a leg shot (hit or miss); leg shot penalty −1 |
| Lucky | none | Luck Points reroll a Called Shot attack without the penalty persisting, or force a target to reroll their save |
| Piercer | Piercing weapon proficiency | Crits: reroll one damage die, +2 damage; called shots bypass 2 DR at the location |
| Crusher | Bludgeoning weapon proficiency | −2 to Head/Arms penalties; a called-shot kill pushes a creature 10 ft |
| Slasher | Slashing weapon proficiency | Arm/leg called shots reduce movement an extra 10 ft; a hit applies 1d6 ongoing slashing damage |
| Alert | none | Advantage on Passive Perception to detect Called Shots; a detected shot lets you move 5 ft as a reaction |
| Shield Master | Shield proficiency | −2 penalty targeting torso/arms; blocking a called shot with a shield grants a melee attack as a reaction |
| Tavern Brawler | none | Improvised weapons count as proficient for Called Shots, no penalty; grappled targets take no called-shot penalty |
| Sniper's Precision *(new)* | Ranged weapon proficiency | −2 to ranged penalties; critical range expands to 19-20 on called shots; crits impose disadvantage on the save |
| Masterful Strikes *(new)* | none | −1 Head, −2 Arms/Legs; crits deal +1d8 damage; advantage on called shots vs. creatures already damaged this round |
