# Pit Fighter — Rank 1-4 (from Grim Hollow)

*"You fought for survival in underground arenas."* Tier 3 (Grim Hollow-inspired dark fantasy).

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Scrapper | Street Tough | Attract the notice of a patron among fight handlers |
| 2 | Prize Fighter | Combat Reputation | Last three rounds or knock out a high-ranked fighter |
| 3 | Contender | Arena Champion | Win a major championship bout |
| 4 | Arena Champion | Legendary Fighter | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Scrapper

**Skill Proficiency**: Intimidation, Athletics. **Tool Proficiency**: one gaming set. **Equipment**: tough leather gloves, a gaming set, mess kit, 10 gp.

**Feature — Street Tough**: while in a large town or city, spend 1 hour and make a DC 10 Charisma (Intimidation) check — success attracts attention from underground fight circles: you learn arena fight locations, guild-friendly businesses, patron names, and hear of bodyguard/collection jobs.

**Holdings**: room and board at a guildhouse near an underground arena; an invitation to an exhibition match attended by promoters.

## Rank 2: Prize Fighter

**Holdings**: a spot in a stable of up-and-coming fighters; a mentor (handler, trainer, or promoter) who organizes fights; access to a small training area with dummies and sparring partners; small fight income (15 gp/month).

**Feature Expansion — Combat Reputation**: in cities where the Fighter's Guild operates, you earn money through fighting (25-50 gp/match); local tough guys give you respect; you can challenge others to earn coin or settle disputes.

## Rank 3: Contender

**Holdings**: a private room at a high-quality inn; several arena-patron admirers (gifts worth 10-30 gp/month); entry into major tournaments with significant prize money; training facilities and a dedicated trainer; income from fights and appearance fees (60 gp/month).

**Feature Expansion — Arena Champion**: you can earn 100-500 gp per major fight; nobles and criminals seek your services as bodyguard; Intimidation checks have advantage against those who know your reputation; you can call upon other arena fighters for backup.

## Rank 4: Arena Champion

**Holdings**: living in luxury (maintained by your patron); a collection of devoted fans and hangers-on; major income from fights, appearances, and endorsements (250 gp/month); job offers from nobles and criminals; a fancy championship trophy (belt, cup, or trinket).

**Feature — Legendary Fighter**: you can demand 1,000+ gp per fight; your matches draw massive crowds; Intimidation checks automatically succeed against common folk; once per month, arrange a fight that serves as a distraction for a major operation (hundreds attend, the city watch is focused on keeping order); your patron bends to your wishes (fears losing you to a rival). Statues or paintings of you appear in major arenas, and younger fighters seek to challenge you to make their names.
