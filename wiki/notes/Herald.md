# Herald (Town Crier) — Rank 1-4

*"You're the voice of authority, making public announcements and managing information."* Batch 3, Social/Performance category. Source dual-names this "Herald / Town Crier"; filed under the first name.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Town Crier | Public Voice | Deliver a critical announcement perfectly, prevent panic through oratory, or become the preferred crier for important news |
| 2 | Official Herald | Authority's Voice | Deliver a message that prevents war, manage information during a crisis, or become herald for royalty |
| 3 | Royal Herald | Voice of Power | Deliver a history-changing declaration, manage communication during war, or become chief herald of the realm |
| 4 | Grand Herald of the Realm | Master of Proclamations | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Town Crier

**Skill Proficiency**: Persuasion, Performance. **Tool Proficiency**: one musical instrument. **Equipment**: a musical instrument, a bell or trumpet, a scroll of announcements, fine clothes, 10 gp.

**Feature — Public Voice**: gather a crowd quickly with an announcement; advantage on Performance checks to make announcements; know protocol for public speaking and ceremonies; your voice carries 200 ft; common folk recognize and listen to you.

**Holdings**: an official post or corner where you cry announcements; a daily wage (modest lifestyle); an official badge or symbol; regular announcements to make.

## Rank 2: Official Herald

**Holdings**: quarters in a noble house or government building; a salary (25 gp/month); quality clothing and herald's regalia; a horse for longer announcements; authority to make official proclamations; access to important news before public release.

**Feature Expansion — Authority's Voice**: command attention and silence from crowds; advantage on Persuasion checks delivering official messages; your announcements are legally binding notifications; access restricted areas to make proclamations; once per week, gain audience with officials to deliver urgent messages.

## Rank 3: Royal Herald

**Holdings**: private apartments; a major salary (80 gp/month); magnificent regalia; a staff of 3-5 junior heralds; a horse and covered travel expenses; authority throughout the realm; presence at all major royal events.

**Feature Expansion — Voice of Power**: your announcements are law when from proper authority; command crowds of thousands; advantage on all social checks acting as herald; once per month, request anything "in the name of" your patron; your presence legitimizes events and declarations.

## Rank 4: Grand Herald of the Realm

**Holdings**: a luxurious estate; an excellent salary (200 gp/month); a college of heralds under your command (20+); presence at every state function; you can speak with royal authority; you manage all official communications; your voice opens all doors.

**Feature — Master of Proclamations**: your official announcements become unquestioned truth to commoners; declare a state of emergency or other major event with authority; once per month, shift public opinion on a major issue; kings and queens trust your counsel on public communication; your voice can inspire or terrify thousands. You manage protocol for royal events, your heralds are everywhere, and history records your great announcements.
