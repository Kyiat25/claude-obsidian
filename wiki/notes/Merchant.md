# Merchant — Rank 1-4 (Modified from expanded backgrounds)

*"You understand commerce, trade routes, and the value of everything."* Tier 4 (Specialized Niche).

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Peddler | Merchant Connections | Complete a major trade deal, establish a regular trade route, or join a merchant guild |
| 2 | Merchant | Market Sense | Open a second shop, corner the market on a commodity, or broker a major trade deal |
| 3 | Master Merchant | Trade Empire | Establish a trade monopoly, open a foreign market, or become guild master |
| 4 | Merchant Prince | Master of Commerce | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Peddler

**Skill Proficiency**: Insight, Persuasion. **Tool Proficiency**: navigator's tools or one type of vehicle. **Language**: one of choice. **Equipment**: a merchant's scale, sample goods worth 20 gp, a letter of introduction from the merchant guild, traveler's clothes, 25 gp.

**Feature — Merchant Connections**: you know trading routes and can find buyers/sellers for legal goods; you get 10% better prices when buying or selling in bulk.

**Holdings**: a cart or pack animal; a small inventory of trade goods (worth 50 gp); a contact in the merchant guild.

## Rank 2: Merchant

**Holdings**: a small shop or market stall; inventory worth 300 gp; steady trade income (30 gp/month); 1-2 employees; merchant guild membership; contacts in 3-5 major trading cities.

**Feature Expansion — Market Sense**: you can accurately appraise any trade good; advantage on checks to haggle or negotiate prices; you know which goods are valuable in which regions; you can identify forgeries and counterfeit currency.

## Rank 3: Master Merchant

**Holdings**: multiple shops or a warehouse; major inventory and trade goods (worth 2,000 gp); excellent income (100 gp/month); 10-15 employees; a caravan or small ship; trade contacts across the continent; a seat on the merchant guild council.

**Feature Expansion — Trade Empire**: you can acquire nearly any legal good within a week; advantage on Persuasion checks related to commerce; you can arrange large shipments and caravans; your credit is good (you can secure loans up to 1,000 gp).

## Rank 4: Merchant Prince

**Holdings**: a trading empire spanning multiple cities; massive wealth (500 gp/month passive income); a fleet of ships or caravans; 50+ employees; warehouses in every major city; political influence through economic power.

**Feature — Master of Commerce**: you can acquire anything that can be bought, given time; you can manipulate regional markets through supply/demand; once per month, organize a trade action affecting the economy (embargo, flooding the market, cornering supply); governments negotiate with you; you can extend unlimited credit to those you trust. Your name appears on currency or trade documents, nobles seek your loans and backing, your financial support can make or break ventures, and trade routes are named after you.
