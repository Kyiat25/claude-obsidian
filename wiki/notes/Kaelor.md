# Kaelor, The Keeper of Oaths

*"You have made the promise. You have given your word. Do you truly believe you will escape its price?"*

**Domains**: fate-bound deals, divine contracts, bargains that cannot be broken. **Symbol**: a sealed contract with a red thread tied around it. **Worshiped by**: kings, those who strike supernatural deals, warlocks, oath-bound warriors. **Allies**: [[Vel'Zyren]], [[Nytheris]], [[Oriveth]]. **Enemies**: [[Vaithis]], [[Nyzara]], [[Telys]].

Ensures fate-bound deals are honored no matter the cost — does not judge whether an oath is good or evil, only that it is fulfilled. Does not answer prayers; he merely waits for the moment a price must be paid.
