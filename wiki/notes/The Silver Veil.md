# The Silver Veil

*"You blinked. It didn't."*

**Origin**: appeared when a city of illusionists built a hall of perfect reflection — only to find new guests. **Behavior**: forms across large mirror surfaces and waits silently, sometimes moving, sometimes watching. **Effect**: if looked at too long, it switches places with the viewer; no one notices unless someone was watching both. **Danger**: it always smiles — but only after the viewer does.

A **Reflected** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a mirror-entity spawned from corrupted identity.
