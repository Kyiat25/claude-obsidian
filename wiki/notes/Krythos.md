# Krythos, The Frozen Tide

*"You burn bright. I do not burn at all. And yet, when your fire dies, I will still be here."*

**Domains**: ice, glaciers, water's slowest form. **Symbol**: a frozen wave, locked in place. **Worshiped by**: arctic nomads, monks who seek patience. **Rivals**: [[Soltharion]], Xethar.

Does not act — he waits, and he does not forget.
