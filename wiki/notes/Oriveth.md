# Oriveth, The Keeper of Hidden Truths

*"There are things that even the gods fear to speak. I ensure they remain buried."*

**Domains**: forbidden knowledge, sealed truths, the lies that protect reality. **Symbol**: a closed eye surrounded by a ring of ancient glyphs. **Worshiped by**: those who seek the most ancient and dangerous knowledge, priests who guard divine secrets. **Allies**: [[Nyzara]], Varthos, [[Astralis]]. **Enemies**: Lythra, Xelthar, Zeythis.

Ensures some knowledge is buried — not lost, like [[Astralis]] governs oblivion, but deliberately hidden for a reason. Doesn't deceive, protects, ensuring truths that could shatter reality remain unspoken. His followers split between preserving knowledge and erasing it from history entirely.
