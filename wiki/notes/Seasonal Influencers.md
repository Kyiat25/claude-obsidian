# Seasonal Influencers

`status: excluded`. The GM random-event tables underlying the **Threads Turn** roll referenced on [[Eldritch Moon Rise Procedure]] — a per-season two-step procedure: roll 1d6 to determine which of the five elemental Pantheons (or [[Eldritch Spirits|Eldritch Forces]], on a 6) holds sway that season, then roll again on that Pantheon's own season-specific table for the concrete effect. Covers [[Verdant Loom Calendar]]'s seasons: **Verdant Rise** (renewal, planting, strongest Earth/Water influence), **Scorchpeak**, **Deepveil**, and **Auroratide** (the intercalary period); a fifth season's table was not located in this pass.

## Historic precedents (Verdant Rise example)

Each season's table is grounded with named historical examples showing what happens when a roll goes badly: the **Year of the Hollow Flood** (Cycle 281) saw excessive Water Pantheon influence drown crops three years running, causing the Bread Riots of Aeravae; **Ashkarra's Last Spring** (Cycle 364) saw a triple Spirit Thread alignment turn renewal omens to madness when an Eldritch Moon rose during planting season; **the Shifting Furrows** (Cycle 229) saw Air Pantheon dominance shift soil patterns so drastically that planting maps became useless overnight.
