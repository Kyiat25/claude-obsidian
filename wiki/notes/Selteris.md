# Selteris, The Purging Wave

*"You think all water gives life? You are wrong. Some water does not heal—it erases."*

**Domains**: cleansing waters, the flood that washes away, water as a force of renewal. **Symbol**: a crashing wave, its edges turning to white light. **Worshiped by**: healers, priests, those who seek absolution. **Rivals**: Mirethis, Varuthis, Ythiris. **Allies**: [[Itheris]], [[Orannis]], [[Nereus]].

Does not simply cleanse — he purges. Where he flows, sickness dies, but so does everything else; some holy waters burn like acid, stripping impurities but leaving emptiness behind. Those who seek forgiveness without true repentance find only death in his waters.
