# Ilyra, The Bound Shore

*"Not all are meant for the sea. Some are meant to stay on land. And I ensure that they do."*

**Domains**: fear of the ocean, those who never enter. **Symbol**: a footprint on the shore, never touched by the tide. **Worshiped by**: those who fear drowning, land-bound travelers. **Rivals**: [[Vaedoris]], [[Marethis]], Selmira. **Allies**: Nyxthys, Rythos, Jorvethis.

Does not hate the sea — she simply reminds mortals that they do not belong to it. The quiet counterpart to the pantheon's more violent spirits: her domain is refusal, not destruction.
