# The Forgotten Note

*"You know the sound. You do not know where from. You try to sing it. You always get it wrong."*

**Type**: Musical/Mnemonic Virus. **Origin**: possibly the last spell sung by a dying god of memory before their erasure by [[Nhal'Zereth]]. **Behavior**: only heard by musicians or spellcasters, beginning as a dissonant chord that seems oddly familiar. **Effect**: induces neural dissonance, misfires in magical harmony, compulsive repetition; eventually overwrites musical spellcasting glyphs with corrupted equivalents. **Echo power**: performed with correct harmonics (impossible without mutation), it causes a one-minute reversal of reality's recent past — at the cost of the caster's own identity.

An **Echo** — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, tightly bound to [[Nhal'Zereth]]'s erasure domain.
