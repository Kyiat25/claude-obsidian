# Folk Hero — Rank 1-4 (Modified from PHB)

*"You're a champion of the common people, known for standing against tyranny."* Tier 1 (Core).

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Local Champion | Rustic Hospitality | Perform another heroic deed that gains regional attention |
| 2 | Regional Hero | Champion's Call | Lead a popular movement, defeat a major threat, or expose a powerful villain |
| 3 | People's Champion | Voice of the People | Lead a revolution, save an entire region, or become the symbol of a movement |
| 4 | Living Legend | Legend Made Flesh | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Local Champion

**Skill Proficiency**: Animal Handling, Survival. **Tool Proficiency**: one type of artisan's tools, vehicles (land). **Equipment**: artisan's tools, shovel, iron pot, common clothes, 10 gp.

**Feature — Rustic Hospitality**: common folk see you as their champion — they provide lodging, food, and will hide you from authorities unless you've done something truly heinous.

**Defining Event** (choose or roll d10): stood up to a tyrant, saved people from a monster, led a militia to victory, stole from the rich to feed the poor, exposed corruption, led a successful rebellion, won a contest of strength, rescued innocents from disaster, defended a village against raiders, or slew a dangerous beast.

**Holdings**: free room and board in your home village; the gratitude of common folk in your region.

## Rank 2: Regional Hero

**Holdings**: free lodging in any common establishment in your region; small gifts from grateful commoners (5-10 gp/week); 10-20 common folk who consider you their personal hero.

**Feature Expansion — Champion's Call**: once per week, rally common folk to aid you — a militia of 20-30 commoners (use Commoner statistics), information gathering through the peasant network, or emergency shelter and supplies.

## Rank 3: People's Champion

**Holdings**: a monument or statue in your honor; a song/ballad about your deeds (performed commonly); a network of supporters across the region; 50-100 people who would drop everything to aid you; officials must consider public opinion before acting against you.

**Feature Expansion — Voice of the People**: you can organize mass protests or demonstrations (hundreds participate); common folk provide intelligence on nobility and authority figures; once per month, call for significant action (boycott, work stoppage, etc.).

## Rank 4: Living Legend

**Holdings**: multiple monuments to your deeds; widespread fame (recognized everywhere); a network of thousands who consider you their hero; political influence through popular support; invitations to speak at major events.

**Feature — Legend Made Flesh**: common folk will risk their lives to aid you; tyrants fear your involvement; once per month, inspire mass action affecting thousands; nobility must negotiate with you to avoid public backlash; you can raise an army of common-folk volunteers (200-500) if the cause is just. Rustic Hospitality now extends to nobility reluctantly offering you hospitality to maintain public favor.
