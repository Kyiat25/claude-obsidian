# Shadowbinder

> [!contradiction] Retired per Classes_Summary
> [[Classes_Summary]] explicitly lists Shadowbinder among six classes "cut from the roster during development" that "should not appear in new content." This page is preserved as a historical record of [[Welcome to FEARS]]'s original pitch, not as current FEARS canon. Note: a *different* name, "Shadowshaper," is also on the retired list — the two are not stated to be the same class, but the naming overlap is worth flagging in case a future source clarifies a relationship. See [[FEARS Class Roster]] for the active 24-class list.

Manipulates **Shadow Essence** and can assume **Umbral Form** for defense. Mobility feature: **Shadow Step** teleportation, letting the archetype "slip between shadows like doorways" and strike from unusual angles.

No mechanical detail beyond this one-line pitch is given in the source.
