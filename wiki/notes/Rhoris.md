# Rhoris, The Stone Sentinel

**Domain**: craft, metalwork, construction, enduring tools. **Symbol**: an anvil beneath a mountain ridge. **Worshiped by**: builders of cities, fortress architects, divine engineers.

Patron of craftsmen, stonemasons, smiths, and builders — the architect-god who inspires visions of permanence and shields. Myth holds he was the first to shape stone with his hands; every hammer strike echoes the First Blow. Allied with [[Grathis]] (demigod of alloys); together they created the first divine tools.
