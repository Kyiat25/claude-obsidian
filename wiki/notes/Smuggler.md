# Smuggler — Rank 1-4 (Runner of Contraband)

*"You move illegal goods, people, or information where others cannot."* Tier 2 (Specialized). Fulfills the "Smuggler" slot [[FEARS Advanced Backgrounds]] flagged as not-yet-developed after Batch 1.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Courier | Know the Routes | Complete a major smuggling run, avoid capture in dramatic fashion, or establish a new route |
| 2 | Independent Smuggler | Ghost Paths | Establish a regional smuggling network, move something incredibly valuable/dangerous, or avoid capture by major authorities |
| 3 | Smuggling Lord | Shadow Trade | Control smuggling in an entire region, move something that changes the political landscape, or rival legal trade |
| 4 | Smuggling Kingpin | Master of the Black Market | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Courier

**Skill Proficiency**: Deception, Stealth. **Tool Proficiency**: forgery kit or vehicles (land or water). **Equipment**: dark common clothes, a false-bottom bag, forged documents, 15 gp.

**Feature — Know the Routes**: know secret paths and hidden routes; identify customs officials and patrols; understand contraband market values; advantage on Sleight of Hand to conceal small items.

**Specialty** (choose what you smuggle): Drugs/narcotics, Weapons/armor, Magical items, People (refugees or slaves), Information/documents, Forbidden books/art.

**Holdings**: a safe route you control; a contact who provides goods to smuggle; 2-3 safe houses along your route.

## Rank 2: Independent Smuggler

**Holdings**: a network of 8-12 contacts along routes; income from smuggling (35 gp/month); multiple safe routes; hidden cache locations; a vehicle (wagon, boat, etc.); forged documents and disguises; contacts in customs/guards (bribed or blackmailed).

**Feature Expansion — Ghost Paths**: know hidden routes through any settled area; move contraband through checkpoints (DC 15 Deception or Sleight of Hand); advantage on checks to avoid patrols; arrange for goods to "disappear" from warehouses; know which officials can be bribed.

## Rank 3: Smuggling Lord

**Holdings**: a large smuggling network with 30+ operatives; major income (120 gp/month); multiple vehicles and routes; warehouses and safe houses across the region; corrupt officials on payroll; you can move large shipments; front businesses as cover.

**Feature Expansion — Shadow Trade**: acquire nearly any illegal good; once per week, move something large/dangerous past authorities; your network provides intelligence on law enforcement; arrange for rivals to be caught with contraband; your operations are nearly impossible to shut down.

## Rank 4: Smuggling Kingpin

**Holdings**: a vast smuggling empire spanning multiple regions; massive income (400+ gp/month); a fleet of vehicles and ships; 100+ operatives; high-level officials corrupted or blackmailed; a legitimate business empire as cover; you can move anything anywhere.

**Feature — Master of the Black Market**: acquire or move anything illegal, given time; once per month, organize an operation affecting national security or the economy; your network knows everything about illegal activities in your region; governments secretly deal with you; shutting you down would destabilize economies. Law enforcement knows of you but can't prove anything, rulers unofficially use your services, and some of your operations are "legal" fronts that operate openly.
