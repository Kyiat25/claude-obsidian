# Glyph of Will — Astralor's Forge, the First Hammer

*"From the great void, Astralor struck the First Blow, and fire and stone were born."*

**Layer**: Primal, Tier 1. **Domains**: creation, cosmic command, order from void. **Opposing glyphs**: the Glyph of Unbeing, the Glyph of Endings. **Symbol**: an anvil cracked with light inside.

The first cosmic assertion — the divine necessity that there must be something at all. Not desire, intent, or thought, but inevitability: the glyph that struck the void and carved the cosmos from it. All that exists must exist because Will has made it so. It manifests wherever something new is forged: a world, the birth of a god, the shaping of a soul, a child's first thought — each a fragment of the First Blow. Even the gods are merely artisans shaping what this Glyph has provided; creation demands cost, and that cost is claimed by [[The Whispering Dark|the Maw]].

## Divine associations

[[Astralor]] is its patron, called the First Forger; [[Gaeldros]] invokes it when declaring divine laws; [[Aeloria]] uses its echo in the [[Glyph of Threads]], ensuring fate flows from a place of fixed existence.

## Eldritch corruption risk

[[Nhal'Zereth]] seeks to nullify this glyph by unraveling its cosmic memory; when breached, entire timelines may retroactively never have existed. [[Ashkarra]]'s World Anchor, before its collapse, was said to hum with the purest tone of Will ever heard.
