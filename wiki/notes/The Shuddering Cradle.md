# The Shuddering Cradle

**Origin**: a failed attempt to clone a god's essence into mortal form. **Appearance**: an ancient bonewood cradle, rocking on its own. **Effect**: those who dream near it "give birth" in sleep to fragments of thought. **Myth**: if it rocks long enough, whatever is inside will hatch — and the first sound it makes erases the last three things you loved.

An **Unfinished** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a being that tried to form but failed.
