# Natural Philosophy of Elarion

Informal alchemical knowledge traditions operating outside — or alongside — the formal [[Eight Guild Professions]] Chemistry Guild: regional apprenticeships, traded recipes, and oral tradition rather than continental certification. Mechanically identical foundation (vital essences from creatures + mineral essences + technique), differing in specialization, essence sourcing, and secrecy. Where Guild certification guarantees consistent, standardized results anywhere on the continent, Natural Philosophy traditions are region-aligned, reputation-based, and often more practically adapted to local conditions.

## The Five Great Traditions

Named but not deeply detailed this pass: the **Imperial Academy Tradition**, the **Druidic Tradition**, the **Patient Tradition**, the **Blood-Knowledge Tradition**, and a fifth not read in this pass — plus secondary traditions and a dedicated marketplace presence in [[Lyrran]]. Covers campaign integration hooks and a mechanical reference for Tome of Alchemy-style crafting rules, neither transcribed in full here.
