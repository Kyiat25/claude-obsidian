# Durnath, The Dwarven Anvil

*"Tools do not betray. Steel does not lie. Stone does not break before its time."*

**Domain**: craftsmanship, masonry, forge-tradition. **Symbol**: an anvil carved into a mountain face. **Worshiped by**: dwarves, ancestral smith-lines, rune-forgers.

The discipline of creation — where [[Rhoris]] governs inspiration, Durnath governs the long labor, the repetition, the truth of the hammer's rhythm; ritualized, legacy-driven craftsmanship over invention. Myth: he forged a gate so strong even gods cannot open it without his blessing, still guarding a tomb no one dares speak of. **Aspect of the Lock**: he forged the hinges on the divine seal that cages what lies beneath — the anvil upon which all oaths are hammered into permanence.
