# Pilgrim — Rank 1-4

*"You journey to sacred sites, carrying your faith across the world."* Batch 3, Specialized Religious category. Devotional-travel focused, distinct from [[Acolyte]]'s institutional temple-hierarchy career track — a Pilgrim belongs to no single temple's chain of command.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Wandering Faithful | Pilgrim's Way | Complete a difficult pilgrimage, visit a particularly holy site, or experience a religious vision |
| 2 | Seasoned Pilgrim | Blessed Journey | Visit a legendary shrine, experience a miracle at a sacred site, or complete a dangerous pilgrimage |
| 3 | Master Pilgrim | Holy Wanderer | Visit a site untouched for centuries, survive impossible terrain, or carry a relic to a legendary destination |
| 4 | Saint of the Road | Sacred Wanderer | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Wandering Faithful

**Skill Proficiency**: Religion, Survival. **Language**: one of choice. **Equipment**: a holy symbol, a prayer book, a pilgrim's staff (quarterstaff), traveler's clothes, 5 gp.

**Feature — Pilgrim's Way**: find shelter at temples and shrines along pilgrim routes; advantage on Religion checks related to sacred sites; fellow pilgrims share information and resources; know major pilgrimage routes and sacred sites; your faith sustains you (survive on less food and water).

**Holdings**: a pilgrim's medallion showing sites visited; a map of sacred sites; a network of fellow pilgrims; a blessing from your temple.

## Rank 2: Seasoned Pilgrim

**Holdings**: pilgrim's gear (quality staff, blessed items); a small church stipend (10 gp/month); a collection of holy relics from sites visited; a reputation among pilgrims; multiple completed pilgrimage routes; you know safe rest stops on major routes.

**Feature Expansion — Blessed Journey**: advantage on saves against travel exhaustion; sense when approaching sacred ground; once per week, your prayers provide guidance (as *augury*); know stories and miracles associated with sacred sites; strangers are inclined to help pilgrims.

## Rank 3: Master Pilgrim

**Holdings**: a sacred staff said to contain blessings; a good church stipend (50 gp/month); a relic collection from dozens of sites; you've visited legendary holy places; written accounts of your pilgrimages; followers who journey with you; the church missions you to visit specific sites.

**Feature Expansion — Holy Wanderer**: cast *bless* once per long rest; advantage on all checks while traveling to sacred sites; identify cursed or unholy ground instantly; once per month, your pilgrimage to a site cleanses it of minor corruption; your stories inspire faith in others.

## Rank 4: Saint of the Road

**Holdings**: a legendary, magical pilgrim's staff; an excellent stipend (100 gp/month); you've visited every major sacred site in the world; you wrote the definitive pilgrimage guide; followers make pilgrimages in your footsteps; the church considers you a living saint; your presence blesses locations.

**Feature — Sacred Wanderer**: immune to travel exhaustion; cast *sanctuary* at will on yourself; once per year, your pilgrimage restores a desecrated site (major purification); see invisible sacred paths connecting holy sites; your touch can bless water or food. Pilgrims seek your blessing before journeys, new shrines are built where you rest, and you may have carried messages from deities.
