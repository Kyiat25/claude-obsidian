# Miner — Rank 1-4

*"You extract valuable minerals from deep underground."* Batch 3, Working Class category.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Tunnel Worker | Underground Worker | Find a valuable vein, prevent a mine collapse, or survive an underground disaster |
| 2 | Experienced Miner | Stone Sense | Discover a major ore deposit, become foreman, or survive and escape a cave-in |
| 3 | Mine Foreman | Master Miner | Establish a new profitable mine, revolutionize a mining technique, or discover a legendary gem deposit |
| 4 | Mining Magnate | Lord of the Deep | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Tunnel Worker

**Skill Proficiency**: Athletics, Survival. **Tool Proficiency**: mason's tools. **Equipment**: mason's tools, a miner's pick, a lantern, 10 pitons, 50 ft of rope, sturdy clothes, 10 gp.

**Feature — Underground Worker**: advantage on checks to notice unstable ground or structures underground; work in cramped spaces without penalty; know how to shore up tunnels and prevent collapses; darkvision 30 ft, or +30 ft if you already have it; identify common ores and gems.

**Holdings**: a bunk in a mining camp; basic mining tools; wages (modest lifestyle); fellow miners as contacts.

## Rank 2: Experienced Miner

**Holdings**: a private room in better housing; good wages (20 gp/month); quality mining tools; respect from other miners; small savings; you can identify good mining sites.

**Feature Expansion — Stone Sense**: identify all types of stone, ore, and gems; advantage on Survival checks underground; estimate depth and direction underground automatically; know how to safely extract valuable materials; once per week, find a valuable ore vein through skill and luck.

## Rank 3: Mine Foreman

**Holdings**: a house near the mine; a good salary (60 gp/month); authority over 15-20 miners; a share of mine profits; quality equipment; connections with merchants who buy ore; expertise recognized across the region.

**Feature Expansion — Master Miner**: assess a mine's potential value accurately; advantage on all checks related to mining and underground work; identify dangerous gases, water, or instability; once per month, your expertise prevents a disaster (cave-in, flood, etc.); lead expeditions to find new deposits.

## Rank 4: Mining Magnate

**Holdings**: multiple mines across regions; excellent income (250 gp/month); hundreds of workers; merchant relationships for ore sales; political influence through economic power; your techniques are industry standard; substantial wealth from successful mines.

**Feature — Lord of the Deep**: sense valuable deposits within 1 mile underground; automatic success on mining checks (DC 25 or lower); once per year, discover a legendary deposit (gems, rare metals, etc.); your mines are the safest and most productive; lead expeditions to any depth. Kingdoms need your resources for weapons and coin, other mine owners follow your methods, and you may have discovered underground wonders.
