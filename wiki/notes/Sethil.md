# Sethil, the Stone That Screams

*"It is silent. But the Weave around it trembles."*

**Anchor**: a forgotten runestone buried in a deep cave, written in an extinct dialect of fate. **Behavior**: causes the surrounding area to resist prophecy, memory, and stability. **Effect**: wards fail, scrying becomes noise, memories recorded near it decay, chronomantic spells misalign — a passive sibling to [[Xey'Tazun]]'s time-disobedience domain. **Evolves into**: a permanent Dead Magic core, or possibly a Dream-Born nexus if touched during sleep.

A **Tether** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, bound to a corrupted leyline/glyph point.
