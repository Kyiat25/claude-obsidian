# Xalros, The Final Inferno

*"You think fire is eternal? You think it will always burn? Watch as it consumes all, and tell me what is left."*

**Domains**: destruction, chaos, the apocalypse of fire. **Symbol**: a burning world, breaking apart. **Worshiped by**: doomsday cults, nihilists, those who believe in the inevitable end. **Allies**: [[Mordrath]], [[The Whispering Dark]]. **Enemies**: [[Ignis]], [[Vaeldros]], Kaelith.

Not fire as civilization or war, but fire as pure annihilation — the final blaze leaving nothing behind. Followers don't seek to rule or conquer, only to burn and watch the world turn to ash; no temple survives him, so his priests burn cities instead of preaching. Myth holds he was the last fire of the First Creation, the blaze that consumed the gods before the world was remade, and he waits now, patient, knowing all things must burn once more.
