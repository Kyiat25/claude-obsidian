# Nyssara, The Veil of Mists

*"You thought you saw land? You were wrong. You thought you were alone? You were wrong."*

**Domains**: mist, illusions, hidden paths upon the sea. **Symbol**: a single, drifting wisp of fog. **Worshiped by**: tricksters, illusionists, those who travel by sea at night. **Rivals**: Selmira, Kaemor, Vaedoris. **Allies**: [[Vaelora]], [[Eryndal]], Rythos.

The mist that hides what should be seen — the illusion that changes what is real, sometimes called the laughter of the ocean leading the foolish to their fate. Does not lie; she simply ensures you see only what she allows.
