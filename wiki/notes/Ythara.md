# Ythara, The Hoarfrost Warden

*"You think the ice is gone? It will return. It always returns."*

**Domains**: permafrost, ice that never melts, frozen oceans and guardianship. **Symbol**: a towering wall of ice, unbroken. **Worshiped by**: arctic tribes, icewalkers, keepers of ancient knowledge, those who fear time. **Rivals**: [[Soltharion]], Luthan, Sarvion. **Allies**: [[Krythos]], Vithras, Iskorel.

Ensures ice never truly melts and glaciers return after being thought gone. Some say that if she ever truly awakens, the world will be locked in ice forever.
