# Eldritch Moon Rise Procedure

`status: excluded`. *"They do not rise. They awaken. They do not glow. They remember."* A GM procedure for determining when an [[Eldritch Moons|Eldritch Moon]] manifests, tied to the seasonal calendar rather than random chance alone. The Moons are Weave-wounds and lunar overlays, not celestial bodies obeying natural law — they "open their eyes" specifically when the Weave's seasonal re-stitching (the **Threads Turn**) finds the Pattern already too frayed to fully retie.

## The check

At every seasonal change, after the GM's standard Threads Turn roll (which produces Pantheon Influence and Seasonal Mood for the new quarter), an Eldritch Moon Appearance Roll is triggered if **any** of: the season's dominant Pantheon Influence rolled as [[Eldritch Spirits|Eldritch Forces]] itself; Seasonal Mood rolled Chaotic; or a **Season Mechanics Escalation** condition is already active. Most rises cluster in **Deepveil** season, when prophecy and memory are most volatile, but Eldritch influence can force a rise in any season if the world is already sufficiently strained.

## Escalation triggers (pre-existing Weave strain)

**Omen Tripwire** — a single potent event (a major prophecy/curse fulfilled, mass hallucination across a settlement, an unnatural celestial event) that lingers for the rest of the season and guarantees a Moon check at the next turn. **Zone Pressure** — three or more simultaneously active [[Unstable Magic Zones]], ruptured leylines, or planar bleed sites anywhere in the world (need not be geographically close), raising cascade chance the more are active. **Dual Eldritch Seasonal Rolls** — if both Pantheon Influence and Seasonal Mood roll Eldritch in the same Threads Turn, treat as high-risk: +1 to cascade chance, and an automatic Moon Appearance Roll with no further checks if it happens during Deepveil.
