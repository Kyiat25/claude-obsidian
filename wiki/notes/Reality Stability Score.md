# Reality Stability Score

A single 0-100 "doom clock" tracking how close the setting is to another Ashkarra-scale catastrophe, meant to give [[Campaign Arcs (The Great Deceivers and Truth-Seekers)|campaign-arc]] revelations and player choices a visible, escalating cost. 0 = fully stable, 100 = Ashkarra-level unmaking. At the default campaign start (Year 487, 4A, Month 3, Day 18 — 6 months after [[The Threadbearer Crisis]]'s assassinations), the score sits at **58/100 ("Wounded, Stressed")**, having dropped from a pre-crisis baseline of 65. Pre-accumulated damage sources: Ashkarra's still-unhealed wound sites (+12, after 582 years), Cycle 4 Collapse aftermath echoes (+8), the Threadbearer assassinations themselves (+8), Cycle 5 Thread instability (+10), suppressed archive manifestations bleeding into physical space (+5), and recent Burner suppression-backlash (+7).

## Threshold levels

- **Stable (0-20)**: physical laws function normally, no visible effects, healing trivial. "Revelation feels genuinely dangerous" because nothing visibly threatens yet.
- **Stressed (21-40)**: occasional phantom manifestations, rare dead-visible-briefly sightings, mostly-predictable magic. Only educated NPCs notice; healing requires real effort. The published campaign starts here.
- **Wounded (41-60)**: daily phantom manifestations visible to everyone, the dead manifest for hours, buildings partially exist in Pattern-space, dreams bleed into waking reality. Public fear and faith-cracking become explicit plot elements.
- Higher thresholds (61-100) escalate toward full Divine Response — the gods intervening directly as the score nears catastrophic failure, per the source's own escalation table (not transcribed in full this pass).

## Campaign use

Framed explicitly as a metric players can watch move — 58 → 52 → 47 as the campaign progresses — turning abstract cosmological stakes (whether the [[Campaign Arcs (The Great Deceivers and Truth-Seekers)|BBEG arcs']] revelations should be suppressed, revealed, or exploited) into a concrete, trackable threat level with a known 100-point failure state everyone already knows the shape of, from [[Ashkarra]].
