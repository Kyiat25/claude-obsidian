# Black Market

Governs access to illicit goods (forged documents, cursed blades, rare spells) and hidden magical services (curse removal, memory recovery, forbidden rituals) once "the law shuts its doors." Tracks a separate **Black Market Reputation** determining trust/fear/respect within criminal circles, distinct from the public-facing [[Reputation]] and [[Wanted Score]].

## Risks

- Betrayal, faulty goods, or hidden costs
- Drawing attention from rival gangs or law enforcement
- Entanglement with morally grey or monstrous figures

Framed as morally flexible — usable for villainy or for folk-hero smuggling (e.g. medicine into a city that's banned healing magic).
