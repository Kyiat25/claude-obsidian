# Initiative System

Titled "Initiative & Combat Flow" in the source — not to be confused with the existing [[Combat Flow]] page (that one covers the 12-step attack-resolution sequence; this document's "combat flow" means the turn-order rhythm of a whole encounter, a different scope entirely).

## Confirms the excluded draft's preview

[[The Dynamic Combat System (Excluded Draft)]] previewed a two-phase Initiative system and was flagged as unverified pending this file. It holds up: Round 1 traditional `1d20+DEX`, Round 2+ driven by each side's average Momentum Score, tie broken by a Focus-based leadership roll, routed creatures excluded from the average. This is the first piece of the excluded draft's content to be independently confirmed rather than superseded — see [[Initiative]] for the full current rules.

## What's genuinely new

**[[Seize the Moment]]** — a universal PC reaction not hinted at anywhere, including the excluded draft. One PC per round may spend their reaction on a Focus check to seize initiative outright, with a DC that scales against the party's own struggling members (10 + PB + allies at negative Momentum). Full outcome table, two optional variants, and hooks for 10 classes.
