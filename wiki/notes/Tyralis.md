# Tyralis, The Spell That Should Not Exist

*"Not all magic was meant to be cast. Some spells exist against the gods' will."*

**Domains**: unstable spells, forbidden magic, magic that defies structure. **Symbol**: a broken spell circle, unable to contain its energy. **Worshiped by**: rogue spellcasters, wild mages, those who seek power beyond the gods. **Allies**: [[Zeythis]], [[Xelthar]], Varethis. **Enemies**: [[Aelys]], [[Nyzara]], [[Astralis]].

Not a god or creator — a mistake within the Weave itself. His magic is wild and unpredictable, breaking rules even the gods set in place.
