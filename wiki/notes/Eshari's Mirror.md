# Eshari's Mirror

**Origin**: a [[The Null Reflection|Null Reflection]] shard from a forgotten elf city. **Behavior**: haunts the lineage of anyone who has looked into the mirror. **Effect**: false memories, competing inner voices, a second self acting autonomously. **Danger**: if multiple people believe the reflected version is real, it can exit the mirror entirely.

A **Reflected** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a mirror-entity born from corrupted identity.
