# Teyvros, The Buried Vein

*"Water is never truly gone. You only need to know where to find it."*

**Domains**: hidden wells, underground streams, divination through water. **Symbol**: a drop of water sinking into cracked earth. **Worshiped by**: water-seekers, diviners, those who dig wells. **Rivals**: Yltheris, Mirethis, Othrys. **Allies**: [[Istriel]], [[Marethis]], Sarvion.

Neither kind nor cruel — he simply waits for those who know how to seek him. Water is never truly gone; it only moves, and Teyvros decides where. His wells can be found by the lost, but never by the greedy.
