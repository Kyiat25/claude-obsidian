# Criminal — Rank 1-4 (Modified from PHB)

*"You survived on the wrong side of the law, learning skills that serve you well in adventure."* Tier 1 (Core).

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Street Thug | Criminal Contact | Pull off a major heist, eliminate a rival, or prove yourself to a criminal organization |
| 2 | Made Member | Urban Shadow | Become indispensable to your organization or run a major criminal operation |
| 3 | Lieutenant | Criminal Network | Take over a major criminal enterprise, eliminate a rival crimelord, or blackmail someone of significant power |
| 4 | Crime Lord | Master of Shadows | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Street Thug

**Skill Proficiency**: Deception, Stealth. **Tool Proficiency**: thieves' tools, one gaming set. **Equipment**: crowbar, dark clothes with hood, belt pouch with 15 gp.

**Feature — Criminal Contact**: a reliable contact acts as your liaison to a network of criminals; you know how to get messages to and from them even over great distances.

**Specialty** (choose or roll d8): Blackmailer, Burglar, Enforcer, Fence, Highway robber, Hired killer, Pickpocket, Smuggler.

**Holdings**: safe house (shared with other criminals); a contact in the local underworld.

## Rank 2: Made Member

**Holdings**: private room in a criminal safe house; network of 5-8 informants across the city; monthly criminal "income" (10 gp from fencing, protection, etc.); one fence who gives 10% better prices than standard.

**Feature Expansion — Urban Shadow**: in any settlement where your organization operates, you know the location of every major criminal enterprise, guards are −2 to passive Perception to spot you, and you can arrange a meeting with local crime bosses within 24 hours.

## Rank 3: Lieutenant

**Holdings**: personal hideout/criminal establishment (gambling den, flophouse, etc.); crew of 10-15 criminals (use Bandit or Thug statistics); monthly income (50 gp); a corrupt city official on your payroll; access to the [[Black Market]].

**Feature Expansion — Criminal Network**: once per week, activate your network to find the location of any person or item in the city within 24 hours, arrange for someone to be "taken care of" (intimidated, roughed up, or worse), or fence any non-magical item at 75% of its value immediately.

## Rank 4: Crime Lord

**Holdings**: multiple criminal enterprises across the city; network of 50+ criminals, informants, and corrupt officials; monthly income (250 gp); influence with city guard (some on your payroll); legitimate business fronts.

**Feature — Master of Shadows**: you can arrange nearly anything illegal within reason; authorities hesitate to move against you (you have too much dirt on them); your crimes are often pinned on patsies. Once per month, call in a major favor — a hired assassin, mass distraction, or leaked secrets. You have spies in most major organizations in your city and receive weekly intelligence reports.
