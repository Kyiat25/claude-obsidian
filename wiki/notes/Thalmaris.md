# Thalmaris, The Wave-Breaker

*"You thought the sea was only soft? You thought it only eroded, only wore things away? No. The sea is strong. Stronger than stone. Stronger than kings."*

**Domains**: strength of water, resilience, water's power to endure. **Symbol**: a great wave striking against an unbroken cliff. **Worshiped by**: warriors, sailors, those who fight upon the sea. **Rivals**: [[Keldros]], [[Marethis]], Torvath. **Allies**: Kaemor, Vaedoris, Syralis.

Proves that water bends but does not break. Worshiped by warriors as much as sailors, who see in him the lesson that yielding is not the same as losing.
