# Diviner (Oracle) — Rank 1-4

*"You see beyond the present moment, glimpsing threads of fate and possibility."* Batch 3, Magic-Focused category. Source dual-names this "Diviner / Oracle"; filed under the first name. Shares its "Oracle" rank-3 title with [[Hermit]]'s own Rank 3 title — coincidental naming overlap between two unrelated backgrounds, not a mechanical link.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Fortune Teller | Portent Sense | Make a startlingly accurate prediction, divine crucial information, or experience a prophetic vision |
| 2 | Seer | True Sight | Predict a major event that comes true, prevent disaster with a timely warning, or receive a vision from a powerful entity |
| 3 | Oracle | Prophetic Power | Fulfill a history-shaping prophecy, see a vision of apocalyptic importance, or be chosen as fate's instrument |
| 4 | Prophet of Fate | Master of Prophecy | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Fortune Teller

**Skill Proficiency**: Insight, Perception. **Tool Proficiency**: divination cards or bones. **Language**: one of choice. **Equipment**: divination tools (cards, bones, crystal, or tea set), mysterious robes, 5 sticks of incense, 10 gp.

**Feature — Portent Sense**: advantage on Insight checks to read intentions; perform divinations for customers (usually vague, occasionally eerily accurate); once per long rest, get a "feeling" about immediate danger; interpret dreams and omens with reasonable accuracy.

**Holdings**: a small fortune-telling booth or tent; regular clientele, mostly entertainment seekers; a collection of divination tools; a local reputation.

## Rank 2: Seer

**Holdings**: a personal divination chamber; income from readings (20 gp/month); quality divination tools (worth 150 gp); clientele including merchants and minor nobles; a network of 5-8 other diviners; a dream journal spanning years.

**Feature Expansion — True Sight**: advantage on all Wisdom-based checks; once per long rest, cast *augury* without components; your warnings are taken seriously by those who know you; detect when someone lies to you (surface intentions only); trace lost items or people through divination.

## Rank 3: Oracle

**Holdings**: a sanctum or temple dedicated to divination; major income (80 gp/month); an extensive divinatory-materials collection; wealthy clients seeking guidance; 3-5 divination students; a record of many fulfilled predictions; requests from rulers and powerful organizations.

**Feature Expansion — Prophetic Power**: once per long rest, cast *divination* without components; genuine prophetic dreams (DM provides cryptic but real future information); sense disruptions in fate; immune to being surprised; read any divination tool with clarity.

## Rank 4: Prophet of Fate

**Holdings**: a sacred pilgrimage site; excellent income (200 gp/month from donations and consultations); a divination chamber with perfect conditions; a collection of legendary divination artifacts; dedicated followers and interpreters; your prophecies are recorded and studied; influence over believers in fate.

**Feature — Master of Prophecy**: cast *divination* at will; once per month, cast *foresight* on yourself; detailed, clear prophetic visions (DM provides significant future/hidden information); perceive threads of destiny connecting people and events; your predictions carry real weight in probability. Kingdoms seek your counsel before major decisions, your prophecies become historical record, some believe you can see alternate timelines, and you may have a connection to **Threads or Eldritch entities**.
