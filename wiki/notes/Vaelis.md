# Vaelis, The Laughing Breeze

*"Luck? Fate? Nonsense. The wind blows as it wills—sometimes in your favor, sometimes against you."*

**Domains**: luck, wanderers, trickery of the wind. **Symbol**: a wind-swept coin with two faces, one worn away. **Worshiped by**: halflings, gamblers, sailors, thieves who trust luck over skill. **Allies**: [[Zephir]], Thyvael, Telys. **Enemies**: Kaelor, [[Astria]], Nytheris.

The unseen gust of luck — both benevolent and mischievous, helping one moment and causing disaster the next. Halflings say his laugh is in every rolling die.
