# Thyvael, The Unraveled Thread

*"The Weave does not obey. It does not follow rules. Some destinies are written in madness."*

**Domains**: random fate, the chaos of the Weave, unpredictable destiny. **Symbol**: a tangled knot that can never be fully unraveled. **Worshiped by**: tricksters, gamblers, those who believe fate is pure chaos. **Allies**: [[Zephir]], Nyxaris, [[Ytheis]]. **Enemies**: [[Vaithis]], [[Astria]], [[Aelys]].

The force of pure randomness in fate — not all destinies are designed, manipulated, or destroyed; some simply happen.
