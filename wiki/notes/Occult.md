# Occult

One of three knowledge skills (with [[Memory]] and [[Streetwise]]). Lets a character interpret forbidden glyphs, rituals, and dream-visions; understand the motives/codes of cults, corrupted zones, or glyph-warped NPCs. Carries a stated social-fallout risk — using it can reveal you "know too much."
