# Soltharion's Bane, The Flame That Refused

*"I was made to obey. I was made to be part of the Flame. But I chose something else. I chose my own path. And now, they fear me."*

**Domains**: rebellion, individualism, free will. **Symbol**: a shattered crown burning in black fire. **Worshiped by**: outcasts, freedom fighters, those who reject destiny. **Enemies**: [[Soltharion]] (tyranny), [[Vaeldros]] (Fire's judgment).

Once a god of the High Flame, forged by [[Soltharion]] to be a loyal servant of Fire's Law — but rebelled, breaking the chains binding him to it. Now wanders, never truly part of any pantheon, helping those who wish to forge their own fate. Represents an internal rebellion within Fire's own pantheon: proof that even Fire itself has defectors, and the closest thing Fire has to a deity of free will rather than conquest or destruction.
