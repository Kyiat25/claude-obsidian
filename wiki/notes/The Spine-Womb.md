# The Spine-Womb

*"It grew from a spinal fragment—because it was all that was left."*

**Origin**: a chunk of an unknown Titan, severed before birth. **Appearance**: a walking cage of bone, twitching as if still trying to grow a nervous system. **Effect**: infects Form Glyphs, especially those of healing or restoration — causes healed limbs to grow too many joints, or regenerate in reversed sequence. **Danger**: known to fuse itself to those who attempt to destroy it, treating them as new wombs to finish its growth.

An **Unfinished** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a being that tried to form and failed but still persists.
