# Orris, The Wandering Breeze

*"I do not carry gold. I do not own ships. But without me, your merchants are nothing."*

**Domains**: trade winds, safe navigation, the breath of fortune. **Symbol**: a gold coin carried by a gust of wind. **Worshiped by**: merchants, sailors, sea-traveling traders. **Allies**: [[Aetheris]], [[Zephir]]. **Enemies**: Howlar, Morgathis.

The wind that fills the sails and ensures commerce flows. Does not control wealth, only whether it moves. Sailors offer coins to the wind before long voyages.
