# The Deepstone Cantons — the Vaults of Civilization

A trade confederation of seven independent dwarven holds (population ~2.8 million, plus ~200,000 in tunnel communities) in the eastern mountains of [[Elarion]], along the only major overland route (the Dawnreach Expanse) to Kerythra. No official capital; **Ironhold Canton** (~520,000) hosts the Trade Assembly. Not the largest, most aggressive, or most magical power on the continent — but **indispensable**: every legitimate trade coin on Elarion is minted here, five of the [[Eight Guild Professions|Eight Great Guilds]] have their primary headquarters here, and ~800,000 tons of goods pay Canton tolls annually crossing the Dawnreach passes. "Invade Deepstone, and the coins in your pocket become worthless" — no power in Elarion's history has been foolish enough to try.

## The Seven Cantons

**Ironhold** (Trade Assembly seat, Blacksmith Guild HQ), **Gemheart** (Jeweler Guild HQ), **Runemark** (Enchanting Guild HQ), **Gearstone** (Tinker Guild HQ), **Stillwater** (Chemistry Guild HQ), **Goleli** (the Mint and gold reserves, home to the **Sovereign Vaults** — 3,000m of solid rock, dwarven engineering, and ancient wards protecting the reserves backing every coin on the continent), and **Dawnwatch** (pass control and military coordination). Only ~20% of Canton civilization is on the surface; the rest descends through the Upper Halls, the Living Strata, the Deep Forges, and the Vault Depths (3,000m+).

## The Currency Monopoly's origin

After [[Ashkarra]]'s fall (~487 years ago) shattered continental currency into a chaos of incompatible regional coinages, Deepstone proposed a single standardized currency minted in their secure vaults and backed by verified metal reserves — the arrangement that still underpins Elarion's economy.

## Guild headquarters in more detail

Each of the five Canton-hosted guilds has a named physical seat: the Blacksmith Guild's **Anvil Halls** in Ironhold (47 interconnected forge-chambers around a 40-ton meteoric-iron Grand Anvil; highest-ranked practitioner Grandmaster **Thokk Emberfist**, Dragonborn, 187, works in [[Veltharyn]] but holds Ironhold certification); the Jeweler Guild's **Crystal Chambers** in Gemheart (whose gem-grading and precious-metal-purity certification underpins the Minthold's currency production directly); and the Enchanting Guild's **Glyph Vaults** in Runemark (sited underground specifically to shield its work from Thread interference, near the Dawnreach Expanse leyline nexus).

## Notable figures

**Trade-Prince Borin Goleli** (187, "Gold does not panic," conservative voice on currency matters), **Guildmistress Thira Gemheart** (203, Jeweler Guild Grandmaster, controls continental gem-grading standards), **Engineer-General Kazrak Dawnwatch** (156, military commander pushing for more defense spending amid surface instability), **Mastersmith Hilda Ironhold** (178, Minthold Director, famously incorruptible), and **the Tunnel King** — a possibly-mythical, possibly-composite figure said to coordinate the Cantons' smuggling networks and know every hidden passage.
