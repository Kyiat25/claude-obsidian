# Iskorel, The Icebound Sage

*"You think time flows as the river does? You think all things change? You are wrong. Some things do not move. Some things do not change. Some things do not melt."*

**Domains**: ice, preservation, water that does not flow. **Symbol**: a frozen river, perfectly still, with no cracks. **Worshiped by**: icewalkers, northern mystics.

Where [[Krythos]] governs ice's endurance, Iskorel governs what is locked within it — frozen history, forgotten things, moments trapped in time; doesn't move or act, simply preserves. Icewalkers meditate at frozen lakes believing his wisdom is held in the silence.
