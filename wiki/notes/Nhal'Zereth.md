# Nhal'Zereth, The Unbeing Writ

*"Nothing should be. And yet, here you are."*

**Domain**: retroactive erasure from time, thought, and the Weave. **Symbol**: a spiral that ends before it begins. **Truth**: wherever Nhal'Zereth touches, history ceases to recall it ever existed. **Mythic tie**: the Severing of the Forgotten Glyph. **Greatest achievement**: "The Missing Thread" — an entire god erased so thoroughly not even memory remains.

Spawned, per [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]'s cosmic hierarchy, when [[The Whispering Dark]] first touched the Weave — the archetypal example of an Eldritch God (Tier 1: anti-creation forces with apocalyptic campaign threat level).

## How it bleeds into reality

- **Spirit Pantheon**: [[Spirit Pantheon|Aelys]] fears Nhal'Zereth most; her glyphs warp near Hollow Vault regions; spells vanish before they're cast; wards unravel in reverse.
- **Earth Pantheon**: [[Earth Pantheon|Gaeldros]] inscribes divine laws that unravel upon reading; stone-law becomes infected with unbeing; records of sealed threats simply stop existing.

## Corruptions

- **Beasts — Fraywolves**: bodies disappear mid-step, can't be fully seen in mirrors, fade partially from reality (always half-gone).
- **Monsters — Ghostborn**: undead who retroactively never died, existing in violation of causality itself.
- **Mortals — Hollow-Walkers**: no one remembers their names, faces, or that they existed; suffer erasures of history while still physically present; eventually become tools of [[The Hollow Thread|the Hollow Vault]].

## Preferred manifestation zones

Dead Magic Zones, collapsed leyline junctions, sites of mass forgetting (lost cities, erased battlefields).

## Warning signs

Written records of recent events simply don't exist; multiple people insist an event happened but no evidence remains; mirrors show nothing where someone stands; names become hard to remember or pronounce.

## Role in the Hollow Thread

Nhal'Zereth is one of the named forces actively working to erode the memory of [[The Hollow Thread]]'s seal — its specialization (retroactive erasure) is aimed specifically at making gods and mortals forget why the seal matters, targeting [[Spirit Pantheon|Aelys]], memory-based seals, and written records directly.
