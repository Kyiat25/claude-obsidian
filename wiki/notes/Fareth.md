# Fareth, the Waking Fog

*"The dream didn't end. It just got very quiet. And then very cold."*

**Origin**: a vaporous entity from [[The Null Reflection]] that drifted into the world during an overlapping [[Umbral Veil|Shadowfell]] dream-plane convergence. **Behavior**: appears at twilight or dawn — mist that hums in tones only sleepers hear. **Effect**: victims trapped inside become unable to tell whether they're dreaming or not; some never wake. **Danger**: if you die in the fog, your body becomes a sleeping statue, perfectly preserved but with no mind left.

A **Dream-Born** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification.
