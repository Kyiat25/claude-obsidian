# Istriel, The Everflowing Cup

*"I am the drink that saves. The water that heals. But I am not yours to take freely."*

**Domains**: drinkable water, sacred wells, rivers of life. **Symbol**: a simple clay cup overflowing with clear water. **Worshiped by**: farmers, healers, settlers, those who dig wells. **Rivals**: [[Yltheris]], Mirethis, Luthan. **Allies**: [[Marethis]], [[Itheris]].

Does not punish, but does not give freely either — a god of fair exchange rather than pure generosity. The deity most literally responsible for whether a well runs dry or full.
