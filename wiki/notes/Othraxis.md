# Othraxis, The Daggerwave

*"You did not see it coming. You never do."*

**Domains**: stealthy oceanic death, rogue waves, assassins of the sea. **Symbol**: a single, curved wave, striking like a blade. **Worshiped by**: feared by sailors, respected by warriors who seek silent kills. **Rivals**: Vaedoris, Marethis, Rhaldris. **Allies**: [[Vorthys]], Nyssara, Rythos.

Unlike [[Zorveth]], who destroys with fury, Othraxis is the rogue wave that strikes without warning — the cut of a blade hidden beneath the water. No one sees him coming; by the time they do, it's already too late.
