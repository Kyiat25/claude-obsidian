# Rythos, The Drowning Grasp

*"The water was calm. The sea was gentle. And then, without warning, it took him."*

**Domains**: rip currents, undertows, water's unseen pull. **Symbol**: a single hand reaching up from beneath the waves. **Worshiped by**: coastal villagers, divers, those who fear being pulled into the abyss. **Rivals**: [[Nereus]], Rhaldris, Theroneth. **Allies**: Nyxthys, [[Jorvethis]], Morgathis.

Does not rage or warn — he simply pulls. Unlike [[Aelthys]] (storms) or [[Voraxis]] (tsunamis), he does not destroy out of anger; he waits, and the ocean provides. Children are warned of "Rythos' Hand," the current that takes swimmers who go too far.
