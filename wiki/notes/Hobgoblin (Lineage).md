# Hobgoblin

*"They cast us out without law. So we became Law itself—sharper than any blade they wielded against us."*

**Origin myth**: per the Chain Ascendant interpretation of the Faith of the Gnawing Crown, hobgoblins are the echo of divine law warped into militaristic order — the same discarded Fragment as [[Goblin (Lineage)]] and [[Bugbear (Lineage)]], but the part that remembered structure and command. Ravva ("Law Unacknowledged") is worshipped as proof that discipline transcends divinity.

> [!note] Flagged by the lineage design-critique document
> `FEARS_Corrected_Lineage_Analysis.md` singles Hobgoblin out as the "worst offender" for cultural contamination — Martial Training, Saving Face, and Formation Fighter are argued to be 100% learned military skills that should live in a proposed "Ironbound Legion" Heritage rather than innate Lineage traits, since "babies don't know how to use weapons." Documented as an unresolved design critique, not applied to the traits below (which follow the lineage source as written).

**Physical**: mature by 14, adult by 18, live ~80 years; Medium, 5½–6½ ft., 30 ft. speed, broad and muscular.

## Traits

- **Darkvision** 60 ft.
- **Martial Training**: light/medium armor, shields, 2 martial weapons of choice
- **Saving Face**: on a missed attack/failed check/save, add a bonus = allies visible within 30 ft. (max +5), uses = proficiency bonus/long rest
- **Fortune from the Many**: can grant the Saving Face bonus to an ally instead
- **Tactical Discipline**: Insight/Intimidation/Perception proficiency; Help action gains 10 ft. movement + extended advantage window
- **Formation Fighter**: +1 AC/attack near one ally, +2 near two+
- **Languages**: Common + Goblin (spoken with military precision — "Chain-Tongue" for sacred matters)

**Mental**: Focus +1 (tactical training encoded into consciousness) / Sanity +0.

**DCS Integration**: Dodge +0 / Endure +1 / Deflect +1; DR +0 (protection from armor/formation, not biology). Advantage on Rally with allies present, disadvantage alone; forced Rally check at disadvantage if the unit leader falls or formation breaks.

## Corruption: The Broken Chain

Corruption enters through broken oaths, shattered formations, or betrayed trust. At Corruption 10+: Command Confusion, Formation Sickness, Oath-Strain. **Shattered Chain table** (1d6): False Command, Chain-Deafness (Formation Fighter disabled), Link Rejection (1d4 psychic + attack disadvantage near allies), Weight of Chaos (Sanity save or −1d4), Eldritch Command (+1 Whisper Point, Saving Face auto-fails), or Chain Holds (Corruption −1).
