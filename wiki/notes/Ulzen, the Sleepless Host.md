# Ulzen, the Sleepless Host

**Origin**: a collective dream-entity born from the 7th Sleep of Kings. **Behavior**: infects via shared dreams during Eldritch Moons, especially the [[Slumbering Moon]]. **Effect**: groups speak in sync, dreamwalk while awake, and remember things that never happened. **Goal**: merge enough minds together that it manifests as a "real person."

A **Dream-Born** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a nightmare that has breached into waking life.
