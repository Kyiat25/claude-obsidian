# The Pane-Creep

**Origin**: arcane towers using scrying crystals during a Rift pulse. **Behavior**: appears in glass or crystal, always distant, each time closer. **Effect**: victims feel they've met it before; it leaves smears on the *inside* of mirrors. **Manifestation**: if a door reflected in glass opens when it arrives, it steps through.

A **Reflected** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a mirror-entity born from corrupted identity.
