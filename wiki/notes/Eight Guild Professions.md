# The Eight Guild Professions of Elarion

Continental craft organizations that transcend political boundaries and survive regime changes on [[Elarion]], deliberately headquartered in neutral trading territories — the Deepstone Cantons and the Lyrran Trading Cities — rather than in any major power or Loom-City, so no kingdom can claim exclusive guild authority. Rank hierarchy: Apprentice → Journeyman → Artisan → Expert → **Master** (requires presenting a Masterwork before the Guild Council at Primary Headquarters — a "pilgrimage" that is itself part of the test) → Grandmaster (continental recognition, guild leadership).

**Deepstone Canton guilds** (primary HQ): Blacksmith ("Iron Remembers," ~45,000 practitioners, HQ the Anvil Halls of Ironhold Canton), Jeweler, Enchanting, Tinker, Chemistry.

**Lyrran Trading Cities guilds** (primary HQ): Leatherworker, Tailor, Woodworker.

A smith from [[Veltharyn]] still needs Deepstone Canton approval for Master rank; a Talanthar woodworker must journey to the Lyrran coast to present their masterwork — the cross-continental pilgrimage requirement is what keeps the guild system neutral and economically interdependent.
