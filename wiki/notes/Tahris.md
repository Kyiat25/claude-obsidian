# Tahris, The Stormbreaker

*"I do not fight for kings. I fight because the storm commands it."*

**Domains**: war, lightning, the wrath of the storm. **Symbol**: a spear crackling with lightning. **Worshiped by**: skyborne warriors, aerial cavalry, those who fight beneath the open sky. **Allies**: [[Thalmar]], Gryphor, [[Veydras]]. **Enemies**: [[Soltharion]], Keldros, Xethar.

The storm's warrior, the weapon of the sky — his lightning strikes with purpose, not randomly. To fight beneath an open sky is to be within his domain.
