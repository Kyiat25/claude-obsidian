# Zerah, The Ember-Speaker

*"The fire whispers. It does not tell you what you want. It tells you what you fear."*

**Domains**: knowledge, arcana, twilight. **Symbol**: a burning book with missing pages. **Worshiped by**: scholars, warlocks, those who seek dangerous truths. **Enemies**: [[Soltharion]], [[Vaeldros]], [[Ignis]].

Knows Fire's greatest secret — that it was never truly first among the elements — but doesn't reveal it directly, letting knowledge spread like embers until a spark ignites it into truth. Hides knowledge in riddles and prophecies, whispers to the ambitious and the heretical, and quietly warns [[Ignis]] of his own eventual downfall. Already established elsewhere in this cluster (see [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]) as having prophetic visions of gods "screaming in chains made of light," which she dismisses as metaphor for divine responsibility rather than the literal truth it is.

**The Last Prophecy of Fire**: "The First Flame flickers, though none dare see it" (Fire's deception will eventually be revealed); "A flame that conquers all must eventually consume itself" ([[Soltharion]]'s war will end Fire's supremacy); "The last fire will be lit by hands not born of flame" (the other pantheons will one day destroy Fire's illusion of primacy). Her final recorded words: "One day, the last fire will burn. And no one will be there to rekindle it" — though [[The Restorer]] sees another path: fire has always passed from hand to hand, and when the last ember dies, a new hand will find it.

**Prophecy Unraveled — The Paradox Threads**: [[Zhurr'Kael]] once whispered into her fire-born visions, causing her prophecies to contradict themselves (a star rising only if it first fell; a king dying only if he lived). Her followers now call this the Burning Paradox and train "paradox-watchers" — glyph-readers who sift truth from contradiction before fire breaks meaning entirely.
