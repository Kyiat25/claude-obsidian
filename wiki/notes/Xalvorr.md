# Xalvorr, The Roaring Land

**Domain**: earthquakes, landslides, volcanic outbursts, Earth's rage. **Symbol**: a screaming maw in a ruptured cliffside. **Worshiped by**: cataclysm cults, disaster prophets, quake-hardened survivalists.

The emotional fury of the earth — a force of chaos and warning rather than pure destruction. Myth: when a city defied the stone by building too high, Xalvorr answered not to destroy but to remind the world the land is not owned. Rivalry with [[Orivak]]: Orivak binds the leylines, Xalvorr cracks them — he is the test of balance.
