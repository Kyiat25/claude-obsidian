# Wells of Power

Stationary locations where magic gathers and condenses, formed where leylines intersect or where fragments of old worlds bleed into the new reality — arcane lakes or reservoirs, as opposed to [[Leylines]]' fluid, moving current. The second of the [[Three Pillars of Magical Flow]].

## Function

Not created, but manifested naturally over time, shaped by the Weave's own cycle. They act as anchors for reality, preventing unstable magic from spiraling out of control. Form at leyline intersections, magical fractures, or sites of extreme mystical significance. They store vast amounts of magic, making them ideal for rituals, summoning, and artifact-forging. Unlike leylines, they're **finite** — a depleted Well may take centuries to refill. Some are sentient, formed from the echoes of old gods, lost civilizations, or fragments of past ages.

## Types by source

| Type | Source | Effects |
|---|---|---|
| **Astral Wells** | Aeloria's Loom & the Glyph of Threads | Amplifies divination, fate-weaving, structured spellcraft |
| **Void Wells** | The Whispering Dark & the Glyph of Endings | Creates unstable magic, consumes spells, enhances entropy-based magic |
| **Elemental Wells** | Elemental Glyphs | Alters elemental spells, sometimes warping properties unpredictably |
| **Restoration Wells** | The Restorer & the Glyph of Echoes | Causes lost spells, knowledge, and artifacts to resurface |
| **Dream Wells** | The Veiled Hand & the Glyph of Dreaming | Generates illusion magic shaping reality — visions, phantoms, living dreams |

## Named legendary Wells

- **The Abyssal Font** — a void-ridden Well that corrupts all magic drawn from it.
- **The Cradle of Stars** — a celestial Well channeling lost knowledge from forgotten constellations.
- **The Rift of Echoes** — formed from the remnants of past worlds, where ancient spells resurface at random.
- **The Maw of Severance** — a forbidden well where the [[Glyph of Severance]] once bled into reality, rumored to be a place where even gods can be erased.

## Planar reflections

In [[Luminous Echo]] (the Feywild), Wells of Power bloom into **Dream Wells** — sources of chaotic, lush magic responding to intent more than ritual. In [[Umbral Veil]] (the Shadowfell), they become **Hollow Wells**, where power seeps away rather than gathers, and spellcasting feeds more from memory and loss than essence.

## Eldritch corruption

Where the Weave thins, Wells may become **Fouled Wells** — twisted by [[The Null Reflection]], infected by beings like [[Nhal'Zereth]] or [[Vael'Akir]]. Such sites erase memories, fold reality backward, or birth spells that cast themselves.

## The Faith's view

The Faith of the Bound Pattern holds that Wells of Power are "Veiled Gifts," seeded by the Weave and guarded by divine intent. Each is marked with sacred thread-runes by Threadbearers, who determine whether it should remain open, be siphoned by sanctioned ritual, or sealed entirely.
