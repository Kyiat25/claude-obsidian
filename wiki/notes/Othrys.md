# Othrys, The Stagnant One

*"Water is meant to flow. But not all water remembers that."*

**Domains**: lifeless water, pools that do not move, stagnation. **Symbol**: a lake with no ripples, perfectly still. **Worshiped by**: feared by swamp dwellers, avoided by well-diggers. **Rivals**: [[Marethis]], Istriel, Luthan. **Allies**: [[Mirethis]], Vaelora, Yltheris.

Water that refuses to flow — the still, unmoving depths where nothing grows. He does not seek followers or take lives; he simply waits, and ensures nothing moves, changes, or grows where he settles.
