# Khareth, The Iceborn Hunter

*"I do not chase. I do not hunt. I only ensure that nothing escapes."*

**Domains**: glacial stillness, ice that does not melt, frozen death. **Symbol**: a single, unmoving footprint in the snow. **Worshiped by**: arctic nomads, winter survivalists, those who hunt in the cold. **Rivals**: [[Soltharion]], Xethar, Rhyzel. **Allies**: [[Iskorel]], [[Ythara]], Vithras.

Unlike Fire, which consumes quickly, Khareth's cold takes its time — patient, unmoving, always waiting. The cold always wins in the end.
