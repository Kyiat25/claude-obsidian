# Spirit Pantheon

Secondary defense against [[The Hollow Thread]], and (per [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]'s Pantheon Vulnerabilities) the **highest**-vulnerability pantheon — its domains (memory, identity, time, dream) are exactly what the Eldritch forces corrupt.

## Named deities

**Aelys, the Weave-Keeper** — magical infrastructure maintenance; knows the Weave has a wound it cannot heal, and that [[The Hollow Thread]] threatens her entire purpose. Stabilizes the Weave around the sealed area and reinforces leyline connections that might slow the Thread's spread. Most vulnerable of any named deity to [[Nhal'Zereth]] specifically — her glyphs warp near Hollow Vault regions.

**Nyzara, the Moon That Watches** — memory guardian; remembers that something must be forgotten, and maintains "productive forgetting" to keep dangerous knowledge from resurfacing. Her dreams sometimes ripple wrong, a symptom of [[Ul'Naresh]]'s corruption.

**Kyren, the Starlost Seer** — per [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]'s Pantheon Vulnerabilities section, already **partially corrupted** by [[Xey'Tazun]] specifically: sees temporal paradoxes and visions he cannot prove. Notably, halfling joy is stated to keep the darkness back for him.

## Strategy against the Eldritch

Memory manipulation, erasing dangerous knowledge, maintaining Weave stability — preservation at any cost.
