# Elythra, The Pearl Guardian

*"The ocean does not only take. Sometimes, it gives. And what it gives is beyond compare."*

**Domains**: beauty beneath the waves, hidden wonders, wealth beyond gold. **Symbol**: a pearl resting within an open shell. **Worshiped by**: divers, pearl hunters, those who seek treasures in the deep. **Rivals**: [[Lyvessa]], [[Morgathis]], Othrys. **Allies**: Theroneth, Selmira, Kaemor.

Governs the ocean's beauty — not the deceptive kind, but true wonder. Not kind, not generous: only those who respect the ocean ever see it. Those who defile it find their nets always empty.
