# Sylkras, The Vanishing Trail

*"Some roads vanish not because they are unused, but because they have seen too much."*

**Domain**: lost roads, fading trails, forgotten paths. **Symbol**: a winding trail that dissolves into mist. **Worshiped by**: exiles, memory-walkers, ghosts, lost scouts.

The eraser of paths — governs roads that vanish from maps, trails that cannot be retraced, pilgrimages never meant to be completed; the god of destinies never taken.

**Myth — The Scout of Erendahl's Road**: Sir Vireth was sent to ride a legendary road to call for naval reinforcements. His horse returned alone; the road vanished from every map overnight, and cartographers who tried to redraw it lost their pens, then their words, then the memory of why they were drawing at all. Sibling-force to [[Tavron]], though they rarely align; a tool and fear of [[Karthas]], who uses her to bury empires from memory. **Aspect of the Lock**: ensures some places may never be found again, especially those near leyline fractures or forbidden gates.
