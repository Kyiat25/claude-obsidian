# Occultist — Rank 1-4 (Mystic of Hidden Things)

*"You deal with the supernatural, mystical, and paranormal."* Tier 3 (Dark Fantasy). Fulfills the "Occultist" slot [[FEARS Advanced Backgrounds]] flagged as not-yet-developed after Batch 1. Overlaps conceptually with [[Eldritch Scholar]] (both grant spellcasting access to forbidden/otherworldly power by capstone) but with a divination/mediumship framing rather than Eldritch Scholar's corrupting-knowledge, humanity-cost framing — no Corruption-style cost is stated anywhere in this background's text.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Seeker of Mysteries | Mystic Senses | Perform a disaster-preventing divination, contact a spirit successfully, or discover an occult secret |
| 2 | Practicing Mystic | Occult Practitioner | Prevent a major calamity through divination, banish a malevolent spirit, or gain recognition from occult societies |
| 3 | Master Occultist | Mystic Adept | Prevent an apocalypse through divination, be recognized by supernatural entities, or found an occult order |
| 4 | Archoccultist | Bridge Between Worlds | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Seeker of Mysteries

**Skill Proficiency**: Arcana, Religion. **Tool Proficiency**: divination tools (tarot cards, crystal ball, bones, etc.). **Language**: one of choice. **Equipment**: divination tools, occult texts, incense, candles, dark robes, 15 gp.

**Feature — Mystic Senses**: sense the presence of supernatural entities within 30 ft; advantage on checks to identify occult symbols, rituals, or sites; perform simple divinations (yes/no questions); know where to find occult knowledge.

**Specialty** (choose your focus): Divination, Necromancy, Spirit contact (mediumship), Astral travel and dreams, Sacred geometry and ley lines, Ritual magic.

**Holdings**: a small occult texts collection; divination tools; a hidden study.

## Rank 2: Practicing Mystic

**Holdings**: a proper occult library (worth 200 gp); income from divinations and séances (25 gp/month); a collection of ritual components; a network of 5-8 occult contacts; clients seeking your mystical services; a private ritual space.

**Feature Expansion — Occult Practitioner**: cast *detect magic* and one divination spell (like *augury*) once per day; advantage on divination-related rolls; perform séances to contact spirits; identify cursed items and haunted places; create minor protective charms.

## Rank 3: Master Occultist

**Holdings**: an extensive occult library and collection (worth 1,000 gp); good income (80 gp/month); a sanctum warded against supernatural intrusion; 2-3 apprentices; a network across occult circles; wealthy and powerful clients; a collection of mystical artifacts.

**Feature Expansion — Mystic Adept**: cast several divination and necromancy spells; once per week, perform a powerful ritual (contact the dead, scry a distant location, divine a major future event); your presence disrupts harmful supernatural effects; see and communicate with spirits freely; create powerful protective wards and charms.

## Rank 4: Archoccultist

**Holdings**: a legendary, priceless occult library; excellent income (200 gp/month); a sanctum existing partially in the spirit world; an order or school of mystics (20-30 students); your research shapes occult practice; powerful supernatural allies; artifacts of immense power.

**Feature — Bridge Between Worlds**: see into the spirit world, astral plane, and ethereal realm at will; cast powerful divination and necromancy spells; once per month, predict or prevent a catastrophic event; spirits and supernatural entities treat you with respect/fear; travel astrally or physically to other planes. You may be partially spirit yourself, rulers consult you on supernatural matters, your divinations shape history, and upon death you likely become a powerful spirit or force yourself.
