# Howlar, The Storm-Wolf

*"The storm does not wait. The storm does not ask. The storm does not leave survivors."*

**Domains**: wild storms, unpredictable winds, the storm that cannot be prepared for. **Symbol**: a snarling wolf's head made of swirling storm clouds. **Worshiped by**: barbarians, nomads, those who embrace chaos. **Allies**: [[Zeraphis]], Xalros, Nyxaris. **Enemies**: [[Astria]], Tahris, Keldros.

Strikes without reason, without pattern, without mercy — no oracle can foresee his path, no city can prepare for his arrival. Not a god mortals seek; a god that finds them.
