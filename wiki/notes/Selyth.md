# Selyth, The Weight of Fate

*"You have seen your destiny. You cannot unsee it. It will weigh on you for the rest of your days."*

**Domains**: fate's burden, the cost of knowledge, the suffering of foreseen destiny. **Symbol**: a figure kneeling under an unseen weight, their shadow stretching infinitely. **Worshiped by**: those cursed with visions of their own future, seers who regret their prophecies. **Allies**: [[Telys]], [[Nyzara]], [[Vaithis]]. **Enemies**: [[Nytheris]], Valthos, [[Ytheis]].

Does not cause prophecies or enforce them — he ensures that those who glimpse their destiny feel its weight. Fate is not just knowledge; it is a burden.
