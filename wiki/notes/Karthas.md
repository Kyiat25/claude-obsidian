# Karthas, The Burier of Empires

**Domain**: the collapse of civilisations, forgotten ruins, the inevitability of decline. **Symbol**: a half-buried crown in cracked stone. **Worshiped by**: ruin-dwellers, historians, scavenger-scholars, fatalistic kings. **Rival**: Solmara (preservation). **Tension with**: [[Dalvora]] — one builds, the other buries. **Linked to**: [[Vorendral]] — where Karthas buries empires, Vorendral buries their rulers.

**Myth**: he did not destroy the First Empire outright — he simply waited, and it fell on its own.
