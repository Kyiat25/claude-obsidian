# Vokir, The Drunken Lord

*"A single drop can bring joy. A single drop can bring ruin. Drink carefully."*

**Domain**: alcohol, brewing, festivities. Governs all things brewed, fermented, and intoxicating — his gifts bring both celebration and destruction, and he doesn't care which. Walks among mortals disguised as a wandering drunk, testing those who drink too deeply.

**Myth — The King Who Drank Without Thought**: a ruler sought to impress Vokir by drinking endlessly in his name, not realizing Vokir rewards appreciation, not excess. The king drank himself into madness; when he died, his kingdom forgot his name.
