# Water Pantheon

Per [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]'s Pantheon Vulnerabilities, a **high**-vulnerability pantheon — its depths hold secrets better forgotten.

## Named deities

**Vaelora, the Deep Mother** — her abyss projects nightmares, a symptom of [[Ul'Naresh]]'s corruption; may have a hidden connection to [[Xey'Tazun]], described elsewhere as "Vaelora's betrayal — the Deep Mother's deepest secret." Dreams of the ocean spread corruption outward.

## Strategy against the Eldritch

Patience — drowning corruption slowly, waiting for Eldritch forces to exhaust themselves. "Time defeats all things."
