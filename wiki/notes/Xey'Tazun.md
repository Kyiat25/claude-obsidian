# Xey'Tazun, The Time Unwritten

*"You should not be here. And yet you remember being here. Which is worse?"*

**Domain**: time disobedience — futures that refuse to arrive, pasts bleeding into now. **Symbol**: a shattered hourglass with sand flowing upward and sideways. **Truth**: time itself forgets the order of events. **Mythic tie**: Vaelora's betrayal — the Deep Mother's deepest secret may involve this. **Effect**: prophecy works backward, death occurs before life.

## How it bleeds into reality

- **Air Pantheon**: [[Air Pantheon|Astria]]'s omens arrive backward; stars reflect events that never occur; prophecy becomes retroactive.
- **Spirit Pantheon**: [[Spirit Pantheon|Kyren]] is haunted by visions from Xey'Tazun — cannot unsee, cannot prove, sees multiple timelines bleeding together. Per [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]'s Pantheon Vulnerabilities section, Kyren is already **partially corrupted** by Xey'Tazun specifically.

## Corruptions

- **Beasts — Timeblinks**: die, then reappear alive hours before death, then vanish — exist in temporal loops, carrying memories of future deaths.
- **Monsters — Chimeric Yesterdays**: half-born in different timelines, heads whispering conflicting futures, bodies drawn from multiple moments.
- **Mortals — Echoed**: exist in several personal timelines at once; actions contradict causality; remember futures that never happen; age backward while aging forward.

## Preferred manifestation zones

Arcane Rifts, Wild Magic Zones, sites of temporal magic, chronomancer towers.

## Warning signs

Déjà vu becomes constant; events happen out of order; people remember meeting you before you met them; clocks run backward or sideways.

## Campaign tiers

| Tier (Levels) | Scale |
|---|---|
| 1 (1-4) | Simple time loop in a single building |
| 2 (5-10) | A town experiences yesterday and tomorrow simultaneously |
| 3 (11-16) | Historical events begin unhappening |
| 4 (17-20) | Timeline collapse threatens reality |

## Role in the Hollow Thread

Xey'Tazun's specialization — temporal corruption — is aimed at breaking the causality that maintains [[The Hollow Thread]]'s seal, making it exist in multiple timelines simultaneously (some where it holds, some where it fails), with the goal of merging a timeline where the seal never existed into reality itself.

## Unwitting agent: Kethyr Flameheart

Per [[Arc 2A - The Prophet of Ashes|Campaign Arc 2A]], Xey'Tazun granted a grieving Ashkarra-survivor priest, **Kethyr Flameheart**, a centuries-long warlock pact after his family was unmade in the Day of Twelve Shadows. Kethyr believes his subsequent crusade to reveal the gods' lie about creation is righteous truth-seeking; in fact his "visions" are Xey'Tazun's guidance, and every Faith temple he burns and every theological certainty he shatters weakens the belief-maintained containment referred to there as "the Nameless One Below" — i.e. [[The Hollow Thread]]'s seal.
