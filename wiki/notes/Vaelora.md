# Vaelora, The Deep Mother

*"The ocean is not empty. The deep is not silent. You think you are alone? You are not."*

**Domains**: the deep ocean, forgotten knowledge, secrets beneath the waves. **Symbol**: a pair of unseen eyes, submerged beneath dark waves. **Worshiped by**: deep-sea explorers, those who seek forbidden knowledge. **Allies**: [[Jorvethis]], Nyxthys, Morgathis.

The secret that should have remained buried — doesn't give answers or stop anyone from looking, but those who search too deep never return. Some believe drowned cities aren't empty; Vaelora keeps them hidden, alive but forgotten. Already established elsewhere in this cluster (see [[Guide to the Eldritch Forces]]) as projecting nightmares into the tides — a symptom of [[Ul'Naresh]]'s corruption — and possibly connected to [[Xey'Tazun]] through a deeply-guarded secret even her fellow Water gods don't know.
