# Varuthis, The Black Bloom

*"It grows where it should not. It spreads in the dark. And by the time you notice, it is already too late."*

**Domains**: rot in water, parasites, the bloom of death. **Symbol**: a lily floating atop dark, unmoving water. **Worshiped by**: feared by herbalists, water-dwellers, and healers. **Rivals**: [[Istriel]], [[Itheris]], Ormara. **Allies**: [[Mirethis]], [[Othrys]], Vorthys.

Does not rage — she waits, and ensures that in time, all things rot. Some believe she was once a flower upon the first river, cursed to spread forever in poisoned waters.
