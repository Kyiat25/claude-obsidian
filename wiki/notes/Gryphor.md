# Gryphor, The Winged Hunt

*"The hunt never ends. If you run, I will chase. If you hide, I will find you. If you fight, you will lose."*

**Domains**: eternal pursuit, the wind that never rests, the predator's flight. **Symbol**: a pair of wings, stretched in full pursuit. **Worshiped by**: hunters, bounty-seekers, storm-warriors. **Allies**: [[Thalmar]], [[Tahris]], [[Veydras]]. **Enemies**: Howlar, Xerathis, Vaethis.

Never stops, never slows, never yields — the storm that chases forever. Bounty hunters invoke him before setting off, swearing the hunt must never end.
