# Marethis, The River-Warden

*"The river remembers its way. So should you."*

**Domains**: rivers, travel by water, guidance. **Symbol**: a winding river splitting in two. **Allies**: [[Nereus]], Vaedoris, Rhaldris.

Does not demand worship — simply expects mortals to follow their own path. Disliked by [[Sylthara]] (Earth's river-goddess of boundaries); rival to [[Dalvora]] as one of several who compete over water's flow through cultivated land.

> [!note] Naming collision with an Air Pantheon deity
> `.raw/The Air Pantheon.md` separately names a demigod, "Marethis, The Rain-Bearer" (seasonal monsoons) — see [[Marethis (Air)]]. Unrelated domain, no cross-reference in either source; documented as a distinct entity sharing this god's name.
