# Magus — Blade and Spell as One

**Half-Caster tier**. "The wizard memorises how magic speaks. The Magus speaks back — through a longsword." A hybrid striker who escalates danger as the fight continues, combining the Fighter's reliable weapon attacks with the Wizard's spell access in one body. Complexity self-rated HIGH (7/10) by its own per-class deep-dive.

> "The wizard memorises how magic speaks. The Magus speaks back — through a longsword."

## Signature abilities

- **Spellstrike** — delivers spells through attacks
- **Arcane Combat** — seamless hybrid play
- **Combat Casting** — fluid transitions

**Gameplay style**: hybrid striker, combining magic and melee into efficient bursts. **Core loop**: combine → strike → reposition → repeat. Struggles when unable to close distance or when spell slots drain without payoff; excels in sustained melee pressure combining physical Momentum with magical escalation.

## Class basics (from Magus.md deep-dive)

- **Hit Dice**: 1d8/level. HP at 1st: 8 + CON mod. HP at higher levels: 1d8 (or 5) + CON mod/level after 1st.
- **Proficiencies**: Armour — light, shields. Weapons — simple, martial. Tools — none. Saves — Constitution, Intelligence. Skills — choose two from Arcana, Athletics, Crafting, History, Insight, Investigation, Perception, Religion, Resolve.
- **Starting Equipment**: (a) longsword or (b) any martial weapon; (a) light crossbow + 20 bolts or (b) any simple weapon; (a) scholar's pack or (b) explorer's pack; leather armour and an arcane focus or component pouch.
- **Quick Build**: Intelligence highest, then Strength or Dexterity (matching weapon preference). Any lineage (Half-Elf, Human, Tiefling thematic). Sage/Soldier or an Arcana/Athletics background. Spellstrike for magical delivery; Spell Combat to attack while defending; Arcane Enhancement early to match enemy vulnerabilities.

## Core class features (full text)

### Spellcasting (1st level) — eighth confirmed caster DC formula

Intelligence is the spellcasting ability. **Spell Save DC = 8 + PB + Intelligence modifier. Spell Attack Modifier = PB + Intelligence modifier.** Eighth independent confirmation of the `8 + PB + ability mod` formula, and the second on an Intelligence-based caster after [[Inventor]]. Knows spells per the table; may swap one known spell on levelling.

### Spellstrike (1st level) — new Momentum gain source

Casting a damaging or single-target cantrip can be delivered through a melee weapon attack: the weapon attack roll determines the hit, applying both weapon damage and the cantrip's effects (auto-hits if the cantrip normally needs an attack roll; the target saves after taking weapon damage if it needs a save). **Counts as a weapon attack for all Momentum purposes; a successful Spellstrike generates +1 Momentum (+2 on a critical hit).** Tenth class-specific Momentum-gain trigger in this cluster.

### Spell Combat (1st level)

Casting a non-damaging cantrip (*blade ward*, *guidance*) allows a bonus-action melee weapon attack — defend magically and attack martially in the same turn.

### Weapon Mastery (1st level)

Mastery with one weapon of choice (simple or martial); Weapon Skill usable once per turn on a hit. **Spellstrike Synergy**: a Weapon Skill and a delivered spell can be combined on the same hit.

### Fighting Style (2nd level)

Choose one: Archery, Agile Defence, Dueling, Great Weapon Fighting, Interception, Shield Master, or Two-Weapon Fighting — the same list as [[Fighter]]'s, minus Blind Fighting, Armoured Defence, and Unarmed Fighting.

### War Tradition (2nd level, and 7th, 11th, 15th)

Choose: **Consuming** (offence-focused, burns resources for max damage), **Distorting** (battlefield control, warps positions/sight lines), or **Protecting** (defensive magic as armour). Features at 2nd/7th/11th/15th — not detailed further in this source.

### Arcane Enhancement (3rd level)

Bonus action: imbue the weapon with elemental damage (fire, cold, or lightning), +1d6 for 1 minute — chosen to match enemy vulnerabilities in real time.

### Extra Attack (5th level)

Two attacks instead of one on the Attack action.

### Arcane Guard (5th level) — Damage Reduction clarification

Bonus action: spend 1 point from the Arcane Reservoir for **+1 DR** until the start of the Magus's next turn. This deep-dive gives a flat, single-point-spend DR value rather than the "scaling DR" framing on [[Damage Reduction]]'s existing DCS-summary-derived Magus entry — not a contradiction (nothing here rules out spending multiple points for more DR, just not stated explicitly), but the flat-DR framing is the more precise of the two and worth preferring.

### Arcane Deflection (6th level)

Reaction: on being attacked or forced to save, gain +2 to the Defence Roll against the attack, or advantage on the save. Cannot cast levelled spells on the following turn.

### Aether Step (7th level)

Bonus action: spend 1 spell slot to teleport up to 10 ft to a visible unoccupied space, no opportunity attacks provoked. Does not count as a levelled spell (relevant to Arcane Deflection's restriction above).

### Empowered Weapon (9th level)

Once per short rest, doubles Arcane Enhancement's elemental damage for 1 minute (1d6 → 2d6).

### Battlemind (10th level)

INT modifier added to initiative rolls.

### Heroic Boon (10th level)

Choose: **Boon of Arcane Harmony** (after casting a spell ≥1st level, bonus-action melee attack; if the spell was ≥3rd level, that attack gets a Momentum-bonus die = PB; Spellstrike adds INT-mod force damage) or **Boon of the Warding Aegis** (reaction: expend a spell slot when self/ally within 30 ft is hit, reduce damage by 2d6+slot level+INT mod, next attack deals force damage = damage prevented; while holding a spell slot, gain temp DR = PB against physical damage at the start of each turn).

### Arcane Momentum (11th level)

Casting a spell ≥1st level empowers melee attacks until the end of the Magus's next turn: +INT mod to weapon attack damage, +10 ft movement speed.

### Arcane Counterstrike (13th level)

Reaction: when a creature misses with a melee attack, cast a damaging cantrip against it — no attack roll, auto-hits if the attacker is within 5 ft.

### Spell Warfare (14th level)

Spell Combat no longer limited to cantrips — a levelled spell can be the action while a melee attack follows as a bonus action.

### Spellbreaker's Edge (15th level)

On a melee hit: **Disruptive Strike** (if the target is concentrating, disadvantage on its next Concentration check) and **Arcane Vulnerability** (if the target is affected by a Magus spell, +1d6 force damage until the Magus's next turn).

### Greater Spellstrike (17th level)

Spellstrike can now channel a levelled damaging spell of 1st or 2nd circle; the weapon's attack roll determines the hit, weapon damage adds to the spell's effects.

### Counterstrike (18th level)

Reaction: when a creature within 30 ft casts a spell, disrupt it with a melee attack — on a hit, the caster must succeed a Concentration check (DC = 10 + spell level) or the spell is disrupted.

### Epic Boon (20th level)

Choose: **Boon of Spellblade Mastery** (any spell ≤3rd level applies to a weapon attack's damaged creatures via Spell Strike; once/long rest, a weapon attack deals +5d10 force damage and imposes shaken until the Magus's next turn) or **Boon of Arcane Supremacy** (once/long rest, a spell ≤5th level deals max damage instead of rolling; once/turn, casting a spell as an action can be immediately followed by a melee attack dealing +2d10 arcane damage as part of the same action).

## Last Stand (confirmed universal, 11th class — standard permanent-death clause)

| Option | Effect |
|---|---|
| Arcane Finality | Channel a damaging spell ≤5th level through the weapon; auto-hits and crits all creatures in its area/chosen targets (up to 3 within melee range if no AoE); weapon attack +5d10 force damage plus a 1-minute burning mark (2d10/turn) |
| Blade of Continuum | For 5 minutes, every melee hit is an automatic critical + delayed damage (weapon dice + INT mod) next turn; enemies within 60 ft can't regain HP; on expiry, the blade shatters for 6d10 force damage in 30 ft |
| Arcane Aegis Unbroken | 40 ft Arcane Aegis, 1 minute: allies inside gain resistance to all damage + temp HP (4×Magus level) on first entry; enemy spells targeting the Magus or allies within 60 ft are reflected back |

Eleventh class confirming [[Last Stand]] as universal; all three options reaffirm the standard permanent-death clause (no exception — Bender remains the lone confirmed exception).

## Class Reactions (confirmed universal, 11th class — heaviest resource-layering yet)

Draws from the shared **Proficiency Bonus pool**, refreshing short/long rest — but every reaction here also layers a spell-slot cost on top, the heaviest resource-stacking of any class's reactions so far (beyond Bard's/Apothecary's Bardic-Inspiration/Reagent-Point layering, this spends the Magus's actual spellcasting resource).

| Reaction | Level | Trigger | Effect | Budget |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Arcane Deflection | 2nd | Hit by a weapon attack | Expend a spell slot: reduce damage by 1d8/slot level + INT mod; move 10 ft without provoking | 1 PB use **and** a spell slot |
| Arcane Riposte | 7th | A creature within 5 ft hits with a melee attack | Expend a spell slot: deal slot-level×d8 force damage to the attacker, guaranteed (no roll/save) | Spell slot (PB cost not explicitly restated, but implied by the pool intro) |
| Adaptive Mind | 11th | Hit by an attack | Reduce that attack's Momentum loss by 1 (min 0) | PB uses **per short rest** (its own cadence, distinct from the pool's short-or-long-rest refresh) |

**New Momentum interaction**: Adaptive Mind is the second confirmed Momentum-loss-mitigation reaction after [[Cleric]]'s Ward of Faith — but where Cleric's protects an *ally* within 30 ft, Adaptive Mind protects the Magus *themself*, the first self-directed version of this exact mechanic shape.
