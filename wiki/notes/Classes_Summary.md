# Classes_Summary

A definitive, structured 24-class roster — the first source in this cluster to present itself explicitly as a **correction**: "This replaces an earlier version of this summary, which was generated from an incomplete vault snapshot." That line is important: it confirms the underlying FEARS design material lives in some external vault/tool, and everything ingested into this wiki (across all six sources so far) is a periodic, sometimes-inconsistent snapshot of it — which is exactly why contradictions like the retired-classes list below exist.

## The 24-class roster

Full breakdown filed at [[FEARS Class Roster]]. Four tiers:

- **Full Casters (10)**: Wizard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Cleric, Druid, Bard, Witch, Sangromancer, Shaman, Bender
- **Half-Casters (6)**: Paladin, Ranger, Magus, Inventor, Tattooist, Warden
- **Martial (6)**: Barbarian, Fighter, Monk, Rogue, Reaper, Investigator
- **Specialized (2)**: Apothecary, Binder

## Contradiction: retired classes

**Explicitly cut from the roster, "should not appear in new content":** Shadowshaper, Runesmith, Psion, Tamer, Thaumaturge, Shadowbinder.

Five of these six have existing wiki pages, created from [[Welcome to FEARS]] (the very first source ingested into this cluster) as part of the "original 12 archetypes": [[Runesmith]], [[Psion]], [[Tamer]], [[Thaumaturge]], [[Shadowbinder]]. **Not deleted** — per this vault's contradiction-handling convention, each of those five pages now carries a `[!contradiction]` callout citing this source, and their frontmatter `status` has been changed to `retired`. Their content is preserved as a historical record of what [[Welcome to FEARS]] originally pitched. Shadowshaper was never separately documented in this wiki (no contradiction to flag — just noting it existed and was cut before any source here mentioned it).

## New class: Binder

Replaces a **Summoner** class this wiki never had a page for (implied to have existed pre-Binder, per "retires the former Summoner's Binding Points"). Binder "hosts fragments of the dead directly rather than summoning a separate creature," resource: **Bleed Points**. See [[Binder]].

## Relationship to Narrative Identity's proposed currencies

[[Narrative Identity]] proposed *narrative* currencies for several classes (Death Resonance for Reaper, Invention Heat for Inventor, Elemental Imbalance for Bender) that are distinct in name from the *mechanical* resources this source gives the same classes (Reaper: Soul Tokens; Inventor: Upgrades; Bender: combo sequencing on spell slots). **Not merged or reconciled** — flagged per-archetype-page as an open question: are these the same system under two names (one narrative-framing, one crunch-framing), or genuinely two different, coexisting systems?
