# Orynthos, The Wandering Tides

*"The sea does not stay where you left it. It never has. It never will."*

**Domains**: shifting tides, changing shorelines, the sea that moves without reason. **Symbol**: a wave crashing on a shore that no longer exists. **Worshiped by**: nomads, coastal settlers, travellers who move with the ocean. **Rivals**: [[Marethis]], Kaemor, Selmira. **Allies**: Rhyzel, Xaltheis, Syralis.

The reason maps of coastlines must always be redrawn — restless, never content. Some say he is the reason no one can find certain islands twice.
