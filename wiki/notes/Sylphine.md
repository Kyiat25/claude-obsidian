# Sylphine, The Whispering Wind

*"You do not know who I serve. You never will."*

**Domains**: secrets, lies, the wind that listens. **Symbol**: a single feather, carried on an unseen breeze. **Worshiped by**: spies, messengers, assassins. **Rivals**: [[Vaelora]] (Water's secrets), Nyxara (Air's illusions), [[The Whispering Dark]].

The god of whispers, of words never meant to be heard, of voices carried where they shouldn't go. Serves only herself — if she serves anyone at all.
