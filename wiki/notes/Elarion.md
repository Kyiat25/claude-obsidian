# Elarion — The Heart of Law and Loom

The most populous continent of Dór-o Estel (~40 million, 22 million km², 9th largest), occupying the southern-central, geographically and politically central position. Named from ancient Elven "El-Arel-Ion" ("The Land of Meeting Waters"), for the convergence of three great rivers at [[Kel Varyan]]. Home to 2 of the 5 [[Loom-Cities]] ([[Kel Varyan]] — Water, [[Veltharyn]] — Fire), 8 kingdoms, 6 confederations, and the 12-family Marshlands River-Keeper confederacy. Year 487 of the Fourth Age at time of writing, 18 days into [[The Threadbearer Crisis]].

Population is 45% Human, 20% Elf, 15% Half-Elf, 10% Half-Orc, 5% Dwarf, 5% other. Official language High Varyani, with Trade Common as lingua franca; state religion is the Faith of the Bound Pattern, with the five elemental pantheons and local ancestor/nature-spirit traditions practiced alongside it. One major World Tree (**Vaelthir**, anchoring [[Talanthar]]) and 9 lesser World Trees, several showing corruption — including **the Unspoken Root**, a whisper-tainted corrupted World Tree slowly spreading malign influence southward.

## The Marshlands (River-Keepers)

Twelve independent families (~1 million combined) control river passage and maintenance across Elarion's waterways via passage taxes (5-12% of cargo value, ~375K-3.3M silver annual revenue), wielding "nearly invisible" political influence despite being essential infrastructure. Largest: **Wavebreak** (~85K, northern navigation), **Deepwater** (~72K, poison-craft, prophecy-dependent), **Marshroot** (~78K, food/fishing), **Tideborn** (~70K, tide-reading). Smallest and most peaceable: **Willowmere** (~43K, mediation).

## The Ashcrown Khanate

A Cossack/Mongol-inspired orc border khanate (~45,000, Orcs 70%/Half-Orcs 20%) guarding the desolate Ashcrown Marches between Corvessa and the approaches to Vashkora's undead kingdoms — watch-fortresses every 30 miles, perpetually burning fire-lines maintained by fire-priests, and a no-man's-land "Grey Zone" before the cursed territories proper. They guard the **Corpse Road**, an ancient trade route now used mainly by undead envoys and necromantic traders, preventing unauthorized passage and containing outbreaks. Governed as a military democracy under an elected Khan-Council (current Khan: **Grazak Firehand**, veteran of forty battles) where every warrior has a voice and rank is earned through valor alone.

## Geography

Bordered by Vashkora (undead kingdoms, north, across the Greymarch), Kerythra (wild steppes, east, via the Dawnreach Expanse — the only major land bridge), and 2,400 miles of western coastline on the Veiled Ocean. The **Ivora River** (900 miles) is Corvessa's commercial lifeline. The **Three Sisters** — the Amethyst, Silver, and Merchant Rivers — converge at Kel Varyan's Delta of Convergence (3,000+ sq mi). The **Vaelrun** and **Thalaris** converge at Talanthar, carving the mile-wide Vaelthir Gorge where the World Tree Vaelthir stands.

## The Threadbearer Crisis, as seen from Elarion

Four of five Threadbearers were assassinated 18 days ago; only Naleth Spiralbound (Spirit Thread) survived, and one World Anchor inverted. Effects rippling across the continent: prophecy-dependent systems failing (hardest hit: Zhen-Lan's bureaucracy, the Deepwater/Moonlight Marshlands families), Kel Varyan's imperial authority shaken since prophetic guidance underpinned its legitimacy, Corvessa reportedly viewing the chaos as an opportunity to plan a "seventh crusade" against Talanthar, and rising insurance costs and refugee flows continent-wide. Veltharyn, isolated and self-sufficient, is the least affected major power.
