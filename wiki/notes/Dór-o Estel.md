# Dór-o Estel

The campaign setting used to demonstrate FEARS' social and mental-state systems in [[A World that Watches]]. Described only atmospherically so far — a world with Eldritch truths, glyph magic, cults, corrupted zones, and a functioning black-market/bounty-hunter underworld. Not named or referenced anywhere in [[Welcome to FEARS]] itself, so its relationship to the core FEARS system (default setting vs. one example setting among others) is not yet established — open question.
