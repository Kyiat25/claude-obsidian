# Dór-o Estel

The campaign setting used to demonstrate FEARS' social and mental-state systems in [[A World that Watches]]. Described only atmospherically at first — a world with Eldritch truths, glyph magic, cults, corrupted zones, and a functioning black-market/bounty-hunter underworld. Not named or referenced anywhere in [[Welcome to FEARS]] itself, so its relationship to the core FEARS system (default setting vs. one example setting among others) is not yet established — still an open question.

## Cosmology is setting-level, not ruleset-level

[[How the Five Layers Shape the Creation World]], [[Hidden Cosmology Thread System]], and [[Twins of Creation]] all elaborate directly on the "glyph magic" atmosphere first gestured at above — [[The Weave]], the [[Creation World]] (not yet ingested), the four Primal Forces, [[Threadbearers]], the [[Loom-Wardens]], and the Five Layers structure are all **Dór-o Estel worldbuilding, not FEARS mechanics**. This distinction matters: this cosmology would remain true if Dór-o Estel were run under a completely different ruleset than FEARS — it describes how the world and its magic came to exist, not how combat or character advancement work. Filed under FEARS MOC for now since that's the vault's only domain-level MOC and everything currently traces back to it, but conceptually this content belongs to the setting, not the rules layer. This domain is now sizeable enough (3 sources, 9 pages) that a dedicated Dór-o Estel hub page — separate from the mechanics-focused FEARS Advanced Backgrounds/class hubs — is worth considering soon, especially since a further ~11 uningested cosmology/Eldritch/Thread/World-Anchor sources remain in `.raw/`. Notably, three of these sources now actively **disagree with each other** on who in this cosmology knows what — see [[The Weave]] for the three-way comparison — which is worth preserving deliberately as in-universe unreliable mythology rather than "fixing" into one consistent account.
