# Valthir, The Firebrand

*"You do not understand. I do not destroy because I hate what stands. I destroy because nothing that stands now is worthy of what will come next."*

**Domains**: destruction, upheaval, the annihilation of the old order. **Symbol**: a burning banner, reduced to nothing but embers. **Worshiped by**: revolutionaries, warlords, those who seek to erase the past. **Allies**: [[Xilthos]], [[Xethar]]. **Enemies**: [[Soltharion]], [[Vaeldros]], [[Ignis]].

Doesn't fight for justice or liberation — fights because nothing that exists is worthy of what comes next; followers seek to tear down not just empires but ideas and traditions. Myth holds he set the first fire to the gods themselves, trying to erase the pantheon so something new could take its place. He failed, but hasn't stopped trying.
