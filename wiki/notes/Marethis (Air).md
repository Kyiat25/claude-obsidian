# Marethis, The Rain-Bearer

*"I bring the storm that gives, the flood that feeds. Pray that I do not come too soon, or too late."*

> [!note] Naming collision with the Water Pantheon's Marethis
> [[Water Pantheon]] already names a Greater God "Marethis, The River-Warden" (rivers, travel, guidance — see [[Marethis]]). `.raw/The Air Pantheon.md` separately names a demigod of seasonal monsoons under the identical name, with an unrelated domain and no cross-reference between the two source files. Documented here as a distinct entity, disambiguated as "Marethis (Air)" — not merged, since the two write-ups share nothing but a name.

**Domains**: monsoons, the rains that bring life, the floods that take it away. **Symbol**: a river overflowing beneath storm clouds. **Worshiped by**: farmers, villagers, rain-callers, flood-priests. **Allies**: [[Zeraphis]], Elythis. **Enemies**: Cyrnith, Khazir.

The giver of life and the bringer of floods — revered where rains are needed, feared where waters rise too high. Does not care for prayers, only balance.
