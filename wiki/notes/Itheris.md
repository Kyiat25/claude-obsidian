# Itheris, The Cleansing Wave

*"The water does not cleanse because it is told to. It simply does. That is its nature."*

**Domains**: healing, purity, sacred waters. **Symbol**: a single wave flowing into a pool. **Worshiped by**: healers, priests, those who seek to wash away sins or sickness. **Rivals**: [[Tzhor]], [[Vaelora]], Mordrath. **Allies**: [[Istriel]], Luthan, Astralis.

Heals not out of kindness but because purification is water's nature — it does not hold onto what it no longer needs. Distinct from [[Selteris]], whose cleansing is remorseless rather than gentle.
