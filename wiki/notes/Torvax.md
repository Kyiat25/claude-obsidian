# Torvax, The Weight of Ages

*"Stone does not mourn. Stone does not grieve. Stone simply waits. And so do I."*

**Domain**: mortality, the grave, the inevitable passing of all things. **Symbol**: a cracked tombstone, half-buried. **Worshiped by**: the dying, undertakers, those who accept their end. **Rivals**: [[Vel'Zyren]] (fate manipulation), [[Vorendral]] (the forgotten dead), those who seek immortality. **Allies**: [[Atherion]] (Weigher of Souls), [[Varlen]] (the Grave-Maker).

**Myth**: a king fled him, changed his name, rewrote his own history — Torvax found him anyway, and when he did, there was no grave left behind at all. To die without a name is to invite his silence; some believe that when the Lock breaks, he will be the last thing remaining.
