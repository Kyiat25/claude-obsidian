# Tovna, The Fold-Warden

**Domains**: herds and flocks, the herd-bond, the good dog, safe folding at dusk. **Symbol**: a crook laid across a gate.

Closes the pastoral gap other Earth deities left unclaimed: the bond between keeper and kept — the ewe that knows a voice, the dog that counts without numbers. Shepherds pour the first milk of spring at the fold-gate. A herd-animal killed by its own keeper's neglect is confessed *to the flock*, aloud — reportedly considerably worse than confessing to a priest.
