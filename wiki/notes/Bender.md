# Bender — Element Made Motion

A FEARS-specific class not mentioned in [[Welcome to FEARS]]; first appeared in [[Narrative Identity]] and confirmed active by [[Classes_Summary]]. **Full Caster tier**. "Everyone else channels the elements. The Bender *is* the motion." Complexity self-rated HIGH (8/10) by its own per-class deep-dive — recommended for experienced players who enjoy multi-layered action sequencing.

## Signature abilities (from Classes_Summary / Class_breakdown)

- **Elemental Strikes**
- **Combo System**
- **Disciplines**

**Gameplay style**: combo-based striker, building and releasing power in sequences; rewards sequential thinking (strike, then combo follow-up, or spell to set up a physical follow-up). **Core loop**: act → build → release → repeat. Currency named in [[Narrative Identity]]: **Elemental Imbalance**, "gained from over-leaning into one element" — not defined in detail there, and not mentioned at all by the per-class deep-dive below (silent, not contradicted).

## Class basics (from Bender.md deep-dive)

- **Hit Dice**: 1d8/level. HP at 1st: 8 + CON mod. HP at higher levels: 1d8 (or 5) + CON mod/level after 1st.
- **Proficiencies**: Armour — light. Weapons — simple, chakrams, nunchaku, shortswords, shuriken. Tools — one artisan's tools or musical instrument. Saves — Strength, Constitution. Skills — choose two from Acrobatics, Athletics, History, Insight, Religion, Stealth.
- **Starting Equipment**: (a) quarterstaff, (b) chakram, or (c) any simple weapon; (a) dungeoneer's pack or (b) explorer's pack; leather armour and 10 shuriken.
- **Quick Build**: chosen spellcasting ability (INT/WIS/CHA) highest, then Dexterity. Any lineage works (Human, Tiefling, Wood Elf thematic). Hermit/Outlander or an Acrobatics/History background. Open with an Elemental Strike to build Momentum, follow with Elemental Combo as a bonus action, cast a spell if available — hit, combo, spell, hit.

## Core class features (full text)

### Spellcasting (1st level) — second confirmed caster DC formula

Choose spellcasting ability at 1st level: Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma. **Spell Save DC = 8 + PB + spellcasting ability modifier. Spell Attack Modifier = PB + spellcasting ability modifier.** Exactly matches [[Bard]]'s formula — the second independent confirmation of this shape in the cluster, strengthening it as the likely universal caster formula. Prepares spells = spellcasting mod + half Bender level (rounded down, min 1) from the Elemental Affinity list. Regains all slots on a long rest. **Concentration uses Focus checks instead of Constitution** — a new, not-yet-fully-reconciled connection to [[Focus]]; not stated to be the same thing as the still-undefined "Focus Save" from [[Four Threads of the Mind]], so not assumed identical.

### Elemental Affinity (1st, 6th, 10th, 14th level)

Choose an element at 1st: **Air** (thunder), **Earth** (acid/bludgeoning), **Fire** (fire/lightning), or **Water** (cold) — adds that element's spell list. At 6th/10th/14th, choose again: a **new element** adds its list plus a free bonus cantrip, or a **repeated element** casts that element's spells one level higher per repeat (stacks — 4 affinities in one element casts 3 levels higher than the slot used; example given: a 3rd-level slot casts as 5th-level with 3 Fire affinities).

### Elemental Strikes (1st level)

Attack action option: melee or ranged spell attacks dealing the affinity's damage type. Melee: unarmed, 10 ft reach, 1d6 + spellcasting mod. Ranged: 60 ft, 1d4 + spellcasting mod. Generates Momentum normally on a hit, same as any weapon attack.

### Elemental Combo (2nd level)

After a weapon attack, elemental strike, or levelled spell cast: bonus action, melee (10 ft) or ranged (60 ft) spell attack for 1d4 elemental damage (+spellcasting mod if within 10 ft). Scales to 2d4 at 6th, 3d4 at 10th, 4d4 at 14th.

### Class Reactions (2nd level) — confirmed universal, 4th class

A pool of **Reaction Points** = Proficiency Bonus per short or long rest — the same shared-pool structure as [[Barbarian]]/[[Bard]]/[[Apothecary]], confirming [[Class Reactions]] a fourth time. Full reaction list below.

### Bender Discipline (3rd level, and 7th, 15th, 20th) — fullest subclass detail seen so far

Choose one, with features at 3rd/7th/15th/20th — the first class in this cluster to receive full subclass mechanical detail rather than an incomplete "such as" example list:

- **Disciple of Ferocity** (Offense): proficiency with medium armour, shields, martial weapons; use spellcasting ability for weapon attack/damage rolls; discipline list includes *magic missile*, *magic weapon*, *blink*, *guardian of faith*, *arcane hand*. 7th: once/turn, expend a spell slot on a hit for 2d8 + 1d8/slot level bonus damage (max 6d8), ignores resistance. 15th: Elemental Combo damage → 5d4, can hit two targets. 20th: three attacks instead of two.
- **Disciple of Fortification** (Defense): proficiency with medium/heavy armour, shields; discipline list includes *shield*, *aid*, *protection from energy*, *resilient sphere*, *wall of force*. 3rd: bonus-action Primordial Shield grants 1d6 temp HP to self or an ally within 30 ft (scales with level). 7th: reaction, reduce elemental damage to a creature within 30 ft by 2d6 per affinity in that element. 15th: casting a Bender spell grants a creature +AC equal to the spell's level until the Bender's next turn.
- **Disciple of Fusion** (Multi-element): two extra cantrips + one spell from any element list; expending a slot to cast a Bender spell can augment it with a second element for an additional area effect (5×slot-level ft) — Air+Earth blinds with dust, Air+Fire obscures/silences, Air+Water creates icy difficult terrain, Earth+Fire deals 1d6/slot lava fire, Earth+Water slows with mud, Fire+Water obscures/damages with steam. Uses = spellcasting mod/short rest. 7th: change a Bender spell's damage type between affinities. 15th: casting a spell on your action also casts a cantrip as a bonus action (spellcasting-mod uses/long rest).
- **Disciple of Invigoration** (Support): discipline list includes *bless*, *healing word*, *mass healing word*, *aura of vitality*, *mass cure wounds*. 3rd: once/turn on an elemental strike, an ally within 30 ft may reaction-move half speed without provoking. 7th: once/turn, casting a Bender spell on an ally also removes a condition (blinded/charmed/deafened/frightened/poisoned/stunned), adds 1d6 to their next roll, grants a reaction attack, or grants reaction movement. 15th: entering Primordial Avatar grants up to 5 willing allies within 30 ft a level-1 benefit from an affinity element.

### Elemental Armor (3rd level)

Choose one affinity element: DR 1 against its damage types, scaling to DR 2 at 10th and DR 3 at 17th. Changeable on a long rest. Multiple affinities still only protect via one chosen element at a time.

### Extra Attack (5th level)

Two attacks on the Attack action; either or both may be elemental strikes.

### Elemental Flow (5th level) — two new confirmed Momentum gain sources

**Spellcasting Momentum**: casting a damage/condition Bender spell of 1st level+ grants +1 Momentum. **Strike Momentum**: hitting with an elemental strike as part of the Attack action grants +1 Momentum, once per turn. Both are new [[Momentum]] gain sources not previously listed.

### Reactive Surge (6th level, reaction)

Trigger: hit by an attack. 2 Reaction Points: attacker DEX saves (DC = Bender's spell save DC) or takes 2d6 elemental damage (half on success), scaling to 3d6 at 11th and 4d6 at 17th.

### Heroic Boon (10th level)

Choose: **Elemental Mastery** (one affinity element: resistant creatures take full damage, immune creatures take half, ignore special immunities to that element's effects), **Primal Resilience** (Primordial Form grants temp HP = 3×Bender level, advantage on CON saves to maintain concentration while active), or **Flowing Combat** (once/short rest, immediately after Elemental Combo, make one additional elemental strike against a different target as part of the same bonus action).

### Primordial Form (11th level) — new Sanity/Trauma sighting

Bonus action, embody a chosen affinity element for 1 minute (or until unconscious): cast that element's 1st-level spells without slots (2nd-level or lower at 17th). Elemental benefit: **Air** (resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning/piercing/slashing), **Earth** (+2 AC), **Fire** (end-of-turn fire damage = spellcasting mod to chosen creatures within 10 ft), **Water** (a 5×Bender-level vitality pool; up to 3 creatures within 10 ft regain 5 HP each from it per turn). Once per long rest.

**Sanity Integration**: activating requires a Sanity check (DC 12); failure grants 1 **Trauma Point** with the "Elemental Obsession" trauma type — the first sighting of "Trauma Point" as a mechanic anywhere in this wiki, undefined beyond this one trigger. Not assumed to be the same thing as [[Corruption]] or the still-unresolved [[Fracture Save]] — flagged as new, unconfirmed terminology pending a future source.

### Reactive Casting (11th level, reaction)

Trigger: hit by an attack. 3 Reaction Points: cast a Bender cantrip (1-action casting time, must target or include the attacker) as part of the reaction.

### Primordial Avatar (18th level) — new Corruption-gain sighting

Action: become a primordial incarnation. 50 temp HP plus stacking benefits per Elemental Affinity (below), lasting 10 minutes or until unconscious. Once per long rest.

**Corruption Integration**: activating grants 1 **Corruption Point** — the fourth class (after [[Apothecary]]'s four triggers) to give a mechanical Corruption-gain trigger. Uses "Corruption Point" where [[Corruption]]'s formalized term is "Corruption Score (CS)" — treated as terminology drift, not a distinct mechanic, consistent with how other point/score naming variance has been handled in this cluster (e.g. Focus/Technique Point).

**Stacking benefits by element** (one benefit tier per affinity count in that element, cumulative):

### Epic Boon (20th level)

Choose: **Boon of Elemental Apotheosis** (Primordial Avatar becomes indefinite once activated, still once/long rest; while active, activate Primordial Form as a bonus action without its charge; one element becomes permanently damage-immune), **Boon of Worldshaper** (once/long rest, reshape terrain in a 100 ft radius — walls, part water, raise/flatten earth, persistent winds; lasts 1 hour, physical not magical; Bender terrain spells extend to 24-hour duration if shorter), or **Boon of Primordial Immortality** (stop aging, no food/water/air needed; once/long rest, dropping to 0 HP triggers a free Primordial Form activation and heals 10×Bender level; once/long rest cast *magnificent mansion* free, manifesting as the chosen elemental material).

## Weapon Mastery hook — silent, not contradicted

[[Weapon Mastery System]] states Bender shares [[Monk]]'s **Flow Strike** and Technique Point resource exactly (move 5 ft on hit without provoking; regain 1 Technique Point at +3 Momentum). This per-class deep-dive never mentions Weapon Mastery, Flow Strike, or Technique Points at all — its full resource suite is spell slots, Reaction Points, and Primordial Form uses. Treated as an out-of-scope omission (this document doesn't cover the Weapon Mastery subsystem for any class), not a contradiction — silence isn't the same as conflicting evidence. Worth re-checking if a source ever directly addresses both systems together.

## Last Stand (confirmed universal, 4th class — with a genuine exception)

| Option | Effect | Best use |
|---|---|---|
| Elemental Dissolution | Choose an affinity element; body dissolves into a 60 ft radius zone for 1 minute with element-specific battlefield effects (Air: 60mph winds, ranged/STR disadvantage, forced movement; Earth: difficult terrain, 2d10 bludgeoning/turn, ranged disadvantage; Fire: 4d10 fire/turn, obscuring smoke, exhaustion risk; Water: deep water, half speed, forced pull+prone) | Controlling the battlefield |
| Primordial Sacrifice | A willing ally within 60 ft gains (10 min): full 18th-level Primordial Avatar state with the Bender's element benefits, temp HP = 2×Bender level, free casting of the Bender's prepared spells (Bender's DC/attack mod), +2d8 elemental damage on their strikes. After 10 minutes, the ally may permanently accept the transformation for **3 Corruption Points**, or revert | Empowering a key ally |
| Elemental Rebirth | Transform into an Air/Earth/Fire/Water Elemental (Large), retaining mental scores/PB/non-humanoid-incompatible features, for 24 hours; reforms as the character at 1 HP at the death location afterward, **reverted to 1st level**. **Usable only once per character's lifetime** | Avoiding permanent death, continuing the fight — at the cost of starting over |

> [!note] Ruled: intentional exception, not an error
> [[Last Stand]]'s confirmed rule across [[Barbarian]]/[[Bard]]/[[Apothecary]] is that the character "permanently dies... cannot be returned by any means" once resolved. Elemental Rebirth explicitly breaks this — the character survives (reforms at 1 HP after 24 hours) — but the flavor justifies it (the character effectively dies and is reborn as something new) and the level-1 reversion functions as a soft-retirement lever: the option exists as an alternative to the death clause, but the mechanical cost is severe enough that most players will choose permanent death or genuine retirement over restarting the character from scratch. Confirmed as the sole intentional exception to the permanent-death clause, not a documentation gap.

## Class Reactions (confirmed universal, 4th class)

- **Elemental Counter** (2nd, 1 RP) — trigger: a creature within 30 ft makes an attack roll against the Bender; attacker has disadvantage on that roll.
- **Primordial Deflection** (2nd, 2 RP) — trigger: the Bender or an ally within 30 ft would take damage of an affinity type; reduce it by 2d8 + spellcasting mod.
- **Reactive Surge** (6th, 2 RP) — see Core class features above.
- **Reactive Casting** (11th, 3 RP) — see Core class features above.
