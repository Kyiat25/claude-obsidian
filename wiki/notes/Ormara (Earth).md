# Ormara, The Crystal Sage

> [!note] Naming collision with Water Pantheon's Ormara
> [[Water Pantheon]] separately names an "Ormara, The Frozen Tear," a spirit of mourning and ice — see [[Ormara]]. Both are documented as they appear in their respective source files — not treated as the same entity, since domains and myths are unrelated. Disambiguated here as "Ormara (Earth)."

*"Memory is not held in minds. It is held in stone. And crystal does not forget."*

**Domain**: memory, crystal archives, eternal thought. **Symbol**: a six-pointed crystal set into basalt. **Worshiped by**: lorekeepers, archivists, crystalline diviners, dwarven librarians.

Preserves what would otherwise be lost, not with ink but in lattice and quartz — her crystals are said to hum with voices of the past. Myth: when a god tried to erase a war from the world's memory, Ormara carved it into a diamond so pure not even [[Thamur]] could bury it. Close to [[Durnath]]; silent rival of [[Aelys]] (Spirit's Weave of fate). **Aspect of the Lock**: she remembers the truth beneath the silence — a backup seal, etched in mineral code.
