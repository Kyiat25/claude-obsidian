# Rymora, The Abyssal Chanter

*"You heard it, didn't you? The song beneath the waves. The voice calling your name. You thought it was someone you knew. You were wrong."*

**Domains**: oceanic echoes, siren songs, voices beneath the waves. **Symbol**: a spiraling wave with a single open mouth at its center. **Worshiped by**: deep-sea divers, sailors, mystics who seek knowledge from Water. **Rivals**: Eryndal, Vaedoris, Luthiris. **Allies**: [[Varashti]], [[Lyrithis]], Nyssara.

Neither kind nor cruel — she simply sings, and those who listen decide whether to follow. Some say she was once a mortal singer lost at sea, whose voice now belongs to the ocean itself. Also named in `.raw/Pantheon_Perspectives_on_Godhood.md` as one of Water's figures on the nature of godhood — see [[Water Pantheon]] for that cross-reference.
