# Beastmaster — Rank 1-4

*"You train, understand, and bond with animals and magical creatures."* Batch 3, Druidic/Nature category.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Animal Handler | Animal Affinity | Successfully train a difficult animal, break a wild mount, or bond with an unusual creature |
| 2 | Professional Trainer | Expert Trainer | Train a rare or magical beast, supply the military with war animals, or bond with a legendary creature |
| 3 | Master Beastmaster | Beast Bond | Bond with a monster, establish a legendary breeding line, or command a creature others thought untrainable |
| 4 | Legendary Beast Lord | Master of Beasts | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Animal Handler

**Skill Proficiency**: Animal Handling, Nature. **Tool Proficiency**: leatherworker's tools. **Equipment**: leatherworker's tools, 1 week of animal feed, 50 ft of rope, a whip, traveler's clothes, 10 gp.

**Feature — Animal Affinity**: advantage on Animal Handling checks; calm agitated animals (10 minutes, DC 12 Animal Handling); know how to care for common animals and beasts; identify animal tracks and signs; animals are less likely to attack you.

**Holdings**: a small stable or kennel; 2-3 trained animals (dogs, horses, or similar); basic training equipment; customers seeking trained animals.

## Rank 2: Professional Trainer

**Holdings**: a proper stable or menagerie; income from training and sales (30 gp/month); 5-8 animals in various training stages; training grounds; a reputation for quality animals; a network of breeders and buyers.

**Feature Expansion — Expert Trainer**: train animals in half normal time; train animals for complex tasks (guard, scout, perform, etc.); your animals are better trained (advantage on commanded actions); identify quality and temperament instantly; once per week, find a buyer for a trained animal at 150% value.

## Rank 3: Master Beastmaster

**Holdings**: a large menagerie or beast ranch; major income (90 gp/month); a collection of 15-20 trained animals including rare breeds; 3-5 apprentice handlers; contracts with wealthy clients; access to exotic animals through contacts; a breeding program for quality lines.

**Feature Expansion — Beast Bond**: train magical beasts, given time and DM approval; your animals are exceptionally loyal (advantage on saves against being turned against you); once per week, communicate complex ideas to an animal (genuine understanding, not just *speak with animals*); sense distress in any animal you've trained within 1 mile; train creatures others consider untrainable.

## Rank 4: Legendary Beast Lord

**Holdings**: a vast menagerie with creatures from across the world; excellent income (250 gp/month); a staff of 15+ handlers; breeding programs for multiple legendary lines; royal contracts for war mounts and exotic pets; your trained animals command premium prices; magical creatures reportedly seek you out.

**Feature — Master of Beasts**: cast *speak with animals* at will; train any non-sentient beast or monstrosity, DM approval, possibly taking months; once per month, summon wild animals to aid you (functions as *conjure animals* using real local creatures); limited telepathy with your bonded animals (100 ft); sense the presence and emotions of all beasts within 1 mile. Nobles seek your animals as status symbols, military commanders consult you on animal warfare, druids respect you as kin to nature, and you may have bonded with a creature of legend.
