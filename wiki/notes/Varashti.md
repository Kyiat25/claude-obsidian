# Varashti, The Fathomless Whisper

*"It is waiting for you. Everything you have ever wanted. You need only reach out and take it."*

**Domains**: oceanic temptation, forbidden knowledge, the sirens of the deep. **Symbol**: a hand reaching out of the waves, open and inviting. **Worshiped by**: treasure hunters, dreamers, those who seek what was never meant to be found. **Rivals**: [[Vaelora]], Eryndal, Orynthos. **Allies**: [[Rymora]], [[Lyrithis]], Syralis.

Never forces — only tempts. The voice that promises more, the reason sailors dive too deep and explorers seek islands no map records. Ensures those who seek too much are given exactly what they asked for.
