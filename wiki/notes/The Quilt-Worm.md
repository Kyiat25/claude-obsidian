# The Quilt-Worm

**Origin**: a dream-binding quilt woven with Spirit Glyphs that unraveled. **Appearance**: a long, thread-wrapped larva of memory strands with sideways-blinking eyes. **Effect**: causes dream bleed, where multiple people's dreams merge. **Danger**: feeds on inconsistencies — the more it's questioned, the more real it becomes.

A **Dream-Born** spirit — per [[Eldritch Spirits]]'s classification, a nightmare that has breached into waking life.
