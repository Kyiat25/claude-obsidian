# Drow

*"They think they buried us. They gave us a kingdom."* — [[Vel'Zyren]], The Web-Spinner

**Origin myth — The Descent into Truth**: per the Doctrine of the Descent, Drow were surface elves who [[Vel'Zyren]] called below when the surface gods offered only silence — not punishment, but *purification*, shedding the "weakness of mercy" for predators perfected in darkness. Generations of transformation are literal biology, not mere culture: eyes for absolute dark, ashen skin, shadow-carrying blood. This is the standalone, fuller treatment of Drow — [[Elf (Lineage)]] also lists Drow as a brief subrace entry; treat this page as primary. See [[The Fall of the Drow]] for the historical conversion narrative this lineage's theology assumes.

**Physical**: mature by 20, "adult" at 100 (few die of old age — most die to intrigue/assassination/battle); Medium, 5–6 ft., 30 ft. speed; obsidian/ash-gray/purple-black skin, white/silver/pale-blonde hair, glowing eyes.

## Traits

- **Superior Darkvision** 120 ft.
- **Sunlight Sensitivity**: disadvantage on attacks/Perception in direct sunlight
- **Keen Senses**: Perception proficiency
- **Fey Ancestry**: advantage vs. charm, immune to magical sleep
- **Trance**: 4-hr meditation = long rest — but spent *planning ascension*, not reliving ancestral memory (a deliberate divergence from surface-elf Trance)
- **Shadow Step**: bonus action, teleport 30 ft. into dim light/darkness, uses = proficiency bonus/long rest
- **Drow Magic**: *dancing lights* → *faerie fire* (3rd) → *darkness* (5th), CHA
- **Drow Weapon Training**: rapier, shortsword, hand crossbow
- **Languages**: Common + Elvish + Undercommon

**Mental**: Focus +0 / Sanity +0 — resilience is cultural (control/obedience/ambition training), not biological.

**DCS Integration**: Dodge +1 / Endure −1 / Deflect +0; DR +0 — avoidance-based, wins by "not fighting fairly." Advantage on Rally under clear authority/position; disadvantage fighting for ideals without personal gain.

## Corruption: The Cracked Mirror

Advantage vs. Corruption from chaos/disorder sources; **disadvantage** vs. mirrors, reflections, and divine judgment — the same pathway to [[Vel'Zyren]] becomes a corruption conduit. At Corruption 10+: Disturbed Trance, Mirror Distortion, Pattern Strain, Hierarchy Confusion. **Disturbed Reflection table** (1d6): False Perfection, Web-Strain (disadvantage vs. divine magic), Watched and Judged, Reflected Truth (Sanity save or −1d4), Shadow Communion (+1 Whisper Point, learn forbidden knowledge), or Web's Grace (Corruption −1). At Tier 3+, corrupted Drow may be hunted by their own people.
