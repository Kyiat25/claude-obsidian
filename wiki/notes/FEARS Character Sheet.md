# FEARS Character Sheet

The physical/digital tracking tool [[FEARS Character Framework]] describes — "a complete reimagining of how you engage with your character." Organizes seven new systems into three groups:

| Mental | Combat | Narrative |
|---|---|---|
| [[Sanity]], [[Focus]], [[Corruption]] Score | Evasion, Damage Reduction, [[Called Shot]] | [[Whisper Points]], [[Luck]], [[Memory]]/[[Occult]]/[[Streetwise]] |

## What you need at the table

- A mental tracker (Sanity, Focus, Corruption) — see [[Mental State]]
- A reaction reminder (mark when evasion is forgone for a free reaction)
- A body map for Damage Reduction (head, torso, arms, legs)
- A Whisper Point pool tracker
- Per the source: "a willingness to let your character fail, fracture, and evolve"
