# Astralis, The Silent Void

*"Not all things should be known. Not all memories should remain. The stars are not just light—they are silence."*

**Domains**: the empty spaces, forgotten knowledge, the keeper of lost things. **Symbol**: a black sphere with silver threads breaking away. **Worshiped by**: those who seek to forget, those who fear knowledge's burden. **Rivals/allies vary by source**: linked to Xelthar and Zeythis; opposed to [[Nyzara]].

The counterbalance to [[Nyzara]] — where she remembers, he erases. His domain is the forgotten, the broken, the knowledge that fades; some see him as cruel, others merciful, but all agree his silence is final. Worshiped not to learn, but to forget — invoked when a name must be erased forever.
