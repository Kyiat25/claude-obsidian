# Goliath

*"We were not shaped by gods. We were shaped by the climb."*

**Origin myth**: per [[The Path of the Horizon Soul]], Goliaths weren't created but born from memory of ascent — Valkaan the Wind-Shouldered simply stood up from stone and began walking toward an unseen summit, followed by Orenya, Koraak, and Uldra. When they reached where even wind could not breathe, the sky broke and scattered stars became the Horizon-Soul; Goliaths descend from those echoes. The soul is a horizon — always ahead, never reached.

**Physical**: mature by 18, live ~80 years; Medium (barely — 7–8 ft.), 30 ft. speed, mottled gray-white skin.

## Traits

- **Natural Athlete**: Athletics proficiency
- **Stone's Endurance**: reaction, roll d12 + CON mod to reduce incoming damage, uses = proficiency bonus/long rest
- **Powerful Build**: counts one size larger for carrying capacity
- **Mountain Born**: cold resistance, altitude-acclimated, ignores ice/snow difficult terrain
- **Little Giant**: extra skill choice; Stone's Endurance can also end a frightened/charmed condition
- **Horizon-Sense**: 1/long rest, 10-min meditation for perfect direction/distance to a known location
- **Languages**: Common + Giant

**Mental**: Focus +1 / Sanity +0 (hardship processed as necessary, not traumatic).

**DCS Integration**: Dodge +1 / Endure +1 / Deflect +0; DR +0 (protection via reactive Stone's Endurance, not passive). Advantage on personal Rally to prove worth; standard for unit cohesion (may break formation to pursue individual glory).

## Corruption: The False Summit

Corruption enters through accepting shortcuts, abandoning the ascent, or worshipping power without effort. At Corruption 10+: Directional Confusion, Summit-Sickness (standing still causes discomfort), Echo-Loss. **Fractured Path table** (1d6): False Height, Wind-Deafness (Stone's Endurance disabled), Stone-Rejection (1d4 psychic + lose Athletics), Weight of Stagnation (Sanity save or −1d4), Eldritch Shortcut (+1 Whisper Point, Stone's Endurance auto-fails), or Path Holds (Corruption −1).
