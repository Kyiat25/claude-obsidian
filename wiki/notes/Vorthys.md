# Vorthys, The Abyssal Maw

*"The sea does not need a storm to take you. Sometimes, it only needs an opening."*

**Domains**: whirlpools, oceanic traps, vanishing ships. **Symbol**: a spiral vortex pulling a ship downward. **Worshiped by**: feared by all, truly known by none. **Rivals**: Theroneth, [[Kaemor]], [[Nereus]]. **Allies**: Nyxthys, [[Jorvethis]], Morgathis.

Does not rage, scream, or warn — he is simply the opening beneath the waves, the place from which nothing returns. Unlike Zorveth (fury) or Othraxis (precision), Vorthys simply takes, and never gives back.
