# Zeythis, The Fractured Glyph

*"The Weave is not perfect. It has cracks. I am the voice within them."*

**Domains**: magic's flaws, the Weave's imperfections, lost glyphs. **Symbol**: a scroll, torn apart in a thousand places. **Worshiped by**: forbidden spellcasters, sorcerers who practice unstable magic. **Allies**: [[Xelthar]], Nytheris, [[Vel'Zyren]]. **Enemies**: [[Aelys]], [[Vaithis]], [[Astria]].

The force of magic's instability — the spells that defy structure, the lost fragments of the Weave. Worshiped by those who wish to push magic beyond its limits and feared by those who know the Weave cannot be fully controlled. His followers don't seek balance — they seek power beyond the gods' control.
