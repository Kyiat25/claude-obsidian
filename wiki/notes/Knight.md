# Knight — Rank 1-4 (Warrior of Honor)

*"You're bound by a code of honor, serving a lord or ideal."* Tier 2 (Specialized). Fulfills the "Knight" slot [[FEARS Advanced Backgrounds]] flagged as not-yet-developed after Batch 1.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Squire | Knight's Service | Distinguish yourself in battle, save your knight, or complete a quest that proves your worth |
| 2 | Knight | Code of Honor | Win a tournament, defend the innocent from a great threat, or perform a legendary deed |
| 3 | Knight Commander | Knight's Banner | Defend your realm from invasion, become champion of your order, or establish a new chapter of knights |
| 4 | Grand Master/Knight Exemplar | Perfect Knight | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Squire

**Skill Proficiency**: Athletics, Persuasion. **Tool Proficiency**: one gaming set. **Language**: one of choice. **Equipment**: a banner bearing your knight's sigil, fine clothes, a signet ring, 25 gp.

**Feature — Knight's Service**: invoke your knight's name for authority; learn noble customs and etiquette; train in combat and chivalry; protected by your knight's status.

**Knightly Order** (choose or roll d6): Crown knights (serve the monarch), Religious order (serve church/god), Tournament knights (focus on glory), Border knights (defend frontiers), Merchant knights (protect trade), an Order of a specific ideal (justice, mercy, etc.).

**Holdings**: quarters in your knight's household; training in arms and armor; probably a warhorse; basic equipment.

## Rank 2: Knight

**Holdings**: your own heraldry and coat of arms; income from lands or a stipend (35 gp/month); a warhorse and quality equipment; a small manor house or quarters in a keep; a squire of your own; authority to speak for your order.

**Feature Expansion — Code of Honor**: advantage on saves against being frightened; common folk provide aid and shelter; you can challenge others to honorable combat; your word is believed (oath-bound to truth); +1 AC when fighting for your oath's ideals.

## Rank 3: Knight Commander

**Holdings**: an estate with lands generating income (80 gp/month); command of 5-10 knights; a garrison of men-at-arms (20-30 soldiers); a seat on the order's council; fine armor and weapons, possibly magical; authority to knight others; a reputation across the realm.

**Feature Expansion — Knight's Banner**: allies within 30 ft gain advantage on saves against being frightened; once per day, Rally allies as a bonus action (allies within 60 ft gain temp HP equal to your level + CHA mod); your challenges must be answered by other knights; you can organize tournaments and melees.

## Rank 4: Grand Master/Knight Exemplar

**Holdings**: a castle or fortress headquarters; leadership of the entire knightly order (50+ knights); major income from lands (250 gp/month); an army of men-at-arms and soldiers (200+); political influence equal to nobility; legendary armor and weapons; your banner is recognized everywhere.

**Feature — Perfect Knight**: immune to being frightened; Rally now affects up to 100 allies; once per month, call your order to arms for a crusade or great quest; rulers seek you as champion; your judgment in matters of honor is final. Songs are sung of your deeds, squires from across the land seek to serve you, your order grows or declines based on your example, and upon death you may be canonized or your armor becomes a relic.
