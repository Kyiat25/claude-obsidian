# Herbalist — Rank 1-4

*"You're an expert in medicinal and magical plants."* Batch 3, Druidic/Nature category. Overlaps thematically with [[Hedge Witch]] (also plant/remedy-focused) but stays entirely mundane — no cantrips, no Druid spellcasting, no folk-magic framing anywhere in this background's text, unlike Hedge Witch's eventual full Druid spellcasting at Rank 4.

## Rank progression

| Rank | Title | Feature | Progression trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Plant Gatherer | Herb Lore | Find a rare herb, create a life-saving remedy, or successfully cultivate a difficult plant |
| 2 | Skilled Herbalist | Master Healer | Discover a new medicinal application, cure a rare disease with herbs, or cultivate a legendary plant |
| 3 | Master Herbalist | Botanical Mastery | Cultivate a legendary plant, write the definitive herbal, or cure an incurable disease |
| 4 | Arch-Herbalist | Green Wisdom | — (capstone) |

## Rank 1: Plant Gatherer

**Skill Proficiency**: Nature, Medicine. **Tool Proficiency**: herbalism kit. **Equipment**: herbalism kit, a book of herb lore, a collecting bag, a small shovel, common clothes, 10 gp.

**Feature — Herb Lore**: identify common herbs and their medicinal uses; advantage on Nature checks related to plants; forage for herbs effectively (double useful plants found); know which plants are poisonous or dangerous; create basic remedies (healing poultices, fever reducers, etc.).

**Holdings**: a small garden or growing space; a collection of common dried/preserved herbs; basic drying and preparation equipment; local customers who buy herbs.

## Rank 2: Skilled Herbalist

**Holdings**: a proper herb garden; income from remedies and teas (20 gp/month); a drying shed and workshop; an extensive herb collection (common and uncommon); a regular healing practice; a network of 5-8 other herbalists and foragers.

**Feature Expansion — Master Healer**: your herbalism kit can create potions of healing (50 gp materials, 1 day); treat diseases with herbal remedies (advantage on Medicine checks); identify any plant and its properties instantly; know rare herb locations in your region; once per week, create a special remedy curing one specific condition, DM approval.

## Rank 3: Master Herbalist

**Holdings**: a botanical garden with rare specimens; major income (80 gp/month); a greenhouse and laboratory; a very-rare-herb collection; 3-5 apprentices; clients including nobles and wealthy merchants; published herbal texts; contacts with druids and rangers who supply rare plants.

**Feature Expansion — Botanical Mastery**: create greater potions of healing (100 gp materials, 3 days); create antidotes to any natural poison (1 day, appropriate herbs required); cultivate magical plants at DM discretion; once per month, create a remedy curing what magical healing cannot; identify plant-based components in any mixture.

## Rank 4: Arch-Herbalist

**Holdings**: a botanical institute or vast garden; excellent income (200 gp/month); a collection of every known herb, and some unknown ones; research facilities; 20+ students and assistants; your texts are standard references; you supply healers across the continent.

**Feature — Green Wisdom**: cast *speak with plants* at will; create supreme potions of healing (200 gp materials, 1 week); once per month, create an herbal preparation mimicking a spell effect up to 5th level, DM approval; command plants to grow or wither; know the location of any specific plant within 10 miles. Healers study under you, your garden contains plants found nowhere else, you can identify and neutralize any plant-based poison instantly, and you may have discovered a plant granting long life.
