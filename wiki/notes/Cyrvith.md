# Cyrvith, The Unyielding Ember

*"They tried to snuff me out. They failed. They always fail."*

**Domains**: endurance, survival, the last stand. **Symbol**: a single ember, still glowing in a pile of ash. **Worshiped by**: survivors, those who refuse to give up, warriors who fight to their last breath.

The last ember that refuses to go out — the will to survive when all hope is lost, felt in those who should have fallen but still rise. Myth holds he was the last spark left after [[Xalros]] burned the First World to the ground; he should have died, but didn't, and never will.
