# Nytheris, The Fate-Tearer

*"Your fate is a lie. You will not die as it has been written. You will not rule as it was foretold. I will tear your prophecy apart."*

**Domains**: the breaking of prophecy, the unmaking of destiny, rebellion against the Weave. **Symbol**: a shattered hourglass with no sand. **Worshiped by**: outcasts, revolutionaries, those who wish to erase their foretold fate. **Allies**: [[Vel'Zyren]], Valthos, Ytheis. **Enemies**: [[Astria]], Kaelor, [[Vaithis]].

Doesn't bend fate, annihilates it — ensures some prophecies never come to pass, called upon in desperation by those seeking to erase their own foretold doom. Where [[Vaithis]] illuminates alternative paths, Nytheris burns the predestined road to ash entirely.
