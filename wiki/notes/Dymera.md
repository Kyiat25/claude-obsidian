# Dymera, The Maiden of the Foam

*"Not all water seeks to destroy. Some waves exist only to dance."*

**Domains**: gentle waves, the playfulness of the ocean, light upon water. **Symbol**: a curling wave tipped in white foam. **Worshiped by**: children, beach-dwellers, those who find peace by the sea. **Rivals**: [[Voraxis]], [[Jorvethis]], [[Aelthys]]. **Allies**: Selmira, [[Marethis]], Syralis.

One of the few Water spirits who brings no fear — the tide that kisses the shore rather than drowning. Even her joy is fleeting; brief, beautiful, but never permanent.
