# Kelphor, The Drifting One

*"The ocean does not stay in one place. Why should you?"*

**Domains**: wandering tides, aimless journeys, the power of the lost. **Symbol**: a wave curling in an unnatural direction. **Worshiped by**: nomads, wanderers, those who leave home behind. **Rivals**: [[Marethis]], Rhyzel, Kaemor. **Allies**: Nyssara, Orynthos, Ilyra.

The reason ships drift off course for no reason, the pull that calls people from home and ensures they never return. Some say he is not a god at all, but a sailor who once drifted too far. Offers no destination, only the journey — unlike [[Tylos]], who guides travelers *to* somewhere.
