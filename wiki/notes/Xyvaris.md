# Xyvaris, The Broken Promise

*"You thought fate would bind me forever. You were wrong. But you do not yet understand what it has taken from me."*

**Domains**: the breaking of divine bargains, the unknown cost of escaping fate, unraveling oaths. **Symbol**: a severed red thread unraveling from an ancient scroll. **Worshiped by**: those who regret their divine oaths, oathbreakers, dealmakers who wish to escape their promises. **Allies**: [[Nytheris]], [[Thyvael]], [[Elyon]]. **Enemies**: [[Kaelor]], [[Vel'Zyren]], [[Telys]].

Ensures divine oaths can be broken — but never without consequence. Some who defy their oaths find loved ones forgotten, names erased, or victories turned into defeats.
