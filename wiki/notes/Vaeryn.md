# Vaeryn, The Ashen Star

*"You were cast out. You were exiled. You were forgotten. But you are not alone. Follow the flame, and you will find your way."*

**Domains**: exiles, outcasts, those who have nowhere left to go. **Symbol**: a lone flame in the darkness, forming a guiding star. **Worshiped by**: the displaced, the hunted, the persecuted. **Allies**: [[Kaldris]], Vaelcor, Lysara. **Enemies**: [[Soltharion]], [[Vaeldros]], Xalros.

The star flickering in the darkness for the lost, the promise there's still a path forward. Myth holds he was the last of his kind, his people burned away by the gods, yet he carried their fire with him, wandering the world so their memory would never fade.
