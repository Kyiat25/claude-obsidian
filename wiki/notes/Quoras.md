# Quoras, The Sunken Sage

*"You wish to know what lies beneath? Then you must first understand why it was hidden."*

**Domains**: forgotten knowledge, the secrets of the deep, wisdom that should not be found. **Symbol**: a submerged book, its pages dissolving into water. **Worshiped by**: scholars, deep-sea explorers, those who seek ancient wisdom. **Rivals**: [[Vaelora]], [[Lyrithis]], Nyxthys. **Allies**: Varashti, Rymora, Iskorel.

Does not guard knowledge — he ensures those who seek it understand its price. Those who wish answers from Quoras must first give up knowledge or a secret of their own.
